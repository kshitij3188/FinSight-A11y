# What is my billing account and how do I change it?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/billing-account

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Having the option to change your billing account or to have a dedicated billing account is really useful, especially when you don't want your bunq invoices to get mixed up with your other payments.

This gives you the option to have a dedicated account just to pay your bunq bills!

### How can I change my billing Bank Account?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Click on Accounting

  3. Select Invoices and Billing

  4. Select "Invoice To"

  5. Select one of your Bank Accounts to do the job!

## What's a billing account?

A billing account is the account you use to pay your bunq invoices! When you open a bunq account, your first default account is selected as your billing account. When your billing date comes, all standing invoices for your contracted products will be deducted from your selected billing account.
