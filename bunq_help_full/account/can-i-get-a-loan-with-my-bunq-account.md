# Can I get a loan with my bunq account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-i-get-a-loan-with-my-bunq-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We’re here to break free from the status quo, and create the bank with you in mind. Giving you the freedom to live life on your terms.

In order to do so, we ensure that you can't fall into debt by **not offering loans or overdraft.** This way you can freely use your money without the burden of debt.

## What about Mortgages?

Buying a home is one of the biggest moments of your life. And we're here to make this easy! As announced back in December 2021 - bunq becomes the first digital bank in Europe to offer mortgages 🎉 Learn all about bunq Easy Mortgages [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-easy-mortgages>)

## What about Instalments?

If you’re buying a product or service at a company offering you to pay in instalments, you can use your bunq card or bunq account to pay the instalments to the seller.
