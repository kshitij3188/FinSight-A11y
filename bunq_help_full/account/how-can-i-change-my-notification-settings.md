# How can I change my notification settings?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-change-my-notification-settings

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

You can always stay on top of your account, payments and requests notifications, with real-time updates from the bunq App. 

Find out how to access your Notification Settings below. 

## How do I change my notification settings in the app?

You can access and edit your notifications by simply following these steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Choose **Notifications**

  4. Select the notifications you want to activate/deactivate:

     * Account updates

     * Payments and transactions

     * Payment requests

     * Travel tips

     * Trees updates

     * Updates from us
