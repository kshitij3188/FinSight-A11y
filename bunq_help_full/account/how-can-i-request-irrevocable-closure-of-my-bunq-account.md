# How can I request irrevocable closure of my bunq account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-request-irrevocable-closure-of-my-bunq-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We understand that change is part of life and there may come a time when we'll need to part ways. 

Closing your bunq account is made to be as easy for you as everything else that we do. That's why you can close your bunq account by yourself via the app in just a minute — with the option to reactivate it at any later point. You can read more about how you can do so [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-close-my-bunq-account>). 

If you’d like to irrevocably close your account with us, our team is always here to take care of it for you. 

## How can I request irrevocable closure of my bunq account? 

If you’d like to disable the option of ever reactivating your bunq account, you can reach out to us with a request to permanently close your account via Support by following the steps below: 

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on 🟢 Support

  3. Ask to close your account permanently and we’ll take care of it for you. 

## What happens to my permanently closed account? 

Closing your account permanently means: 

  * You won't have access to your account again, such as for tax information, Support, or other cases. 

  * You can’t reactivate your account. 

  * You lose access to export any bank statements. 

  * Your contract with us is irrevocably terminated.

💡Pro Tip: Make sure to export all necessary bank statements prior to your account’s permanent closing. You can see how to export a bank statement [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3207>). 

## Do you want to close one of your Bank Accounts instead? 

[Click here](<https://www.bunq.com/link/close-account>) or follow the steps mentioned below:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select your Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Close Bank Account**

## What if I’d like to re-open my bunq account? 

If you close your bunq account permanently through our Support team and decide you want to reopen your account, you’ll have to open a brand new account and go through the verification process anew. 

## Are there any fees for closing my bunq account? 

There are no fees for closing your bunq account. We do **not** ask for any fees to close bunq accounts nor do we work with any third parties such as:

  * [www.terminationexperts.com](<http://www.terminationexperts.com/>)

  * [www.123opzeggen.nl](<http://www.123opzeggen.nl/>)

that provide account termination services. 

You can close your bunq account by yourself at any time or reach out to us in your secure in-app Support Chat — and we’ll take care of closing your account for you 🌈
