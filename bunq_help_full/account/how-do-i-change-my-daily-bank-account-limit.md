# How do I change my daily bank account limit?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-change-my-daily-bank-account-limit
**Topics:** Account, Bank Accounts

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We want to give you full control over your money, including deciding how much money you can spend every day. That's why we created an account limit you can easily adjust to the limit that fits your needs.

## How can I change my limit?

Do you want to change your limit to an amount that suits you best? This is how you do it:

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Select the Bank Account you wish to adjust the daily limit of

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on "Daily limit"

  5. Adjust to the maximum daily amount you wish 🙌 

  6. Keep in mind that **your daily limit per Bank Account cannot be over €1,000,000**

Your safety is our top priority, and we are always careful with the money you send after increasing your daily limit. Therefore, some payments may be delayed, due to extra measures like our [Safety Shield](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/security-measures-safety-shield >) security measure.

**Note: You can follow these steps exclusively in the bunq app only. Currently, this feature hasn't been introduced yet to bunq Web.**
