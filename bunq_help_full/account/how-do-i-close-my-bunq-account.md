# How do I close my bunq account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-close-my-bunq-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Of course we’d love for you to always be a bunqer but we get that life happens and sometimes closing your bunq account might be the right choice for you.

We want to make sure you have a convenient experience throughout your whole time with us, from start to finish. That’s why we provide a way for you to close your bunq account by yourself straight from your bunq app within minutes. 

### How do I close my bunq account?

You can close your bunq account by yourself straight from your app by following the steps below:

  1. Tap on your 👤Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on your **bunq Plan**

  3. Tap on "Close Account"

  4. Confirm by tapping on "Close my account"

When you close your bunq account this way, you can’t receive or send payments and you'll no longer be charged a monthly fee if you’re on a paid plan. You can always re-activate it at a later time and continue banking with us.

❗️Please keep in mind that if you close your Business account, your Personal account will remain active. You'll need to close your Personal Account separately. 

## What happens when I close my bunq account?

When you close your bunq account via the app: 

  * You can't send or receive payments.

  * You'll no longer be paying monthly fees.

  * You can't sign up for a new bunq account with the same credentials of your closed account.

  * The Deposit Guarantee Scheme protection continues.

After closing your account this way, there are several actions you can still perform:

  * Login and see your account.

  * You can switch Users from your Profile. 

  * [Contact our Support Center](<https://together.bunq.com/d/32181>).

  * Export a Bank Statement by following the steps mentioned [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50053>). 

  * Export an Annual overview. 

  * [Reactivate your account](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24392>) at any time. 

## Do you want to close one of your bank account instead?

[Click here](<https://www.bunq.com/link/close-account>) or follow the steps mentioned below:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select your Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Close Bank Account**

## Change your mind? Once a bunqer, always a bunqer! 

To reopen your bunq account, follow the steps below:

  1. Download and open the bunq app

  2. Use your telephone number to log in

  3. "This account has been closed" screen appears

  4. 🟢 Reopen account

  5. Tap on **Reactivate account**

  6. Done! 

**Important note:** Keep in mind that there’s no need to open a brand new bunq account from scratch - simply log back in to reactivate your old account. Forgotten your security code? See how to restore it [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3980>). 

## FAQ

  1. **Can I close my bunq account if I still have money in my bank accounts?**  
Yes, you can close your account with money in it (i.e. with a positive balance). Your funds will stay within the app. You can access them any time by [re-opening your account](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24392>).

  

  2. **Can I close my bunq account if I’m in debt?**  
You can't close your account if you have more than €15 debt.

  

  3. **Can I close my bunq account if I have an activated Switch Service?**  
You can't close the account if you have Dutch Switch Service active from another bank to bunq, but you can do so if you have an active Switch Service from bunq to another bank.

  

  4. **How can I export a statement from my closed account?**  
Simply open the bunq app and enter your security code to enter your closed account. Once you login successfully you’ll see a screen confirming that your account is closed. From there, follow these easy steps:

  
1\. Click on 🟠 Export Statement  
2\. Select Bank Account for export  
3\. Set the time Period  
4\. Choose the Format  
5\. Export the statement

  

  5. **How can I stop receiving emails from bunq?**

When you close your account, please keep in mind that bunq reserves the right to send you further marketing communication. If you want to unsubscribe from our email updates, you can simply follow these easy steps:

  
1\. Go to your email  
2\. Click on the last marketing email you’ve received from us  
3\. Scroll to the bottom  
4\. Select **Unsubscribe**  
5\. Follow the hyperlink’s flow

  

  6. **Why do I see my closed bunq account in annual tax returns?**

If you’ve closed your bunq account but still see your bunq IBAN in your tax returns for the year, don’t worry — this does not mean that your account is active. To make life easy for all of our users, we share your information with the Dutch Tax Authority once a year in a single report, and they may forward them to your local tax authorities. That’s why your IBAN can appear in your tax declaration, even if you closed your bunq account. 

  

  7. **What if I want to close my bunq account with no option to re-open it?**

Read more [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-request-irrevocable-closure-of-my-bunq-account>) to find out how you can request this type of closure for your bunq account.
