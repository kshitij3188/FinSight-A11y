# How do I display or change my full legal name?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-display-or-change-my-full-legal-name

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Looking to have your full legal name used for [payments](<https://together.bunq.com/d/115>) and [export statements](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3207>)? Now you can do so by selecting your preferred legal name from within your app Profile!

The options from which you can choose are:

  * Display your initials and last name.

  * Display your first and last name only.

  * Display your full name.

### How can I check my legal name in the app?

Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Personal information**

  3. Tap on **Legal name** and select your full name ✅

## How do I change my Personal legal name?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Personal Information**

  3. Tap on **Legal Name**

  4. Select **I'd like to use a different name** and follow the flow

  5. A conversation with our Support Centre will automatically open here. Your personal AI assistant Finn will forward your request to the relevant team who will review your request and provide you with their decision as soon as possible ✅

**Note:** Keep in mind that the name displayed should match the one in your Identification Document. If your Identification Document still doesn't match that name, you should provide us with one of the following [documents](<https://together.bunq.com/d/49797>).

## How do I change my Business Name?

  1. Switch to your Business Home Tab 

  2. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  3. Tap on **Company Information**

  4. Select **Business Legal Name** and follow the flow

  5. A conversation with our Support Centre will automatically open here. Your personal AI assistant Finn will forward your request to the relevant team who will review your request and provide you with their decision as soon as possible ✅

**Note:** Keep in mind that you would be asked to attach a KvK excerpt containing the new legal name as a PDF.

## Can I add my professional title to my legal name?

Indeed, you can! All you need to do is to contact our support team and provide a legal document proving your title. For example: if you want to add Dr. to your name you can do so by providing a document proving that you claimed this title. From there we'll make sure to update the information accordingly in your account 🙌
