# How do I edit my Refill Notifications?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-edit-my-refill-notifications

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We’ll notify you when your balance is low so you can top it up within a few taps. This way, your account will always be ready for the activities you want and love to do.

### How can I edit my notifications?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Select the Bank Account

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on 🟡 Refill Notification

  5. Turn on the toggle

  6. Choose metrics

  7. Done ✅

## Good to know

  * You can also choose if you want to receive these notifications or not just by selecting the **Bank Account > Settings > Refill Notification (On / Off depending on your wishes)**.

  * If you tap on a **Bank Account > Settings**, you'll also be able to select **how much money you'd like to add to your account when you don't have much money** and **when the notifications should be sent (if balance is below X amount of money, you'll then receive the notification if you've enabled it)**.
