# How do I export a statement for my closed Bank Account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-statement-for-my-closed-bank-account

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

Follow these steps to export a bank statement for your closed Bank Account:

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home tab

  2. Go to Your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and tap on "Archive"

  4. Tap on Bank Accounts

  5. Select the Bank Account in question

  6. Tap on 🟠 Export Statement

## Do you need a different statement?

  * You can see [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3207>) how to export **a regular bank statement**. You may need one from the government when tax season rolls around, or even just to have for your own personal administration. 

  * You can see [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/11710-export-statement-of-a-single-transaction/8>) how to download a statement for **a single transaction**. This is useful when you want proof of a single payment, without other transactions being shown on the same document.

  * You can see [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2523-how-do-i-export-my-annual-account-overview>) how to download **an annual statement** , with an overview of all your bunq IBANs for the previous year.

  * You can see [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50030>) how to export a **card statement** for all payments that were done with one card during a certain period of time
