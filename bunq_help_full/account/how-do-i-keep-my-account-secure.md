# How do I keep my account secure?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-keep-my-account-secure
**Topics:** Frequently Asked Questions

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Your security is our top priority! That’s why we’re sharing some easy and effective tips to help keep your account and your personal data safe.

### What should I be aware of?

  * Keep your [bunq app updated](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3629>).

  * Don’t share your personal data (login details, card information, TIN, phone and email, etc.) with anyone.

  * Always check the emails, SMS, and messages sent to you for spelling mistakes and any other alarming signs like strange addresses, numbers or fake accounts.

  * Always check URLs before clicking on them.

  * Don’t pick up the phone to strange, foreign or unknown numbers.

We created a few features to further increase your account’s security! See how to set them up below 👇

## Auto-Logout

To ensure you’re always logged out when you close the bunq app, you can follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Click on ⚙️ **Settings** in the top right corner

  3. Tap on [**Security**](<https://go.bunq.com/link/security>)

  4. Tap on **Auto Logout**

  5. Tap on **Stay logged in for**

  6. Choose **0 seconds**

Further information on this can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/27057>).

## Rotating CVC

A crucial detail for making online purchases with your card is the CVC code. Unfortunately, this is also a key piece of information for fraudsters attempting to misuse your card. To enhance your card's security, you have the option to set up a rotating CVC code.

You can click [here](<https://go.bunq.com/link/cvc>) or follow the steps below to find your CVC:

  1. Go to the 💳 **Cards** Tab

  2. Select the card that you want to set up a rotating CVC for

  3. Tap on **CVC Code**

  4. Toggle on/off for **Automatically Regenerate**

More details can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50055>).

## Biometric login

Biometric login can also increase the safety of your account. For the activation of this feature, follow the steps below:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Click ⚙️ **Settings** in the top right corner

  3. Select **Security**

  4. Enable the **Biometric Login** feature

Further information and details can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/56658>).

## Using Virtual Credit Card for online purchases

Although using your Virtual Credit Card for online transactions doesn't guarantee protection against data theft if the website is fraudulent, it does give you the flexibility to block your card instantly when you need to. This is because you won't need to replace a physical card and wait for a new one to be delivered.

Additionally, to protect your card(s), you can enable the **Localized card protection feature**. When enabled, your cards will only work within a 100 km radius of your phone’s location. Learn more about it [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2815>).

## Multiple failed payments

Mastercard has incorporated a security feature into its cards to detect fraudulent transactions. If your card is used for more than three unsuccessful payment attempts, Mastercard will permanently block it for your protection.

## Two-Factor Authentication

In case you initiate a transaction exceeding €1,000, you'll be prompted to authenticate the payment. This can be done by entering your **Security Code** or using **Biometrics** , provided you've set it up.

For transactions under €1,000, there's a preset limit of €250. Once this limit is reached, two-factor authentication is required to continue with the payment. 

## What can I do if I'm a victim of fraud? 

Dealing with fraud can be a really unpleasant experience. It’s completely natural to feel angry or frustrated in a situation like this, but please know that you’re not alone and bunq has got your back.

We've put together a guide on what you can do if you find yourself to be a victim of fraud which you can check out —[ here](<./anti-fraud-guide-ive-been-scammed-what-can-i-do>).
