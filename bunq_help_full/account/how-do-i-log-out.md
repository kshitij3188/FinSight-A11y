# How do I log out?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-log-out
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Wondering how you can secure your bunq account to keep it protected at all times? Keep reading to find out! 🔐

At bunq, keeping you safe is our highest priority. This is why we give you the option to quickly log out manually as well as to set the bunq app to automatically log you out after a specific period of time.

### How do I manually log out?

To manually log out, you can follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Click on ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Scroll all the way to the bottom and click on **Logout**

As a shortcut, you can also follow these steps to quickly log out:

  1. Tap and hold on the **Profile** icon in the top left corner of the bunq app.

  2. Tap on **Log out**.

## Automatically log out when closing the bunq app

You can also adjust your **Auto Logout** to ensure that you're always logged out as soon as you leave the bunq app.

You can do this via these steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ **Setting** in the top right corner

  3. Tap on **Security**

  4. Tap on **Auto Logout**

  5. Tap on **Stay logged in for**

  6. Choose **0 seconds**
