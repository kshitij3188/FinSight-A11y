# How do I open an account as a child?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-open-an-account-as-a-child
**Topics:** Signup

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

You can easily open a Personal account as a child or a teenager, even if you don’t have your own Identification Document yet.

Child accounts are available across all [supported countries](<./who-can-open-a-bunq-personal-account>) which we operate in. 

### How do I open my account?

  1. Open the app

  2. Tap on **Get Started**

  3. Enter and confirm your phone number 

  4. Tap on **Get bunq For Me**

  5. Fill in your personal details and select your parent 

  6. Accept T&Cs 

  7. Invite your parent to approve your account as your legal guardian

  8. You can start using bunq as soon as your parent approves your account ✨

 _Note 1: You’ll have 90 days to provide us with your Tax Identification Number (TIN) after the verification process is completed._

_Note 2: Your parent can approve your request only when they open and verify their own Personal account first._

## How can I verify my identity?

  * **If you’re under 16** \- If you’re under the age of 16, there's no need for identity verification.

  * **If you’re at an age between 16 and 18** \- Once you get verified and turn 16, you’ll receive an in-app notification to complete [identity verification](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>). This step secures your account and allows you to log in on a new device without needing parental approval. After we review your Identification Document, we might ask you to verify it again if the previous attempt didn’t meet our requirements (expired or blurry ID, no selfie match).

  * **When you turn 18** \- When you turn 18, the link to your parent’s account will be removed automatically, with no changes to your account. About a month before your 18th birthday, you'll need to accept the Terms & Conditions yourself. If not accepted by then, your account will temporarily be unusable until this step is completed.

**Important note for our Spanish users:** To comply with Spanish regulations, we need to have a valid identity document for you, regardless of your age. This means that if you reside in Spain or hold a Spanish IBAN, we might request a picture of your ID if we don't already have it.

## How can I share my Tax Identification Number (TIN)?

You don’t need to provide your TIN during signup. You’ll have 90 days after the account creation to submit it. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the 🏡Home Tab 

  2. Tap on the TIN event under "Action Needed"

  3. Provide us with the relevant proof of your TIN

## Which subscription plans can I choose from?

When you sign up and you’re under 18, you automatically get a [bunq Pro](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3762>) account which you can enjoy for free.

You can then choose to upgrade to bunq Elite for just €9 per month!

## What steps should my parents take after I invite them to approve my account opening?

After you invite your parents, they’ll get a notification to confirm your account. Once confirmed, you can start using it with all the benefits of your plan.

## What’s included in my account?

When you open your bunq account, the following bank accounts will be automatically set up for you:

  * 1 Savings Account, if you're under 6 years old 

  * 1 payment Bank Account and 1 Savings Bank Account, if you’re between 7 and 17 years old

You can then open up to 25 bank accounts which are supported on the bunq Pro plan.

Depending on the plan you choose, you can also order a card and create additional Bank Accounts.

## What if my Parent doesn't have a bunq account yet?

No worries! If your parent doesn’t have a bunq Personal account yet, they’ll receive an invitation via SMS to create one.

They’ll need to sign up, complete their verification, and get their account verified to accept your invite and approve your account. Once your account gets confirmed, you can start using it with all the benefits of your plan.

## My Parent created my account. How can I gain access?

To grant you access, your Parent can follow these steps:

  1. Go to their Home Tab 

  2. Switch to the Child Account from top of the screen

  3. Go to the Child Profile and tap on “Give Access” 

  4. Tap on **Give Account Access**

  5. Select You from their Contacts 

  6. Set a Security Code

  7. Done ✅

Afterwards, you need to log in from your own device following the steps bellow:

  1. Open the bunq app

  2. Sign in with your telephone number and the above-set Security Code

  3. You should authenticate yourself by uploading a relevant Identification Document

  4. Done ✅

## How can I login without an Identification Document?

If you don’t have a valid identification document due to your age and need to log in on a new device, you can send your Parent a login approval request via an in-app notification. Your Parent can then approve or decline it directly from their account.

## Why isn't my account verified in 5 minutes?

We have several automated checks to verify your account. If one of these checks fails, we may need to ask you for additional information. This could be any of the following:

  * Your birth certificate

  * Adoption papers

  * Court order confirming custody

  * Court order establishing legal guardianship

  * Official documents from the Government or Embassy that clearly show your guardian’s name, your name, and the relationship between you both.

_**Note:** Make sure you provide us with a clear picture of the entire document._
