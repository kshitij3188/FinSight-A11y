# How do I reopen my closed Bank Account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-reopen-my-closed-bank-account

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

You've closed one of your Bank Accounts because you thought you didn’t need it anymore, but now you expect a transfer or refund to this account? Or you simply just change your mind and want to keep using the account. Don't worry, we got your back!

### How do I reopen my closed Bank Account?

If you closed the account less than **60 days** ago, you can reopen the account from your archived accounts. After 60 days, the account is permanently closed and can’t be reopened anymore.

Follow the steps below to reopen your Bank account:

  1. Go to the 🏠Home tab

  2. Go to Your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap on **Archived**

  4. Tap on **Bank Accounts**

  5. Choose the Bank Account in question

  6. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  7. Tap on 🟢 Reopen account

  8. Done!

**Note:** Currently, it's not possible to reopen [Joint Bank Accounts](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48586>).
