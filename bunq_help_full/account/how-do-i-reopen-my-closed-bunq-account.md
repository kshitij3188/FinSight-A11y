# How do I reopen my closed bunq account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-reopen-my-closed-bunq-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Great to see that you want to reactivate your account and start using bunq again. We’re happy to welcome you back!

## How can I reopen my bunq account?

  1. Download and open the bunq app

  2. Use your e-mail or telephone number to log in

  3. "This account has been closed" screen appears

  4. 🟢 Reopen account

  5. Click on Reactivate account

  6. Done! 

If you're requested to provide an Identity document, check out [this page](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/why-is-an-identity-document-requested-from-me-when-i-reactivate-my-account>) for more information.

## How can I reopen my Business account?

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home Tab

  2. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  3. Tap on **Archive**

  4. Select the Business Account you'd like to reopen

  5. Tap on 🟢 Reopen Account

  6. Confirm by tapping on **Reactivate my account**

## Good to know

  * Your cards get reactivated automatically.

  * You get the same plan that you had before.

  * Your billing day is moved according to the days you were frozen. For example: your billing day is usually on 17th. If you freeze your account for 4 days, your billing day will be on 21st from now on.
