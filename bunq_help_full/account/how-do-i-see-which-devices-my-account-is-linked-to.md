# How do I see which devices my account is linked to?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-see-which-devices-my-account-is-linked-to
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

If you’ve logged into your bunq account on multiple devices, you can easily keep track of which ones from your Security Settings.

### Where can I find my devices?

To see which devices are linked to your bunq account.

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  3. Select Security

  4. Tap on Devices 📱

## How to remove a device linked to my bunq account?

To maintain control over your account, you can manually remove devices linked to your bunq account. Here's how you can do it in a few simple steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  3. Select Security

  4. Tap on Devices 📱

  5. Tap on **Forget** next to the device you want to remove. You will not be able to log into your bunq account from this device until you have verified your identity. By [resetting your Security Code](<./how-do-i-reset-my-security-code>), all other active login sessions will end. If you want to log into your bunq account on a new device, you need to again verify your identity. This measure enhances the security of your account, particularly if you suspect it has been compromised.
