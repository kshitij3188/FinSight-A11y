# How to add an already existing account to my overview?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-add-an-already-existing-account-to-my-overview
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

In order to have a full overview of your account, you can always add existing accounts to your Home tab. Simply follow these easy steps to add any active account to your overview:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Long-press on the **Profile** button

  3. Select **More options**

  4. Click on **+**

  5. Follow the flow and add the account right away!
