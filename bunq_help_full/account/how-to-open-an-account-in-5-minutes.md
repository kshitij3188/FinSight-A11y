# How to open an account in 5 minutes? 🚀

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-open-an-account-in-5-minutes
**Topics:** Signup

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Our aim is to help you make life easy.

That’s why we promise to open your bank account in just 5 minutes from your phone. You don’t have to waste time on paperwork or visiting a branch! For us to deliver our 5-minute-promise, we need a few things from you. You can find an overview below 🚀

During the account signup process, it's important to disable your VPN. VPN usage may show that you're not in your actual country of residence, which could prevent you from proceeding further with the signup.

## Let’s get started

Never used bunq before? Let’s get you signed up — simply:

  1. Open your bunq App

  2. Tap on **Get Started**

  3. Enter your phone number 

  4. Verify your phone number

Welcome to the bunq App – this is your gateway to explore our app. Get a feel for the app by browsing through the Tabs and see how our [new V5](<https://together.bunq.com/d/62331>) design comes to life 🌈

Love what you see? Signing up takes less than 5 minutes. Just tap on any screen to get started — it’s that easy.

## How do I sign up in 5 minutes?

From any tab in your bunq app, you can:

  1. Tap on **Sign up in 5 minutes**

  2. Select an account type

  3. Fill in your personal information 

  4. Fill in your email address 

  5. Agree to T&Cs 

  6. Fill in your residential address 

  7. Let’s verify your identity - tap on **Start Verification**

  8. Follow the verification flow: scan your identification document and take a selfie 🤳

  9. Wait 5 minutes while we get your account verified

  10. All done ✨

Heads up: if you’re signing up for a Business Account, we’ll ask for some additional details about your Business during the sign up process — so keep your Business documents at hand!

## Why do I see a Welcome Back screen?

Fill in your phone number and see a **Welcome Back** screen? We’ve got you covered. This means you already have an active or previously verified account with us. Therefore, we redirect you to log into your existing account instead of creating a new one. To log back in, simply:

  1. Fill in your security code 

  2. We’ll need to verify it’s really you - tap on **Start Verification**

  3. Follow the verification flow 

  4. Welcome back to your account ✨

Forgot your Security code, or that you already have an account? No worries - it happens to the best of us. You can read up on how to recover your Security code and account in a matter of minutes [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3980>).

## What’s included in my account?

Whether you open a **Personal** or **Business** account, you can enjoy 1 month of the [bunq Elite plan](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-benefits-of-the-bunq-elite-plan>) for free.

#### Personal Accounts

You’ll get the following by default when you open a Personal account from the sign up flow:

  * 1 Payment Bank Account

  * 1 Savings Account

#### Business Accounts

You’ll get the following by default when you open a Business Account from the sign up flow:

  * 1 Business Bank Account 

  * 1 Business Savings Account

Since our Business Accounts require a Personal Account, you will also automatically receive:

  * 1 Personal Bank Account 

  * 1 Personal Savings Account

All Personal Accounts opened alongside a Business Account will be defaulted to the bunq Free plan ✨

#### Joint Accounts

When you invite a partner during sign-up, or join bunq through a Partner invite, you’ll receive your own Personal and Savings Accounts, plus shared access to:

  * 1 Joint bank account 

  * 1 Joint savings account

#### Child Accounts

You can open a free bunq Pro account for your child, which includes the following by default:

  * 1 Child Savings Account, if your child is under 6 years old 

  * 1 Payment Bank Account if your child is between 7 and 17 years old

These are bank accounts which are open by default for you. You can then open additional Payments and Savings Accounts. Read more about how you can add a new Bank Account [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/39139>).

You can also order digital and physical cards on your free bunq Elite trial. You can read more about how you can order a card [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936>).

You’re always in control. You can always switch your bunq Plan during or after your trial. To do so, follow the steps outlined [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-change-my-plan>).

## 5-minute-Personal account 💸

Opening a Personal account with us in 5 minutes is possible when you have all the required information at hand, so that we make the necessary checks right away and welcome you on board!

  * During the verification we’ll ask you for your Identification Document, Passport, Residence Permit, or Driver's license. You can check all the accepted documents [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3355>)

  * Take the perfect picture! When we ask for a photo of your Identification Document, make sure to stand still and ensure the lighting is good enough so that the info on your Identification Document is readable.

You can read more about identity verification [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>). If you've left the verification process for later, [click here](<https://www.bunq.com/link/verify-account>) to proceed with it.

## 5-minute-Business account 💼

In order to open a Business account in 5 minutes, it’s important you have the necessary documents at hand. Here are some pro-tips on how to prepare for the process:

  * Check the accepted legal forms in advance so that we can open your account in 5 minutes. You can see all the legal forms we accept [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/221>)

  * Collect all the documents we need for your verification. You can see a detailed explanation on all of the necessary documentation [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24017>) 👀
