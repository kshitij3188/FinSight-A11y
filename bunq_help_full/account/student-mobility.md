# Earn Cashback with Student Mobility 🚋

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/student-mobility
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, Netherlands 🇳🇱

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Get €15 Cashback by signing up with us through Student Mobility!

All new students studying in 🇳🇱the Netherlands who sign up between August 14, 2025 to December 31, 2025 get €15 Cashback on their next payment to Student Mobility when paying **directly** with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [Student Mobility signup link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/Studentmobility>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your [bunq account](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2933>).

  3. Add your new bunq credit card details to your Student Mobility account.

  4. Pay for your first Student Mobility invoice.

  5. Earn €15 Cashback on your payment, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

To be eligible for this promotion, you must: :

  * Live in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands.

  * Sign up for bunq through the Student Mobility link.

  * Fill out your credit card details in your Student Mobility account - iDEAL invoice payments aren't eligible for this Cashback.

  * Be 18 years old or older.

  * Sign up between August 14, 2025 to December 31, 2025.

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Student Mobility in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment 

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via iDEAL, PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
