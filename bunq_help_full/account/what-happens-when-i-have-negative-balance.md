# What happens when I have negative balance?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-happens-when-i-have-negative-balance
**Topics:** Plans

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

If you don’t have enough funds in your account to cover an invoice, your balance may drop below zero.

### What happens when you have a negative balance?

Once your account balance falls into the negative, we’ll notify you via an in-app reminder and email. **If your balance remains negative for 14 days, we’ll automatically switch your plan to**[**bunq Free**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1502>). Some accounts aren’t eligible to switch to the bunq Free plan. If this is the case, and you don’t top up your balance for 30 days, we’ll freeze your account.

## What makes my account ineligible for bunq Free?

Your account is not eligible to be switched to the bunq Free plan if:

  * You have an active Joint Account;

  * You’re managing a Child Account as a parent;

  * You have Business Account for a business that’s not freelance; 

  * If you have a Foreign Currency Account with funds deposited.

## What will happen with my account?

  * If we switch your plan to bunq Free:

    * **Close Bank and Savings Accounts:** In case you have more than 3 Bank Accounts, we’ll close all but the first 3 you opened. All balances will be moved to these accounts.

    * **Freeze Cards:** All cards, except for your oldest Virtual Card, will be frozen.

  * If we freeze your account:

    * You won’t be able to use it until you add money to your account using one of our [top up methods](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2933>).

## How do I resolve my negative balance?

  * You can transfer money to your IBAN from one of your other accounts that has enough balance.

  * You can also use one of the other [top-up methods](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2933>) to add money to your bunq account.

Once you’ve topped up your account, you can switch back to your previous plan by following these steps:

  1. Go to 👤 Profile

  2. Tap on Plans

  3. Select Switch plan

 _**Important:** Keep in mind that closed Bank Accounts and cards won’t reopen automatically—you’ll need to reactivate them manually._

## How can I reopen my closed Bank Accounts?

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home Tab

  2. Go to Your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap on **Archived**

  4. Tap on **Bank Accounts**

  5. Choose the Bank Account or card in question

  6. Go to ⚙️Settings

  7. Tap on 🟢 Reopen account
