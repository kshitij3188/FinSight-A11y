# What is an e-money account and how do I use it?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-an-e-money-account-and-how-do-i-use-it
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

As a new user, you can instantly start using bunq upon downloading the app, initially accessing an e-money or 'bunq Demo' account. However, to fully unlock all of bunq's features, you should complete the verification process. You can find more information about the verification process [here](<./how-long-does-it-take-to-verify-my-paperwork>).

> Please note, this product is not eligible for the _Deposit Guarantee Scheme_. For more information, please check our page [here](<https://www.bunq.com/doc/dgs>).

### How does it work?

To start your e-money account, download the app and agree to the [Terms & Conditions](<https://www.bunq.com/doc/personal-account>). Enter your name and birth date to start. To order a Credit Card or Debit Card (Matercard), upgrade to a full bank account and provide your delivery address 💳

## What can I do with my e-money account?

  * Total transaction limit of €150 during the whole e-money period of your account (until you verify your account)

  * Not included international payments

  * Not included physical bunq cards

  * Not included Virtual Cards

  * Not included Joint Accounts

  * Not included opening of additional business accounts

  * Not included **iDEAL top up** Keep in mind that the e-money accounts aren’t covered by the [Deposit Guarantee Scheme](<https://www.bunq.com/doc/infosheet>)

## How do I manually upgrade to a full bank account?

You’ll automatically be prompted to upgrade after reaching the limits of your e-money account but you can also switch manually to a full bank account:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Select **Personal Information** 3\. You can then upgrade your account (make sure to keep your ID at hand!) by tapping on **Make sure you're secured**

> Your account may need verification. Learn more about verifying accounts [here](<./how-long-does-it-take-to-verify-my-paperwork>). Unverified accounts can also be invoiced, starting right after your 1-month free trial.

## Who can open an e-money account?

You can open an e-money account if you live in the following countries: Germany, France, Belgium, Italy, Ireland, Spain, Sweden, Greece, Bulgaria, Croatia, Austria, Czech Republic, Finland, Luxembourg, Lithuania, Malta, The Netherlands

## My child has opened an account without my knowledge - what should I do?

Your child has opened an e-money account, which can be created without identity verification under European regulations. These accounts have limited functionalities to prevent misuse. This type of accounts can be opened without parental approval. If you wish to upgrade your child's plan to a full bank account, your permission will be required. For instructions on how to open an account for your child, visit [this topic](<./how-do-i-open-an-account-for-my-child>).

## Why can't I use e-money when I sign up for bunq?

We always listen to your feedback in order to make your bunq experience even better! To make your signup experience even faster, easier, and more secure — we said goodbye to e-money accounts in our newest app redesign, V5. If you can't use e-money, it's because you're on the newest version of the app. Simply complete your signup in order to enjoy all the benefits of bunq's easy banking 🌈
