# Who can open a bunq Personal account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/who-can-open-a-bunq-personal-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

Currently, we're active in most countries in the European Economic Area (EEA), which you can check out below. We aim to make life easy for everyone, so our services are available for users of all ages who are in line with our Acceptance Policy. 

### Availability for EEA citizens and residents

All EEA citizens who reside in one of our supported EEA countries can sign up for bunq. Our supported EEA countries are the countries where we operate. See the full list below: 

🇦🇹 Austria, 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇧🇬 Bulgaria, 🇭🇷 Croatia, 🇨🇾 Cyprus, 🇨🇿 Czech Republic, 🇩🇰 Denmark, 🇪🇪 Estonia, 🇫🇮 Finland, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇬🇷 Greece, 🇭🇺 Hungary, 🇮🇪 Ireland, 🇮🇹Italy, 🇱🇻 Latvia, 🇱🇹 Lithuania, 🇱🇺 Luxembourg, 🇲🇹 Malta, 🇳🇱 the Netherlands, 🇵🇱 Poland, 🇵🇹 Portugal, 🇷🇴 Romania, 🇸🇰 Slovakia, 🇸🇮 Slovenia, 🇪🇸 Spain and 🇸🇪 Sweden

If you're not a European citizen but you live in one of the countries that we operate in, please check [which documents can be used to sign up](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3355>).

#### Non-EU territories linked to EU countries

Some countries are connected to European Union (EU) countries but aren’t part of the EU themselves. Residents of these places - known as Overseas Countries and Territories (OCTs) - can’t open a bunq account:

Aruba (NL), Guadeloupe (FR), Bonaire (NL), Curação (NL), French Polynesia (FR), French Southern and Antarctic Territories (FR), Greenland (DK), New Caledonia (FR), Saba (NL), Saint Barthélemy (FR), Saint Barthélemy (FR), Sint Eustatius (NL), Sint Maarten (NL), St. Pierre and Miquelon (FR), Wallis and Futuna Islands (FR).

## Availability for Business users

You can see who can use our Business services [here](<./who-can-open-a-bunq-business-account>) 🚀

## Availability for Minors

It’s possible to open a bunq account if you're under 18 years old, if you reside within one of our supported EEA countries. 

You'll need permission from a legal guardian who will also need to have an account with us before you can start using your own. Submitting the correct documents on your first verification attempt will ensure your 5-minute verification. Further information on the matter can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2961>).

## When can't I open a bunq account? 

Currently it's not possible to open a bunq account if:

  * You're under legal guardianship 

  * Have a pending bankruptcy 

  * Are under an active garnishment 

## What are the minimum device specs to use the app?

  * iOS: 13.0+

  * Android Lollipop 5.0+
