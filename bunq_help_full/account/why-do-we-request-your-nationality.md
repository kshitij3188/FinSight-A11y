# Why do we request your nationality?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/why-do-we-request-your-nationality

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

## What is required from me?

If we don’t have the necessary information regarding your nationality, we’ll reach you out through the app. All you need to do then is inform us by following the in-app flow mentioned below:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Awaiting Event**

  3. Tap **Add Nationality**

  4. Select your nationality

  5. Tap on **Submit**

  6. Done 🎉

## What if I have more than 1 nationality?

If you have more than 1 nationality, you should always inform us about all your nationalities.

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Scroll down to Personal Information

  3. Tap on **Personal Information** and scroll down to the **Nationalities** section

  4. Tap on **Add Nationality**

  5. Tap on **Submit**

  6. Done 🎉

## I’m a US citizen. Is there something additional required from me?

As a US citizen, we’ll also ask you for additional information through the app. To provide us with it, follow the instructions mentioned below:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on “Extra information needed” under Activity

  3. Select your nationality and tap on Submit

  4. Enter your SSN number

  5. Tap on Submit

  6. Done 🎉
