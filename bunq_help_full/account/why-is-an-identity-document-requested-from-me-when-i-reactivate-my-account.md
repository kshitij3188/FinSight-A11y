# Why is an Identity document requested from me when I reactivate my account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/why-is-an-identity-document-requested-from-me-when-i-reactivate-my-account
**Topics:** Signup

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

For the safety of your bunq account and funds, we will sometimes require an extra precautionary measure. This extra step is to ensure that access is only given to the appropriate individual. In this case we will need you to upload an Identity Document which you can send directly to us. 

### How do I reactivate my account?

  1. Open your bunq app 

  2. Enter and confirm the phone number of your account 

  3. Tap on Forgot Security code 

  4. Follow the reverification flow

## Photo requirements

Keep in mind that **we ONLY accept direct photos** of the Identity document*. Make sure the document provided:

  * Is still valid;

  * Belongs to you;

  * Is not blurry, cropped or scanned;

  * Has a front and back side;

  * Is original.

**We DON’T accept documents which are copies, print-screens or scanned** 🚫

## Reactivation of your Business account is also super easy

You need to be:

  * Director/Shareholder or Authorised Representative of the Business Account (be present in the Chambers of Commerce excerpt and the documents of the company);

  * Upload your Identity Document.

The submitted documents are reviewed manually. We aim to review all documents within 12 hours, however in some cases it might take longer.

If you are still locked out of the app or you forgot your security code, you can reset it in the app. Check out [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3980-help-im-locked-out-of-the-app-how-do-i-reset-my-security-code>) for more information.
