# bunq is expanding to the USA 🇺🇸

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-is-expanding-to-the-usa

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We’re very happy to share a major bunq milestone with you: on April 4, 2023, we officially applied for a **US banking license**.

We’d like to take a moment to thank you for helping us get here. Over the past 10 years, it has been truly amazing to see so many Europeans enjoy the bunq app and use it to make life easy🌈

If it wasn’t for your **support, trust and enthusiasm** , we wouldn’t be where we are today, and we’d love for you to join us for all the exciting things that lie ahead!

We’re more than excited to do the first step “across the pond” and **make life easy on not one, but two continents**! To stay updated on the latest developments, subscribe for updates via this [link](<https://www.bunq.com/blog/bringing-the-bank-of-the-free-to-the-land-of-the-free>). See you soon, USA! 🗽
