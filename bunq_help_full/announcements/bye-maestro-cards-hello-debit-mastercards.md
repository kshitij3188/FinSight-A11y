# Bye, Maestro cards! Hello, Debit Mastercards 👋

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bye-maestro-cards-hello-debit-mastercards
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Since August 30, 2024, we no longer offer Maestro Cards but Mastercard Debit Cards instead. These cards have wider acceptance worldwide, and they’re also suited for online payments.

In this topic we’ve listed the changes that will happen in the upcoming months.

### What’s a Mastercard Debit card?

Mastercard Debit cards will work as a regular Debit card - you’ll be able to pay with Mastercard Debit everywhere you go, the same way you were with Maestro. One difference is that your bunq Mastercard Debit card is accepted worldwide 🎉

## What’s the difference between Maestro Debit and Mastercard Debit?

The only differences are:

  * The name of the manufacturer (Mastercard Debit instead of Maestro Debit)

  * The wider range of merchants accepting your card (Now you’ll be able to use your Mastercard Debit card at more places than before, mostly internationally)

  * Mastercard Debit allows for online payments

## I have a bunq Maestro Debit card, what does that mean for me?

Your card will still work the same in every store until it expires - you can see the expiry date of your Maestro Debit card by going to the card details (Learn more [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3019>)).

Once your Maestro Debit Card expires, you can replace it with a Mastercard Debit Card by following the steps below:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards tab 

  2. Select the card you want a replacement for

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Replace

  5. Edit card details (if applicable) and select Next

  6. Review the information and tap **Order Card**

  7. You can Add your Card to Apple Pay or select **Not now** on the confirmation screen and you're all set!

More details about card replacements can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1974>).
