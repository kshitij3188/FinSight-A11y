# The bunq app now in Ukrainian and Russian

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/the-bunq-app-now-in-ukrainian-and-russian

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Together with the People for People foundation, we want to help Ukrainian and Russian refugees find shelter and safety in any way we can. That’s why, at the start of the Russia-Ukraine war, we made bunq available for free for refugees. From their feedback we learned that bunq would be easier to use if it was available in their native language.

We’re happy to announce that you can now use bunq in Ukrainian and Russian. 

### How can I use my app in Ukrainian or Russian?

All you need to do is **download the latest version of the app** and **make sure that your phone’s language is set to Ukrainian or Russian**. To change the language setting of your phone, click here for more info.
