# Update on Foreign Currencies

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/update-on-foreign-currencies
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

As of **August 23, 2022** , the opening of Foreign Currencies Bank accounts will be possible only for bunqers residing in the following countries:

  * 🇳🇱 The Netherlands

  * 🇩🇪 Germany

  * 🇪🇸 Spain

  * 🇮🇹 Italy

  * 🇫🇷 France

  * 🇮🇪 Ireland

These changes are applied due to recent changes in the Acceptance policy of Currency Cloud.

**Important:** If you have an already opened Foreign Currencies Bank account, and you don’t reside in any of the above-mentioned countries, worry not - the account will remain active and will operate as intended.
