# V4: bunq - easier than ever

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/v4-bunq-easier-than-ever

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Thanks to your constructive feedback, we've made bunq easier than ever. We took the features you love the most and put them in a new, sleek design. Welcome to bunq V4!

Want to see what’s new with the new fresh version of the app? Keep on reading and let’s catch up 🙌

## What's new? 

  * **Easy Budgeting** 💸

Budget as you spend - Set up dedicated Bank Accounts and budget from them automatically. Want to try it out? See how [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53929>)

  * **Widgets** ✨

Add widgets to your Home screen, so you always have instant access to what's important to you. Want to see how it works? Click [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53932>)

  * **Wheel of Fortune** 🍀

In the app, spin the Wheel of Fortune for great prizes for you and your friends. Get one spin every day, and earn more spins simply by using bunq. Check out more about it [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53930>)

  * **Places** 🗺

Find all the places your friends like under one tab, and effortlessly recommend places you like whenever you make a payment. Check out this and more exciting features from the freshly updated Activities Tab [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/47189>)

  * **Group Expenses** 🕺

Automatically track and easily split expenses with a group of friends. How? See [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/41417>)

  * **Update to: bunq Web** 🖥

Access all the bunq features you need to easily manage your money from any device you like. Check all the cool features you can use with the updated bunq Web [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48395>)

## What has been improved?

The whole app! Remember the Community tab? We decided to improve your app overview and we moved the tap in [bunq Web](<https://web.bunq.com/user/4387024>).

Now you can access Together and post in our Community from the comfort of your desktop. Just make sure to click on **“Visit Together”** In its place now you can find the brand new Green tab - check your CO2-free progress in just a tap.

Want to see how to operate with the freshly updated tabs?

  * Click [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53973>) to see more about the 🏠Home Tab

  * Click [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53974>) to see more about the 👯‍♀️Friends Tab

  * Click [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53931>) to see more about the **brand new** 🌳Green Tab

  * Click [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53976>) to see more about the 👤Profile Tab

## What was removed?

To make the bunq app easier than ever, we gave the features you love the most a more prominent stage, while taking your constructive feedback to heart and discontinuing some less popular ones:

  * bunq Jackpot

  * Community Tab

  * Subscriptions

  * Trophies

  * Tutorials

  * Support Announcements - now whenever we need to contact you, we’ll directly open a ticket for a faster and simpler discussion

  * Places To Go

  * Green Widget

  * Match Receipt

  * Add-ons section

  * Deals section

  * Cash Register
