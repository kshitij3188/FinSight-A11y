# Business account FAQ

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-account-faq
**Topics:** Frequently Asked Questions, Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Our business features are engineered to help you save time, every time! So you can focus on what’s truly important: your business. 💼

Please keep in mind that it's not possible to upgrade from Personal to Business plan — you'll need to open a separate Business account for your business 🚀

### What are the costs for a Business account?

The monthly fee for an [bunq Pro Business account](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3762>) is €13.99 per month. We also offer [bunq Elite Business](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38432>) which is available for €23.99 per month. With both plans 3 cards are included for free 🌈 We also offer [bunq Core Business account](<https://together.bunq.com/d/44002-easy-bank>) for €7.99 per month.

Our free plan, [bunq Free](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48095>), is exclusively available for freelancers. You can check the eligible legal forms for this plan [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50213>) for more details.

Additionally, bunq Pro Business users enjoy a 20% discount, and bunq Elite Business users receive a 50% discount on their Personal account plan, regardless of the plan type**.**

## How many Bank Accounts can a business user open?

With your Business account you can open as many Bank Accounts as you like. **In the monthly fee 25 Bank Accounts are included.** After this, you pay a small fee which can be found in our [pricing list](<https://www.bunq.com/pricing-sheet>) 📄

## Which card can I order with my Business account?

With your Business account you can pick whatever combination suits your needs. **We offer a Debit Card (Mastercard) and a Credit Card.** Can’t decide which card is best for your needs? Check [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/12503-which-bunq-card-best-matches-my-lifestyle>). 💳

## How can I share access to my Business account?

Do you want to **share the right access level to your Business account with your team, in a simple and fast way?** You can share Full Access with a Director or Co-founder, so they can manage the account too. Or, let your employees handle company expenses securely and independently with Make Payments access. Learn more about the different [access levels](<./access-levels>).
