# Business accounts: Chamber of Commerce Excerpt

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-chamber-of-commerce-excerpt
**Topics:** Business, Signup, Germany 🇩🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When opening your Business account in Germany, we might ask you for a Chamber of Commerce excerpt (in German: **Handelsregisterauszug**). We ask for this document as part of our business verification and compliance process.

## How can I get the Chamber of Commerce Excerpt?

### Option 1: Online via Official Handelsregister

The easiest and fastest way is through [the federal register](<https://www.handelsregister.de>)

Here’s how to do it:

1\. Go to the site and search your company name

2\. Click your entry

3\. Choose "**Aktueller Ausdruck** " (current extract) or "**Chronologischer Ausdruck** " (historical extract) 

4\. Download as PDF or request a certified version

5\. Cost: Free for basic info, the PDF is enough for your onboarding

  
  
  

### Option 2: Through your local amtsgericht

You can request it in person or by mail at the district court where your company is registered.

### Option 3: Through a notary or legal service

Your notary or a commercial online provider can request and forward an official extract for you.

## What is a Handelsregisterauszug?

A **Handelsregisterauszug** is an official extract from the **Handelsregister** (Commercial Register) in Germany. It contains legally relevant information about a business, including: 

\- Company name and address

\- Legal form (e.g., GmbH, UG)

\- Registration number

\- Date of formation

\- Shareholders and managing directors

\- Capital amount (Stammkapital)

  
  
  

## Who needs a Handelsregisterauszug?

Businesses that are registered in the Handelsregister, such as: 

\- GmbH

\- UG (haftungsbeschränkt)

\- e.K. (eingetragener Kaufmann)

  
  
  

> ❗️**Note** : Einzelunternehmer (sole proprietors) are not listed unless they voluntarily register.

## Why does bunq request the Handelsregisterauszug during onboarding?

The **Handelsregisterauszug** serves as **proof of company existence and legal status**. It confirms **who is authorized to sign on behalf of the company** (e.g., opening a bank account, signing contracts).

## Where can I get a Handelsregisterauszug?

Online (most common) at the www.handelsregister.de 

\- Search by company name or number

\- Download the extract as a PDF
