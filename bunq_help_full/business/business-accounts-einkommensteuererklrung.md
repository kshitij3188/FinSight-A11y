## When is bunq asking for an Einkommensteuererklärung?

When opening your Business account in Germany, we might ask you for an Einkommensteuererklärung. We ask for these documents as part of our **business verification and compliance process**. ​As a German business owner, you can download the necessary forms for your Einkommensteuererklärung (income tax return) through **ELSTER** , the online tax office in Germany. Here's how you can proceed:

  1. **Access ELSTER's Forms Section** : Visit the ELSTER website's forms page to find all available tax forms. ​[Elster](<https://www.elster.de/eportal/formulare-leistungen/alleformulare?utm_source=chatgpt.com>)

  2. **Select the Appropriate Form** : For individuals with unlimited tax liability, the primary form is the "Einkommensteuererklärung unbeschränkte Steuerpflicht (ESt 1 A)". Depending on your specific situation, you may also need to download additional annexes (Anlagen) such as: 

\- Anlage G: For income from trade or business.​

\- Anlage S: For income from self-employment.​

\- Anlage V: For income from renting and leasing.​

  3. **Download the Forms** : Click on the desired form to download it in PDF format. You can then print and fill it out manually.​

## How can I use Mein ELSTER?

  1. **Register for an Account** : Go to the Mein ELSTER portal and create a user account. The registration process involves several steps for security purposes, including receiving an activation code by mail.

  2. **Log In and Access Forms** : Once registered, log in to your account. Navigate to the "Forms & Services" section to find and complete the necessary tax return forms online.​

  3. **Submit Electronically** : After filling out the forms, you can submit them directly through the portal. This method is efficient and allows for quicker processing by the tax authorities.​

## Why is bunq asking me for an Einkommensteuererklärung?

When opening a business bank account, we want to verify: - The **legitimacy and performance of your business** \- That **you're registered and paying taxes**

This information helps us verify just that.
