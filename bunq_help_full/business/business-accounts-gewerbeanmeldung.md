# Business accounts: Business Registration

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-gewerbeanmeldung
**Topics:** Business, Signup, Germany 🇩🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When opening your Business account in Germany, we might ask you for a Gewerbeanmeldung. We ask for these documents as part of our business verification and compliance process.

### What is a Gewerbeschein?

The Gewerbeanmeldung is the official business registration certificate for self-employed people and companies in Germany. It confirms that you've registered your trade/business with your local Gewerbeamt (Trade Office).

## How can I get the Gewerbeanmeldung?

You need to go to the **Gewerbeamt** (Trade Office) responsible for your business location. It is usually part of the **city administration (Stadtverwaltung)** or **municipal office (Rathaus).** You can: - **In person** at your local **Gewerbeamt** \- **By mail** \- **Online** (available in many cities now) You’ll need to complete the **Gewerbeanmeldung form** and provide necessary documents (ID, address registration, etc.).

## How do I apply online?

Online sites for getting the document can change depending on where you’re operating from. To find whether there is a site to get the document online, you can search: 

“Gewerbeanmeldung + [your city name]”

For example: _Gewerbeanmeldung Berlin or Gewerbeanmeldung München_ After going to the office, you will receive a **Gewerbeschein** (the actual Gewerbeanmeldung certificate).

  
  
  

## What is a Gewerbeschein?

The Gewerbeschein is the official certificate of trade registration. It proves that you’ve registered your business with the local Gewerbeamt (Trade Office). It's also referred to as the Gewerbeanmeldung.

## How much does it cost?

Typically **between €20 and €50,** depending on the city or municipality.

## Can I offer something else instead?

No, we need the Gewerbeanmeldung unless you are registered as an freiberufler in which case an Einkommensteuererklärung would be enough.

## Additional tips

Please ensure that the Gewerbeanmeldung is the official registration document, either signed and stamped, or bearing a valid digital signature.
