# Business accounts: Proof registration registro mercantil

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-proof-registration-registro-mercantil
**Topics:** Business, Signup, Spain 🇪🇸

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When creating a new Business account with us in Spain we might ask for your proof of registration from the Registro Mercantil "certificación registral" .

To obtain proof of registration from the Registro Mercantil (Commercial Registry) for your Spanish business, you can request a "certificación registral" (certificate of registration). This official document verifies your company's registration details and legal standing.

### How can I obtain the certificate?

  1. Go to the [Registrars of Spain](<https://www.registradores.org/>) website.

  2. Click on “**Acceso al Registro Online** ” (You may need to register or log in.)

  3. Search for your company. Enter the company’s name or tax ID (NIF/CIF).

  4. Request a “**Certificación Registral** ” This document shows that your business is officially registered.

  5. Pay the small fee

  6. Download or receive the document. You can get it digitally or request a paper version by post. For more detailed information and to access online services, visit the [Registrars of Spain](<https://www.registradores.org/>) official website.

## What is a proof of registration in the registro mercantil or “certificación registral”?

A certificación de inscripción is an official certificate issued by the Spanish Commercial Registry confirming that your company is registered and providing key details such as:

  * Legal name

  * Tax ID (CIF/NIF)

  * Registered address

  * Company form (e.g., S.L., S.A.)

  * Date of incorporation

  * Names of directors or legal representatives

## Where and how can I request this certificate?

You can request it online through the [**official website of the Spanish Registrars**](<https://www.registradores.org>). Here’s what you can do:

  1. Go to **“Registro Online”**

  2. Search for your company by name or CIF/NIF

  3. Request the **certificación registral**

  4. Pay the fee (usually €15–€30)

  5. Download it digitally or request it by post

## How long does it take to get the certificate?

  * **Digital version** : Usually available within minutes after payment.

  * **Printed version by mail** : Typically arrives in a few business days (depending on the registry’s workload and postal delivery).

## Why do I need to provide bunq with the proof of registration in the registro mercantil (or certificación registral?

We request the “certificación de inscripción” to verify that your company is legally registered and active in the Spanish Commercial Registry. It confirms key details such as the company’s legal name, tax ID, and authorized representatives. This helps the bank comply with anti-money laundering regulations and ensure accurate, up-to-date records.
