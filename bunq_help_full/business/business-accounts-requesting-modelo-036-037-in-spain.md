# Business accounts: Requesting Modelo 036 & 037 in Spain

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-requesting-modelo-036-037-in-spain
**Topics:** Business, Signup, Spain 🇪🇸

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

**‼️ Important update for businesses in Spain: Form 037 is no longer in use**

As of **February 3, 2025** , the Spanish government has officially retired **Form 037**. This change was announced in the Boletín Oficial del Estado (BOE) on **January 9, 2025** , under **Order HAC/1526/2024.**

### What does this mean for you?

If you're self-employed or a professional in Spain, you'll now need to use Form 036 to register, update, or deregister your details in the Census of Entrepreneurs, Professionals, and Withholders.

**Please note:** we can only accept the official document issued by the tax authorities — no substitutes or alternative formats are allowed.

## Option 1: Request the form online

You can request the modelo 036 Online via the Spanish Tax Agency (Agencia Tributaria). This method is the quickest and most convenient. You will need a digital certificate, electronic DNI, or be registered in the Cl@ve system. Here’s how you can do it:

  * Visit the Tax Agency's website: Navigate to the "Agencia Tributaria - [Modelo 36](<https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es/Sede/procedimientoini/G322.shtml>)" section.

  * Log in securely: Use your digital certificate, electronic DNI, or Cl@ve credentials to access your account.

  * Find submitted forms: Look for the "Modelo 036. Consulta de declaraciones presentadas" option to view submitted declarations.

  * Select the model and tax year: Choose the Model 036 and the year you wish to download.

  * Download the form: Obtain the PDF copy of your submitted form.

💡 **Note:** Sometimes, instead of the exact form you submitted, the tax agency might provide a "census certificate" ("certificado de situación censal") with your registration info, which is also valid for most official purposes requiring the 036.

## Option 2: Request it in person at a Tax Agency office

If you prefer handling the process physically, follow these steps:

  * **Locate your nearest office:** Find the Agencia Tributaria office that corresponds to your registered business address.

  * **Book an appointment:** Schedule an appointment via their website or by phone, as it is required.

  * **Attend your appointment:** Bring your ID and any relevant documents with you.

⏳**Note:** This option may take longer due to appointment availability and wait times.

## Why is bunq asking me for the Modelo 36 form?

**Form 036** is your **official registration with the Spanish Tax Agency (AEAT).** It shows:

  * **Your business is active**

  * **You're registered to pay taxes**

  * **You've declared your activity and obligations**

Without it, we can’t be sure your business is actually registered to operate.

## I only have a Modelo 037 at hand, will you accept that as well?

If you registered your business a while ago, you might just have a Modelo 037 at hand. We currently still accept this document for onboarding, so you can send it instead of the Modelo 036 when doing **business verification for your Spanish business**.
