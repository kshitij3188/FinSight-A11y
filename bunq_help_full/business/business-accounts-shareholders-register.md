# Business accounts: Shareholders Register

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-shareholders-register
**Topics:** Business, Signup, Germany 🇩🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When opening your Business account in Germany, we might ask you for a **Shareholders Register** (in German: **Gesellschafterliste** or **Liste der Gesellschafter**). We ask for this document as part of our **business verification and compliance process**.

### How can I get my Shareholders Register?

#### Option 1: Download it Online

You can easily get a copy from the official Handelsregister site:

  1. Go to www.handelsregister.de

  2. Search for your company

  3. Select your business entry

  4. Download the **Gesellschafterliste** (usually under “Dokumente” or “Veröffentlichungen”)

  5. Cost: Free to view, ~€4.50 to download the official PDF.

#### Option 2: Ask Your Notary

If you recently founded your business or had ownership changes, your notary will have the latest version and can send it to you (digitally or by mail).

## What is a Shareholders Register? (Gesellschafterliste)

The **shareholders register** is an official document listing all owners (shareholders) of a company, especially for:

  * **GmbH** (Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung)

  * **UG** (haftungsbeschränkt)

It includes:

  * Full names and addresses of shareholders

  * Number and value of their shares (Stammeinlage)

  * Total capital share per person

## Who needs a Gesellschafterliste?

Every **GmbH** or **UG** must have one. It is required by law and must be submitted to the **Handelsregister** (Commercial Register).

> ❗️ **Note** : Not required for sole proprietors (Einzelunternehmen).
