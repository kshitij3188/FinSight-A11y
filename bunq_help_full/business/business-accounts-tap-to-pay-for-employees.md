# Business accounts: Tap to Pay for Employees

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-tap-to-pay-for-employees

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

**Tap to Pay** lets you accept contactless payments using just your phone. If your business has employees with Make Payments Access, they can also use Tap to Pay for payments to your Business Account.

## How can I enable Tap to Pay for my employee?

When adding an employee, you have the option to enable this feature right away by following these steps:

  1. Tap on your name at the top of your Home Tab

  2. Select your Business Account from the top of the screen

  3. Go to your Business Profile and select **Manage Tap to Pay Access**

  4. Tap on **Give Tap to Pay Access** and select the user you'd like to share Tap to Pay access with 

  5. Enable the Tap to Pay feature and select which Account you’d like to deposit the money into 

## How can I accept payments on my phone as an employee?

To use Tap to Pay as an employee, someone with Full Access to the Business Account must first enable it for you. If that’s done, follow the steps below to get paid using Tap to Pay:

  1. Go to your Profile 

  2. Tap on **Tap to Pay**

  3. Enter the amount you want to get

  4. Tap**Start Tap to Pay**

  5. Tap your phone to the phone of the person paying

  6. Done ✅

## How can I disable the Tap to Pay feature for an employee?

  1. Tap on your name at the top of your Home Tab

  2. Select your Business Account from the top of the screen

  3. Go to your Profile and select **Manage Tap to Pay Access**

  4. Select the employee you want to disable Tap to Pay for

  5. Disable the Tap to Pay toggle
