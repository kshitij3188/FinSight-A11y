# Business verification: I need to provide Registration Number

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-verification-i-need-to-provide-registration-number
**Topics:** Business, Signup

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

During your **Business verification** , you'll need to provide your country's **Registration Number** or **Tax ID** during sign up. This means that your company will need to be registered at your country's respective **Chamber of Commerce**.

**Through the registration number we obtain:**

  * Info and identification of your company directors.

  * Info related to your company activities and legal form.

  * Authorized representatives (if applicable).

  * Deed of Incorporation (DOI). This is a legal document issued by the civil notary that proves the registration of your company. We’ll ask for this document if your Chamber of Commerce excerpt doesn’t provide all of the info we need to confirm your business.

You can check out accepted numbers and where to locate them below

## 🇧🇪 Belgium

  * [Ondernemingsnummer](<https://economie.fgov.be/fr/themes/entreprises>)

## 🇫🇷 France

  * SIREN or SIRET number

  * [Chambre de Commerce et d'Industrie](<https://www.cci.fr/web/organisation-du-reseau/annuaire>) (CCI), if you want to set up a shop or a commercial company with no ‘craft, trades or artisan’ element.

  * [Chambre de Métiers et de l'Artisanat](<http://deconfinetonavenir.artisanat.fr/deconfine-ton-avenir/reseau-des-cma/un-reseau-de-proximite/annuaire-des-cma>) (CMA), for manual/trades and craftspeople.

  * [URSSAF](<https://www.cfe.urssaf.fr/saisiepl/>) for the professions and intellectual services like translators or website designers.

## 🇩🇪 Germany

  * [Eingetragener Kaufmann](<https://www.ihk.de/>) (e.K.,e.Kfm, or e.Kfr)

  * [Handelsregisternummer](<https://www.ihk.de/>)

## 🇮🇹 Italy

  * [numero Registro Imprese](<http://www.registroimprese.it/>) (numeroRI).

## 🇳🇱 The Netherlands

  * [Kamer van Koophandel](<https://www.kvk.nl/>) (KvK number)

## 🇵🇹 Portugal

  * [Autoridade Tributária e Aduaneira](<https://eportugal.gov.pt/entidades/autoridade-tributaria-e-aduaneira>)
