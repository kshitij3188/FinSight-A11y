**Hey bunqers 🌈**

As a bank, we are required by law and by our internal company guidelines to perform specific checks to make sure everything will run smoothly with your bunq business banking experience. Thus, we might need some additional documents for your verification.

## What is a Shareholders registry?

A [shareholder register](<https://www.investopedia.com/terms/s/shareholder-register.asp>) is a list of active owners of your company's shares. **Record of Shareholders is a legal document** and must be registered with a civil-law notary. We ask you to provide this in the event that a share transfer takes place and the ownership of your company changes. By providing this, we can verify if your company shares have been transferred between different parties.
