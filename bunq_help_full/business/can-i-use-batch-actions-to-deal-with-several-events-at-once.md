# Can I use Batch actions to deal with several events at once?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-i-use-batch-actions-to-deal-with-several-events-at-once

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Want to split 5 payments after a night out with friends? Or add multiple payments to an activity? Simply select all the events in the app, and sort them out with one tap!

## How does it work?

You can do so by following this easy process:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on your preferred Bank Account

  3. Select the events desired by tapping on their icon

  4. Tap on the action you want to perform

  5. Follow the steps shown in the app

  6. Done ✅

## Why can't I use batch actions in my app? 

We always listen to your feedback in order to make your bunq experience even better! To make room for cool, new features, we said goodbye to batch actions in our newest app redesign - V5. If you can't use batch actions in your app, it's because you're on the newest version of the app.
