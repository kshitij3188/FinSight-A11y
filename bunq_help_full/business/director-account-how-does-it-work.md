# Co-Founder Account: how does it work?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/director-account-how-does-it-work
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

With your Co-Founder account you can manage your business account with ease. In order to manage your Business account, you need a verified personal account. For this you can learn more [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/why-do-you-need-a-personal-account-with-your-business-account>) 💪 

In your bunq app, you may notice we reference **Co-founders. A Co-Founder** has the same role as a **Director** for your Business Account. You can invite them to help manage the account, giving them the same access and responsibilities as you. Together you can stay on top of payments, accounts, and everything your business needs. 

_If you’re looking to add a Director but see Co-founder in the app, no worries — it’s simply another name for the same role and works exactly the same._

### How many Directors can join the Business account?

  * **bunq Free Business**

➡️ 1 x Included Director

➡️ No extra directors 

  

  * **bunq Core Business**

➡️ 3 x Included Directors

➡️ €5.99 per extra director (per month) 

  

  * **bunq Pro Business**

➡️ 3 x Included Directors

➡️ €5.99 per extra director (per month) 

  

  * **bunq Elite Business**

➡️ 3 x Included Directors

➡️ €5.99 per extra director (per month)

You can see how to add extra Directors to your Business account [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-add-a-director-to-my-business-account>)

## How does Director's access work?

**Director's access is included in each plan (3 for bunq Core, Pro, and Elite)**. Account Directors have complete access to the company accounts and can add or remove other Account Directors directly within the app. However, if an attempt is made to delete the last remaining Account Director, who is also the **sole fully authorized legal representative of the company** , this action will be blocked.
