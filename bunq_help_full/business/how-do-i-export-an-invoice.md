# How do I export an invoice?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-an-invoice

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can now export an invoice from any of your bunq bank accounts - straight from the bunq app! Want PDF files for all of your invoices? You can grab them from the app and add them to your admin.

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Accounting**

  3. Select **Invoice and billing**

  4. Select the invoice

  5. Tap on **Share**!

## How do I receive my invoice via e-mail?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Accounting**

  3. Tap on **Invoices and billing**

  4. Enable **Send Invoices by Email**
