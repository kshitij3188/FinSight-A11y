# How do I manage access to my Business Account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-manage-access-to-my-business-account
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Need to share access to your bunq Business account with your employees in a fast and simple way? We’re here to make that easy for you.

You can share various levels of access with your Director or Co-founder, your employees, or even your accountant. Decide between sharing: 

  * **Full Access:** Your invitee will help manage the account, giving them the same access level as you. Together you can stay on top of payments, accounts, and everything your business needs.

  * **Make Payments access:** Your invitee will get access to your chosen Business Bank Accounts, giving them the ability to easily make payments from those accounts. 

  * **View-Only access** : Your invitee will get access to your chosen Business Bank Accounts, giving them the ability to view the payments made from those accounts. 

Learn more about access levels [here](<./access-levels>). 

## How do I share access to my Business account?

### Full Access

To share **Full Access** to your Business Account with the company Director or Co-founder, invite them by following these steps: 

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Tap on **Add User**

  5. Select **Full Access**

  6. Confirm that this person has been registered with the **Chamber of Commerce**

  7. Send the invite! 

Once the invite has reached your Director or Co-founder, here’s what they can do to accept it:

  1. Sign up for a bunq account, if they don't have one already 

  2. We’ll perform a secure check to verify that they’re registered with the Chamber of Commerce. This is usually instant, but can sometimes take up to 24 hours

  3. Accept the invite to get access. As soon as the check’s complete, they can accept your invite from their Home Tab and get instant access to the entire Business Account

Learn more about [accepting an invite to access a Business Account](<./accept-access-level-invite>). 

**Please note** : To grant someone full access, they first need to be registered as a Director with the Chamber of Commerce. 

### Make Payments Access

To share Make Payments Access to your Business account with someone like the company treasurer or employee, invite them by following these steps: 

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Tap on **Add User**

  5. Select **Make Payments Access**

  6. Choose which Bank Accounts they can make payments from and **continue**

  7. Select the person you’re inviting 

If they’re: 

  * **A bunq user** : They’ll appear in your bunq Contact book 

  * **Not a bunq user yet** : You’ll see their name from your **Phone** **Contacts**. Tap on **Invite** to invite them to join bunq and get Make Payments access to your Business

Once they accept your invite, they’ll instantly have access to make payments from the selected Bank Account(s) from your Business Account. The selected Bank Accounts will be visible straight from their Personal bunq Accounts. 

### View-Only Access

To share **View-Only** Access to your Business Account, invite the company accountant or your Home Owner’s Associations member by following these steps: 

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Tap on **Add User**

  5. Select **View-Only Access**

  6. Choose which Bank Accounts they can view and **continue**

  7. Select the person you’re inviting 

If they’re: 

  * **A bunq user** : They’ll appear in your bunq Contact book 

  * **Not a bunq user yet** : You’ll see their name from your **Phone** **Contacts**. Tap on **Invite** to invite them to join bunq and get View-Only access to your Business. 

Once they accept your invite, they’ll instantly have access to view the payments made from the selected Bank Accounts of your Business. The selected Bank Accounts will be visible straight from their bunq Personal Accounts. 

## How do I revoke access to my Business account?

### Full Access 

To revoke Full Access, the person must be de-registered from the Chamber of Commerce. This is to ensure that your Business Account information remains accurate and up-to-date with the official Chamber of Commerce documents. Once this change is officially registered with the Chamber of Commerce, simply: 

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Select the person you’d like to remove 

  5. Tap on **Remove User**

  6. Confirm your choice 

  7. We’ll run a secure check to make sure they’re de-registered from the Chamber of Commerce

  8. All done! 

The person removed will receive a notification that their access to the Business Account has been revoked. 

### Make Payments Access

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Select the person you’d like to remove 

  5. Tap on **Remove User**

  6. Save and all done

### View-Only Access

  1. Switch to your Business Account in the bunq app 

  2. Tap on your Business Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Scroll down and select **Bank Account Access**

  4. Select the person you’d like to remove 

  5. Tap on **Remove User**

  6. Save and all done
