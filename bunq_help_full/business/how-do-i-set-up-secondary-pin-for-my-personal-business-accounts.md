# How do I set up Secondary PIN for my Personal & Business accounts?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-set-up-secondary-pin-for-my-personal-business-accounts
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

We help you save time, every time. You can now use Secondary PIN in combination with your Personal and Business accounts for your activated Physical Debit or Credit cards!

### How can I set up a Secondary PIN?

Check out the simple steps below to set up Secondary PIN for your Personal and Business accounts:

  1. Go to your 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want

  3. Tap on Setting 

  4. Tap on Set up Dual PIN and enable the toggle 

  5. Choose a sub-account from your Business account your Dual PIN will be linked to

  6. Choose the Secondary PIN and confirm with your security code

## What does a Secondary PIN mean?

This means that you can use your personal bunq card to pay for a business purchase. All you have to do is go to your Personal account and add one of your Business sub-accounts as a backup (or vice versa)! No more Connect accounts needed.

Keep in mind that you must have access to both a Business and Personal account in order to enable this.
