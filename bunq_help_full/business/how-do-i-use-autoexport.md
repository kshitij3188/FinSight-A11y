# How do I use AutoExport?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-use-autoexport
**Topics:** Automation

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

With **AutoExport** , you'll never waste time exporting and sending statements to your accountant. Your bunq app will take care of it for you. Every month, automatically.

### How do I enable Auto Export Statement?

To enable Auto Export, you can: 

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to Accounting

  3. Go to 🔵 Auto export statements

  4. Follow the flow ✨

Alternatively, you can enable this feature by following these steps:

  1. Tap on the bank account you want to enable Auto Export

  2. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  3. Under Account Statement, tap on Auto Export Statements

  4. Tap on Enable Auto Export Statements 

  5. Fill in the settings, enter recipient

  6. Tap on Save 👍

**While enabling you need to:**

  * Choose the person you want to share the statement with from your contacts

  * Choose for which Bank Accounts (IBANs) you want a statement to be sent

  * Decide if you want to secure the statements with a password

## How do I disable Auto Export Statement?

To disable Auto Export, simply: 

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to Accounting

  3. Go to 🔵 Auto export statements

  4. Select "Disable Auto Export"

  5. You get a confirmation window, select "Disable"

  6. All done 👍

You can also disable Auto Export by following these steps: 

  1. Tap on the bank account you want to disable Auto Export

  2. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  3. Under Account Statement, tap on Auto Export Statements

  4. Disable the Auto Export Statement toggle

  5. Tap on Save 👍

## Who can use this feature?

This feature can be used by all **Personal and Business** users with verified accounts ✅

## FAQs

**When will I get my AutoExport?**

No extra time from your side is needed. A statement will automatically be sent to your bookkeeper on the 1st of every month.

**What are the costs?**

The costs are already included in the payment costs.

**I forgot the password, how do I open the zip file?**

You can view the password in the app. Just follow these steps: 

  1. Go to the your Profile

  2. Go to Accounting 

  3. Tap on 🔵 Auto export statements

  4. Select 🟢 Password 

  5. You can select or change the password for your **Auto Exported** statements

## Good to know

  * This feature will send a zip file with the statements of the selected accounts on every 1st day of the month.

  * It'll contain all the transactions for the previous month.

  * The users can choose to protect their statements. In this case they can choose a password (at least 5 characters)

  * The email will have a zip file with a statement per Bank Account.
