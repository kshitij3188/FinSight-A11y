# How to open a bunq Business account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-open-a-bunq-business-account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

At bunq it’s very easy to open a Business account. Simply follow the 3 steps below:

### Step 1: Open and verify your bunq Personal account first

As a bank, we're legally required to securely store all our users’ personal information. By creating a Personal account for the director of your Business account, we ensure both your and your company’s information is protected while staying compliant with regulations. More details can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38241>).

There are two ways to open a Business account, depending on whether you’re an existing bunq user or a new one:

#### Existing bunq user

If you already have a bunq account, just follow these steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab 

  2. Go to your Profile 

  3. Scroll and tap on **Business** under **Add New Account**

  4. Add the name of your Business 

  5. Accept T&Cs and fill out your Business information 

  6. All done ✨

#### New bunq user

For new users, here’s how to get started:

  1. Download the bunq app

  2. Tap on **Get Started**

  3. Enter and confirm your phone number 

  4. Tap on **Sign up in 5 Minutes**

  5. Choose "For my business”

  6. Add your business information

  7. Add your personal information

  8. Identify yourself with our verification flow

Our KYC team will review your application and let you know if we need more information about your company. We’ll keep you updated!

The moment you’ve applied for your bunq Business account, we’ll get to work in order to verify it for you. This will likely take less than 5 minutes. In some cases however we’ll require additional information or documentation about your business. Should this happen, we’ll contact you personally in the app. To keep our communication with you safe and secure we’ll never approach you by e-mail or by phone.

## Step 2: Open and verify your bunq Business account

Once your bunq Personal account is verified, up and running, you can open your bunq Business account in just a few taps as explained above. To help you through the process, we recommend you keep the following documentation and info at hand:

  * Registration number of the Chamber of Commerce 

  * Names and ID-documents of the other directors/board members

  * Names, and in some cases the ID-documents, of the business' Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs). These names should’ve been previously provided to the Chamber of Commerce as well.

  * Information about the activities of your business and how you expect to use your bunq Business account

  * Other info such as the Deed of Incorporation or Articles of Association, depending on the legal form of the company and on the [country of registration](<https://together.bunq.com/d/221-what-are-the-accepted-legal-forms-to-open-a-bunq-business-account/9>)

## Step 3: Sit back and relax

The moment you’ve applied for your bunq Business account, we’ll get to work in order to verify it for you. This will likely take less than 5 minutes. In some cases however we’ll require additional information or documentation about your business. Should this happen, we’ll contact you personally in the app. To keep our communication with you safe and secure we’ll never approach you by e-mail or by phone.

## Some useful tips

  * If you need a personal account just so you can create a business account with us and don't want to pay double fees, rest assured we've thought of you: you can open an [bunq Free](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48095>) personal account for exactly this purpose 💪

  * For the safety of you, your money and that of the banking system, bunq maintains strict acceptance criteria for companies opening a bunq Business account. You can read all about them [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1014-who-can-open-a-bunq-business-account/10>) and use this information to get your Business account up and running even faster!
