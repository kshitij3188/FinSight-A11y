## Who can open a bunq Business account?

If you wish to open a Business account with us, you can do so if:

  * You are an authorized representative of the company.

  * You are resident in the country where your business is registered.

  * Your business is registered in the Netherlands, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Austria, Belgium or Ireland.

## How to open a new bunq Business account?

Opening a new business account is quick and easy! Just follow these easy steps below:

  1. Open the app

  2. Tap on “Get Started"

  3. Enter and confirm your phone number 

  4. Select account type **For my business**

  5. Add your business’ information”

  6. Fill in your personal details

  7. Start the verification process

 _Note: To open a Business account, you’ll need a Personal account as well. By following the steps above, a Personal account will be automatically created for you, with the director's account set to the bunq Free subscription plan You can always upgrade your plan, following the steps mentioned_[ _here_](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3317>) _._
