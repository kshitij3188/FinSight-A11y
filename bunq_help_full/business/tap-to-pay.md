# Tap to Pay 📱

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/tap-to-pay
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can use your bunq app to accept all types of in-person, contactless payments for your Business. From physical debit and credit cards to Apple and Google Pay, and other digital wallets, no terminal or hardware is needed. It’s easy, secure, and private.

### How can I request a payment through Tap to Pay?

Easily request money and get paid through Tap to Pay. Here's how:

  1. Switch to your Business Account 

  2. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  3. Tap on **Tap to Pay**

  4. Enable **Tap to Pay** and follow the flow 

  5. Choose the currency, request amount, and bank account to receive the payment 

  6. Tap on **Next**

  7. Hold your phone to the phone of the person paying

  8. All done 🎉

## Who can use this feature?

Tap to Pay is available for Business users in 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇭🇷 Croatia, 🇨🇾 Cyprus, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇬🇷 Greece, 🇮🇸 Iceland, 🇮🇹 Italy, 🇱🇺 Luxembourg, 🇳🇱 Netherlands and 🇪🇸 Spain who have completed the verification process. To use Tap to Pay, you'll need either an Android device or an iPhone XS or later with iOS 18.5 or later.

  * You can see a detailed list of all eligible merchants (based on their SBI code) who can use the feature [here](<https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/documents/eligible-merchants-tap-to-pay>).

  * Employees with Full Access to a Business account can also use the feature.

## How fast will my money arrive?

The money will be available in your Bank Account in about 7 days.

## How can I share the receipt?

To do so, follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to 🏠 Home Tab

  2. Tap on the Bank Account you’ve received the payment on

  3. Tap on the payment event

  4. Tap on Receipt

  5. Tap on the Share icon in the top right corner

  6. Share the receipt with your client

  7. Done ✅

## Types of payment event statuses

There are three types of payment event statuses:

#### Payment pending

When a card payment is made, it initially goes through a **successful authorization process.** This means that the amount for the transaction is approved and reserved for the cardholder. In this scenario, the cardholder is the end client who is making a purchase from you. However, even though the payment has been authorized, it is still in a pending state. This is because the amount has not yet been transferred to your Bank Account. It takes 7 days for the amount to be received successfully and appear in your Bank Account.

#### Payment received

After 7 days, the payment will be successfully received by you and visible in your Bank Account.

#### Refunded payment

You can always refund the desired amount to your client after the payment has been made and a refund was requested. The refund can be partial or full, depending on your agreement. Refunds are instant and when successful, a new refund event is created called “Refund successful”.

If your customer asks for a refund before the payment reaches your account, they will get their money back within 5 business days.

## How can I refund a payment?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Select the Bank Account 

  3. Tap on a payment that you want to refund

  4. Press **Refund Payment**

  5. Choose amount and confirm 

## Which merchants aren’t eligible for Tap to Pay?

The following merchant categories can’t use the Tap to Pay:

  * Home care

  * Other paramedical practitioners (no physiotherapy and psychology) and alternative healers

  * Social work for adults

  * Sale and repair of passenger cars and light motor vehicles (no import of new cars)

  * Counseling and welfare for disabled

  * Social work for children

  * Residential care for the elderly

  * Practices of psychotherapists and psychologists

  * Bars

  * Specialist medical practices and outpatients’ clinics (no dentistry or psychiatry)

  * Tour operators

  * Social assistance with residential and day care for children

  * Travel agencies

  * Residential and day care for mental retardation

  * Welfare for elderly

  * Residential nursing care

  * Tourist information and reservation services

  * Renting of passenger cars and light motor vehicles (no operational lease)

  * Art galleries

You can see a detailed list of all eligible merchants (based on their SBI code) who can use the feature [here](<https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/documents/eligible-merchants-tap-to-pay>). **Note:** Employees with Full Access to a Business account can also use the feature.

## Is there a fee?

Yes, the fee for receiving Tap to Pay payments is **1.5% of the amount received.**
