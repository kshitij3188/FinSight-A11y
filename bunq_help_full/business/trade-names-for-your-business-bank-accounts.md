# Trade names for your Business Bank Accounts

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/trade-names-for-your-business-bank-accounts

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

With your Chamber of Commerce excerpt, you can instantly add your new trade name to your bunq account.

As a Business account user, this feature lets you send payments under your preferred trade name, helping you showcase products and promote new services more effectively, especially if you have multiple trade names.

_Note: Each trade name linked to a Bank Account costs €2.99/month. Check our_[ _pricing page_](<https://www.bunq.com/en/pricing-sheet>) _for full details._

## How can I add a trade name to my Business Bank Account?

#### Dutch Bank Account

If you have a bunq Business account in the Netherlands, you can select which KvK-registered trade name to link to your bank account. We’ll automatically update the names for you! Just follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the Business 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap the Bank Account in question

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on "Trade Name"

  5. Choose your trade name

#### Austrian, Belgian, French, German, Irish, Italian, and Spanish Business Bank Accounts

To add a trade name to your 🇦🇹Austrian, 🇧🇪 Belgian, 🇫🇷 French, 🇩🇪 German, 🇮🇪 Irish, 🇮🇹Italian, and 🇪🇸 Spanish Business Bank Accounts Business account, contact us via our [Support Center](<https://together.bunq.com/d/32181>) and provide the necessary documentation for the trade name. This typically involves submitting your Chamber of Commerce excerpt.

## How to add a trade name during Bank Account creation?

  1. Go to the Business 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Create new Bank Account

  4. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  5. Tap on "Trade name"

  6. Select the trade name you want to link to the bank account.

## Can I add a new trade name to my list in the bunq app?

Austrian, Belgian, Dutch, French, German, Irish, Italian, and Spanish users can link another trade name to one of their Bank Accounts by reaching out to us through our [Support Centre](<https://together.bunq.com/d/32181>). Our team will handle the request for you 🚀

Please note that a trade name can be changed for any Bank Account only if relevant documentation with the new registered trade name is provided to us.

For example, Dutch users need to submit their request with the new trade name registered at the Chamber of Commerce ([Kamer van koophandel](<https://www.kvk.nl/>)). German users need to submit their request with either a Gewerbeanmeldung or a Chamber of Commerce excerpt.

## How long does it take for other banks to see the updated trade name?

We synchronize the trade names linked to your Bank Accounts twice a day! While we're quick, other banks may take up to 30 days to synchronize the new trade names. If they notify you that they can't verify your trade name(s) yet, that's why. Once the synchronization is complete on their side, you're all set to go.

## Good to know

  * The feature is only available for Austrian, Belgian, Dutch, French, German, Irish, Italian, and Spanish users Business users with an bunq Pro or bunq Elite plan

  * Every trade name linked to a Bank Account comes with a monthly fee of €2.99.

  * The trade name needs to be registered at the KvK.
