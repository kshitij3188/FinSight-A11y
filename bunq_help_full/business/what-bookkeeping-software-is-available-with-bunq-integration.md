## What can I do once I've linked a bunq account to my bookkeeping software?

Every accounting software has its **unique strengths** , and they all **boost various aspects of our API**. Here are some of the **exciting capabilities that the bunq API offers** :

  * Create, schedule, batch, draft, and view your Payments

  * Send automated Payment Requests.

  * Easily export and organize your receipts for tax season

  * Order personalized cards for your employees and manage their card limits individually

  * Get live conversion rates and convert into 21 different currencies

  * Create and retrieve notes and attachments on your payments

  * Create new spending categories to personalize your Spending Insights

You can review our **API documentation** [here](<https://doc.bunq.com/>). For the tech-lovers, you can even **build your own software** and **share it with other bunqers** [here](<https://www.bunq.com/developers>).

## How do I set it up or remove access?

You can **securely give access to bookkeeping softwares with OAuth**. The bookkeeping software will provide a **QR code** for you to scan using the bunq app, and the bunq app will allow you to **choose your access level** before accepting.

Removing an **OAuth connection** once you're done is also easy, follow these simple steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Click on ⚙️**Settings**

  3. Select **Security**

  4. Tap on **My app connections**

  5. Select any connection

  6. Select "Cancel Connection"
