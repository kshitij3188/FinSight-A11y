# Who can open a bunq Business account in Portugal?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/who-can-open-a-bunq-business-account-in-portugal
**Topics:** Signup, Business, Frequently Asked Questions

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We have a straightforward acceptance policy designed to protect you and your business. Check out the requirements for opening a Business Account in France below. 

## Who can open an account?

Here are the basic conditions you need to meet in order to open a bunq business account:

  * You need to have a verified personal account with bunq.

  * You as a director cannot be a minor.

  * Your business needs to be legitimate and registered in Portugal.

  * You must live permanently in the European Economic Area.

  * Your business should operate under one of the legal forms currently accepted by bunq. Find the full list [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24017>).

  * Your company’s corporate structure and activities should comply with bunq’s requirements. More details on this below.

  * Your business entity cannot have more than one account with bunq.

  * If your company has multiple trade names (under the same registration on the Chamber of Commerce) - you can effortlessly open a Bank Account with the different names like we explain [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24341>).

  * If the various layers of your company are registered as separate legal entities with the Chamber of Commerce, each one would need its own Business accounts.

  * You cannot open a Business account if any UBOs or non-executives reside outside of the EEA. 

Once we confirm the above-mentioned conditions are met, we need to determine whether your business entity is registered with the local Chamber of Commerce or, in specific situations, you are operating on a freelance basis. Depending on that, we require different documents in order to approve your account.

Please also keep in mind that as a bank, we are legally required to store all of our user’s personal information in a secure and protected way. If there is more than one director, we create personal accounts for each director of the business accounts. It allows us to keep your information and your company’s information safe while complying with regulations. Check out [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38241>) for more information.

## Legal forms and documents

In order to open a business account with bunq, you need to provide a legal form together with a few documents. We accept the following legal forms.

  * Empresário em nome individual (ENI)

  * Estabelecimento Individual de Responsabilidade Limitada (EIRL)

For each legal form, we require the following documents.

**Empresário em nome individual (ENI)**

  * Chamber of Commerce excerpt

**Estabelecimento Individual de Responsabilidade Limitada (EIRL)**

  * Deed of incorporation (DOI)

  * Record of shareholders

  * Joint authorisation (if the business has more than one director)

  * Chamber of Commerce excerpt

  * IDs for all directors and Ultimate beneficial owners (UBOs)

Please find more information on legal documents [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24017>).

## Company Activities

In accordance with our acceptance policy, we do not support all types of businesses and activities. You can find more information on activities and check whether your business activity meets the requirements [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/9256>).

## What IBAN will I get?

If you open an account in Portugal with us, you will get a NL IBAN.

However, we also provide bunq Pro and bunq Elite users the option to open a Local IBAN Bank Account, allowing you to obtain a German, French, Irish, or Spanish one. Further information on how to open one can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>).

## How do I open a new Business Account? 

Opening a new business account is quick and easy! Just follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Go to your Profile

  3. Scroll and tap on **Business** under **Add New Account**

  4. Add the name of your Business

  5. Accept T&Cs and fill out your Business information

  6. All done ✨
