# Zapier integrations: automate your finances and start saving time

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/zapier-integrations-automate-your-finances-and-start-saving-time
**Topics:** Automation

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Bring your banking experience to the next level!

To save you time, you can now use **Zapier** to connect your finances to your favorite apps. It’s the perfect way to let your account run seamlessly, and all by itself!

_Zapier is available for bunq Pro and bunq Elite plans 🌿_

## What are the benefits of Zapier?

With **Zapier** , you can connect bunq to your favorite apps. This way you can automate banking actions and reduce those boring day-to-day tasks. Long story short, saving you time and energy.

**Zapier** can help you do things like:

  * **Get a real-time spreadsheet of your expenses**

No more manual exporting of bank statements. Your finance department can follow your expenses in real-time on Google Sheets or Excel.

  * **Keep you and your finance department up to date**

You don’t need to worry about your balance. If it drops below a chosen amount, an email or Slack message with the text of your choice will be triggered.

  * **Easily keep track of employee spending**

Follow your employees' purchases by logging their card transactions to a spreadsheet and sharing it with your finance department.

  * **Not searching through a box of receipts**

Always know when you bought something by mapping your purchases to your calendar.

  * **Save without thinking about it** Trigger automatic transfers to your savings Bank Account when different actions are completed, so you can keep saving without noticing.

## Can I connect Zapier to my Personal account?

Only **bunq Pro** and **bunq Elite** users can connect **Zapier** to their Personal accounts.

## Is there any billing?

This feature comes at no extra cost.

## Connect bunq to hundreds of other apps with Zapier

[Zapier](<https://zapier.com/apps/APP/integrations>) lets you connect bunq to more than 1.500 other web services. Automated connections called **Zaps** , set up in minutes with no coding, can automate your day-to-day tasks and build workflows between apps that otherwise wouldn't be possible. Each **Zap** has one app as the **Trigger** , where your information comes from and which causes one or more **Actions** in other apps, where your data gets sent automatically.

## Getting Started with Zapier

Sign up for a free [Zapier](<https://zapier.com/apps/APP/integrations>) account, from there you can jump right in. To help you hit the ground running, [here](<https://zapier.com/apps/bunq/integrations>) are some popular pre-made Zaps.

## How do I connect to Zapier?

  1. Log in to your [Zapier account](<https://zapier.com/sign-up>) or create a new account.

  2. Navigate to **Connected Accounts** from the top menu bar.

  3. Now click on **Connect new account** and search for "bunq"

  4. Use your credentials to connect your bunq account to **Zapier**.

  5. Once that's done you can start creating an automation! Use a pre-made Zap or create your own with the **Zap Editor**. Creating a **Zap** requires no coding knowledge and you'll be walked step-by-step through the setup. Need inspiration? See everything that's possible to do with [your bunq app and Zapier](<https://zapier.com/apps/bunq/integrations>). If you have any additional questions, you can reach out to contact@zapier.com.

## Good to know

  * You should visit the website of **Zapier** to set up the connection.

  * Like with other API connections you will allow the service to make use of your data.

  * You can set up the zaps yourself on the website of **Zapier**.
