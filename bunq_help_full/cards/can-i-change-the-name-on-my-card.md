# Can I change the name on my card?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-i-change-the-name-on-my-card
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

Just purchased your new bunq card? Amazing 🚀

Keep in mind that once you finalize your order there is no way to edit the name or description on your card. So make sure to double-check that everything on the card is correct before submitting your order 👍

### How can I change the name on my card after ordering?

In the case that you wish to change any information on your card, you would then need to [order a replacement](<https://together.bunq.com/d/711>).

You can check out how to do this below:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want a replacement for

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select **Replace**

  5. Check your address

  6. Add your name

  7. Order your card 🚀
