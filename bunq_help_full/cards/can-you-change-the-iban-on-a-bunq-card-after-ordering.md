# Can you change the IBAN on a bunq card after ordering?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-you-change-the-iban-on-a-bunq-card-after-ordering

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Accidentally ordered a card for the wrong IBAN/account? Not to worry!

Even though you can't change the IBAN that's on the card after ordering it, bunq cards aren't connected to your IBAN. After activating the card in the app, you can always switch the account it's connected to at any time.

### How can I change the IBAN my card is linked to?

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab 

  2. Tap on the desired card 

  3. Tap on **Auto Select** next to “Pay From”

  4. Select any new Bank Account and you are done ✅

**Note:** The IBAN on your card will be the initial IBAN, but your card will be connected to the IBAN you selected in the app.
