# Cards Tab 💳

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/cards-tab

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Welcome to your Cards Tab — with just a tap in the app, you can see all your cards at a glance, create new ones in seconds, add them to Apple Pay or Google Pay, and review your most recent transactions.

Whether it’s tracking your spending, freezing a card or ordering a new one, the Cards tab makes it effortless to stay organized. Read more below to discover all the features available in your Cards Tab.

## ZeroFX

Zero FX shows you how much you save when you use your bunq card abroad. Read more about Zero FX payments [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-send-one-time-fx-payments>).

## Cards 💳

With bunq, you can choose the card which matches your lifestyle best! Once your card arrives, managing it is just as easy — check its details, replace it, or even freeze it instantly.

In your Card Tab, simply swipe through your cards or add new ones in seconds. When you select a card, you’ll see all the key details right below — your card number, expiry date, and CVC. To add a card, swipe to the end of your card carousel and tap on **Add Card**.

Want to refine your card setting further? Tap the ⚙️Settings in the top-right corner to:

  * Freeze and block your card [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3509>)

  * Replace your card [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-replace-my-card>)

  * Personalize its look [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-personalize-my-bunq-card>)

  * Fine-tune security features like: [set daily limits](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-change-my-daily-card-limit>), [allowed countries](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-does-localized-card-protection-work%E2%80%8B%E2%80%8B>), and [tip protection](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-tip-protection>). 

  * Export your card statements [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-bank-statement>)

## Recent Transactions

Get a clear overview of all your recent card transactions.

  * **Looking for something specific?**   
Just tap the search icon in the top-right corner to find any transaction in seconds.

  * **Want the full transaction list?**   
Tap See All to browse through all your recent activity.
