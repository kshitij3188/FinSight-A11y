# How do I change my card's PIN code?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/change-a-cards-pin
**Topics:** Cards, Frequently Asked Questions

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Changing your PIN from the app is both safe and easy!

As you will always have access to your account, changing your PIN happens in real-time, and it's super easy to do. The moment you enter your new PIN in the app, you can instantly use it with your linked card!

### How do I change my PIN code?

You can follow the steps below to choose a new PIN:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab 

  2. Choose the Card for which you want to change the PIN 

  3. Tap on **Settings** below your card 

  4. Tap on PIN

  5. Choose a new one

  6. Confirm and it’s done!
