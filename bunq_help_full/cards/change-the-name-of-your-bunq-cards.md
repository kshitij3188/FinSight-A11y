# Change the name of your bunq cards

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/change-the-name-of-your-bunq-cards
**Topics:** Cards

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Once you personalize the name of a card, its new name will be visible in the **Cards Overview** and in every **Card Event**. Keep in mind that the original card name (as printed on the physical card) will still be visible within the card details.

## How can I change my card's name?

  1. Select one of your cards from the Card's tab.

  2. Tap on Settings

  3. Tap on "Personalize Name"

  4. Enter your new card name. You're good to go! 🚀

## How can I personalize a card's name on bunq Web?

  1. Go to https://web.bunq.com

  2. Click on the card

  3. Select ⚙️

  4. Click on "Personalize Name"

  5. Select the name and apply
