# Credit card payments for your bunq.me link

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/credit-card-payments-for-your-bunqme-link
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Your personal payment link just went international 🌍

**bunq.me links** now support **iDEAL, and debit and credit card payments**. That means if you cover the bill for a colleague or friend, you can get paid back easier than ever! **No hassle, no waiting.**

### How do I pay a bunq.me link with a credit or debit card ?

  1. **Open** the bunq.me link you’ve been sent

  2. Fill in **the amount** you want to pay

  3. **Tap** on Pay now

  4. **Select** Credit or Debit Card

  5. Fill in your **card details**

  6. **Tap** on Pay securely with card

## What is a bunq.me link?

**bunq.me** is the easiest way to get money back from people all over Europe. In just a matter of seconds, you can create **a personal payment link** and share it as many times as you want - it never expires! You only need to select a payment amount, your preferred payment method and confirm the transaction. Then you’ll instantly receive the money alongside a confirmation notification!

## How can I create my own bunq.me link?

You can see how to create your own personal payment link [here](<./what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>).

## Good to know

Keep the following in mind before adding money to a bunq.me link with a credit or debit card:

  * There is a processing fee of 2.5% for the recipient.

  * For regulatory reasons, **Visa or Mastercard payments** are only available from these countries. Only personal debit and credit cards are accepted, not business or company cards.

  * For safety reasons (and to save the recipient money on transaction fees), credit and debits card payments to your bunq.me link must be higher than €10 and lower €500.

  * **Personal** : you can receive up to €1000 per day 

  * **Business** : you can receive up to €5000 per day
