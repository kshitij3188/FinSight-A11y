# Easily distinguish all your Apple Pay & Google Pay cards with custom designs

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/easily-distinguish-all-your-apple-pay-google-pay-cards-with-custom-designs

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

To make it easier for you to differentiate your bunq cards in your Apple & Google Wallets, you’ll now be able to customize the design of your cards so you can instantly spot the right one 🙌

### How do I customize the appearance of my bunq cards in Apple Pay and Google Pay?

When adding a new card to your Apple or Google Wallet, you’ll be able to choose between 6 different card customizations. This will help you find the right one at a glance and spread your favorite bunq color into your Wallet.

## How do I add my card to Apple and Google Pay?

If you haven’t added your bunq cards to your Wallet yet, you can find how to so with your Apple Wallet [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5826>) and your Google Wallet [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/8480>). 📲

  1. Go to 💳Cards Tab

  2. Tap on one of your cards

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on "Set up Apple/ Google Pay"

  5. Choose your Apple/ Google Pay Appearance. 

  6. Click on **Add to your Wallet** You’ll be redirected to your your Wallet app. 

  7. Press "Next" after filling in your name and card number. 

  8. Agree to the Terms and Conditions.

_Note: Apple Pay & Google Pay are services that aren’t available in every country yet. You can see if they’re currently available in your country _[_here_](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5826>) _._

## How many times can I change the design of my bunq cards?

You can change your card’s design in Apple Pay and Google Pay as many times as you want. If you decide to give a new look to one of your cards, it will be updated in your Wallet automatically! Customization at its finest. 🎨
