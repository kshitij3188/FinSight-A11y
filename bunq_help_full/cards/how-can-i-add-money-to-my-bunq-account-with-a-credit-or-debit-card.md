# How can I add money to my bunq account with a credit or debit card?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-add-money-to-my-bunq-account-with-a-credit-or-debit-card
**Topics:** Account, Cards

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

As most of you know, here at bunq we offer you several [top up](<https://www.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-add-money-to-my-bunq-account>) options. Now we offer you the possibility to do so with a Credit or Debit card 🙌

There are a few things to bear in mind before you try to top up with your Credit/Debit card:

  * There's a fee of 0.5% for EEA cards

  * You can use a Visa or Mastercard from these countries. Only personal cards are accepted, **not business/company cards** \- bunq Elite, Pro, Core, and Free users can top up €500 by card for free every 30 days, starting from your last billing date.

### How can I top up my account using my card?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on **Add Money**

  3. Select the account

  4. Select **Credit or Debit Card**

  5. Fill in your card number, expiry date and CVC

  6. Tap on **Next**

## What cards are supported?

> _For euro accounts, we currently only support cards that originate from the EU. We're working on improving this coverage to allow cards from other countries to be used!_

If your country isn’t supported yet, no worries! You can always [top up your bunq account](<https://www.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-add-money-to-my-bunq-account>) using a bank transfer, iDEAL, Instant Bank Transfer or TransferWise!
