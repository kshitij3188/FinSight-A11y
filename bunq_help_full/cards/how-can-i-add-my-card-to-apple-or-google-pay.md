# How can I add my card to Apple or Google Pay?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-add-my-card-to-apple-or-google-pay

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Whether you'd like to add a Digital or Physical bunq card to Apple or Google Pay, it's easy with the bunq App. Here's how you can add your card to Apple or Google Pay. 

### How do I add my card to Apple Pay?

Follow these easy steps:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards Tab

  2. Select one of your cards

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Set up Apple Pay**

  5. Tap on Add Card in the pop-up below your chosen card 

  6. Choose your Apple Pay Appearance 

  7. Click on **Add to Apple Wallet** – you’ll be redirected to your Apple Wallet app

  8. Press **Next** after filling in your name and card number

  9. Agree to the Terms and Conditions

You can read more about adding your card to Apple Pay - [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-add-my-bunq-card-to-apple-pay>). 

### How to add my card to Google Pay?

Follow the easy and fast steps below:

  1. Go to 💳 Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want to add

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Set up Google Pay**

  5. Personalize the card

  6. Fill in your info at Google!

  7. All done & ready to use your bunq card with Google Pay 🙌

You can read more about adding your card to Google Pay - [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-add-my-card-to-google-pay>).
