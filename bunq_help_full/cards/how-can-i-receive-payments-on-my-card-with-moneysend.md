# How can I receive payments on my card with MoneySend?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-receive-payments-on-my-card-with-moneysend
**Topics:** Cards, Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Wondering how you can receive instant payments on your card? It’s now possible with Mastercard MoneySend. You can receive a payment easily - the sender only needs to know your card number. Moreover, you can also get refunds from merchants directly on your card!

## How can I do that?

  1. Provide your **card number** and the **cardholder name** to the sender

  2. They should initiate a transaction to your **card number**

  3. That's it! You will receive your money instantly 💸 Our mission is to make your life easy every day, so this is why we’ve diversified the way you receive payments and refunds. Interested to see all other options? Just take a look here.
