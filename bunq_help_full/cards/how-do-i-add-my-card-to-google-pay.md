# How do I add my card to Google Pay?

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Excited to start using [Google Pay](<https://together.bunq.com/d/8480>) with your [physical card](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936-how-can-i-order-a-card>) or [Virtual Card](<https://together.bunq.com/d/7960>)? _Note: Keep in mind that the label of the card's country is determined by the country from which it was ordered. This label is what influences its compatibility with Google Pay._

### How to add my card to Google Pay?

Follow the easy and fast steps below:

  1. Go to 💳 Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want to add

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on "Set up Google Pay"

  5. Personalize the card 

  6. Fill in your info at Google!

  7. All done & ready to use your bunq card with Google Pay 🙌

## How can I add my card to Google Pay on my smart watch?

You can do so by following the steps given [here](<https://support.google.com/wearos/answer/12059876?hl=en>) by Google.

## How can I pay with Google Pay?

  * Make sure the terminal supports contactless payments.

  * Unlock your device (unless the amount is below €25).

  * Hold your device against the terminal. Done!

Check out how it works [here](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z5M5n8ZOBfg>).

Please keep in mind that the NFC function on your phone needs to be switched on to use Google Pay. You also need to change the NFC settings if you’ve used Tap&Pay in the past. You can do this from your phone's Settings, then follow these steps:

  1. Tap Apps & Notifications and then Default apps

  2. Your Default apps might be under Advanced

  3. Select Tap & Pay

  4. Set Google Pay as the default app

  5. Done 🎉

## How can I set Google Pay as my default paying app?

  1. Tap Apps & Notifications and then Default apps

  2. Your Default apps might be under **Advanced**

  3. Select **Google Pay**

  4. Set Google Pay as the default app

  5. Done 🎉

## Adding Google Pay as a minor

If you're a minor and trying to activate Google Pay, please be aware that you must be a minimum age in order to do so. The minimum age varies by country, so be sure to check out country-specific age requirements in the list below:

  * Netherlands: 16 years

  * Germany: 16 years

  * France: 16 years

  * Italy: 16 years

  * Spain: 14 years

  * Belgium: 16 years

  * Ireland: 16 years

 _**Note:** Parents or legal guardians must provide their child with permission to use Google Pay and agree to the Terms and Conditions._

## Why am I receiving 2 notifications for my Google Pay payment?

Instant notifications from bunq give you a quick overview of how much you’ve paid in euros, how much you’ve saved with ZeroFX, how much you paid in euros when making a payment in a foreign currency, and which account was used via AutoSelect.

You may also receive a notification from Google Pay for the same payment.

## How can I turn off the Google Pay notifications?

If you’d like to turn off Google Pay notifications, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Google Wallet app

  2. Tap on your Profile

  3. Tap on Wallet Settings

  4. Tap “Manage Wallet Notifications"

  5. Toggle off “Notifications”
