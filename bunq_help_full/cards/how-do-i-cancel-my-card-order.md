# How do I cancel my card order?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-cancel-my-card-order
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Did you accidentally order the card with wrong details or did you change your mind? You can still cancel your card if you just created the order. Please follow these steps:

**For iOS users**

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Choose the card you’ve just ordered 

  3. Tap on **Cancel Card Order**

**For Android users**

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Choose the card you’ve just ordered 

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Cancel Card Order**

If you wish to change your address, check the steps on [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3748>)

## How to order a new card?

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Tap on **Add Card**

  3. Choose your card of preference and follow the flow

You can see more on the matter [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936>).
