# How do I export a card statement? 💳

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-card-statement

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

A card statement is an overview of all payments that were done with one card during a certain period of time. In the statement you'll see your name, the last four digits of your card number and the name on the card. It's got your name, the last four digits of your card, and the cardholder's name. It also shows which IBAN was used for each transaction - super useful if you've got multiple IBANs linked to your card! You can use this document for your own financial records or to prove that you are the card owner 📑

**Note:** Keep in mind that we won't be able to export a bank statement for you if your account is unverified. To export a statement make sure to [verify your account in just 5 minutes](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>)

### How can I export a card statement?

These are the steps to export the statement easily:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Select Accounting 

  3. Click on **Card Statements**

  4. Export your statement 🚀

Or you can also follow these steps:

  1. Go to 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the card in question 

  3. Click on ⚙️Settings, below your card 

  4. Click on 🟢 Export card statement 

  5. Export your statement 🚀
