# How do I find my card details?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-find-my-card-details

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Do you want to make an online payment and need to find your card details? Have a look below to see how to start doing this.

### How do I find my card details?

You can easily do that in the app! Just follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the 💳**Cards** tab

  2. Select the card in question 

  3. Your card details are visible below the card

## Where can I find my CVC?

You can find your CVC code for all of your bunq cards within the app. To locate it, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the**Cards** tab 

  2. Select the card in question 

  3. The CVC details are visible below the card

## How do I find my card details on bunq Web?

  1. Go to <https://web.bunq.com>

  2. Select the card in question 

  3. The info will be shown on the screen 🎉

## How can I set up a rotating CVC?

  1. Go to the 💳Cards tab

  2. Select the card in question 

  3. Tap on CVC 

  4. Toggle on/off for Automatically Regenerate

Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50055>).
