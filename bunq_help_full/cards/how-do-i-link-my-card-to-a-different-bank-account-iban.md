# How do I link my card to a different Bank Account (IBAN)?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-link-my-card-to-a-different-bank-account-iban
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Want to use your card to pay with another account during your holidays? Or would you like to connect it to a friend's account that he/she connected to you?

With bunq you're free to switch the accounts attached to your cards whenever you wish!

### How do I do that?

  1. Go to the Cards Tab 

  2. Tap on the desired card 

  3. Tap on **Auto Select** under “Pay From”

  4. Select any new Bank Account and you are done ✅

**Note:** If you want to reverse this action, you should do it manually.

## Good to know

  * Keep in mind that you can link only regular Bank Accounts to your card. This means that you won't be able to link and use your Savings Account or Easy Investments Account as your card's billing account. 

  * You can also set a Dual PIN linked to a Secondary Bank Account on the same card. To do so, see the steps mentioned [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1993>). Keep in mind that this feature is available only for bunq Pro and bunq Elite bunqers.
