# How do I pay with Google Pay?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-pay-with-google-pay
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

## How to use Google Pay in physical stores

  * Make sure the **terminal supports contactless payments**.

  * **Unlock your device** (unless the amount is below €25).

  * Hold your device against the terminal. Done! Check out how it works [here](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z5M5n8ZOBfg>). Please keep in mind that the **NFC function on your phone needs to be switched on to use Google Pay**. You also need to change the NFC settings if you’ve used Tap&Pay in the past. You can do this from your phone's Settings, then follow these steps:

  * Tap **Apps & Notifications** and then **Default apps**

  * Your **Default apps** might be under **Advanced**

  * Select **Tap & Pay**

  * Set **Google Pay as the default app**

  * Done 🎉

## How to use Google Pay online

  * Select ‘**buy with Google Pay** ’ when you’re in the checkout of the webshop.

  * Select the **card you want to pay with**.

  * Click continue. If there is no card yet at the checkout, you can add one and it will appear when you use Google Pay next time.

  * Confirm your purchase. Check out how it works [here](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yl5SGXiPnDY>).
