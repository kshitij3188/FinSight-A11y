# How do I reactivate my bunq card?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-reactivate-my-bunq-card
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

You can always block your bunq card, temporarily or permanently, directly from the app or bunq web.

If you have temporarily blocked your card, you have the option to reactivate it at any time. To reactivate your bunq card, follow these easy and fast steps:

#### bunq App

You can reactivate your card from the bunq app by following these steps:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want to activate

  3. Tap on Reactivate Card

  4. Choose and confirm your new PIN

#### bunq Web

You can also reactivate your card via bunq Web by following these steps:

  1. Log in to bunq Web

  2. Select the card you want to activate

  3. Click on Reactivate Card

  4. Choose and confirm your new PIN

You're all set to start using your card!

**Note:** Keep in mind that a card blocked permanently **can’t** be reactivated but you can [order a new one](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936>) in seconds.
