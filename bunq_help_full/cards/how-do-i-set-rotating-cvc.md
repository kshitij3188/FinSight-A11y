# How do I set rotating CVC?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-set-rotating-cvc

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

Your security is our number one priority, and we always want to make sure you're protected 🔒

Now you can enjoy the peace of mind that comes with a rotating CVC code. You have the option to manually create a new CVC that doesn't expire, or configure it to be automatically generated. This process is straightforward and quick, allowing you to personalize your CVC settings with ease.

### How do I set it?

  1. Go to the 💳Cards tab

  2. Swipe through and select your preferred card 

  3. Tap on CVC Code under the card 

  4. Toggle on/off for Automatically Regenerate

## How is rotating CVC safer than the static one?

Unlike static CVCs that are vulnerable to phishing, skimming, and data breaches, rotating CVC codes offer a safer alternative, adding an additional level of security. If compromised, the code becomes invalid once it changes, significantly reducing the risk of fraudulent transactions.

## How do I find my CVC?

You can find your CVC code for all of your bunq cards within the app. To locate it follow the steps below:

  1. Go to 💳 Cards tab

  2. Swipe and select a card of your choice

  3. The CVC info will be on the screen below your card

## How do I find the rest of my card info?

  1. Go to 💳 Cards tab

  2. Swipe and select a card of your choice

  3. The info will be shown on the screen below your card

Further information on how to find your card's details can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3019>).
