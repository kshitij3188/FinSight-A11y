# How do I top up my account with Google Pay?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-top-up-my-account-with-google-pay
**Topics:** Account, Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

### How do I top up my account with Google Pay?

Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on Add Money

  3. Select Google Pay

  4. Follow the flow and top up ✅

## What are the limits?

Keep in mind that adding money via Google Pay is a **card top up method**. This means that every deposited amount with Google Pay will count towards your **card top up limit**.

For example: If you top up your bunq account with €100 by using Google Pay and €50 by using a regular card, both amounts (€150 in total) will count towards your **daily card top up limit**.

You can see what are the **card top up limits** [here](<./what-are-my-daily-card-cash-deposits-and-withdrawals-limits>)

## Are there fees for Google Pay top ups? 

Yes, there can be fees when you top up with Google Pay.

The first €500 you top up each month is free of charge. After you reach the €500 limit and if you top up with Google Pay, you will be charged:

  * a fee of 0.5% for European Economic Area (EEA) issued cards or;

  * a fee of 2.5% for non-EEA issued cards.
