# How do I use my cards before delivery?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-use-my-cards-before-delivery
**Topics:** Cards

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Just ordered your shiny new bunq card? We totally get that you can't wait to start using it 🚀

Here's some fantastic news for you - there's absolutely no need to wait until your physical card arrives. As soon as you've ordered your card, you can activate it by following the steps mentioned [here](<./how-do-i-activate-and-deactivate-my-card>).

### Can I use it for payments?

You can find the card details right under the card in your app. Start using these details for online shopping straight away 🛍️

For those in-store purchases, you'll need to wait until your physical card arrives. But don't worry, you can also activate your card for **Apple Pay or Google Pay** and start using it in physical stores right away.

> To add your card to Apple Pay, follow the steps mentioned [here](<./how-do-i-add-my-bunq-card-to-apple-pay>). To add your card to Google Pay, follow the steps mentioned [here](<./how-do-i-add-my-card-to-google-pay>).
