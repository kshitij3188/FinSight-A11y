# How does bunq keep my card secure?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-does-bunq-keep-my-card-secure
**Topics:** Cards

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Looking to bring your colorful bunq card on your next big adventure? We’ve taken all the necessary steps to make sure your card is protected anywhere you go 🌏

In this topic, you'll find all the information regarding features that will ensure your card's protection, so you can use it wherever you are without any worries.

## Localized Card Protection

Keep your card secure by enabling the “**Localized Card Protection** ” toggle. By doing so, you’ll be able to use your card only within a 100km radius of your location. More information about this feature can be found [here](<./how-does-localized-card-protection-work>).

## Tip Protection

The bunq app automatically detects an overcharged tip for you, so you can get your money back easily. We will automatically notify you through the app. More information about **Tip Protection** can be found [here](<./what-is-tip-protection>).

## Block a merchant

Have you forgotten to stop your subscription and the merchant is still charging you? You can now **block merchants** within a few simple steps, mentioned [here](<./can-i-block-a-merchant>).

## Illegal and repeated transactions protection

We would automatically block high-risk and repeated payments to keep your card secure from fraudulent activities. You can easily decide which merchant you want to **Auto Accept or Block**. Further information can be found [here](<./illegal-and-repeated-transactions-protection>).

## Pro tips

> Don't forget to bring your bunq Credit Mastercard when traveling outside of Europe. If you wish to block your card, don’t hesitate to reach our [SOS Support at +31208083666](<./how-can-i-contact-sos-support>). Using this automated flow, you’ll be able to temporarily block your card within 1 minute. This will keep your account and funds safe until you regain access. If you suspect your account was hacked, please report the case by filling [this form](<https://www.bunq.com/contact/report/hacked-account>). Enjoy your trip! 🛫 🌍
