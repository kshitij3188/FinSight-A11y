## Extra billing

You can review the applicable ATM fees for withdrawals [here](<../>). Keep in mind, that some ATMs have their own additional fees.

## ATM didn't gave you the cash

Request a refund right away by following the steps mentioned [here.](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-a-chargeback>)

### When can I request a chargeback for an ATM withdrawal?

  * If the ATM didn’t dispense part or all of the cash, and the amount was deducted from your account and didn’t return.

### When can’t I request a chargeback for an ATM withdrawal?

  * If the ATM didn’t dispense part or all of the cash, but the amount was automatically reversed and returned to your account.

  * If the ATM charged a withdrawal fee. Some ATMs have their own fees, and these aren’t eligible for a refund or chargeback.

**Important:** Repeatedly requesting chargebacks for successful ATM withdrawals or ATM fees is considered abuse of the chargeback process. To protect all users, we may offboard accounts that misuse chargebacks.
