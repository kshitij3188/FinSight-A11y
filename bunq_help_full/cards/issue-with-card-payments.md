## How can I troubleshoot a card payment?

In case of card payment issue, you can always try to resolve it simply by following our guidelines for troubleshooting. 

  * You can learn more about this in: [How do I solve a failed card payment?](<./how-do-i-solve-a-failed-card-payment>)

  * If you notice an **unrecognized transaction** , you can **report it directly to us, request a refund and freeze your card for safety** in just a few taps. You can learn more about this in: [I don't recognize this payment](<./i-dont-recognize-this-payment>)
