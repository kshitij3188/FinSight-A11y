# Loyalty Cards

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/loyalty-cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Never worry about bringing dozens of loyalty cards with you. Simply add them to the app and have full access anytime, anywhere.

### How do I add Loyalty Cards?

Follow the steps mentioned below:

  1. Tap on the 💳Cards tab

  2. Swipe right and tap on **Add Card**

  3. Choose Loyalty Card

  4. Find the Loyalty Card you want to add

  5. Scan the barcode or add the card number manually

  6. Done! You can start using it right away 🎉

It’s now easier than ever to enjoy the benefits of your loyalty cards! When the time comes to pay, you can find them all quickly with a few taps in the app ⭐️

## Where can I find the Loyalty Cards in the app?

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab

  2. Loyalty Cards will be visible here

## How can I share my Loyalty Card?

You can easily share your Loyalty Card by clicking on the card on your Home tab and tapping 🟠 Share. Or by following these easy steps:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards tab

  2. Swipe and find your Loyalty Card 

  3. Select 'Share' below your card 

  4. Select the recipient's contact

  5. Share the details

  6. You're done!

Once whoever you sent it to opens the link, they'll be able to add it to their bunq app too by clicking on 🟦 Add Loyalty Card.

Keep in mind that you can share your Loyalty Cards only with family and friends who have a verified bunq account 🙌

## Good to know

  * Introduced with [bunq Update 16](<https://together.bunq.com/d/41414>)

  * This feature is available for bunq Core, Pro, and Elite users. 

  * Local merchants can be added to the loyalty cards too

  * We support QR codes, data matrix, code 39, or ITF based codes

  * Two users can use the same loyalty cards.
