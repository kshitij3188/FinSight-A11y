# Metal Credit Card: How does it work and how can I order?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/metal-credit-card-how-does-it-work-and-how-can-i-order
**Topics:** Cards, Frequently Asked Questions

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Pay in style wherever you go with the Metal Credit Card: 22 grams of heavy stainless steel, engraved with your preferred name. bunq Pro and bunq Elite users can now enjoy our new Metal Credit Card, for a one-time charge of €99.

_Note: Since your Metal Card is made from 100% stainless steel, the black notch next to the chip acts as its antenna. It lets you make contactless payments regardless of which side you hold to the payment terminal, so you can pay in style effortlessly._

### How can I order a Metal Credit Card?

  1. Go to the 💳Cards tab

  2. Tap on **Add Card**

  3. Choose the **Metal Card**

  4. Choose the name you want displayed and the delivery address

  5. Tap on **Order My Card**

  6. Done ✅

### Limited edition cards

After launching the new Metal Card in bunq Update 26, the Limited Edition of the first 1.000 cards sold out in 1 hour! Thank you for your enthusiasm 🚀

If you didn’t catch the Limited Edition cards, don’t worry - you can still order your Metal Card by following the steps explained above!

## How does the new Metal Credit Card work?

The new Metal Credit Card is available to all verified bunq Pro and bunq Elite users. It’ll cost you a one-time fee of €99 and you can link it to any of your Bank Accounts as the other physical cards from bunq. Once you receive and activate your Metal Credit Card, you can use it right away.

## Is the Metal Credit Card a real credit card?

The Metal Credit Card functions as a real credit card, allowing you to make purchases without the risk of going into debt.

As it is recognized as a credit card by merchants, it ensures optimal acceptance both online and globally. Simply load money onto the card and it's ready for use. For more comprehensive information, please visit [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/23426>).

## When does my Metal Credit Card expire?

Metal Credit Cards expire in **4 years** after the date of order (example: If you've **ordered** the card on September 27, 2024, the card will be active until September 27, 2028).

## Can I replace my Metal Credit Card?

While we don't offer free replacements for Metal Credit Cards like we do with our regular cards, you can order a replacement Metal Credit Card for a fee of €99.

However, if your card was damaged due to a production error, please contact our [Support Center](<https://together.bunq.com/d/32181>) and we’ll look into it for you.

Please note that the Metal Credit Card is made from 100% stainless steel and features a black notch next to the chip, which acts as its antenna. This is often mistaken for a dent or damage but is actually there to make sure your payments go through seamlessly. The notch isn't a production defect and isn't covered by our free replacement policy.
