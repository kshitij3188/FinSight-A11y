# My bunq card hasn't arrived

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/my-bunq-card-hasn-t-arrived
**Topics:** Cards, Frequently Asked Questions

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Looking to replace a card that didn't arrive? You can easily request a new card within the bunq app.

Keep in mind that cards take up to 14 days to be delivered. Delays in delivery can be a result of delays in your local postal services or your address not visible at your residence 💳

### How do I replace my card that didn't arrive?

Follow the steps below to see how to replace your card that didn't arrive:

  1. Go to the 💳 **Cards** tab 

  2. Select the card which didn't arrive

  3. Tap on **My Card Never Arrived**

  4. Order your replacement card

  5. Review the information and select **Get Your Card**

  6. You can Add your Card to Apple Pay or Google Pay to start using your card immediately, or select **Not now** on the confirmation screen and you're all set! 

## Fees and replacement possibilities

You get one free card replacement each year with the [bunq Pro](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3762>) and [bunq Elite](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38432>) plans. This applies to cards that:

  * Have not arrived within 2 weeks. You can check your estimated delivery date in the app to confirm this.

  * The delivery address provided was incorrect.

  * May have gotten some extra wear and tear.

  * You can also check if you ordered a Virtual Credit Card instead of a physical one.

If you are on the [bunq Core](<https://together.bunq.com/d/44002>) plan, you can order a replacement card for a replacement fee of €9.99 per card.

## How can I change my address?

To change your address, follow the steps mentioned bellow:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on Personal Information

  3. Change the details

  4. Edit or update information accordingly.

  5. Select Done

## Already used your 1 free card replacement?

If you are a bunq Pro or Elite user, you can get one free card replacement per year.

Anything over this replacement per year will cost €9.99 for production costs (or €99 for a Metal Card). You can find more information in our pricing doc [here](<https://www.bunq.com/en/pricing-sheet>).

The free replacement does not apply to Metal Cards. Replacing Metal Cards comes with a €99 fee.
