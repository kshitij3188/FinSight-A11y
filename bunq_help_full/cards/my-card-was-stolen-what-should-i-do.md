# My card was stolen - what should I do? 💳

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/my-card-was-stolen-what-should-i-do
**Topics:** Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Your card was stolen? Breathe in, breathe out - we’ve got you covered! All you have to do is follow the steps in this topic.

## Block your card temporarily

The first thing to do is protect your current balance and prevent the possibility of unrecognized transactions. This is done by blocking your card temporarily:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want to block

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings under your card

  4. Tap on **Block Card**

  5. Select Block Temporarily 

  6. Done

You can see more details about blocking your card temporary or permanently [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3509>) 💳

## Order a replacement

Once you’ve secured your money by blocking your stolen card temporarily, you can order a replacement and keep making payments everywhere you go.

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the card you want a replacement for

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select **Replace**

  5. Edit card details (if applicable) and select **Next**

  6. Review the information and select **Order Card**

You can learn all about card replacements and the applicable fees [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1974>) 👀

## I followed the steps above, but I spotted an unrecognized payment. What should I do?

If you see payments in your account you don’t recognize - don’t worry about it. You can follow the steps explained here to request a refund right away.

If the refund you’ve requested was refused by the merchant, make sure to contact them for further assistance as explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/27036>).

Keep in mind that if the unrecognized payments for which you want a refund exceed €150.00, you'll need to provide a police report with your request.
