# What are my daily card, cash deposits and withdrawals limits?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-my-daily-card-cash-deposits-and-withdrawals-limits
**Topics:** Cash, Cards

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Here you can check your daily limits for PIN and online card transactions, card top-ups, cash deposits, cash withdrawals and contactless payments.

  * Looking for your daily payment limits? Check [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/49784>) 💸

  * Looking for your daily Foreign Currencies limits? Check [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/46763>)💰

## PIN and online card transactions

  * Default limit: €1,000 per 24 hours

  * Maximum limit: €50,000 per 24 hours

💡Card limits only apply to card payments. You can set your limit per card or apply the [same limit](<https://together.bunq.com/d/4757>) to all of your cards.  
💡When performing a chip payment, the limit for the chip is used but the calculation of money spent today doesn't take the contactless + PIN into account

## Card top-up

**Important note:** Apple Pay and Google Pay top-ups count towards your monthly card top-up limit. Learn more about [Apple Pay here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/56643>) and [Google Pay here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61572>).

#### New accounts up to 1 month

  * You can top up a maximum €1,200 in 30 days

  * You can top up a maximum €800 in 7 days

  * You can top up maximum €500 per 24 hours

#### Regular accounts older than 1 month

  * You can top up a maximum €2,000 in 30 days

  * You can top up a maximum €1,500 in 7 days

  * You can top up maximum €500 per 24 hours

## Cash deposit

#### Paysafe

**Deposits**

  * Minimum (per deposit): €10

  * Maximum (per deposit): €250

  * Daily limit: €999

  * Monthly limit: €9,999

  * The limits apply for both Personal and Business users.

#### Barzahlen

**Deposits**

  * Minimum (per deposit): €50

  * Maximum (per deposit): €900

  * Daily limit: €999

  * Monthly limit: €9,999

**Withdrawals**

  * Minimum daily limit (per withdrawal): €1

  * Maximum daily limit (per withdrawal): €200

  * The limits apply for both Personal and Business users.

## Cash withdrawals

Personal Users

  * Account verified less than 3 months ago: €250 per calendar day, per person

  * Account verified longer than 3 months ago: €1000 per calendar day, per person

Business Users

  * Default limit: €1000 per card, per calendar day

  * Maximum limit: €1000 per card, per calendar day

Keep in mind that:

  1. the ATM may charge its own transaction fee or have its own limit (e.g. some ATMs in the Netherlands don't let you withdraw more than €250/300 per 24 hours).

  2. The above limits are the maximum technically possible. It is not possible to set a higher daily limit.

## Contactless payments

There's a **€50 limit per contactless payment that doesn't require a PIN** in:

Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Cyprus, Estonia, France, French Guiana, Greece, Germany, Guadeloupe, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Malta, Martinique, Monaco, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, St Pierre and Miquelon.

❗️ Contactless limits in a currency other than EUR are listed [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/27058-what-are-the-limits-for-contactless-payments>).

Your **PIN** will be requested **after 5 contactless payments** or when you've made contactless payments for more than 150 EUR (cumulative). So if the 4th contactless payment makes the sum of the 4 payments higher than 150 EUR, the PIN will be requested.

## How are limits calculated?

#### Daily

The limit is based on the previous calendar day.

#### Weekly

The limit is based on the last 7 days.

#### Monthly

If today’s date exists in the previous month, the limit is set till that same date (e.g., July 5 checks June 5). If the date doesn’t exist in the previous month (e.g., July 31 since June only has 30 days), the limit is set till the first day of the previous month (e.g. July 31 checks June 1).

## Good to know

  * The language of the terminals depends on the language of the card. Your card language is set automatically as the phone language at the point of ordering the card. The only way to change the terminal language is to order a new/replacement card with the preferred language set.

  * You have one card limit in place that applies to all transactions and in addition, you can choose to have the limit apply to all cards, or set a limit for each individual card. Keep in mind, this limit is a cumulative limit and is applied per card. This means, if your limit is €1000 and it applies to all cards, you can spend €1000 on each card.

  * The contactless limit in the Netherlands is €25 (keep in mind this limit may vary per country). As such, anything above €25 will require a PIN☝️.

  * You can however spend up to €50 contactless cumulatively. So, when you reach the €50 limit, you'll need to provide a PIN and the counter will reset again
