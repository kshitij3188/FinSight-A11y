# What are my daily payment limits?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-my-daily-payment-limits
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We’ve created a list of daily payment limits to make life easy for you. Check them out below:

### Contactless payments

There's a **€50 limit per contactless payment that don't require a PIN code** in:

Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Cyprus, Estonia, France, French Guiana, Greece, Germany, Guadeloupe, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Malta, Martinique, Monaco, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, St Pierre and Miquelon.

❗️ Contactless limits in a currency other than EUR are listed here.

Your **PIN** will be requested **after 5 contactless payments** or when you've made contactless payments for more than 150 EUR (cumulative). So if the 4th contactless payment makes the sum of the 4 payments higher than 150 EUR, the PIN will be requested.

## Apple Pay and Google Pay

The security limits for Apple Pay can be found [here](<./how-do-i-add-my-bunq-card-to-apple-pay>) and the ones for Google Pay [here](<./how-to-set-up-and-use-google-pay>).

## Incoming transfers

Bank transfers (sent to EUR currency Bank Account): No limit!

iDeal:

  * €50,000 per top-up

  * €10,000 per payment request

## Outgoing transfers

[Instant Payments](<./does-bunq-support-instant-payments>): 

  * Personal: €1,000,000 total per day

  * Business: €1,000,000 total per day 

iDeal and SOFORT:

  * Personal: €50,000 total per day

  * Business: €50,000 per transaction and €100,000 total per day

Limit per IBAN (Personal):

  * Default limit: €5,000 total per day

  * Maximum limit: €1,000,000 total per day

‼️ Keep in mind that your overall daily Personal account limit remains €1,000,000 in total.

Limit per IBAN (Business):

  * Default limit: €5,000 total per day

  * Maximum limit: €1,000,000 total per day

‼️ Keep in mind that your overall daily Business account limit remains €1,000,000 in total.

## Incoming payment via bunq.me

  * iDeal payments: €2,000 per day

  * SOFORT payments: €250 total per day

  * Bancontact: €1,000 per transaction

  * Debit/credit card payments: from €10 to €500 [per transaction](<./credit-card-payments-for-your-bunqme-link>)

## Good to know

  * Bank Account limits only apply to non card-transactions (e.g. a payment to another bunq user, or paying with iDEAL at a webshop).

  * Bounce fees: For an incoming payment that bounced, transaction costs apply. These costs are deducted from the payment upon return.

  * If you try to make a payment with an incorrect CVC, the payment will fail, regardless of what your limit is. If you use the correct CVC, or the webshop doesn't ask for a CVC, then the payment will be accepted only if it falls under the cards daily limit and you have enough funds to make the payment.
