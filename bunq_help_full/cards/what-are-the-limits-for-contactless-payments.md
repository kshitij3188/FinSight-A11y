# What are the limits for contactless payments?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-limits-for-contactless-payments
**Topics:** Support, Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Contactless payments have become increasingly popular due to their convenience and speed. However, to ensure security and prevent potential misuse, we've set certain limits for these transactions.

## What are the limits?

To avoid having to enter your pin code on terminals as much as possible, we have set new single payment contactless limits in some countries:

  * A **€50** limit per contactless payment will apply in: **Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Cyprus, Estonia, France, French Guiana, Greece, Germany, Guadeloupe, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Malta, Martinique, Monaco, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, St Pierre and Miquelon**.

  * A **100 BGN** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Bulgaria**.

  * A **15’000 HUF** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Hungary**.

  * A **200 RON** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Romania**.

  * A **400 SEK** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Sweden**.

  * A **45 GBP** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **United Kingdom**.

## Which limits were increased?

  * A **4500 ALL** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Albania**.

  * A **60 BAM** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Bosnia and Herzegovina**.

  * A **45 FKP** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **Falkland Islands (Malvinas)**.

  * A **350 DKK** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **Faroe Islands**.

  * A **45 GIP** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Gibraltar**.

  * A **350 DKK** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Greenland**.

  * A **300 ILS** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Israel**.

  * A **40 EUR** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Kosovo, United Nations Mission in Kosovo**.

  * A **40 EUR** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **Liechtenstein**.

  * A **2000 MKD** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Macedonia**.

  * A **40 EUR** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **Montenegro**.

  * A **5000 RUB** limit per contactless payment will apply in the **Russian Federation**.

  * A **4000 RSD** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Serbia**.

  * A **45 SHP** limit per contactless payment will apply in **St Helena**.

  * A **500 NOK** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Svalbard and Jan Mayen**.

  * A **80 CHF** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Switzerland**.

  * A **250000 UZS** limit per contactless payment will apply in **Uzbekistan**.

For more information about the recent contactless limit changes, have a look at the [Mastercard press release](<https://www.mastercard.com/news/europe/en/newsroom/press-releases/en/2020/march/mastercard-enables-contactless-limit-raise-across-29-countries-and-champions-permanent-increase/>).
