# What's the difference between blocking a card temporarily and permanently?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/whats-the-difference-between-blocking-a-card-temporarily-and-permanently
**Topics:** Cards, Frequently Asked Questions

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Lost your card or want to get a new one? You have the option to block your card Temporarily (deactivate) or Permanently.

### What's the difference?

Blocking your card temporarily is a reversible action. If you deactivate your card, you can activate it once again in the future. If you want to reactivate your card, you can find out more here.

Blocking your card permanently is an irreversible action. If you block your card permanently, you can no longer unblock it and you will need to order a new card. You can find out how to order a new card here.

### How do I block my card temporarily?

To temporarily block your card, follow the steps below:

#### bunq App

  1. Go to 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the card you want to deactivate by swiping through your cards

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on Block Card

  5. Select 🟦 Block Temporarily

  6. Done 🎉

#### bunq Web

  1. Log in to bunq Web

  2. Select the card you want to deactivate

  3. Click on ⚙️Settings in the top right corner

  4. Click on Block Card

  5. Select 🟦 Block Temporarily

  6. Done 🎉

### How do I block my card permanently?

To block your card, follow the steps below:

#### bunq App

  1. Go to 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the card you want to block

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on Block Card

  5. Select 🟧 Block Permanently

  6. Done 🎉

#### bunq Web

  1. Log in to bunq Web

  2. Select the card you want to block

  3. Click on ⚙️ Settings in the top right corner

  4. Click on Block Card

  5. Select 🟧 Block Permanently

  6. Done 🎉

### Where can I find my blocked cards?

Wondering what happens to your cards when you block them?

#### Temporarily blocked cards

Your temporarily blocked cards will stay visible in your 💳 Cards Tab.

They will feature a little ❄️ Frozen sign to remind you that they are deactivated. If you would like to reactivate your card, you can do so by following the steps outlined [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-reactivate-my-bunq-card>).

#### Permanently blocked cards

To keep your 💳 Cards Tab tidy and easy-to–navigate, we move your permanently blocked cards to the *_Archive_.

If you need to access a permanently blocked card to check your card details, review payments or export a statement, you can do so by following these steps:

#### bunq App

  1. Go to your 🏡 Home tab

  2. Go to your Profile in the top left corner

  3. Select Archive

  4. Select Cards - your permanently blocked cards are visible here 

  5. Choose the permanently blocked card you want to view.

#### bunq Web

  1. Log in to bunq Web

  2. Click on Archive

  3. Select Cards

  4. Choose the permanently blocked card you want to view.

## Can my card get automatically blocked?

Due to security reasons, your card will be automatically blocked temporarily if there are multiple failed card transactions due to insufficient balance. If the same card keeps experiencing more failed transactions, it'll be blocked permanently.

In case your card is only blocked temporarily, you can re-activate it by simply following the steps explained above - once you do, you'll be able to either top-up the Bank Account linked to the card in question or link the card to a Bank Account with sufficient balance.
