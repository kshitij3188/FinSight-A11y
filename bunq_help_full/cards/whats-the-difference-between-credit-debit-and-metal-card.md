# What’s the difference between Credit, Debit and Metal Card?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/whats-the-difference-between-credit-debit-and-metal-card
**Topics:** Cards

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Can’t decide which pocket-sized rainbow to order?

With your bunq Pro and bunq Elite plans, you can pick a combination of any 3 physical cards (except Metal Cards) for free.

## What's the difference between them?

  * **Debit Card (Maestro)** : The perfect card to use within the Netherlands. Accepted country-wide at all Dutch terminals 🇳🇱 **Important note** : Keep in mind that Maestro Cards shouldn’t be used for online purchases due to Mastercard’s fraud prevention guidelines. Those guidelines are secure-proof and created in order to guarantee your protection online. Instead, you can always create a [Virtual Credit Card](<https://www.bunq.com/articles/what-are-virtual-cards-and-how-do-i-create-one>) for more secure online shopping experience 🚀

  * **Debit Card (Mastercard)** : A debit card, which is widely accepted by merchants and allows you to make online purchases 🛍️

  * **Credit Card** : A great choice for **international travel** 🌎 The best card to travel around the world - accepted worldwide for online payments. It's a credit card without the risk of falling into debt, accepted to make car rentals and hotel reservations. Have you ever had issues paying with a Mastercard in Albert Heijn in the Netherlands? See why here

  * **Metal Credit Card** : An excellent option for making payments worldwide. It features a sleek, full stainless steel design that adds a touch of elegance to your transactions ✨ For a one-time fee of €99, you can get your Metal Credit Card. This card is available to all active Easy Core, Pro, and Elite users.
