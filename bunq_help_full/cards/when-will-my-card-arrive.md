# When will my card arrive?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/when-will-my-card-arrive

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Congratulations on [ordering your bunq card](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936>)! Wondering what happens now?

  * You will receive your card in the mail within 10 business days.

  * You can check your estimated delivery date within the app.

Please keep in mind that we don't offer speed or expedited delivery (even for an extra cost).

### When will my card arrive?

You can check the estimated delivery date of your card within the app. Check out the steps below to see how:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Select the ordered card

  3. Check its status and the expected delivery date

## Your card hasn't arrived?

As an [bunq Pro](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3762>) or [bunq Elite](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38432>) user you get one free card replacement each year and can request as many card replacements as you'd like. This applies to cards that:

  * Have not arrived within 2 weeks. You can check your estimated delivery date in the app to confirm this.

  * The delivery address provided was incorrect.

  * May have gotten some extra wear and tear.

If your card doesn’t arrive by the estimated delivery date, make sure to double-check that your [address is correct](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3748-can-i-change-the-address-that-my-card-will-arrive-to>). If you confirm that your address is correct, you should then [order a replacement](<https://together.bunq.com/d/711-my-card-didn-t-arrive-how-do-i-replace-it>). Keep in mind that you receive 1 free card replacement per year.

## How can I change my address?

To change your address, follow the steps mentioned bellow:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Personal Information**

  3. Change the details

  4. Edit or update information accordingly.

  5. Select Done

## Already used your 1 free card replacement?

Anything over the one free replacement per year will cost €9.99 to account for production costs. You can find more information in our pricing doc [here](<https://bunq.com/pricing>).
