# Where can I see my card transaction overview 💳

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/where-can-i-see-my-card-transaction-overview

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Until now, you were able to track your payments history through your Bank Account. But from now on, you’ll also be able to track your card payments through your card’s screen! All the transactions that you make with your bunq card will be visible in the card overview in the app. How easy is that? 🚀

### How can I check this?

Simply follow these quick steps:

  1. Go to the 💳Cards Tab

  2. Click on the desired card

  3. Scroll down to check your recent transaction overview 💸
