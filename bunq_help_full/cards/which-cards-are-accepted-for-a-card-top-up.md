## Do you want to top up a Foreign Currency account?

**Foreign Currency accounts** can only be topped up with a card in the same currency as the account. For example you can only top up your Canadian dollar account with a card with CAD balance. A British pound card tops up GBP currency accounts, a USA dollar card tops up USD accounts, etc. To top up your **Foreign Currency Bank Account** , follow the steps below:

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on your Foreign Currencies Bank Account

  3. Select Add Money

  4. Chose your topping-up method

  5. Ka-ching! All done 🌈

More info can be found [here](<./how-can-i-add-and-convert-money-to-my-foreign-currency-account>) 🚀

## What if my country of origin is not supported?

We’re already working with our card processing company to expand this list, so everyone can soon top up their bunq account as easily as possible!

If your country isn’t supported yet, no worries! You can always top up your bunq account using a **bank transfer, iDEAL, Instant Bank Transfer or TransferWise!**

***Only personal cards are accepted, not business/company cards** 💳
