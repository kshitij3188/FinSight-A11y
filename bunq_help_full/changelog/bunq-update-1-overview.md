**Hey bunqers 🌈,**  
  
In 2015, bunq officially introduced itself to the world with its very first update—marking the birth of a new kind of bank. Built from the ground up by a small, passionate team, bunq set out to redefine how people experience money: fast, transparent, and joyful. What began as a startup idea during the height of the financial crisis soon became the first new independent bank in the Netherlands in 35 years.

At the launch, bunq unveiled the foundation of its philosophy—**real-time banking built for people, not institutions**. Every payment, transfer, and update would happen instantly, with full transparency and control in the hands of users.  
Founder Ali Niknam introduced bunq’s two guiding principles:

  1. **Technology-first:** banking should be fast, secure, and built like modern tech.

  2. **Customer-first:** bunq’s success depends on creating products people love to use, not have to use.

This vision came to life with bunq’s official beta launch—a fully functional banking app that anyone could join, explore, and help shape.

bunq Update 1 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

Let’s take a look at them!

  * **Real-Time Banking** : All payments are processed instantly, with real-time push notifications for every transaction.

  * **Contact Integration** : Send money directly to friends in your contact list—no more IBANs.

  * **Near Pay** : Pay people nearby without exchanging account numbers.

  * **Request & Split Bills** : Instantly request or split payments with friends, making group expenses effortless.

  * **Multiple Bank Accounts & Debit Cards** : Create and manage multiple accounts and link each to its own card for total flexibility.

  * **Fun & Friendly Design** : Add emojis, photos, and personality to every payment—banking made human.
