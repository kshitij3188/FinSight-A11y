**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

From a small unheated office to a packed Amsterdam venue, bunq celebrates its 10th major update — bigger, bolder, and freer than ever. This milestone marks a decade of proving the skeptics wrong and giving users true financial choice.

**The Bank of The Free** continues to empower you to live on your own terms: no queues, no compromises, and no hidden agendas — just world-class mobile banking built around freedom, transparency, and sustainability.

bunq Update 10 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Improved Invoice Scanning** : Scan, review, and **schedule** multiple invoices at once, saving even more time for business users.

  * **Upgraded Connect** : Share account access **safely in real time** with employees or partners—no full access, no paperwork, no risk.

  * **Dual PIN for Business** : Use **one card** for both personal and business accounts with two separate PINs for effortless budgeting.

  * **Online Cards** : Create **virtual cards** for online purchases and subscriptions, linked to specific accounts for simple spending control.

  * **Google Pay Launch** : Pay anywhere with your Android phone using Google Pay and your bunq card

  * **Joint Savings Goals** : Save together with friends, family, or partners toward shared dreams — from trips to projects.

  * **Freedom of Choice Investments** : Decide where bunq invests your money: only in **green and ethical** projects if you choose.

  * **Expanded Global Coverage** : bunq cards now work seamlessly **online and offline** , worldwide.

  * **Multi-invoice payments** : Batch scan, plan, and pay invoices directly from your phone.
