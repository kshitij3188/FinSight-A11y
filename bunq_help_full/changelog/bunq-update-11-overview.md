# bunq Update 11 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-11-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 11 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

**bunq Web** : The long-awaited web version of bunq is finally here!  
Log in without your phone, manage all your accounts, view balances, send payments, freeze cards, and use keyboard shortcuts for lightning-fast banking.  
  
Perfect for entrepreneurs and anyone who wants a secure fallback when their phone isn’t around.

  * **Travel Card Upgrade** : The**** bunq Travel Card now makes holidays easier than ever, with ZeroFX fees, full Mastercard acceptance, and no monthly costs—the simplicity of a debit card with the power of a credit card.

  * **Group Expenses** : Automatically keep track of who paid what when you’re traveling or hanging out with friends, directly inside the bunq app.

  * **Attachment Search** : Instantly find any receipt or invoice by searching for text within your attachments—no more endless scrolling.

  * **Auto-export Statements** : Automatically send password-protected monthly statements to your accountant, so you never have to export manually again.

  * **Auto VAT** : Set aside VAT automatically with each incoming payment, perfectly calculated per country—your taxes handled before the taxman knocks.

  * **Direct Accountancy Connections** : Stay synced with your accounting software in real time—no more imports, exports, or CSV headaches.

  * **Custom Account Icons** : Thanks to user feedback, every account can now have its own icon for clear and fun budgeting.

  * **Joint Savings Goals** : Save together with friends, family, or partners toward shared dreams.

  * **Expanded Cash Network** : Over 10,000 new deposit and withdrawal locations across Europe through a partnership with **Barzahlen/viacash**.

  * **Advanced Siri Shortcuts** : Pay or split bills hands-free with commands like “Hey Siri, split my last card payment with Tom.”

  * **AI-Powered Safety** : bunq’s intern team from TU Delft brought cutting-edge machine learning to make your money even safer.

  * **A European Vision** : bunq continues to grow rapidly beyond the Netherlands—now loved across Germany, France, Spain, Italy, and beyond.
