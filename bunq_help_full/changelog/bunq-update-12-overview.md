# bunq Update 12 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-12-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 12 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Apple Pay launches in Netherlands** : Pay instantly and securely with your iPhone or Apple Watch across millions of stores worldwide.

  * **Automatic slice groups** : bunq now adds **your card payments to slice groups automatically** , so trips and group outings settle themselves. Create a group, travel with friends, and bunq will keep track of who paid what in real time.

  * **Seamless settlement** : After your holiday, just tap _View Balances_ and _Settle All Expenses_ —bunq calculates and finalizes everything for you.

  * **In-App card refunds** : Request refunds directly from the bunq app in seconds. No calls, no forms, and real-time status tracking.

  * **Smarter AutoVAT** : Now tracks both **incoming and outgoing payments** , ensuring your VAT pot stays perfectly balanced.

  * **Match Receipts** : Snap multiple receipts at once, and bunq’s smart scanner will automatically match them to the correct payments and send them to your accountant.

  * **Auto-Export Statements** : Automatically email password-protected monthly statements to your accountant—set it once, forget forever.

  * **Travel Assistant** : Get timely travel tips: enable cards for your destination, receive cash reminders, live FX rates, and track how much you’ve saved thanks to Zero FX.

  * **Balance Graph + AI Forecast** : A beautiful visual timeline of your daily balances, with AI predicting your future cash flow—helping you decide if those new Nikes can wait.

  * **Automatic Spending Insights** : bunq now categorizes all your spending automatically into clear visual groups. Tap any slice of the donut chart to view detailed transactions, and if you recategorize one, bunq learns your preferences for the future.
