**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

And just like that, lucky number 13 was full of amazing new features to make life easy and save you time, every time. Here’s a summary of what’s new:

## Time-saving business features 💼

  * **The most powerful banking API is now also the easiest.** Enjoy maximum automation with minimal lines of code. Read more…

  * **Add all your trade names to your account without wasting time.** Get total control of the trade name your client sees so you can build trust, and get paid even faster. The moment your trade name is registered, you can add it to your bunq account and use it right away. [Read more…](<./trade-names-for-your-business-bank-accounts>)

  * **Accept credit card payments and never set up a PSP again.** Your personal payment link goes international! bunq.me now supports credit card payments too. [Read more…](<./credit-card-payments-for-your-bunqme-link>)

  * **Automate your finances and start saving time with Zapier integrations.** Connect bunq with the apps you use for running your business and automate all your daily tasks. All without hiring a developer or learning how to code. [Read more…](<./zapier-integrations-automate-your-finances-and-start-saving-time>)

## More features to make life easy ⭐️

  * **Easily distinguish all your Apple Pay & Google Pay cards with custom virtual designs.** Using multiple cards for Apple Pay or Google Pay? Pick a different design for each card, so you always spot the right one instantly. [Read more…](<./easily-distinguish-all-your-apple-pay-google-pay-cards-with-custom-designs>)

  * **Sort your payment automatically without having to think about it.** We’ll automatically send your incoming payment to the right Bank Account, so you can enjoy easy budgeting without even having to to think about it. [Read more…](<./how-to-sort-payments-automatically-with-payment-sorter>)

  * **Conveniently bunq now, right from your Apple Watch.** You can enjoy the most amazing bunq features, all from the comfort of your Apple Watch. Swap cards between Bank Accounts, view Bank Account balances, accept payment requests, and pay online (by viewing your CVC and accepting 3D Secure payments). All without your phone. [Read more…](<./bunq-for-your-apple-watch>)

## Contribute to a greener world with this new bunq Green Card feature 🌳

  * **Green Card purchase Purchase Protection and Extended Warranty.** Enjoy carefree shopping! Get Extended Warranty and Purchase Protection on all your Green Card purchases. Read more....
