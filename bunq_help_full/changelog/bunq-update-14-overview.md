# bunq Update 14 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-14-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

**Hey bunqers 🌈,  
  
** This time, bunq Update 14 came straight from bunq’s Amsterdam HQ—live from the legendary bunq Weekly. For the first time ever, fans got an inside look at how the team celebrates wins, shares ideas, and launches new milestones together.  
  
After months of hard work, bunq unveiled a **total redesign of the app** —rebuilt from the ground up to make life even easier. The new version brings simplicity, beauty, and powerful functionality together like never before.

  * **Instant Sign-Up** : Opening a bunq account now takes just seconds. Download the app, tap once, and you’re in—no excuses for your friends anymore!

  * **New Visual Design** : A sleek, colorful interface featuring three interactive dials showing how much time, money, and CO₂ you’ve saved.

  * **All-in-One “+” Button** : Every action, from creating an account to opening a savings goal, happens from one intuitive place.

bunq Update 14 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Instant Cards** : Order a card in seconds, add it to Apple Pay or Google Pay immediately, and start spending right away.

  * **Group Expenses** : Create Slice Groups to track shared spending automatically—even with non-bunq users, who can now join in just 20 seconds.

  * **Personalization** : Customize your profile, add-ons, and account connections easily—including tools like **bunq Desktop** , **Auto VAT** , and **Moneybird** integration.

  * **Freedom of Choice** : Decide what bunq invests your money in—green, ethical, or general funds.

  * **Tree Tracker** : See how many trees you’ve planted with bunq and how your collective impact grows.

  * **Tribes** : Create or join a **Tribe** with friends to plant trees together and compete for top ranks—turning climate action into a fun challenge.
