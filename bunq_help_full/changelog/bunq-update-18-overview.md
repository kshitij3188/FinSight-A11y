# bunq Update 18 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-18-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

Hey bunqers! 🌈

bunq Update 18 brings you more awesome features that make life easy

Let’s take a look at everything that’s new!

  * [**Local IBANs 🌍**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>)**:** The perfect fit for your international lifestyle. Easily add and use IBANs from multiple European countries - all in your bunq app.

  * [**Spanish IBANs 🇪🇸**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45249>)**:** ¡Hola España! Fully integrate bunq with your local life in Spain, thanks to Spanish IBANs. Available for all bunqers as of 9th of September 2021.

  * [**Search 🔎**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45245>)**:** Get to any feature, setting or payment in no time, simply by starting a search from anywhere in your bunq app.

  * [**Auto Accept ✅**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45244>)**:** You never have to manually approve payments to your favorite stores or to your best friends. Simply enable Auto Accept and automatically approve all future payments.

  * [**Local Currencies BETA 💰**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45257>)**:** You have the unique opportunity to be one of the first 100 users to test our new feature: Local Currencies! This is all about receiving, converting, holding and spending in multiple currencies without having to leave your bunq app. 💸

  * [**Profile 👤**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45242>)**:** Your personal information in 1 place. Easily adjust your Settings and see the Trophies you've earned.

  * [**Spending Insights 🧮**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45250>)**:** bunq categorizes all your payments automatically. By knowing where your money goes, you know where to cut back and save without any effort.

  * [**Let me show you how that works 💪**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45247>)**:** Not sure how to do something in the bunq app? Simply reach out to support and we'll mirror your in-app experience, showing you exactly how to find what you need. As if we were standing next to you.

  * [**Subscriptions 💸**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45243>)**:** The easiest way to organize your subscriptions. Set reminders so you always know when a subscription is about to expire, ahead of time.

  * [**Debit reminders 📱**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45254>)**:** You can choose to automatically accept a direct debit that's about to expire. Get an automatic reminder a day before your direct debit expires.

  * [**Top up Notifications 💡**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45256>)**:** Never worry about running out of money. We'll remind you to top up whenever you reach the limit you set.

Stay tuned for more exciting updates and features in the future!
