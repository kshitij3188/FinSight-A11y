# bunq Update 19 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-19-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 19 brings you more awesome features that make life easy. Let’s take a quick look at what’s new:

\- Easy Investments 💸: Put your money to work with Easy Investments and earn more effortlessly.

\- [New Local Currencies 🪙](<./foreign-currency-account>): Receive, hold, convert, and spend in 15 different currencies directly from the app. 

\- AutoCurrency 🚀: Travelling? Keep money in the currencies you'll use and automatically pay in the local tender wherever you go.

\- [Friends 3.0 👯‍♀️](<./travel-tab>): Spend quality time with friends exploring new places and split the bill in seconds. - [bunq Virtual Credit Cards 💳](<./what-are-virtual-cards-and-how-do-i-create-one>): Protect your physical card’s details by using a bunq Virtual Credit Card for online payments. Have your Virtual Credit Card’s details automatically refresh after every purchase, or simply delete it and create a new one with just a tap in the app. 

\- Update to: Joint accounts ✨: Easily keep track of who paid what. Clearly see who made or received which payment and when from your Joint account.

\- Improved Search 🔎: Quickly find specific payments, features, settings and much more with the new and updated search bar.

\- Update to: Payment Requests 💪: Save even more time resolving your payment requests. Accept or deny multiple payment requests all at once in a matter of seconds!
