**Hey bunqers 🌈,**  
  
Just two months after its first launch, bunq took the next bold step in its journey to make banking better for everyone. What started as an invite-only beta, limited to a small circle of early adopters, quickly turned into a community-driven movement. Enthusiastic users submitted **over 280 feature requests** and gave bunq an astonishing **98.6% approval rating** , proving one thing loud and clear: people were ready for a new kind of bank.

bunq Update 2 brought new features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Connect :** Link your account to another user and let them pay directly from it. Perfect for partners, colleagues, or friends — all with full control over budget and duration.

  * **Chat : Money Meets Conversation**  
Send messages or emojis right after payments and requests for a more personal, friendly experience.

  * **Community-Driven Development:** 284 feature requests—every improvement shaped by real users.

**Public Beta Launch**  
bunq becomes available to everyone, free to download, and ready to explore in under five minutes.
