# bunq Update 21 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-21-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 21 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

### Introducing bunq V4 : Sleeker, simpler, smarter

V4 brings a redesigned, more intuitive experience built around freedom and personalization.

  * **New Add Menu:** Add **money, cards, sub-accounts** , partners, employees, or businesses—all in one tap.

  * **Custom Widgets:** Add widgets for **Total Balance** , **Insights** , or **Trees Planted** , resize them, and make your dashboard truly yours.

  * **Wheel of Fortune:** Win something new every day—cash prizes, trees, 10× interest boosts, or even a **Metal Card** you can gift to a friend. 

### New Activity Tab : Shared experiences made easy

A whole new way to connect with friends through banking.

  * **See what’s happening:** Check out where your friends are spending and what they recommend.

  * **Friend map:** Discover local favorites and avoid tourist traps with **friend-approved spots**.

  * **Swipe to recommend:** Swipe right to recommend, left to skip—and **bunq plants a tree** for your first rec and every 10 after.

  * **Group Expense Tracking:** Track, split, and settle group expenses automatically. Turn on **auto-add card payments** , and bunq handles the math—you just close the group to send out payback links.

### New Green Tab : Multiply your impact

All your green progress now lives in one powerful tab.

  * **Track your CO₂-free journey:** A progress bar shows how close you are to becoming fully carbon neutral.

  * **Gift tree-planting:** Plant trees for friends, even if they’re not on Easy Green—included in your plan.

  * **Freedom of Choice:** Decide how bunq invests your money. Choose fully green or your own mix, and even turn off interest if your beliefs call for it.

  * **New ways to plant:** Earn trees by inviting friends or recommending places.

  * **Tribes:** Compete with friends to see who plants the most—bunq users have already planted **8.5 million trees** worldwide.

### AI that saves you time

  * **Invoice Scanning 2.0:** Upgraded AI scans invoices **60% faster** and automatically detects amount, IBAN, and name—just snap and pay.

### AutoSelect : Effortless budgeting

  * Create sub-accounts like **Groceries (€250)** or **Drinks (€100)**.

  * Turn on **Auto Select** for your card, and bunq automatically routes each card payment to the right account—instantly, accurately, and intelligently.
