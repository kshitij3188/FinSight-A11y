# bunq Update 24 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-24-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Discover all the exciting, new ways bunq makes life easy with bunq Update 24. Let’s take a quick look at what’s new:

  * [Update to: Green Team 🌳:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53931>) Еarn even more Cashback by inviting up to 2 of your friends to join your Green Team. Regardless of their current plan, they’ll plant trees and earn Cashback. When they plant a tree, you’ll plant a tree too. And as they use their bunq Credit Card, they’ll earn up to 1% Cashback. When they earn Cashback, you earn Cashback too. The more the merrier!

  * [Free Credit Card:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58286-free-credit-cards>) We added a Free Credit Card to your bunq app that you can use instantly with Apple Pay or Google Pay. So whenever you want you’re always ready to do so! We will not charge you any fees or interest. And yes, if you join a friends Green Team, your Free Credit Card will earn you Cashback too!

  * [Finn 🤖:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58470>) Finn (Beta) uses the power of AI to give you instant insights on your spending, budgets, places you visited, and much more. Simply open the bunq app and ask Finn anything.

  * [Budgeting tab:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58288>) We made budgeting so easy that you'll actually stick to it. Introducing category breakdowns, monthly comparisons and smart automations to help you save more.

  * [Update to: CO2 Footprint:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57059>) Know which categories contribute most to your carbon footprint and see how your impact differs for each month, making it easier than ever to spend sustainably.

  * [Tap to Pay on iPhone 📱:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58287>) Use your phone to accept contactless payments, so you can set up shop anywhere in the world! With the bunq app and Tap to Pay on iPhone, you’ll no longer need a pin terminal.

  * [Payment correction:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58473>) Accidentally paid from the wrong Bank Account? A simple tap is enough to move money between Bank Accounts and get your money admin up to date.

  * [Update to: Activity tab 👯:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/47189>) Head out and explore the places around you that your friends have visited and recommended. Return the favor by leaving your recommendations on their maps too.

  * [Update to: bunq Payday:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>) You can now choose what Bank Account you’ll receive your bunq Payday on.

  * [Update to: Group Expenses:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/41417>) Spend time with friends without having to worry about the bill. Group Expense tracks and splits expenses with whoever you've invited to the group. Consider holidays, nights out, birthday presents, sorted!

  * [Update to: AutoVAT:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/17400>) Save valuable time by having the bunq app automatically calculate and set aside the VAT on all your incoming and outgoing payments.

  * [Update to: Local Currencies:](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45414>) Save and spend in 22 different currencies to save money whenever you travel or shop online.

For these and other improvements, update your [app](<https://app.adjust.com/dqvbt6?campaign=homepage&adgroup=discoverbunq&engagement_type=fallback_click&fallback=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com%2Fget-it%2F&redirect_macos=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com/signup>) today! 🏃‍♂️

Stay tuned for more exciting updates and features in the future!
