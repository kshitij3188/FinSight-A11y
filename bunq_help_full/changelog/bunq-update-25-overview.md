# bunq Update 25 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-25-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Discover all the exciting, new ways bunq makes life easy with bunq Update 25. Let’s take a quick look at what’s new:

  * [Travel Insurance ✈️](<https://together.bunq.com/d/59607>) \- No more triple-checking your travel insurance for trips abroad. Wherever you go, you’re automatically covered. Including rental car insurance. Set it up right from your Travel tab.

  * [Travel Assistant 🧳](<https://together.bunq.com/d/59628>) \- Your Travel tab now includes practical tips for all your trips. Wherever you’re headed, you’ll know the currency exchange rate, local tipping culture or if you need to go to an ATM.

  * [Planting trees](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>) – Offset your CO2 footprint with zero effort. As a bunq user, you now plant trees when paying with any of your bunq cards, regardless of your plan. You spend, forests grow, everyone's a winner.

  * [Add External Bank Accounts](<https://together.bunq.com/d/59056>) \- Add bank accounts from your external bank to your bunq app to get a full overview of all your finances.

  * [Update to: Budgeting tab 🧮](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58288>) \- Spending Insights now work with external bank accounts too, so your categories and insights graphs will update when you add your external bank accounts to your bunq app. Also, you can customize the start dates of the month, so your insights match your personal situation, and order the graphs inside your Budgeting tab just as you like.

  * Update to: Wheel of Fortune 🎡 - Your spin speed influences the outcome, transforming it into a game of skill, and a daily leaderboard will be displayed at 19:00 UTC to showcase your winnings.

  * [Update to: Finn 🤖](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58470>) \- Your personal assistant got a boost! Evolving from 0.1 to 0.2, Finn (Beta) is now twice as fast, answers more accurately and can respond to your follow up questions in a conversation. You can ask Finn anything.

  * [New Tricount app 👯‍♀️](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58911>) \- The Tricount app got a simpler interface and includes a Free Credit Card to automatically add expenses to tricounts. Forget manual work: sharing expenses with housemates, friends or loved ones is easier than ever.

  * [Update to: plans' names](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>) \- We’ve changed the plan names for Easy Money to Easy Bank Pro and Easy Green to Easy Bank Pro XL.

  * [Easy Card plan 💳](<https://together.bunq.com/d/59630>) \- Get an international credit card in just 5 minutes.

For these and other improvements, update your [app](<https://app.adjust.com/dqvbt6?campaign=homepage&adgroup=discoverbunq&engagement_type=fallback_click&fallback=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com%2Fget-it%2F&redirect_macos=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com/signup>) today! 🏃‍♂️

Stay tuned for more exciting updates and features in the future!
