# bunq Update 26 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-26-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Discover all the exciting, new ways bunq makes life easy with bunq Update 26. Let’s take a quick look at what’s new:

  * [Stocks 📈](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60446>) \- Invest on the go with the bunq app. Start with just €10 and enjoy zero fees for the first 3 months.

  * [Metal Card ✨](<https://together.bunq.com/d/23424>) \- 22 grams of heavy stainless steel, engraved with your preferred name, so you can pay in style wherever you go.

  * [eSIM 📱](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60412>) \- Save up to 90% on data costs when traveling thanks to your eSIM, now available through the bunq app. Install it once, then have reliable internet access all over the world.

  * [Card Customization 💳](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5826>) \- Got cards for yourself, your partner(s) or your business? You can now customize the appearance of the cards in your digital wallet, so you always pick the right one in a heartbeat.

  * [Personalized budgeting insights 🧮](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60394>) \- Finn now gives you fun and surprising insights on your daily, weekly, and monthly spending.

  * [Looking for a good place? Finn can help 🍽️](<https://together.bunq.com/d/47189>) \- Wherever you are, your personal assistant will always have the perfect suggestion for you, based on your previous recommendations.

  * [Tap to Pay on Android 🤳](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58287>) \- Accept card payments with your phone. Now available on Android for all bunq Business users.

  * [Let Finn take care of paying invoices 🧾](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5135>) \- Pay invoices in seconds and automatically attach them to your payment. Simply upload your invoice to the bunq app, and let Finn do the rest.

  * [Tricount: Photobook 🎞️](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60473>) \- Whether it's a snapshot of a beautiful sunset, a group selfie, or a picture of the amazing meal you all shared, you can now add a visual touch to your tricounts.

  * [Tricount: Request links 📤](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60470>) \- You can now easily receive money back and make repayments, all through a simple request link made in the Tricount app.

  * [Tricount: link your bunq account 🌈 ](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60458>) \- Link your bunq account with Tricount to ensure that any expenses made using your bunq card are automatically added to your tricounts.

For these and other improvements, update your [app](<https://app.adjust.com/dqvbt6?campaign=homepage&adgroup=discoverbunq&engagement_type=fallback_click&fallback=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com%2Fget-it%2F&redirect_macos=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com/signup>) today! 🏃‍♂️

Stay tuned for more exciting updates and features in the future!
