Hey bunqers 🌈,

Discover all the exciting, new ways bunq makes life easy with bunq Update 28. Let’s take a quick look at what’s new:

  * [Crypto 🪙](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61673>) \- Get started with crypto safely, right from your bunq app. Choose from a wide range of cryptocurrencies—buying and selling takes just a tap.

  * [bunq Deals for Personal accounts✨](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61684>) \- Get discounts from your favorite brands and services—simply by using bunq. Enjoy deals tailored to you, from gadgets to travel bookings.

  * [Personalized cards 💳](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61630>) \- Make your bunq physical card uniquely yours! Add your favorite one-liner, choose a fun sticker from our gallery, or draw your own from scratch. Go wild!

  * [Child Savings 💸](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61680>) \- The bunq app helps your child build smart saving habits to reach their goal, whether they're saving for a Lego set or a pair of sneakers. All the while, you stay in control with smart settings for what they can see and do with their account.

  * [Pocket money 💵](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61635>) \- Set up your child’s pocket money once and never worry about it again. No more forgetting and no cash needed. Not sure how much to give? No problem! We’ll suggest an amount that’s typical for your child’s age.

  * [Finn Tips 💡](<https://together.bunq.com/d/58470>) \- Your personal assistant just leveled up! On top of answering your questions, Finn now helps you discover powerful features in your bunq app, with quick tips.

  * [eSIM integration 📱](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60412>) \- Install an eSIM directly from the bunq app and stay connected worldwide. Now you can top up with data, even without a connection. Plus, get 8GB of free worldwide data every year with bunq Elite, or choose a data package that suits your needs.

  * [Instant notifications 🔔](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5826>) \- Whether you pay with your physical card or your phone, you’ll instantly get a notification with everything you need to know. Paid in a foreign currency? We’ll show you the amount in euros, and how much Cashback and bunq Points you earned.

  * [1% Cashback on personal expenses 🔁](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61684>) \- Link your Business or Employee Card to your Personal Bank Account and start earning 1% Cashback on every purchase. Whether you're buying supplies, grabbing lunch, or booking travel, every payment earns you money back.

  * [bunq Deals for Business accounts 🤝](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61763>) \- Get discounts and special offers from your favorite businesses and services—simply by using bunq. Whether you're booking your next business trip or optimizing business operations, bunq helps you get more for your money.

  * [Let your team manage receipts 🧾](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60958>) \- No more chasing employees to add their receipts! Team members can add their own receipts to the app, and bunq automatically links them to the payments they made with their employee cards. Stay organized, without having to do any extra admin work.

  * [Employee Debit Cards 💼](<https://together.bunq.com/d/60958>) \- Give your team the freedom to pay anywhere, even where credit cards aren’t accepted. With both Debit and Credit Cards, you’re covered everywhere. Stay in control with real-time tracking, custom limits, and effortless management.

  * [WooCommerce x bunq integration 🧩](<https://together.bunq.com/d/61685>) \- No more waiting weeks for your money. Connect bunq to your WooCommerce checkout and get paid directly to your account. Enjoy competitive rates and faster access to your money.

  * [Bookkeeping tools 🔗](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2282>) \- Sync transactions automatically, track payments in real time, and keep your records accurate, all without manual input. With 50+ accounting integrations available across Europe, you’ll get options tailored to you.

  * [Build fintech apps with our API 💻](<https://together.bunq.com/d/26298>) \- Easily connect, merge, and streamline your setup with bunq’s API. With upgraded SDKs, crystal-clear documentation, and a brand new Developer Portal, it's never been easier to integrate, innovate, and scale.
