Hey bunqers 🌈,

Discover the **easiest way to bank** with bunq Update 29. Let’s take a look at what’s new below: 

  * [**V5**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/62331>)**📱** \- The new bunq app design is here! We improved the entire app based on your feedback – everything’s right where you’d expect it, with a clean overview of your accounts and a sleek new look that makes managing your money a breeze.

  * [**Getting started with bunq**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/open-a-bank-account-with-bunq-in-5-minutes>)**✅** \- Gets even easier. We’ve simplified our sign up process and all new users can explore the app before creating an account.

  * [**Invite your friends**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-invite-your-friends-to-join-bunq>)**👯** \- To join bunq and you both can enjoy exclusive rewards like a free Metal Card, bonus interest or €100.

  * [**Win your Groceries back**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/62321>)**🍏** \- Every time you pay with your bunq card at the supermarket, you have a chance to get your grocery bill back or earn extra bunq Points.

  * [**New bunq Points Rewards**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-points>)**✨** \- We’ve added 5 fresh rewards to bunq Points. You can now get a €15 discount at Coolblue or €10 discount from either TicketSwap, Thuisbezorgd, HEMA or Decathlon, simply by using your bunq card.

For these and other improvements, simply update your app to the latest version! 🏃‍♂️
