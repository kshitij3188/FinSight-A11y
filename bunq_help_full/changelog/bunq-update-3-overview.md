**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 3 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Business accounts in 5 minutes** : Open a business account on your phone with **transparent, pay-for-what-you-use pricing**.

  * **Smarter direct debits** : Get a push **before** money is taken, choose which account pays, and approve within **five days** for extra breathing room.

  * **bunq.me** : Send a link, get paid instantly via iDEAL or bunq—no cash, no IBAN hassle.
