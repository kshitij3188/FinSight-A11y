# bunq Update 30 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-30-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Introducing **bunq Update 30** — making banking simpler than ever. Here’s what’s new: 

  * [**Term Deposits**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/lock-in-a-higher-interest-rate-with-term-deposits>)**:** Set your money aside and enjoy a guaranteed rate for the term you choose.

  * [**Interest on foreign currencies**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/multi-currency-savings-accounts>): Grow your savings in foreign currencies like South African rand, Australian dollar, Turkish lira, and more — earning you up to 27% interest, paid weekly.

  * [**Net Wealth**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/net-wealth>): Watch your money grow without effort. Build your budget, automate your saving and investing, and keep track of your net wealth in one place.

  * [**New currencies for fast international transfers**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-send-one-time-fx-payments>): You can now easily send and receive money in 8 more currencies like the Polish zloty, Czech koruna, Hong Kong dollars. 

  * [**AutoSave is now Auto Round Up**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-enable-autosave-for-my-savings-account>): and it’s more powerful than ever. Automatically grow your savings, invest in Stocks or ETFs, or even set your spare change aside into your favorite crypto.

  * [**Crypto Price Alerts**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/never-miss-your-crypto-move>)**:** Get instant alerts for your favorite crypto coins. You’ll know right away when prices move more than 5%, so you never miss an opportunity to buy or sell crypto.

  * [**Shared Access upgrade**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-shared-access-and-how-to-use-it>): Want to share access to your Bank Account? We’ve made it even easier to do it. Share access to your Bank Account with your partner, roommates or friends. You keep full ownership and they get secure access — the simplest way to manage money for any project.

  * [**ZeroFX**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/whats-zerofx-and-how-does-it-save-me-money>)**:** We've improved the ZeroFX screen. You can now see the map of countries you’ve been to and check out our handy Travel Tips. 

  * [**Company Cards**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-accounts-employee-access>)**:** It’s now easier than ever to keep an organized overview of your company spending. Your Company Cards are now conveniently located in your Business’ Cards Tab.

  * [**Emoji customization**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-create-and-manage-my-bank-accounts>)**:** Personalize your accounts even more with emojis. Now you have even more ways to make bunq uniquely yours. 

  * [**Say hello to Finn 1.0**](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/meet-finn-your-personal-assistant>): Got a question? Get all the answers you need in seconds. Chat in your language at any hour, wherever you are.

For these and other improvements, simply update your app to the latest version! 🏃‍♂️
