# bunq Update 31 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-31-overview
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We’re excited to share all the new features we’ve worked on for **bunq Update 31** to Make Life Easy, from giving you total control over your spending to growing your wealth without effort. Here’s what’s new:

  * [🧺 Baskets](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/start-investing-on-the-go-with-stocks >): New to investing? We’ve got you covered. Choose a customized Basket of stocks and ETFs, and invest on your schedule with ease. 

  * [💸 An even easier way to get paid back with bunq.me](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>)**:** Share your own secure link, or accept payments instantly with just a QR code. Easily share reminders and let your friends pay you back instantly, from any bank and in any currency.

  * [🛡️ Safety Shield improvements](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/security-measures-safety-shield>)**:** Your security is our priority. We’ve expanded our Safety Shield to automatically pause any unusual payments for 24 hours, across all of your accounts. This gives you the necessary time to review and cancel, if something doesn’t look right. 

  * [📁 New Access Levels](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/access-levels >): Easily share access to your Business Account with your team, based on their roles. Give Directors Full Access, let trusted employees make payments from specific Bank Accounts, or provide your accountant with View-only Access for easy bookkeeping.

  * [🔐 Four-Eye Approval](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/four-eye-approval >)**:** Keep your company’s money secure with an extra layer of protection. Now, Business Account users with Full Access can sign off on payments, add new users, and share account access. 

> All these amazing BU31 features will be available in your bunq app as soon as Apple releases the update. From that moment they'll be available on both iPhone and Android.   
>   
> 💡If you're already a bunq user, make sure to check your inbox — we'll send you an email the moment these features become available.
