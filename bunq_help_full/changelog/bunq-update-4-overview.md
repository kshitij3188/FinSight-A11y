**Hey bunqers 🌈,  
  
** One year after launch, bunq celebrated its birthday with a bold new identity—**the Bank of the Free**. This update marked a defining moment in bunq’s history, transforming from a promising fintech to a full-fledged movement built on ethics, transparency, and user freedom. With an entirely redesigned app and groundbreaking new features, bunq once again proved what happens when innovation meets independence.  
  
bunq Update 4 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Bank of the Free Philosophy** : bunq defines itself as an ethical, transparent, user-first alternative to traditional banking.

  * **Complete App Redesign** : A rebuilt interface that unites your bank, wallet, and cards in one beautiful, easy-to-use experience.

  * **IBAN-Free Payments** : Send or request money directly through phone contacts or email, no IBANs needed.

  * **Instant Card Switching** : Change which account your card draws from with two taps—perfect for travel, groceries, or shared budgets.

  * **Gesture-Based Navigation** : Swipe to scan QR codes, chat with support, or switch between views instantly.

  * **Hand Recognition Security** : The world’s first banking app to verify users via hand recognition, working even in low light.

  * **Slice App Launch** : Manage and split group expenses in seconds, with automatic syncing of card payments and real-time expense tracking.
