**Hey bunqers 🌈,  
  
** As the **Bank of the Free** , bunq doubled down on privacy, transparency, and a world-class mobile experience. Update 5 unveils the “final piece” toward full financial freedom: simple pricing, powerful cards, and tools that help people create great things together.  
  
bunq Update 5 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **bunq Premium launched** : Fixed pricing of **€7.99/month** including **two Worldwide Mastercards** , **one Maestro** , **free cash withdrawals** , and all bunq features.

  * **Together Native redesigned** : A smoother, faster community hub with **Updates, Ideas, Knowledge, and Experiences** to share and learn.

  * **Shared Requests** : Create one link and collect money via WhatsApp, email, or Messenger; perfect for group gifts, crowdfunding, and clubs.

  * **Worldwide Mastercards** : Instant push notifications, **real-time account switching** , **real-time PIN changes** , and an **in-app CVC that refreshes every 5 minutes** for extra security.

  * **Switch Service** : Move from a traditional bank in a few taps; incoming funds and direct debits forward automatically.

  * **Developer SDKs** : Official SDKs for **C#** , **Java** , **Python** , and **PHP** on top of the real-time bunq API to build new banking experiences.

  * **Mobile payments on Android** : Tap & pay demos showcased as part of Premium’s everyday freedom.
