# bunq Update 6 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-6-overview

**Hey bunqers 🌈,  
  
** Built with (and for) the community, Update 6 doubled down on flexibility and doing money together. From using one card with two accounts to instant fundraising pages, this release turns everyday banking into effortless collaboration.  
  
bunq Update 6 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Dual-PIN, dual-account card** : Link a single card to **two different bank accounts** and choose which one pays by entering one of two PINs; **patent filed**.

  * **Wild Cards** : Pick **any mix of Mastercards/Maestro** in your Premium bundle, swap later as life changes, and activate instantly when they arrive.

  * **Joint Accounts** : Open a **formal, legal** joint account from your phone (with **no limit** on participants), and close it just as easily—no paperwork, no branch.

  * **bunq Pack** : Combine **3–4 Premium memberships** (or **3 Premium + 1 Business**) into one plan for **€19.99/month** —perfect for partners, families, and roommates.

  * **bunq.me** : Create a **public payment link/page** in seconds to raise money for anything; get paid **instantly** via bunq, iDEAL, SOFORT and more.
