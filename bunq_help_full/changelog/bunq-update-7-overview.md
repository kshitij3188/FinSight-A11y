**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

This update was born from a simple conversation about design and turned into a bold step forward: a bank experience that feels unlike any other. Inspired by vivid, pop-art energy and rebuilt around what users said they love most, bunq unveiled a refreshed identity, a sleeker app, and infrastructure built to scale—so you can do whatever you want, whenever you want, with whomever you want.

bunq Update 7 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **New look & website** : A design-led refresh that centers one message: _bunq is an experience_. The new website strips away clutter to showcase that feeling in seconds.

  * **App experience upgrades** : A more elegant Home, a **Total Balance** view right where you need it, and **quick actions** in your activity feed to accept requests or split bills instantly.

  * **One-tap 3D Secure** : Forget clunky passwords: authenticate online purchases by scanning a **QR code** in the app and approving with a tap.

  * **Developer boost** : A radically simplified **API** with fresh SDKs for **PHP, Python, C#** , and **Java** makes building bunq-powered apps faster than ever.

  * **New countries** : bunq is now available in Spain and Italy, expanding modern, app-first banking across Europe.
