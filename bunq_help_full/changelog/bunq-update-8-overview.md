**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 8 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **New Together** : A cleaner community hub with real search that actually finds answers, live rankings, and **virtual high-fives** ; select power users now serve as **community moderators**.

  * **Dark Mode** : A gorgeous night theme you can set **always on** or **auto by time of day**.

  * **Faster checkout** : **3D Secure without QR codes** : approve online purchases via instant push in the app—no passwords, no scanning.

  * **Developer-ready OAuth** : Securely connect third-party apps to bunq with standard OAuth; fresh SDKs (PHP, Python, C#, Java) make building on bunq easier.

  * **bunq.me for everyone in Europe** : Claim a **personal link** once, get paid by anyone with an IBAN (no bunq account needed), and receive funds instantly via bunq, iDEAL, SOFORT, and more.
