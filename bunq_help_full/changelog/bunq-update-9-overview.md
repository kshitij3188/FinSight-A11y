**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 9 brought many amazing features that make life easy 🌈

  * **Card limits** : Fewer settings, fewer headaches, way fewer questions. Using your cards just got effortless.

  * **Savings Goals** that make saving tangible (with gentle “don’t break the piggy bank” nudges).

  * **Auto Save** rounds every purchase up to the next euro and tucks the spare change toward your goals, automatically.

  * **Director access** in a tap: Add company directors with full access in seconds—no paper, no branch.

  * **One-tap batch payouts** : open the email, send the file, pay everyone—right from your phone.

  * **Camera-to-payment** : snap an invoice or acceptgiro and we fill in the amount, IBAN, and reference for you.

  * **Travel without FX fees** : bunq ZeroFX removes up to 3% card markups outside the Eurozone—keep your money for the memories.

  * **Global transfers :** Send money beyond the Eurozone via our **TransferWise** partnership, up to **8× cheaper** than traditional banks, with the real exchange rate.
