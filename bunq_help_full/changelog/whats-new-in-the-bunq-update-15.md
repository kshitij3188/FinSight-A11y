# bunq Update 15 Overview 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/whats-new-in-the-bunq-update-15
**Topics:** bunq Updates 🌈

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Update 15 brings you many amazing features that make life easy and help you save time, money, and CO2! 🌈

Let’s take a look at them!

  * **Improved dashboard:** After updating the bunq app, you’ll immediately notice the difference. We’ve made the app experience better based on user feedback and updated some of the most visible elements of how the app looks. The dashboard dials on the top of the screen, just like in your car, always inform you what’s happening.

  * [**Trees for Minors**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38400>)**:** Everyone under 18 automatically plants a tree for every €100 they spend with their bunq cards!

  * [**Budget Progress**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38402>)**:** Set up a monthly budget and the app will always tell you whether you're ahead, right on track, or if you need to cut your spending a little.

  * [**My Subscriptions**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38404>)**:** Save money by seeing all your subscriptions in one glance and instantly know where you could back a little.

  * [**Auto Request**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38405>)**:** Paying for shared subscriptions? Set up a Scheduled Payment Request, so you get paid without having to think about it!

  * [**Circle of Trust**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38406>)**:** Using bunq with your friends? Settle expenses without any hassle: simply add your friends to your Circle of Trust and the bunq app automatically approves their Payment Requests!

  * [**Receipt Scanning**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38407>)**:** Paid for drinks? Simply scan the receipt, share it with your friends and let them pay you back by tapping their share on the receipt. Not yet a bunq user? You can scan your receipts and share them with your friends too!
