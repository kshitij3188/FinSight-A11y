# Dutch IBANs - Bankier als een local 🇳🇱

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/dutch-ibans-bankier-als-een-local

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Our default IBANs are Dutch, but with the launch of International IBANs, residents in Germany, France, and Spain can now enjoy a local IBAN, for a complete local experience. But don’t worry - you can still get a Dutch IBAN if you prefer!

### What’s a Dutch IBAN?

bunq is a European bank based in the Netherlands meaning that your IBANs are located in the Netherlands. You can recognize this when you see the NL in your IBANs. 🇳🇱

Even if you’re not Dutch, your Dutch IBAN is accepted everywhere, by all authorities. Your IBAN is fully legal and usable in every country. If you experience any kind of discrimination regarding your Dutch IBAN make sure to report this case as explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/43461>)

## How can I get a Dutch IBAN?

  1. Go to the **Home** Tab 

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Select International Bank Account

  4. Choose a Dutch IBAN (NL)

  5. Personalize your Bank Account

  6. Done

**Important:** The Foreign IBANs 🌍 feature is available for bunq Pro and bunq Elite users only. You can learn more about this [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>)

## Switch Service

Looking to easily switch over to bunq? You can do so via the Switch Service within the app! Switch Service allows all of your incoming payments and direct debits to be automatically forwarded to your bunq account.

You can see how to activate your Switch Service [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/4706>) 🚀
