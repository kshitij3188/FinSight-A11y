# French IBANs: Hello France!

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/french-ibans-hello-france

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

After our German IBANs got released, we thought it was time to go a little southwest. We’re more than happy to announce that from now you can have your own **French IBAN**! The French IBAN is available for all bunqers residing in France, Guadeloupe, French Guiana, Martinique, Mayotte and Réunion so you can experience the local French “vie en rose”.

### Très bien! How can I get a French IBAN?

  1. Go to **Home** tab 

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Select International Bank Account

  4. Choose a French IBAN 

  5. Personalize your Bank Account

  6. Done!

**If you’re a bunqer-to-be residing in France 🎉**

  1. Go through the verification process explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>)

  2. Go with the verification flow ✅

You can check which identity documents we currently accept [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3355>) 🔎

And the coolest part about this - the French IBANs are available for all the plans we offer! (including bunq Free, Core, Pro, Elite and bunq Pack)

## What exactly is a French IBAN?

bunq is a European bank based in the Netherlands meaning that your IBANs are located in the Netherlands. You can recognize this when you see the NL in your IBANs. If you’re a bunqer-to-be, all your IBANs since the opening of your new bunq account will have a French IBAN - provided you’re a French resident. 🚀

This is our newest milestone that we’re more than happy to share with you! It lets you bank like a local wherever you go or anywhere you are. ⛵️

## But I have Dutch IBANs and now I want a French one, what do I do?

You’ll be able to get your French IBAN even if you had Dutch IBANs before that. Just keep in mind that **if you choose to go for a French IBAN all your new Bank Accounts will receive French IBANs automatically**. And your Dutch IBANs? You’re free to keep and use them as usual! 💃

After you select your choice to stick to French IBANs, you’ll have to go through the verification process once again as shown right [here.](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112-how-do-i-complete-my-identity-verification>)

## What if I'm not a French resident but I still want a French IBAN?

No need to worry - Foreign IBANs are here, and they’re the perfect fit for your international lifestyle. Easily add and use IBANs from multiple European countries - all in your bunq app. bunq Pro and bunq Elite Personal and Business users can start exploring this feature. You can learn more about this [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>) 🌍
