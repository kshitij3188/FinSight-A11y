# Irish IBANs: Dia duit Éire! 🇮🇪

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/irish-ibans-dia-duit-eire

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

We’re happy to announce that from now you can have your own **Irish IBAN**! The Irish IBAN is available for all bunqers residing in Ireland (and not only). Enjoy the full local Irish experience 🇮🇪

### And what exactly is an Irish IBAN?

bunq is a European bank based in the Netherlands, meaning that your IBANs are Dutch. You can recognize this when you see the NL in your IBANs. If you’re a bunqer-to-be, all your IBANs since the opening of your bunq account will have Irish IBANs - provided you’re an Irish resident. 🚀

This is our newest milestone that we’re more than happy to share with you! It lets you bank like a local wherever you go, anywhere you are.

## How can I get an Irish IBAN as an Irish resident?

  1. Go to 🏡**Home** tab 

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Select International Bank Account

  4. Choose an Irish IBAN 

  5. Personalize your Bank Account

  6. Done

**If you’re a bunqer-to-be residing in Ireland 🎉**

  1. Go through the verification process explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>)

  2. Go with the verification flow ✅

You can check which identity documents we currently accept [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3355>) 🔎

And the coolest part about this - the Irish IBANs are available for all the plans we offer! (including bunq Free, Core, Pro, Elite and bunq Pack)

## But I have Dutch IBANs and now I want an Irish one, what do I do?

You’ll be able to get your Irish IBAN even if you had Dutch IBANs before that. Just keep in mind that **if you choose to go for an Irish IBAN, all your new Bank Accounts will receive Irish IBANs automatically**. And your Dutch IBANs? You’re free to keep and use them as usual!

## What if I'm not an Irish resident but I still want an Irish IBAN?

No need to worry - Foreign IBANs are the perfect fit for your international lifestyle. Easily add and use IBANs from multiple European countries - all in your bunq app. bunq Pro and bunq Elite Personal + Business users can start exploring this feature. You can learn more about this [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>) 🌍
