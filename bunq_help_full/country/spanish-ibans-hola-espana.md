# Spanish IBANs: ¡Hola España! 🇪🇸

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/spanish-ibans-hola-espana

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Fully integrate bunq with your local life in Spain, thanks to Spanish IBANs.

### What’s a Spanish IBAN?

bunq is a European bank based in the Netherlands meaning that your IBANs are located in the Netherlands. You can recognize this when you see the NL in your IBANs. If you’re a Spanish bunqer-to-be, all your IBANs since the opening of your new bunq account will have a Spanish IBAN - provided you’re a Spanish resident.🍹

This is our latest IBAN milestone that we’re more than happy to share with you! It lets you bank like a local, from anywhere in Spain. ✨

 _**Note** : We're not yet integrated with the Spanish Tax Authorities. Therefore, you can't receive your pension in your Foreign Spanish Bank Account at the moment._

### How can I get a Spanish IBAN as a Spanish resident?

  1. Go to 🏡**Home** tab 

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Select International Bank Account

  4. Choose a Spanish IBAN 

  5. Personalize your Bank Account

  6. Done

**If you’re a bunqer-to-be residing in Spain 🎉**

  1. Go through the verification process explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112-how-do-i-complete-my-identity-verification>)

  2. Go with the verification flow ✅

You can check which identity documents we currently accept [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3355>) 🔎

And the coolest part about this - the Spanish IBANs are available for all the plans we offer! (including bunq Free, Core, Pro, Elite and Pack)

## But I have Dutch IBANs and now I want a Spanish one, what do I do?

You’ll be able to get your Spanish IBAN even if you had Dutch IBANs before that. Just keep in mind that **if you choose to go for a Spanish IBAN all your new sub-accounts will receive Spanish IBANs automatically**. And your Dutch IBANs? You’re free to keep and use them as usual! 💸

After you confirm your choice to stick to Spanish IBANs, you’ll have to go through the verification process once again as shown right [here.](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112-how-do-i-complete-my-identity-verification>)

If you have a Savings goal or a Joint account with a Dutch IBAN prior your transition to the Spanish branch, the IBAN will remain with its original Dutch branch number. In case you want to have Savings goal or a Joint account with Spanish IBAN, you should open a new account by following the regular process.

## What if I'm not a Spanish resident but I still want a Spanish IBAN?

No need to worry - Foreign IBANs are here, and they’re the perfect fit for your international lifestyle. Easily add and use IBANs from multiple European countries - all in your bunq app. bunq Pro and bunq Elite Personal users can start exploring this feature. You can learn more about this [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45253>) 🌍
