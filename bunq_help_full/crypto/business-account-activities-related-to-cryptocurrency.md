# Business account activities related to Cryptocurrency

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/business-account-activities-related-to-cryptocurrency
**Topics:** Business, Signup

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

To keep your money safe, we make an individual assessment of each company based on our acceptance policy and risk appetite.

## Do we accept crypto-related activities for companies?

All crypto activities are considered high risk for us, however we are willing to accept some types of activities.

We can accept your crypto-related activities for your company if they fall into the following categories:

**Investing in crypto currencies for your own account**

\- Use of crypto currencies for payments

\- (Investment) Advisory services

  
  
  

## For Business users

For security reasons, we aren’t able to accept your business if it’s related to the following cryptocurrency activities:

  * Exchange of scriptural money to crypto currencies and vice versa

  * Exchange of one crypto currency to another

  * Wealth management (investing third party funds)

  * Investment funds (investing third party funds)

  * Exchanges of cash to crypto currencies and vice versa

  * Mining and mining pools

  * Crypto currency issuers (Initial Coin Offerings)

  * Wallet providers

  * Tumblers and Mixers

In order for us to approve your Business account, the activities and corporate structure should comply with bunq’s requirements which you can check [here](<./why-do-i-have-to-provide-business-activity-information>)
