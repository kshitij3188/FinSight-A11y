**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/crypto-faq

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Skip the hassle of new platforms or confusing processes. Buy and sell top cryptocurrencies directly in your bunq app. It’s beginner-friendly, fully integrated, and powered by Kraken for peace of mind.

## What can I do with crypto at bunq?

  * **Buy:** Easily purchase popular cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others;

  * **Track:** Monitor the value and performance of your crypto portfolio in real time, right from your app;

  * **Swap:** Instantly exchange one cryptocurrency for another, such as swapping Bitcoin for Ethereum;

  * **Sell:** You can easily sell your crypto and have the money securely transferred to your Bank Account.

## What coins can I buy?

You’ll find top coins like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and over 350 others available in the Crypto tab.

## Is there any risk when investing in crypto?

Yes, just like with any investment, crypto values can go up or down. That’s why we show clear info before every trade, so you can make informed decisions. bunq doesn’t provide financial advice. All trades are securely handled by our licensed partner, Kraken. More details about the risks involved in investing in cryptocurrencies can be found [here](<./what-risks-are-involved-in-investing-in-crypto>).

## What are the fees?

You pay a small trading fee, always shown in the app before you buy, sell, or swap:

  * bunq Free: 1.99%

  * bunq Core: 0.99%

  * bunq Pro: 0.49%

  * bunq Elite: 0.25%

## Are trades instant?

Yes, your crypto appears right after your order is completed.

## How do I get started?

Getting started with crypto is easy, and you can begin with just €1. Simply set up your personal crypto wallet by following these steps:

  1. Go to the **₿** Crypto tab

  2. Tap on “Get Started”

  3. Complete the verification flow for Kraken

  4. Your wallet is now ready ✅

## Do I need to verify anything?

Yes, a quick identity check is required when opening your crypto wallet. It helps keep your account secure and ready to use.

You won’t need to go through verification again if you already completed it when creating your account.

However, there are some cases where we may need extra information. For example, this can happen if your Identification Document has expired. If anything is missing from your record, we’ll ask you to complete the verification process again.

## Are my cryptoinvestments protected?

Your cryptocurrencies are safeguarded by **Safety Shield** , an automatic feature that temporarily holds any money you receive from selling crypto for 24 hours before it’s released to your Bank Account. This security measure provides you with extra time to detect and report any suspicious activity, ensuring that even if someone gains access to your account, they cannot immediately withdraw your funds.

**Safety Shield** is enabled by default for all users, and you’ll always see a note in your payment details if it’s active during a crypto sale. You can turn off **Safety Shield** anytime in the Crypto tab, but there’s a 24-hour delay before it’s fully disabled for your protection.

You can read more about **Safety Shield** [here](<./extra-protection-for-your-crypto-with-safety-shield>).

## Can I sell my crypto whenever I want?

Yes, you can sell your crypto whenever you want with bunq. However, thanks to **Safety Shield** , any money you receive from a crypto sale will be held securely for 24 hours before it’s released to your Bank Account.

## Which Bank Accounts can I use to buy and sell cryptocurrencies?

Currently, you can only buy and sell cryptocurrencies from your euro Bank Accounts. You won’t be able to make crypto purchases from your Foreign Currency Accounts or Savings Accounts.

## Is there a minimum to start investing?

Yes, you can start investing with as little as €1.

## How is my crypto stored?

Your crypto is stored securely by Kraken, a licensed and regulated partner.

## Can I set price alerts?

Yes, you can create custom price alerts and get notified when a coin reaches your chosen value. It’s easy to set up. Here’s how:

  1. Go to the **₿** Crypto tab

  2. Choose the crypto currency you want to be alerted for

  3. Tap on the 🔔 in the top right corner

  4. Select the price hit you want to be alerted for

  5. Tap on **Create Alert**

## How can I delete or edit a price alert?

You can change or remove your price alerts whenever you like. Here’s how:

  1. Go to the **₿** Crypto tab

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Select 🔔 Alerts

  4. Select the alert you want to edit or delete

  5. Adjust the sum or delete the alert

## Where do I track my crypto?

You can track your crypto in the Crypto tab. There, you’ll see your entire portfolio, including real-time prices, performance over time, and all the coins you own, all in one convenient place.

## How can I share my Crypto trades with friends?

Your trades are always private by default. You can share your Crypto trades with your contacts in the bunq App by enabling trade sharing. Trade sharing is a fun and secure way to learn about investing together with your friends — you can learn more about it [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/share-trades-with-friends>). 

Here’s how you can share your Crypto trading: 

  1. Go to your Stocks or Crypto Tab

  2. Tap on ⚙️ **Settings** in the top right corner 

  3. Toggle on for **See Friends' Trades**

  4. All done!

When you turn on trade sharing: 

  * You’ll share your Stocks and Crypto trades with your contacts

  * You’ll see trades from your friends who have also chosen to share their trade activity. 

You can change your mind whenever you want. To turn trade sharing off, simply:

  1. Go to the **Crypto** or **Stocks** tab.

  2. Tap on ⚙️ **Settings**.

  3. Toggle off for **See Friends' Trades** to switch sharing off. 

## How is crypto taxed?

Crypto taxes depend on the tax laws in your country of residence. In some countries, you may need to report gains, losses, or even just owning crypto as part of your annual tax filing. We recommend checking with your local tax authority or a financial advisor to understand your specific obligations. More details can be found [here](<./do-i-have-to-pay-taxes-on-crypto>).
