# Never miss your crypto move

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/never-miss-your-crypto-move
**Topics:** Crypto

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get instant alerts for your favorite crypto coins with **Crypto alerts**. When prices change, you'll never miss an opportunity to invest.

Here's what you'll be notified about:

  * Your **favorite cryptocurrencies** increase or decrease in price by more than 5% within 24 hours or hit a **new all-time high**

  * Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) hit a **new all-time high** or reach an important milestone

## How do I manage my Crypto alerts?

You control which cryptocurrencies you get notified about by adding or removing them from your favorites list:

  1. Go to the **₿** Crypto tab in your bunq app

  2. Tap on a cryptocurrency

  3. Tap the ⭐ in the top-right corner to add it to your favorites

  4. Tap the star again to remove it from your favorites

## How do I turn off Crypto alerts?

You can turn off these notifications anytime:

  1. Go to the **₿** Crypto tab

  2. Tap ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Select **Favorite Crypto Alerts** (or **Alerts**)

  4. Turn off the notification toggles

And that's it! Whether you're a seasoned crypto investor or just starting your crypto journey, these alerts keep you updated effortlessly.
