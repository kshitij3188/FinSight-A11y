# Attachment Search: instantly find notes & receipts

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/attachment-search-instantly-find-notes-receipts
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Now you can **add notes & receipts to all your payments**, always finding them instantly!

With **Attachment Search** , you never have to waste time looking for old invoices and receipts. You simply search for the content of any attachment in your bunq app and we'll find it for you. Want to know where _invoice amazon.com November_ is? We've got you covered.

### How to use Attachment Search?

To see how to use **Attachment Search** , check out the steps below:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select a bank account

  3. Use the **Search bar** to locate the payment

  4. Select the payment

  5. Check out the attached invoice receipt 🧾

Please note that it may take a few moments for your attachment to sync in the app. After that, you're free to search for it at any time!

## When can I start using Attachment Search?

Once you upload your first attachment, you can use **Attachment Search** and never worry about finding old receipts ever again!

## How long does it take before I can search for my receipt?

It may take a few moments for your attachment to sync in the app. After that, you're free to search for it at any time!

## How can I remove an attached receipt?

  1. Go to the Bank Account on your Home screen

  2. Find the payment with the attached receipt

  3. Open the payment, the receipt should show up as a photo underneath

  4. Remove the receipt

  5. Done 🎉
