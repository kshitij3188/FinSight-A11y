# Auto Accept ✅

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/auto-accept

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

For optimum control, we always make sure that you authorize a payment before it’s done. However, the Auto Accept feature allows each one of you, regardless of your plan, to automate payments to your favorite online stores or to your best friends. You can simply enable Auto Accept and automatically approve all future payments to your selected list 🚀

### How does it work?

  * [How do I auto-accept direct debits?](<https://together.bunq.com/d/49548>)

  * [How do I auto-accept payments from specific contacts?](<https://together.bunq.com/d/49550>)

  * [How do I auto-accept payments from specific merchants?](<https://together.bunq.com/d/49551>)

### Good to Know

  * Transactions to a merchant from the trusted merchant list are not entitled to  
chargeback/refund.

  * Adding/removing merchants from the list needs to be confirmed with the login code.

  * Adding/removing merchants from the list needs to be acknowledged by confirming  
Terms & Conditions of the feature.
