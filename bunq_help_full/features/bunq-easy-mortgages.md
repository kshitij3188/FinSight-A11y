# bunq Easy Mortgages 🏡

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-easy-mortgages

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Buying a home is one of the biggest moments of your life. And we're here to make this easy! As announced back in December 2021 - bunq becomes the first digital bank in Europe to offer mortgages 🎉

We’ve teamed up with Dutch lending platform Tulp so that, as of the May 17, 2021 you’ll be able to get your own bunq Easy Mortgages 🙌

### What do we offer?

  * A competitive option for **first-time buyers** who need quick and easy support to purchase their first home 🏠

  * Mortgages with **sustainability-minded consumers** in mind, to help you make your homes more environmentally-friendly 🌳

## How do I request a mortgage?

Our partner, Tulp Hypotheken, will make sure to set you up and update you on your mortgage approval within 24 hours of receiving the application. The steps are easy:

  1. Go to https://mortgages.bunq.com/

  2. Find a mortgage advisor based on your location via Search

  3. Select your mortgage advisor

  4. Cooperate with them and get your mortgage application approved within 24 hours

Once you agree on an Interest Offer and share all required documents with your mortgage advisor, you’ll be informed within 24 hours if your bunq Easy Mortgage is approved!

If you have further questions on how to get your Easy Mortgage, you can contact supportmortgages@bunq.com 🚀

## Who can get a bunq Easy Mortgage?

If you want to apply for a bunq Easy Mortgages, then it’s good to check if the following requirements provided by Tulp are applicable for you:

  * You should be a **tax resident in the Netherlands** (meaning that you reside and pay taxes in the Netherlands)

  * The property you plan to buy also should be in the **Netherlands**

  * The **minimum joint income** to be eligible for a mortgage is **€15,000.00 per year**

Keep in mind that the mortgage you request should be for at least **€70,000.00** and **maximum of €1,000,000.00**
