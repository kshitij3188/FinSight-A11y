# bunq Tips - Control your money right from your desktop with bunq Web!

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-tips-control-your-money-right-from-your-desktop-with-bunq-web
**Topics:** Tips

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Controlling your finances from your Desktop has never been easier!

### What can I do in bunq Web?

  * You can see your bank accounts, events and cards 💳

  * You can also [send payments and requests](<https://together.bunq.com/d/115>) as well as accept and decline requests 🙋‍♀️🙅🏽‍♂️

  * [Temporarily block and unblock](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3509>) your colorful cards

  * Accept and decline [Direct Debits](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1972>) 🧑🏼‍💻

  * And you'll be able to see events for card payments, [scheduled](<https://together.bunq.com/d/84>) and [Auto Save](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5148>) payments, [MassInterest](<https://together.bunq.com/d/8298>) and invoice 🕵🏾‍♂️

Of course your safety is our number 1 priority, both in the app and in bunq Web! That's why we implemented 2-factor authentication: scanning a QR code with your phone or sending a login link to your email address.

bunq Web is ideal for when you truly want to take a closer look at all your transactions. Perhaps you dropped your phone and the screen is shattered….it happens to the best of us. Or maybe you've a batch of payments you need to make and you want to save some time.

There are two ways you can log in to bunq Web:

  * Enter your phone login credentials and security code, or 

  * **Scan a QR code** from the bunq app on your phone

## Who can use bunq Web?

bunq Web is available for all bunq users.
