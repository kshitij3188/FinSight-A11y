**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Enjoy the ease of the bunq app on any screen! We’ve improved bunq Web and now you can manage your account from your desktop easier than ever 🚀

### How can I use bunq Web?

Simply visit [https://web.bunq.com](<https://web.bunq.com/>) or click on **Log In** at the top of any page in [https://www.bunq.com](<https://www.bunq.com/>) to enjoy the brand new design we made to fit your needs perfectly. You can log in by entering your email or phone login credentials and security code or by scanning the QR code with your bunq app.

## What can I do with bunq Web?

  * Have a **clear overview** where you can see your Personal and Business accounts separately and operate all of your Bank Accounts at one glance 💪

  * From your latest transaction to all your payments from the beginning of time, use **improved search** to find any payment in seconds.

  * **Create new Bank Account** by simply clicking on “Add a Bank Account” 💸

  * **Paying bunq.me links** \- You can easily pay bunq.me links directly through bunq Web.

  * **Make payments via iDEAL** \- whenever you need to pay with iDEAL through your web browser, you'll be automatically redirected to bunq Web to verify the payment. "

  * **Manage all your cards** \- create, activate, freeze and block cards in just a few clicks! You can also check your cards’ linked accounts, CVC and change a PIN right away 💳

  * **Send XML SEPA payments** whenever you need

  * **Export statements** for all the payments you need

  * **Attach invoices or notes** to your payments to keep track of every payment

  * You can **upload and pay invoices** to save precious time, directly from your desktop 🙌

  * **Contact Support** by simply clicking on "Chat with support"

  * **Contacts Visibility** \- you can use the contacts from your Address Book (seen in your mobile app) to make faster outgoing payments from bunq Web, without having to manually enter any payment details. To do so simply click on the recipient option when creating an outgoing payment.

  * **Rearrange folders and Bank Accounts ordering** \- personalize your Home tab like you do in the bunq app. You can see how [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48393>).
