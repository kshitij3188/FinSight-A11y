# Control your money on any device with bunq Web

Hey bunqers 🌈

With bunq Web, you can control your money right from your desktop. You wanted it and we did it (even though we swore we never would)!

Can't find a specific feature or setting on bunq Web? To get the most out of all our features and the optimal user experience, we recommend using the bunq mobile app on [Android](<https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bunq.android&hl=en&gl=US>) and [iOS](<https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bunq/id1021178150>)📱

### How do I use bunq Web?

To use bunq Web, you need to have a verified bunq account. You can start your account's verification right away by downloading the bunq mobile app and following the steps outlined [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/46776>).

Once you have set up and verified your account, you can simply go to <https://web.bunq.com/> and log in by entering your email or phone login credentials and security code.

For a more detailed overview of bunq Web and its benefits, check [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48395>).

## How safe is bunq Web? 

**Your security is our number 1 priority!** That's why we implemented 2-factor authentication: scanning a QR code with your phone or sending a login link to your email address or phone number.

## Can I access bunq Web on a mobile device?

Indeed, you can! However, we highly advise to use the mobile app on your phone for a **better experience** 🚀
