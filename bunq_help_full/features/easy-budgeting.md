# Easy Budgeting ✨

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/easy-budgeting
**Topics:** Budgeting

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

With Easy Budgeting, you can budget with zero effort! Set up dedicated Bank Accounts and budget from them automatically.

### How does it work?

**Create a new Easy Budgeting Bank Account**

Start budgeting from scratch by following these easy steps:

  1. Tap on your **Net Wealth** tile

  2. Select**Insights** from the top of your screen

  3. Tap the three dots in the Spending Insights graph 

  4. Tap on "Create Dedicated Bank Account"

  5. Pick your budget’s category/categories

  6. Set an amount, budget type and top-up timeframe

  7. Personalize the Bank Account

  8. You’re done 🙌

**Activate Easy Budgeting for an already existing Bank Account**

You can follow these steps to activate the feature for an already existing account:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select the Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Easy Budgeting 

  5. Turn on the toggle and set your budget!

You can turn the feature on and off whenever you want and as many times as needed.

**Insight’s Easy Budgeting suggestions**

When you check your Insights tab, you’ll see a suggestion to automatically budget for the categories where you spend the most.

## How do I know when I’ve reached my budget?

You’ll know you reached your budget by looking at the bar chart - your budget progress will appear as an orange line.

If you reach your budget - don’t worry, you can top up and refill your budget right away by following these easy steps:

  1. Go to the Home tab

  2. Select the budgeting Bank Account in question

  3. Click on 🟣 + Add Money

  4. Top up your budget in just a few taps

## What budgets can I set?

With Easy Budgeting you set your own limits 🚀

  * You can set a one-off budget: you create it, you top it up and if you reach it - you can renew it. Easy as that!

  * You can set a monthly budget: set an automatic monthly top up that equals your budget limit;

## What’s AutoSelect?

With AutoSelect the bunq app will automatically detect the category of your expenses and make the payment from the correct Easy Budgeting Bank Account.

**For example** \- you pay at the cinema with your bunq card which is connected to a regular Bank Account. However, at the same time you have a budget for Entertainment, which isn’t linked to the card. In such cases, AutoSelect will deduct the money automatically from the corresponding budget, if the balance is sufficient - easy as that 🙌

#### AutoSelect and Foreign Currencies

AutoSelect makes paying in foreign currencies easy and hassle-free. When you make a payment in a foreign currency, AutoSelect will use the matching currency account – if you have it open.

For example, if you have a GBP account and you make a payment in GBP using your main card while visiting London, AutoSelect will use your GBP account for the payment.

If you don't have the specific currency account open, AutoSelect will use a EUR account with a matching label for the payment.

For example, if you're in Switzerland and pay for dinner at a local restaurant with your bunq card but don't have a Swiss Franc (CHF) account, AutoSelect will use your Euro Food account to complete the payment.

#### How can I activate AutoSelect?

You can activate and deactivate AutoSelect by following these easy steps:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you want to use AutoSelect with

  3. Tap on **Pay From**

  4. Select **AutoSelect**

  5. Done ✅

## I no longer need Easy Budgeting for my Bank Account. Can I disable it?

Yes, you can! Simply follow these easy steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select the Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Easy Budgeting 

  5. Turn off the toggle

## How can I create a Custom Payment Category?

Want to get personalized insights on your spending? Our Custom Payment Categories let you fully personalize your spending insights with customizable categories!

You can have up to 9 customized categories, which you can create when opening a new Easy Budgeting account by following these simple steps:

  1. Tap on your **Net Wealth** tile

  2. Select**Insights** from the top of your screen

  3. Tap the three dots in the Spending Insights section

  4. Tap on **Create Dedicated Bank Account**

  5. Tap on **Categories**

  6. Select **\+ Add Custom Category**

  7. Personalize the new category 

  8. Click on "Create"

  9. Done!

Once you create a new customized payment category and manually add payments to it, you'll be able to teach the Spending Insights engine to recognize the category better so it automatically adds relevant payments in the future. This way, the app provides you the overview you want to see - all based on the insights you need.

## How can I teach the bunq app to better recognise payments in my Custom Payment Category?

Easy! By allowing the category to be applied to all similar payments to the one you want to categorize. In order to do so, you can simply follow these steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select a Bank Account

  3. Tap on the payment you want

  4. Tap Category

  5. Select "Category"

  6. Choose the Custom Payment Category you want 

  7. Set the category to match "All similar payments"

  8. Done!

## How do I set up a Joint Easy Budgeting account?

Want to set up a dedicated joint budget for groceries, utilities, nights out or holidays with your friends, family or partners? Simply follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Select a Joint Account 

  3. Click on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Easy Budgeting 

  5. Enable Easy Budgeting 

  6. Set your budget's metrics 

  7. Tap on Save

  8. Done!

Now whenever you or any member of your Joint Easy Budgeting account makes a payment in the category assigned to that account (like groceries, travel, etc), the payment will be automatically deducted from the budget you've set accordingly.
