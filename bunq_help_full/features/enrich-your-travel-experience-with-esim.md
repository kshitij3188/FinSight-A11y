### What’s an eSIM and how do I get one?

An eSIM is an electronic SIM card that lets you activate and use mobile data plans without needing a physical SIM. It’s an easy and flexible way to stay connected anywhere in the world. Here’s how to activate one from the bunq app:

**iOS users**

  1. Go to your 🏡Home tab 

  2. Go to your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap **eSIM** and **Install an eSIM for Free**

  4. Follow the steps to install your eSIM

**Android users**

  1. Go to your 🏡Home tab 

  2. Go to your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap **eSIM** and **Install an eSIM for Free**

  4. Follow the steps to install your eSIM

 _*The eSIM service is provided through our partner BetterRoaming_

## What are the prices and plans?

Prices vary by destination based on local offers. To see what’s available, just check the app for plans and prices. You can choose either country-specific or our worldwide package data plans.

## How do I top up my eSIM?

Once we see you’re low on data, we’ll let you know with an in-app notification. Just tap on it to easily choose from the available plans. If you miss the notification, you can always follow these steps to top up your balance:

  1. Go to your Profile 

  2. Tap the eSIM button to open your eSIM

  3. Tap **Add Data**

  4. Select your destination and plan

  5. Tap **Buy Data** , we’ll connect you automatically when you land

## How can I get free worldwide package data?

If you are a [bunq Elite](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38432>) user, you get 8 GB of free worldwide data every year! No need to buy data packages - just install an eSIM from the bunq app and your 8 GB will activate automatically upon arrival at your destination!

_**Note 1:** Even if you’ve used up all your vouchers with the old eSIM model, all bunq Elite users will still get an extra 8 GB of free data to use this year._

_**Note 2:** You’ll automatically get a fresh 8 GB of free data every calendar year._

_**Note 3:** The worldwide package only includes the countries where our eSIM service is available._

## I’ve installed my eSIM with the old model. What now?

If you’ve previously installed eSIMs using the old model, you’ll need to download a **new eSIM** to enjoy the latest benefits:

  * You can always top up your eSIM in the bunq app, even if you don’t have a connection;

  * Get access to better, more competitive pricing;

  * Manage your data directly in the bunq app.

## How do I deactivate my eSIM?

**iOS users**

  1. Go to your iPhone’s Settings

  2. Tap on “Mobile Service”

  3. Tap on the eSIM you want to delete

  4. Scroll down and tap delete eSIM

**Android users**

  1. Go to your phone’s Settings

  2. Tap on “Connections”

  3. Go to “SIM manager”

  4. Select the eSIM you want to delete

  5. Tap “Remove”
