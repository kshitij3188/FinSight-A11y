# Home Tab 🏡

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/home-tab
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Your 🏡 Home Tab is where you’ll find everything you need to make banking easier than ever.

From our newly designed Home tab, you can make, request and approve payments, add Bank accounts, open a Business Account, access your brand new Budgeting tab and much more!

Read on to see all the features you can use in this tab:

## Net Wealth 🧮

At the top of your Home tab, you can easily track your spending at a glance. Net Wealth provides an overview of your budget, lets you automate your saving and investing, and helps you keep track of your net wealth in one place.

👉 Tap it to dive deeper into your Net Wealth breakdown. Learn more about it [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/net-wealth>).

## Pay 💸

Make fast and immediate payments everywhere you go with the bunq app! Want to know how to make a payment? Check [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/115>)

## Request 📥

Went out for dinner with your friends and covered the bill? No worries, you can request money back in just a few taps. See how to do so [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/47291>)

## Add money 💰

Topping up your bunq account is easy and fast! Make sure you follow the simple steps explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2933>)

## Bank Accounts ✨

From your Home screen, you can add more Bank Accounts to make budgeting easier than ever. With Bank Accounts, you can even manage your money in different currencies and use Dutch, German, French, Spanish and Irish IBANs to bank like a local all over Europe.

You can add an extra bank account by tapping on **Add an Extra Bank Account** right below your existing accounts. You can choose to open a Foreign Currency Account, a Business Account, a Joint account or a Child Account from here. Read more on how you can create and manage your Bank accounts [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/39139>).

You can also see the total balance of your accounts in the top right corner of your bank account widget.  
  
⚠️ Your Savings Accounts can be found in the [Savings Tab ](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/savings-tab>)and are not displayed on your Home Tab. 

#### Bank Account Settings

When you tap on any of your Bank Accounts and go to your Account Settings, you can:

  * Personalize your Bank Account: rename and personalize your Bank Account. 

  * Set up Easy Budgeting: to always pay from the right Bank Account, without having to think about it. 

  * Enable refill notifications: to get notified whenever your Bank Account needs a top up. 

  * Set a Daily Limit: for outgoing Bank Account payments. 

  * Share access with other owners: select who can have access to your account 

  * Edit your aliases: edit your phone number or email address, to be easily discoverable to your contacts. 

  * Schedule Payment Requests: to never have to manually make recurring payment requests again.

  * Schedule Payments: to request your money without having to think about it. 

  * Export Account Statements: from your desired timeframe. 

## Recent Transactions

You can review all of your recent transactions straight from your Home tab. You can tap on **See All** to get direct access to all of your transactions.

Looking for a specific transaction? Just use the Search Icon in the top right corner 🔎

## bunq.me 🔗

bunq.me is the easiest way to get your money back: set up your own personal payment link in seconds, from any bank account – straight from your Home tab. See more about it [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>)

## Layout 🧩

Customize your Home screen by adding and creating folders, so you always have easy access to what's important to you. How? Check out [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48393>)

## Awaiting Events 📯

When our responsible department needs more info to finish your account’s verification, an awaiting event will appear as a banner in your Home tab. Same for Payment events - you can immediately see what you need to respond to and swipe right or left to interact with the event faster. Tap on them to see what next steps are needed from you.
