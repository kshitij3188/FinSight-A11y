## How do I request a mortgage?

Our partner, **Tulp Hypotheken** , will make sure to set you up and update you on your mortgage approval within **24 hours** of receiving the application. The steps are easy:

  1. Go to https://mortgages.bunq.com/

  2. Find a mortgage advisor based on your location via **Search**

  3. Select your mortgage advisor

  4. Cooperate with them and get your mortgage application approved within **24 hours**

Once you agree on an **Interest Offer** and share all required documents with your mortgage advisor, you’ll be informed within **24 hours** if your **bunq Easy Mortgage** is approved!

If you have further questions on how to get your **Easy Mortgage** , you can contact supportmortgages@bunq.com 🚀
