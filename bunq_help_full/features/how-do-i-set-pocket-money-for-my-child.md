# How do I set pocket money for my child?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-set-pocket-money-for-my-child
**Topics:** Budgeting

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Set up your child’s pocket money once and never worry about it again. No more forgetting or last-minute scrambles. Not sure how much to give? No problem! We’ll suggest an amount that’s typical for your child’s age.

### How do I set pocket money for my child?

In order to set up pocket money for your child, you'll need to be on the bunq Core, bunq Pro or bunq Elite plan and set up a Child Account. You can check out the easy steps to change your plan outlined [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3317>) and how to set up a Child Account [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2961>).

Once you set up your child's account, you can:

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on your name at the top of the screen and select your Child’s Account 

  3. From your Child’s Home Screen, tap on your child’s Bank Account

  4. Tap on **Set Up Pocket Money**

  5. Choose how often to top up, how much, and which Bank Account to use

  6. Tap **Confirm**

or

  1. Go to your Child’s 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on your child’s Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select **Pocket Money**

  5. Choose how often to top up, how much, and which Bank Account to use

  6. Tap **Confirm**

## How much pocket money should I set?

When setting up the automated pocket money payments, you’ll receive advice on how much a child at that age typically gets. This helps you set an appropriate allowance for your child.

## How can I see my child’s scheduled pocket money?

To help your child understand and manage their finances better, they can see their scheduled pocket money allowance through the Budgeting tab, following these steps:

  1. Go to your Child’s 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on the Bank Account you scheduled the pocket money from

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on **Scheduled Requests**
