# How do I set up AutoVat?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-set-up-autovat
**Topics:** Automation

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

As a bunq Pro and bunq Elite Business user, we want you to enjoy features that save you time, every time. This will give you the freedom to grow your business the way you want 📈

**With Auto VAT, you'll never have to worry about setting aside VAT for the Tax Man. Instead, your bunq app will automatically do it for you. No sweat!**

## How can I set up my AutoVAT?

See how to set up Auto Vat in the bunq app below:

  1. Tap on your Net Wealth tile 

  2. Select Insights from the top of your screen

  3. Scroll down to the AutoVAT widget

  4. Tap on “Set up AutoVAT”

  5. Choose your AutoVAT sub-account or create a new dedicated one

  6. Choose which are the accounts that you will enable Auto VAT on

  7. Enable the option for “Outgoing AutoVAT” and select the sub-accounts included

  8. Done ✅

## How does this help my business?

Automatically claiming VAT from all your expenses helps you **simplify your taxes** and **save money** for those unexpected moments that happen in life. In short, **saving you time and money, every time** 👍
