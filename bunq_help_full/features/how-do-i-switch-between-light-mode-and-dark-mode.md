# How do I switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-switch-between-light-mode-and-dark-mode

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can use bunq anywhere, at any time. We created the experimental Light mode to make that experience even better!

### How do I enable the Light Mode?

You can switch Light mode on and off in a second. You can do this manually by following the steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Tap on **App Preferences**

  4. Tap on **Light Mode**

  5. Select your preference!

## What are the preferences?

  * **Off (Default)** ➡️ Dark Mode

  * **Always On** ➡️ Light Mode

  * **Auto** ➡️ The mode of your phone will automatically change alongside your phone's mode.

## How can I turn on Dark Mode on bunq Web?

  1. Open [bunq Web](<https://web.bunq.com/>)

  2. Click on ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Click on 🟠 **App preferences**

  4. Select your preferred setting

  5. Click on it

  6. You're done!
