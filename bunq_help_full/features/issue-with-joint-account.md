**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

## Your account is still pending

In most cases, this is caused by the other user still not being verified. What we would advise is to check further if you need to redo your verification process in your "Awaiting Events" in your Profile. If the issue still persists after redoing the verification process, you can check further with our [Support Center](<./how-do-i-contact-support>).

## Your friend hasn't received the invite

If your invitee hasn't received the invite, check [this topic](<./my-joint-account-invitee-never-received-their-invitation>) on how to resolve this issue.
