# Merchant Overview - better look at your frequent purchases ✨

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/merchant-overview-better-look-at-your-frequent-purchases

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

With bunq, you can clearly see how you spend your money during your everyday purchases, making life easy. Now, it’s gonna be even easier. Are you often going to the same store, café, or gym? Want to know how much money you spent there? Now you can get a detailed overview with **Merchant Overview** by simply following the steps below.

### How does it work?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on **See All** under Recent Transactions

  3. Select an event made to the merchant in question

  4. Tap on the merchant's logo on top of the page 

  5. Check the overview 📃

## What can I see with Merchant Overview?

  * bunq invoices

  * Paid requests

  * Card payments

  * Direct debits

  * iDEAL payments

  * Any completed payment.

## Good to know

  * The feature is available for all plans 🎉 

  * You can't download a bank statements with payments from one merchant. For now you can only request [single transaction statements](<https://together.bunq.com/d/11710-export-statement-of-a-single-transaction/8>) for those particular transactions.

  * Refunds are removed from the overview. So if you had three charges from a particular merchant, and one of them is refunded, you'll only see 2 charges.
