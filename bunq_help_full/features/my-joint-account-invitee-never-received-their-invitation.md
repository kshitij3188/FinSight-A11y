# My Joint Account invitee never received their invitation

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/my-joint-account-invitee-never-received-their-invitation

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

In rare cases, you’ll find that someone you invite to a Joint Account doesn’t receive their invitation. Luckily, we’re here to help! Read on to see how you can solve this issue!

### What’s the regular process?

Usually, your invitee received an SMS notification with a link and invitation to create a bunq Personal account.

They should then:

  1. Download an open the bunq app 

  2. Enter and confirm their phone number 

  3. Sign up in 5 minutes by providing their personal information and verifying their identity 

  4. After the successful verification of the invitee’s Personal account, your shared Joint Account is ready to be used.

### What should my invitee do next?

If the invitation wasn’t received, we suggest you to assist your invitee to open a bunq Personal account manually by following the steps mentioned [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/46776>). After the successful verification of the account, you can manually invite them to join the Joint Account by using the telephone number they signed up with.
