# How can I check my Net Wealth?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/net-wealth
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We always listen to your feedback. After we rolled out our new app design for BU29, some of you shared that you’re missing having a total overview of your balances — and we listened. 

You can get a complete overview of your finances with **Net Wealth** — right from your Home tab. Net Wealth displays the total value of your Bank Accounts, Joint Accounts, External Accounts, Stocks Portfolio, and even your Crypto Wallet. It’s the ultimate way to stay in control and watch your wealth grow 🚀

Just look for the **Net Wealth** tile at the top of your Home tab. Tap it, and dive in to get a detailed overview of how your money grows.

## So, how does Net Wealth work?

Your Net Wealth is the sum of all your balances, updated in **real-time** to give you a quick snapshot of your finances from your Home Tab. Net Wealth automatically combines the balances from:

  * **Your Bank Accounts**

  * **Your Joint Accounts**

  * **Your External Accounts (from other banks!)**

  * **Your Stocks Portfolio**

  * **Your Crypto Wallet**

Right below your Net Wealth total, you’ll see either: 

  * **Your personal daily change:** This shows you exactly how much your total net wealth has gone up or down that day, keeping you perfectly in the loop. You can check how the difference is calculated by tapping on the tile and viewing the category breakdown. 

  * **A daily market insight:** We'll show you a little insight from the market to help you discover new opportunities, highlighting one of the day's top-performing stocks or cryptocurrencies. As soon as you invest in your first stock or crypto, your Home Tab will automatically switch to showing your daily personal Net Wealth update only.

Tapping the tile on your Home tab opens your Net Wealth screen, where the magic really happens. 

## View your Net Wealth Progress 

An interactive graph shows you how your wealth changes over time. Toggle between daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly views to track your progress. Your total Net Wealth is always displayed right at the top, clear as day.

## How can I check why my balances changed? 

Notice you have a dip and want to check what you were spending on? 

If you’d like to have a deeper understanding of why your graph x increased or decreased on a specific day or month, you can: 

  1. Tap on the tile 

  2. Go to your Bank Accounts 

  3. Tap on Search Transactions 

  4. Select Custom under When 

  5. Input the dates you’d like to check 

  6. Tap on Search 

Below the graph, you’ll find a simple breakdown of all your bank accounts. 

## Your Budgeting Tools 

We’ve also included quick access to your favorite budgeting tools. You can set up Auto Round Up or organize your income right in your Net Wealth screen, making it easier than ever to save more and budget smarter: 

  * You can set up the **Auto Round Up** feature to automatically round up all payments, and send the difference to your preferred savings account. Learn more about **Auto Round Up** [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-enable-autosave-for-my-savings-account>). 

  * You can also automatically organize your income by sorting your salary into separate budgeting bank accounts, stocks or ETFs of your choice. Learn about the **Organize Your Income** feature [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-sort-payments-automatically-with-payment-sorter>). 

## Does Net Wealth count my Business or Child Accounts?

Got multiple accounts? No sweat. 

We keep your Personal, Business, and Child accounts and their Net Wealth completely separate. Simply use the user picker at the top of your Home screen to switch between them and see the dedicated overview for each. Easy.

## What if I don’t want to see my Net Wealth?

You got it! Easily hide your Net Wealth balance by enabling the **Hide Balance** feature. Keep in mind that by enabling **Hide Balance** , you’ll be hiding the balances of all of your Bank Accounts. To hide your Net Wealth, simply:

  1. Go to your Home Tab

  2. Long-press on any Bank Account

  3. Tap on View Options

  4. Tap on **Hide Balance**

  5. All done - your Net Wealth balance is hidden.

You can check your Bank Account balances and total Net Wealth anytime, even when Hide Balance is enabled. Just tap on your Bank Account to open the account overview — the balance is displayed right on top.

Please note that it isn’t possible to only hide your Net Wealth at the moment. You can find out more about our Hide Balance feature [here](<./how-can-i-hide-my-balance>).
