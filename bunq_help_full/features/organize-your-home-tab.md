# Organize your Home tab 🏡

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/organize-your-home-tab

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can now easily organize your Home Tab exactly the way you like it in your bunq App. Simply move around your Bank Accounts and combine multiple Bank Accounts to make a folder 📂 to have a crystal clear overview of your finances. 

### How can I create a folder?

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home tab

  2. Long-press on a Bank Account of your choice

  3. Tap on **New Folder**

### How can I rename a folder in the App?

  1. Long-press on the folder 

  2. Tap on ⚙️

  3. Tap on **Rename Folder**

  4. Type in the new name 

  5. You’re done!

### How can I remove a tile from my folder?

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on the folder in question

  3. Long-press on the Bank Account you want to remove

  4. Slide it back to your Home tab

### How can I delete a folder?

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Long-press on the folder in question

  3. Tap on **Delete Folder**

## Move Bank Accounts 🕹

Simply long press the Bank Account tile you want to move, slide it and restructure your Bank Account layout in just a second.
