# Together knowledge is moving straight to the bunq website 🌈

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/read-together-topics-straight-from-the-bunq-website

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

If you’re a long-term user, you may be familiar with our old Forum **Together**. We’re saying goodbye to Together to make it easier for you to access all of our knowledge directly from our [website](<../categories/knowledge>)!

For topics related to tricount, visit the [tricount website](<https://help.tricount.com/categories/knowledge>).

## Where can I find the content from Together now?

We moved our knowledge articles from Together to our [main website](<https://bunq.com/>) to ensure you always have easy access to accurate and up-to-date information about our products and features from a trusted source.

## What’s new?

You can find the topics you’re interested in faster with our fresh and easy-to-navigate layout!

## How can I access the Help Center on the bunq website?

Simple! You can access all knowledge on our products and features exactly as you did before by Googling a question you have and following the link from the official bunq website.

Alternatively, you can directly:

  1. Visit [help.bunq.com](<https://bunq.com/>) or [help.tricount.com](<https://help.tricount.com/categories/knowledge>)

  2. Browse through the topics and find the information you need ✨

## What happened to the Forum?

The community discussions and user-generated posts on Together are no longer available. While the Forum is gone, we're excited to keep learning and listening to your feedback in order to build a bank that truly makes life easier.

## What if I see an old together.bunq.com link?

You'll be automatically redirected to [help.bunq.com](<https://bunq.com/>) for all your Support needs and to explore all the knowledge from our official sources.
