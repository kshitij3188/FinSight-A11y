# Travel Insurance:  Worldwide travel insurance with zero hassle ✈️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/travel-insurance-worldwide-travel-insurance-with-zero-hassle
**Topics:** Travel

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Thanks to our partnership with Qover, users on bunq Elite effortlessly benefit from Travel Insurance. There's no need to go through the hassle of securing insurance before your travels or dealing with physical offices. Just pay for your hotel, plane ticket or car rental with your bunq card and get covered for your entire trip!

For full details on Qover’s coverage and conditions, see full [Terms and Conditions](<https://www.bunq.com/nl-nl/documents/travel-insurance-terms-conditions>).

_**Note:** Travel Insurance is only available to residents of 🇳🇱 The Netherlands, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇫🇷 France, 🇪🇸 Spain, 🇮🇪 Ireland, 🇧🇪 Belgium or 🇮🇹 Italy._

### How can I activate my Travel Insurance?

You're automatically covered by the Travel Insurance included in your Personal bunq Elite plan if you make a payment for plane tickers, hotels or car rentals abroad. This means you don't need to manually activate the insurance - just upload a copy of the payment when filing the insurance claim!

By accepting bunq's Terms and Conditions when you upgrade to or start with the Personal bunq Elite plan, you also accept the Terms and Conditions of the Travel Insurance. This makes the process seamless and easy, ensuring you can enjoy your travels with peace of mind, knowing you're covered.

Business bunq Elite users need to manually activate their Travel Insurance by navigating to the Travel Insurance section in the Travel tab and accepting the Terms and Conditions of the Travel Insurance.

## How do I file a claim?

Claims can be submitted in the following ways:

You can access the claim by following the steps below in the bunq app:

  1. Go to 🏡Home tab 

  2. Open your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap on “Travel Insurance”

  4. Select “File Insurance Claim”

  5. Follow the flow

Make sure to submit your claims within 30 days of an incident. We recommend submitting a claim as soon as possible and including any relevant supporting documents as soon as you can.

You can also complete the online claim form on Qover's website [here](<https://bunq.qover.com/claims>). If you're unable to use this form, Qover's services are available from Monday to Friday at +31 20 808 0723, from 09:00 to 17:00.

## How can I see my policy number?

You can see the starting date of your insurance and its policy number by following the steps below:

  1. Go to 🏡Home tab 

  2. Open your Profile in the top left corner 

  3. Tap on “Travel Insurance”

  4. See the information on the screen ✅

## What should I do in an emergency situation?

  * **In the event of a severe illness or accident:** If you suffer a severe illness or accident requiring emergency medical assistance, your first point of contact should be International SOS at +31 20 808 0723. They are equipped to provide a range of medical and travel assistance services.

  * **Treatment as an outpatient:** for minor illnesses and injuries, we advise you to cover the treatment costs upfront. Upon returning from your trip, you can file a claim with Qover to seek reimbursement.

## How do I file a complaint?

Together with Qover, we aim to provide a high-class service at all times. However, if you're dissatisfied with our service, you can file a complaint with Qover. You can do this by filling their form available [here](<https://bunq.qover.com>).

## Can I request a travel certificate?

You can apply for your travel certificate through Qover's portal [here](<https://bunq.qover.com/>).

## Who should I contact for travel insurance related questions?

For any other questions related to your Travel Insurance, you can visit Qover’s FAQ section [here](<https://bunq.qover.com/>). Qover's services are also available through phone from Monday to Friday at +31 20 808 0723, from 09:00 to 17:00 CET.
