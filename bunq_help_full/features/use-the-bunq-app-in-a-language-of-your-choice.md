# Use the bunq app in a language of your choice 📖

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/use-the-bunq-app-in-a-language-of-your-choice

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Thanks to Artificial Intelligence (AI), you can now use bunq in the language of your choice. Enjoy effortless banking in 38 languages.

## How can I use the app in my language?

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Select **App Preferences**

  4. Tap **"Language"**

  5. Tap on the language selector

  6. You’ll be prompted to change the app’s language

  7. Tap **“Continue”**

  8. Change app’s language in phone settings _Important note:_ Keep in mind that bunq's original information is presented in English, the **AI Translation** should be viewed as a helpful reference and provided solely for your convenience.

## How can I report a translation issue?

If you spot a translation that can be improved, simply take a screenshot, and the option to report us the problem will appear. Please provide detailed information about the **expected translation** versus **what you received**.
