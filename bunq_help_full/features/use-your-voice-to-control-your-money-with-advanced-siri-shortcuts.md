# Use your voice to control your money with Advanced Siri shortcuts

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/use-your-voice-to-control-your-money-with-advanced-siri-shortcuts

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

Now you can get advanced control over money, using just your voice with **Advanced Siri shortcuts**!

With **Advanced Siri shortcuts** , you can use your voice to do everything you'd want to do with your banking. This means **total control** and **zero effort**.

## How do I use Advanced Siri shortcuts?

To see how to enable **Advanced Siri Shortcuts** , check out the video below:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Tap on **Preferences**

  4. Tap on **Siri**

  5. You can enable or disable this feature with just a tap 👍

## Who can use Advanced Siri shortcuts?

All **bunq Pro** and **bunq Elite** bunqers with an iOS device can use this feature!

## What bunq shortcuts can I ask Siri?

You can ask Siri to:

  * Check your account balance: Siri, what's the balance of my Groceries account?

  * Send a payment: Siri, pay 5 euros to Charlie.

  * Make a payment request: Siri, ask Charlie for 5 euros.

  * Transfer money between accounts: Siri, transfer 5 euros from my Savings account to my Groceries account.

Check this topic or this topic for more tips on the possible shortcuts.

## How do I use Siri?

New to using Siri? No problem! Check out [this link](<https://www.siriuserguide.com/how-to-use-siri>) to find out more about how it works.

## How will I know if Siri heard me correctly?

Don’t worry if you think Siri misheard you. You will get an on-screen confirmation to double check Siri understood you correctly 👍
