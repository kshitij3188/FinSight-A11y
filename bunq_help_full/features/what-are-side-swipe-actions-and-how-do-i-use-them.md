# What are side swipe actions and how do I use them?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-side-swipe-actions-and-how-do-i-use-them

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We have been working hard on making your app experience even more effortless and we are happy to introduce: side swipes and quick actions.

## What does the side swap do?

With side swipes, it is now possible to **swipe to the left within the event timeline to accept or decline a request**. In addition, we also implemented some **quick actions within the event screen** where you can pay, request or split a payment from a specific event. These two new actions will allow you to maneuver your events much easier and smoother.

## Where can I use the side swap?

  1. Swipe a **payment request in your events to the left to accept or decline**

  2. Swipe **payment event (sent/received) to the left to forward, request, redo, split**
