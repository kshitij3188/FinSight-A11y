# What are the benefits of the Easy Savings plan 💸

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-benefits-of-the-easy-savings-plan
**Topics:** Plans

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You might have noticed that we no longer offer our Easy Savings plan.

If you were an Easy Savings user, you can stay on the Easy Savings Legacy plan or choose to upgrade to any other bunq plans we offer.

### What is the Easy Savings Legacy plan?

The Easy Savings Legacy plan enjoys the same benefits as the [bunq Free plan](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48095>) with the exception of:

  1. Businesses with a legal form other than freelancers can use the Easy Savings Legacy plan

  2. If you are a joint account holder, you can keep using your joint account but you can’t open new joint accounts

  3. If you are a Parent, you can keep your existing Child account but you can’t open new ones

  4. You may retain your physical Credit card, with an annual maintenance fee of €9.99 until the card's expiry

## What are my options if I am currently on the Easy Legacy plan? 

Experience the full benefits of bunq with more options tailored for you! If you have a Joint Account, Child Account, or a Business with a non-freelancer legal form, here's how you can maximize your bunq experience:

  1. **Upgrade to one of our paid plans** : Switch to bunq Core, bunq Pro, or bunq Elite, to unlock limitless banking possibilities.

  2. **Choose bunq Free** : Note that you'll need to close any Joint or Child accounts. This option is not available for non-freelancer businesses.

  3. **Continue enjoying the Easy Savings legacy plan indefinitely** : This is still an option, but you won't be able to add more Child or Joint Accounts without upgrading.

You can also choose bunq Free as your plan by clicking [here](<https://www.bunq.com/tutorials/switch-easysavings>) or following the steps shown [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3317>).

You can see all the plans we currently offer [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).
