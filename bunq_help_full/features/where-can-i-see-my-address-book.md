# Where can I see my Address Book?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/where-can-i-see-my-address-book

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Using bunq, you can access your **Address Book** on your phone! To view your **Address Book** , follow these steps in the app: 

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Select **Address Book**

## How do I add a new contact to the list?

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Select **Address Book**

  4. Tap on 🔵 **Add Contact**

## How do I remove a contact?

To remove a **manually added** contact from your Address Book, simply:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Select **Address Book**

  4. Tap on the contact you'd like to remove 

  5. Select 🟠 **Remove Contact**

💡 Alternatively, you can swipe left and tap on **Delete** to remove your selected contact. 

## Can I access my Address Book from bunq Web?

No, you can’t directly access your **Address Book** from **bunq Web**. However, you can use the contacts from your **Address Book** (seen in your mobile app) to make faster outgoing payments from **bunq Web** , without having to manually enter any payment details. To do so, follow the steps mentioned [here](<./how-can-i-make-a-payment>) and click on the recipients option to see your contacts.
