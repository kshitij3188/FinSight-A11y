# Who can get a bunq Easy Mortgage?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/who-can-get-a-bunq-easy-mortgage

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

If you want to apply for a bunq Easy Mortgages, then it’s good to check if the following requirements provided by Tulp are applicable for you:

  * You should be a tax resident in the Netherlands (meaning that you reside and pay taxes in the Netherlands)

  * The property you plan to buy also should be in the Netherlands

  * The minimum joint income to be eligible for a mortgage is €15,000.00 per year

Keep in mind that the mortgage you request should be for at least €70,000.00 and maximum of €1,000,000.00

## Get your bunq Easy Mortgages 🏡

This is easily done by following the steps [here](<https://mortgages.bunq.com/>).
