**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You’re always in full control over your bunq experience and you can manage your personal information, data, settings and app preferences in Your [Profile](<https://www.bunq.com/tutorials/profile>). 

Check out all the things that you can do from Your Profile here: 

## Your bunq.me link 

Your personal [bunq.me](<./what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>) QR link is displayed right at the top of Your Profile, making it super easy to scan and share. It's a fast, simple, and secure way to get paid back, no IBANs needed!

If you don’t have a [bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) set up yet, you can create your own in just a few taps, right from the top of Your Profile screen. Simply tap on the bunq.me banner, select your personal [bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) name, and tap on **Create**.

## Trees Planted by You 

Check out how you’re helping make the planet greener, simply by paying with your bunq Card by tapping on the **Trees Planted by You** banner. Here, you’ll see the amount of trees you’ve planted while banking with bunq. 

The more trees you plant, the more you offset your CO2 Footprint. Better yet, we keep track of and display how much CO2 all your trees are offsetting, as each of the mangrove trees you plant offsets 12.3 kg CO2 per year.

#### Freedom of Choice

## Archive 🗂️

Check this space if you need access to closed bank account or card detail information. Your archived bank accounts and cards are displayed here.

You can access your old IBANS, check past transactions and export account statements here.

Additionally, you can choose to reopen your account or card by:

  1. Go to your Home tab 

  2. Go to your Profile 

  3. Tap on Archive 

  4. Select Bank Accounts or Cards 

  5. Select the Bank Account or Card 

  6. Tap on Settings 

  7. Tap on Reopen Account and Confirm 

  8. All done

## Add Employee ⚙️

From a Business Account, you can share access to a sub-account with one of your employees in just a few taps. Simply select **Add Employee** to share access to the sub-account of your choice with another bunq user of your choice. They’ll be able to view the sub-account you shared access with, use it to make payments, top it up, and even link their own cards to it. Learn more about this [here. ](<https://together.bunq.com/d/1982>)
