**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Want to learn more about the bunq Free plan? You’re in the right place, let’s dive straight into the FAQs!

## How is bunq Free different from the other plans?

bunq Free has the essential you need to get started with bunq ✨

With bunq Free, you get the flexibility to mix and match up to 3 Bank Accounts, whether it's a Savings Account, Foreign Currency Savings Accounts, Foreign Currency Bank Account, or a regular Bank Account - tailored to your needs. Plus, enjoy a Digital Card (Debit or Credit) that works seamlessly with Apple Pay and Google Pay, giving you effortless control over your finances.

Ready to grow your money? Start investing in Stocks with as little as €10!

## Who can use bunq Free?

The Personal bunq Free plan can be used by anyone who wants to try the essentials to get started with bunq.

The Business bunq Free plan is only available for freelancers. Further information about the eligible legal forms can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50213>).

## Are my funds insured?

Of course! All funds on your bunq Free account are covered up to €100,000.00 by the Deposit Guarantee Scheme of the Dutch Central Bank.

## How many Bank Accounts can I have?

As a bunq Free user, you can enjoy a combination of 3 Bank Accounts of your choice: Savings Account, Multi-currency Savings, Foreign Currency Bank Account, or a regular Bank Account.

## Why was I billed €9.99?

If you have previously used the Easy Savings plan and ordered a physical copy of your Free Credit Card, you will be charged an annual maintenance fee of €9.99 until its expiry.

## What’s my interest?

### Personal Accounts

  * **For 🇪🇺 EUR Savings Accounts**

You can earn an annual bonus MassInterest on your savings all year long when you save more than your personalized starting point, called a "threshold." This threshold is based on the highest amount you saved in your account over the past 6 months. It gets calculated twice a year - on January 1 at 00:00 UTC and July 1 at 00:00 UTC.

This means that your threshold is the highest balance in your Savings account between:  
January 1 at 00:00 UTC to June 30 at 23:59 UTC  
July 1 at 00:00 UTC to December 31 at 23:59 UTC

**Here’s how it works:**

  * You earn an annual base interest rate, currently 1.51%*, on savings up to your threshold.

  * If you save more than your threshold, you’ll get the current annual bonus rate of 2.01%* on the extra amount.

If you didn’t have any savings in your account or if you've just joined bunq and opened a Savings Account during the 6-month period before the threshold calculation, your threshold will start at €0. This means you’ll receive the current annual bonus rate of 2.01% on all savings until the next calculation.

_*The base rate and the bonus rate are variable and are subject to change._

## Do I have a card in my account?

As a bunq Free user, you have one Digital card available to you! Enjoy the convenience of using your Digital Card (Credit or Debit) without needing to transfer funds to a different bank - it's all seamless 🎉

❗️If you'd like to have an additional physical card, check out our plans which offer physical cards: [bunq Core](<https://together.bunq.com/d/44002>), [bunq Pro](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3762>) and [bunq Elite](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38432>).

## Is there a maximum or minimum deposit?

No, the amount of the deposit is up to you!

## Is deposit holding fee applicable?

As of December 1, 2022, bunq will no longer be charging a safekeeping fee at all! Check more [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50296>)

## When and how can I withdraw my funds from my Savings Account?

To encourage you to save the money for your next vacation, we've limited the withdrawals to 2 per calendar month. Here's how you can withdraw your funds:

  1. Go to the Savings Tab

  2. Tap on Withdraw

  3. Select the total amount of your Savings Account

  4. Select a Bank Account to withdraw the money to

  5. Withdraw ✨

Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5147>) 🙌

## How can I switch to bunq Free from a paid plan? 

Simple — it only takes a moment to switch to bunq Free: 

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Select **Plan**

  3. Go to **Switch Plan** and pick bunq Free

💡 To switch seamlessly to your new, bunq Free plan, here's a quick checklist to keep in mind: 

  * Have a maximum of 3 open Bank Accounts. If you have more, just close any extra Bank Accounts before you switch. 

  * Have a positive balance. Top up your account to make sure your balance isn't in the negative. 

## How can I switch my card? 

On bunq Free, you get access to a free Digital Card. If you want to change your current card to a different type (Debit or Credit), it's just two simple steps: 

  1. Head over to your Cards Tab and deactivate your current Digital card 

  2. Once done, simply create a new Digital card of your choice
