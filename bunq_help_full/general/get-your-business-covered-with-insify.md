**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Ready to get your business covered in just a few taps? 

You can now get **business insurance** straight from the bunq app with **bunq Business Deals**. We've teamed up with our friends at [Insify](<https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.insify.nl%2F>) to bring you fast, hassle-free coverage designed for entrepreneurs and business owners like you.

Just a heads-up: all insurance products are managed by **Insify** , not bunq. So, if you have any questions about your policy, claims, or what’s covered, they’re the experts to ask!

## What can I get covered for?

Choose from three simple insurance products to protect your business:

Insurance Type| What it Covers  
---|---  
**Disability Insurance (AOV)**|  Protects up to **85% of your income or fixed monthly costs** if you're unable to work due to an accident, illness, or burnout. It's prevention of facing financial hardship in cases of sudden disabled.  
**Professional Liability (BAV)**|  Got your back if your business is hit with claims for **financial damages** from things like professional slip-ups or bad advice. It's not mandatory, but many clients will ask for it.   
  
  
Example: incorrect advice you gave or an error in a calculation.  
**General Liability (AVB)**|  Your shield against **third-party claims for damages**. Perfect for freelancers, contractors, and shop owners.
