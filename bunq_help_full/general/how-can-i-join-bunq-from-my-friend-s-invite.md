# How can I join bunq from my friend’s invite?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-join-bunq-from-my-friend-s-invite
**Topics:** Account, Signup

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Received an invite to join bunq from your friend? Sign up and get rewards like a free Metal Card, extra Bonus Interest, or €50 in Bitcoin.

Find out how you can accept your friend’s invitation and claim your rewards below.

### How can I sign up for bunq through my friend’s invite?

When your friend invites you to join bunq, you will receive an SMS from bunq. To sign up, simply:

  1. Tap on the link in the message

  2. Download the bunq app from the App Store or Google Store

  3. Open your bunq App

  4. Tap on **Get started**

  5. Enter your phone number

  6. Verify your phone number

  7. Tap on **Sign up in 5 minutes** and follow the verification flow ✨

## How do I claim my reward?

Once you’re verified, you can claim your reward by following the steps below:

  1. Open your bunq app

  2. Go to your Home Tab

  3. Scroll and tap on the widget under **Action Needed**

  4. Follow the on-screen instructions

  5. Return to your Home Tab and tap on **Claim reward** under Action Needed

  6. Tap on **Claim Reward**

  7. You’ll receive your reward
