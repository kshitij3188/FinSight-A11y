# How can I request money through the bunq app?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-request-funds-through-the-app

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Requesting money with bunq is easier than ever! 

Went out for dinner and covered the bill for your friend? No worries, you can request money back in just a few taps. Here’s how: 

### How can I send my request?

It’s easy! Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home Tab

  2. Tap on **🔵 Request**

  3. Enter the amount and currency you’d like to request 

  4. Add a description for the request and **continue**

  5. You’re at the Request screen overview. From here you can: 

     1. 💸 Choose which Bank Account you’d like the money sent to 

     2. 🧾Snap a picture of the receipt, or add it from your photo library 

     3. 👤Choose a recipient 

  6. Your request is ready to go 🚀

### Sending your payment request 

Once your request is ready, it’s time to share it! You have three easy options to pick from, depending on who you’re sharing the request with. 

  1. **Choose from Contacts** : When you select **Choose Contact** , you can choose between sharing your request with: 

     * **Your bunq Contacts** : Easily share your request with your bunq contacts. 

     * **Multiple People** :**** Request money from more than one recipient. This will send bunq.me links to the people you’ve selected right away. 

     * **Your Accounts** : You’ll be able to select from which Bank Account of yours you want to request money. 

  2. **Share a payment link:** Tap on the **Export icon** to generate your own secure [bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link. You’ll be able to send this personal payment link through any messaging app you like: WhatsApp, Messenger, text messages, you name it! Anyone with the link can pay you back instantly. 

  3. **Share a QR:** Tap on the **QR icon**. This is a perfect option when you’re with someone in person. Anyone can scan this QR to pay you back on the spot, right away! 

> This amazing feature is part of [bunq Update 31](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-update-31-overview>) and will be available in your bunq app as soon as Apple releases the update. From that moment the new ways to send your payment requests will be available on both iPhone and Android.   
>   
> 💡If you're already a bunq user, make sure to check your inbox — we'll send you an email the moment these features become available.

## Want to request an open amount?

You can do so through our bunq.me feature! Follow the link[ here](<./what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>) to set up your personalized bunq.me link 🏃‍♀️

## How can I use Request in bunq Web?

Follow these easy and fast steps:

  1. Login to your bunq account through https://web.bunq.com

  2. Click on **Request**

  3. Type in the amount you'd like to request

  4. Choose your currency

  5. Choose on which Bank Account you want to receive the funds

  6. Choose the recipient, just as you would in the bunq app 

  7. Send the request 🚀
