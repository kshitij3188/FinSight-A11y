# How do I check my tags and mentions on Together?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-check-my-tags-and-mentions-on-together

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Are you curious about the topics you've been mentioned in on Together, our community platform? Here's a simple guide to help you find them

## How can I see my posts?

  1. Start by heading over to the main **Together screen**

  2. Tap or click on your **Profile's icon** in the top right corner

  3. Tap or click on “**Posts** ”

  4. Review all your **Together posts**

## How can I see my discussions?

  1. Start by heading over to the main **Together screen**

  2. Tap or click on your **Profile's icon** in the top right corner

  3. Tap or click on “**Discussions** ”

  4. Review all the **discussions you participate in**

## How can I see my mentions?

  1. Start by heading over to the main **Together screen**

  2. Tap or click on your **Profile's icon** in the top right corner

  3. Tap or click on “**Mentions** ”

  4. Review all the **mentions you have in Together** Remember, Together is all about connecting and sharing with the bunq community. So, don't hesitate to jump in and join the conversation 🌈
