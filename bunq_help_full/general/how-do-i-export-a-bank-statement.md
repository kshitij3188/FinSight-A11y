# How do I export a bank statement?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-bank-statement

---

Hey bunqers 🌈 ,

A bank statement is an overview of all of the transactions you've made with your bunq account (IBAN) over a select period of time.

You may need one for the government when tax season rolls around, or even just to have for your own personal administration. You can also use the bank statement as proof of ownership because it'll show your full name and IBAN 📄

### How do I export a statement?

Follow the steps below to export a bank statement:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner 

  2. Select Accounting 

  3. Select Export Statement 

  4. Enter the Bank Account and time period you want the statement for and select your preferred format (PDF, CSV or MT940)

  5. Tap on **Export Statement**

**Note:** Keep in mind that we won't be able to export a bank statement for you if your account is unverified. To export a statement, make sure to [verify your account in just 5 minutes](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24112>)

## How do I export a statement from a Bank Account?

You can also export a bank statement for a specific Bank Account through the Bank Account's settings! Simply follow these steps to do so:

  1. Go to 🏠 Home 

  2. Select the Bank Account in question 

  3. Click on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Click on 🟢 Export statement

  5. Enter the time period you want the statement for and select your preferred format (PDF, CSV or MT940)

  6. Tap on **Export Statement**

## How do I export a statement on bunq Web?

Just follow these easy and fast steps:

  1. Go to <https://web.bunq.com>

  2. Click on the Bank Account

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Click on Export statement

  5. Select date and format

  6. Export

## How do I export a statement for my closed Bank Account?

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home tab

  2. Go to Your Profile

  3. Scroll down and tap on “Archive”

  4. Tap on Bank Accounts

  5. Select the Bank Account in question

  6. Tap on 🟠 Export Statement

Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50053>).

## How do I export a statement with the payment's attachments?

If you've attached images to a payment event in the app, now (as of version 22.12) you can export a bank statement which contains the attached file! Want to include the attachments in the bank statement? Simply click on the “Include Attachments” toggle and export the statement afterward ✅

Keep in mind that this feature is applicable only for PDF statement exports, and currently the only attachments supported are images [picture formats].

## Can I display my legal name differently on my bank statement?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to follow the simple steps explained [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/6090>) 🙌

## Do you need a different statement?

  * [**Annual statement**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2523-how-do-i-export-my-annual-account-overview>) with an overview of all your bunq IBANs for the previous year.

  * [Statement for **a single transaction**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/11710-export-statement-of-a-single-transaction/8>). This is useful when you want proof of a single payment, without other transactions being shown on the same document.

  * [**Card statement**](<https://together.bunq.com/d/50030>) for all payments that were done with one card during a certain period of time

  * If you don't find the format you're looking for, simply contact us through the [Support chat](<https://www.bunq.com/link/support>) and we'll assist you further 🚀
