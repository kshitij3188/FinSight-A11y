# How do I export a RIB?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-rib
**Topics:** Bank Accounts, France 🇫🇷

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

RIB stands for **relevé d'identité bancaire** , meaning 'statement of banking identity', or just another way of saying bank account details. It is merely a pre-printed form with your bank account number and sort codes for national and international transactions. You can export one for your bunq account by following the simple steps explained below 🚀

### How can I export a RIB?

You can simply get it by following the process explained below! 

1\. Go to 🏠 Home

2\. Select the Bank Account for which you need a RIB

3\. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

4\. Select Relevé d’Identité Bancaire

5\. Export the RIB and you're done 🙌

or

1\. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

2\. Go to Accounting

3\. Select Relevé d’Identité Bancaire

4\. Export the RIB and you're done 🙌

  
  
  

## Good to know

  * To export your RIB in French, ensure your phone's default language is set to French.

  * When you export a RIB for a Joint Account, it will display only your name as the one exporting it. If you need it for legal reasons, there can be two suggestions:

  * Export both the RIB and a regular bank statement, which will display both account holders' names, and attach them for the requesting party.

  * Export RIBs from both account holders and submit them together as attachments.
