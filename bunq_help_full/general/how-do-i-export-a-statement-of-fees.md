# How do I export a statement of fees?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-a-statement-of-fees

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

A statement of fees is a document on which you’ll see all the fees you were charged for on your bunq account during the previous year. You may need one when tax season rolls around or even just to have it for your own personal administration.

### How do I export a statement of fees for my Personal Account?

  1. Go to 🏡Home 

  2. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  3. Tap on **Accounting**

  4. Select **Annual overview**

  5. There you will see your statements of fees listed per year, you can select the one you need and export it in different ways.

Statement of fees can only be exported as a part of the Annual account overview.

### How do I export a statement of fees for my Business Account?

  1. Go to 🏡Home 

  2. Tap on your name at the top of the screen and select your Business account

  3. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  4. Tap on **Accounting**

  5. Select **Annual overview**

  6. There you will see your statements of fees listed per year, you can select the one you need and export it in different ways.

Statement of fees can only be exported as a part of the Annual account overview.

### How do I export a statement of fees for my Child’s Account?

  1. Go to 🏡Home 

  2. Tap on your name at the top of the screen and select your Child’s account

  3. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  4. Tap on **Accounting**

  5. Select **Annual overview**

  6. There you will see your statements of fees listed per year, you can select the one you need and export it in different ways.

Statement of fees can only be exported as a part of the Annual account overview.
