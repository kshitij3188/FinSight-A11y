# How long does it take to verify my paperwork?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-long-does-it-take-to-verify-my-paperwork

---

**Hey bunqer 🌈,**

The paperwork verification process usually takes **up to 5 minutes.** 👉 For the smoothest experience, check out our **pro tips** before you start. Once your bank account is fully set up, you'll get a notification – and you can start using your bunq account right away!

## Why is my verification taking longer?

In some cases, verification might take longer than expected. If this happens, you'll be placed on the [waiting list](<./waiting-list>) – a queue for users waiting for the next available 5-minute verification slot.

### Common Reasons:

  * **Manual Review:** If your verification needs extra checks or manual review, it can take longer. This might happen if there are discrepancies in your documents.

  * **Document Issues:** Blurry, expired, or incorrect documents can delay the process. You may need to submit new or corrected documents.

  * **High Demand:** During busy times, the queue can grow, causing slower processing.

  * **Technical Glitches:** System maintenance or technical issues can sometimes hold things up.

## I’ve been added to the waiting list. What now?

If your account couldn’t be opened in 5 minutes, you’ll have the option to join the [waiting list](<./waiting-list>), where you can wait until we can offer a 5-minute verification 🙌 ✨ **Bonus:** If the verification of your Personal or Business account takes longer than 1 week, you’ll receive an extra **30 days of free trial** as a gift. You can learn more on how the waiting list works [here](<./waiting-list>) 🌈
