# How to take the perfect selfie with ID?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-take-the-perfect-selfie-with-id

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

In some corner cases, you might be requested to take a selfie with your ID to confirm your identity. Most of the time this is because a support agent needs to identify you before assisting you further.

Here, you’ll see how to take the **perfect selfie with your ID** , so that your identification can happen as fast as possible.

### How does the perfect selfie with ID look?

The perfect selfie for identification ticks the following checklist: 

  * ✅ **Clear and visible face:** the support guide can see your face clearly, without 🧢/🕶 or anything covering the front of your face

  * ✅ **Clear and visible document:** the support guide can clearly read the information on the document you’re holding and can clearly identify your from your ID photo

## Pro tips on how to take the perfect selfie 🤳

  * Make sure the room is**well lit** : turn on the lights or make sure to take the selfie next to a window during the day (the Sun ☀️ is your perfect light)

  * Put the document closer to the front camera: to make sure that the info is readable, you can simply hold your ID closer to the camera. When the document appears closer to the camera, the characters are **bigger** and **more readable**.
