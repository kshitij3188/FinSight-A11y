# My phone's stolen or lost: what should I do?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/my-phones-stolen-or-lost-what-should-i-do

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

## Login from a new device

Make sure to install and login to your bunq account on a new device as fast as possible, so that you can secure it right away and make it impossible for anyone else to reach your account. In case your login info got changed and you can’t log back in, make sure to contact **SOS Support** for further assistance.

## Disconnect your old device

Once you have full access to your account, the first thing to do is to remove the lost/stolen device so no one can access your account through it. Just follow these easy steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Select ⚙️

  3. Click on **Security & Preferences**

  4. Select **Devices**

  5. Click on the 🗑 next to every unrecognized device

## Change your security code

In order to keep your account extra secure, make sure to also change your security code following these simple steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Select ⚙️ **Settings**

  3. Click on **Security**

  4. Select **Change security code**

  5. Follow the flow

## Change your login credentials

Right after, make sure to change your login email by following the easy and fast steps explained [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-change-my-personal-information>).   
  
**Pro Tip:** Make sure to also secure all of your other social platforms and accounts linked to your account by changing your login credentials. This way whoever has your device won’t be able to access any of your personal data 🙌
