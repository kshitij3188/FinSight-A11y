**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-my-daily-foreign-currencies-limits
**Topics:** Travel

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Wondering what your [Foreign Currencies](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45414>) daily limits are? Foreign Currencies daily limits differ based on the type of payment you're making. Click on the type of payment you want to check out down below.

> _Important: Our Foreign Currency partners (Currency Cloud) will stop supporting SWIFT payments in Mexican Pesos (MXN) starting from August 20, 2025. This means that you will be able to buy MXN and receive SWIFT MXN payments. If you have any remaining MXN currency in your portfolio, you can continue to hold on to your balance indefinitely. ❗️**However** , your Mexican Pesos must be converted to another, available currency before pay-outs or withdrawals._

## Important for Incoming payments

  * **Limits** To keep foreign currencies safe and available at all times, **incoming transactions over £90.000,00 cannot be processed and are returned to the sender**.

  * **Bounce fees** For an incoming payment that bounced, transaction costs apply. These costs are deducted from the payment upon return
