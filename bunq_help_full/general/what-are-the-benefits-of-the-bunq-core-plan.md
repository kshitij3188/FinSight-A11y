# What are the benefits of the bunq Core plan?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-benefits-of-the-bunq-core-plan
**Topics:** Plans

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Core is a bank account for everyday use, offering high interest on savings and features designed to make life easier.

[Get a bank account, in just 5 minutes](<https://together.bunq.com/d/46776>) \- bunq Core Personal is €3.99/month and €7.99/month for Business.
