## Business account benefits

  * 💸 [25 Bank Accounts](<https://together.bunq.com/d/39139>)

  * 💳 [3 Physical Card - Free](<https://together.bunq.com/d/2936>) (Mastercard Credit or Mastercard Debit but not the Metal Card Credit). Each additional card will cost you €3.49 per month per card.

  * 🔁 [Free Card Replacement](<https://together.bunq.com/d/18137>) \- when you order a replacement for a card that expires within 3 months.

  * 💥 Discount on Personal Account - 50% on any personal plan, including bunq Pack.
