# What plans are available?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-plans-are-available
**Topics:** Plans

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

Welcome to bunq! Let’s compare our available plans and find the perfect one that makes life easy for you.

## bunq Elite

The bank account designed for **your international lifestyle**.

  * Manage your finances with **up to 25 free Bank Accounts** , each with its own unique IBAN

  * Includes **3 free Physical bunq Cards** and **25 free Digital bunq Cards** (Credit or Debit)

**Pricing** : 

  * **👤 Personal** : €18.99/month

  * **📁 Business** : €23.99/month 

  * **💰 Bank Accounts** : A bundle of 25 additional Bank Accounts is available for €20/month 

  * **💳 Cards** : Additional physical cards can be ordered for a fee of €9.99, plus €3.49/month per card for Personal accounts or €3.99/month per card for Business Accounts. Or, a bundle of 25 additional Digital Cards is available for €19.99/month

## bunq Pro

The bank account that **makes budgeting easy**.

  * Manage your finances with **up to 25 free Bank Accounts** , each with its own unique IBAN

  * Includes **3 free Physical bunq Cards** and **25 free Digital bunq Cards** (Credit or Debit)

**Pricing** : 

  * **👤 Personal** : €9.99/month

  * **📁 Business** : €13.99/month 

  * **💰 Bank Accounts** : A bundle of 25 additional Bank Accounts is available for €20/month 

  * **💳 Cards** : Additional physical cards can be ordered for a fee of €9.99, plus €3.49/month per card for Personal accounts or €3.99/month per card for Business Accounts. Or, a bundle of 25 additional Digital Cards is available for €19.99 per month

## bunq Core

The bank account for **everyday use**.

  * Open **up to 5 Bank Accounts** , each with its own unique IBAN

  * Includes **1 free Physical bunq Card** and **1 free Digital bunq Card**(Credit or Debit)

**Pricing** : 

  * **👤 Personal** : €3.99/month

  * **📁 Business** : €7.99/month 

  * **💰 Bank Accounts** : Additional bank accounts aren't available with this plan

  * **💳 Cards** : Additional physical or digital cards aren't available with this plan

## bunq Free

**The essentials** to get you started.

  * Open up to **3 free Bank Accounts** and effortlessly take control of your finances 

  * Includes 1 free **Digital bunq Card** (Credit or Debit)

**Pricing** : 

  * **👤 Personal** : €0.00/month

  * **📁 Business** : €0.00/month 

  * **💰 Bank Accounts** : Additional bank accounts aren't available with this plan

  * **💳 Cards** : Physical cards and additional digital cards aren't available with this plan

## bunq Pack

Enjoy bunq **together with your friends, family, and partner(s)**.

  * Includes 4 x bunq Pro Personal **or** 3 x bunq Pro Personal and 1 x bunq Pro Business accounts

**Pricing** : 

  * €28.99/month (€7.25/month per person)
