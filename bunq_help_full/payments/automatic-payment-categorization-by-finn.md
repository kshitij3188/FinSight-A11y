# Automatic payment categorization by Finn

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/automatic-payment-categorization-by-finn
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

Finn, your personal assistant, now automatically categorizes payment requests and direct debits. With this new feature we're making sure this process is seamless and you don't need to do it manually.

## How does Finn categorize my payments?

All you need to do is add a description to your payment request. Finn will then automatically categorize the payment request for you, eliminating the need for manual categorization.

## Can I save a certain merchant/person’s category choice?

When accepting certain person/merchant’s payment request or direct debit, you can choose to **Always Accept Requests from This User** or **Always Accept This Direct Debit**. In this procedure, you also have the choice to make a payment from a specific Bank Account or use the Auto Select feature. This feature enables a payment to be automatically deducted from the budgeting category that aligns with the nature of the payment.
