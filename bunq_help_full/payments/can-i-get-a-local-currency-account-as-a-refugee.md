# Can I get a Foreign Currency account as a refugee?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-i-get-a-local-currency-account-as-a-refugee
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Since the beginning of March 2022, we offer bunq accounts for refugees who can [use the bunq Core plan for free for up to 3 months](<./how-to-open-a-bunq-account-as-an-ukrainian-or-russian-refugee>). However, there’re some requirements you need to meet to open a Foreign Currency account with us.

### What do I need to open a Foreign Currencies Account?

If you’re a refugee who opened a bunq account, and want to use the [Foreign Currency](<./foreign-currency-account>) feature to send, receive and hold balance in different currencies, make sure you go through the following:

  1. Open a bunq account

  2. Be **physically located in a country within the European Economic Area**

  3. **Finish the process to receive the necessary residency documents** specific to the country where you currently reside, complete with a **Tax Identification Number** and **Proof of residence** 📄

  4. **Reach out to bunq support via the app** (your Profile in the top left corner > Settings > Need help? Get support)

  5. **Ask them to remove your refugee status** for wanting to open a Foreign Currency account

❗️ **Once your Refugee status is removed, it cannot be added again! This means that your account with bunq will be invoiced monthly.** More info on plans and pricing can be found [here](<https://www.bunq.com/doc/pricing>).

## Additional documents needed

  * Go through an **additional ID verification process** tailored to your country of residence. We have explained the steps [here](<./how-do-i-verify-my-identity-with-incode>) ✅

  * Provide a **document that we can use as Proof of Residence**. You can check out the list of accepted documents [here](<./why-do-i-have-to-send-a-proof-of-residence>) ✅

  * Provide a **document stating your tax number**. You can check this useful [link](<./wheres-my-tax-identification-number-tin>) for more info ✅

## What happens next?

Once done, our registrations team will check the provided information to complete the process. After a successful verification, you’ll be notified via the app and you can immediately proceed to upgrade your plan & set up a Foreign Currencies Account 🙌 Keep in mind that with your new Foreign Currency Account, you will be free to process incoming/outgoing payments only to countries permitted in the jurisdiction of Currencycloud. You can check this link to find the full [list](<https://support.currencycloud.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360017599560-Permitted-Jurisdictions>).
