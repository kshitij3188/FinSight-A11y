# Can I receive payments in another currency or from outside the SEPA region?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/can-i-receive-payments-in-another-currency-or-from-outside-the-sepa-region
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Yes, you can send/receive payments to your bunq account from another bank account outside of the SEPA Region!

### How does it work?

All you need to do is make sure that the **sending bank converts the amount of the payment into euros** before sending the payment to your bunq account. If a payment using a different currency reaches bunq, it will get bounced back automatically as we only support euros as a currency.

## What if I have a Foreign Currency account?

If you have a Foreign Currency sub-account opened, you can receive funds in the relevant currency without any hassle! 

Further information about Foreign Currency sub-accounts can be found [here](<./foreign-currency-account>). Keep in mind that even with a Foreign Currencies account, the transfers are still **SWIFT payments**. It takes time for these payments to arrive. **SWIFT payments can take up to 5 days.** There are also fees involved in receiving a **SWIFT payment**. You can learn more about this [here](<./what-are-the-foreign-currencies-accounts-fees>).
