### How do I open a Foreign Currencies Account?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on 🟣 Add an Extra Bank Account 

  3. Select 🟠 Payment Account

  4. Personalize your Bank Account

  5. Choose Foreign Currency

  6. Done

Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48904>).

## Are my Foreign Currencies Accounts secure?

bunq always keeps your money safe and sound. In order to offer you the best product possible, without taking any risks, we partner with Currencycloud. Your balance from your non-euro bunq accounts is technically held in a third-party bank account which isn’t sensitive to any credit-risks from our counter party.

This way, we ensure that your money are taken care of with the best protection. So yes, your Foreign Currencies Accounts are secure 💪

## Why do I get a UK IBAN on a Foreign Currencies Account?

The foreign currency isn't held in the Netherlands, but in the UK.

## How do I receive money in my Foreign Currencies Account?

When someone sends money to your Foreign Currencies Account, it is usually a SWIFT payment, which can take up to 5 days to arrive. Additionally, fees may apply for receiving a SWIFT payment. To ensure the transfer is successful, the sender will need the following details:

  * The Foreign Currencies IBAN is a **UK IBAN**

  * BIC/SWIFT code: **TCCLGB3LXXX**.

More details can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/48907>).

## What are my Foreign Currencies accepted activities?

Our partner Currencycloud has some legal restrictions on the type of industries, activities, and countries they support and service. Most of the activities are aligned with our [Terms & Conditions](<https://www.bunq.com/legal/personal>), however make sure to check what are the [Non-permitted activities](<https://support.currencycloud.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360017472339-Non-permitted-activity>) and [Permitted jurisdictions](<https://support.currencycloud.com/hc/en-gb/articles/360017599560>) by Currencycloud - the payments from your Foreign Currencies Account are automatically processed and rejected based on the previously mentioned terms.

## Why did I receive questions about my payment?

As your payments relate to currency conversion, this is done via our external partner payment provider - Currencycloud. Sometimes they need supporting information regarding these payments. If needed - you'll receive a notification regarding this and you'll asked to provide the needed information in the in-app chat ✅

After receiving the requested information, our team forwards it to our payment provider partner. Once accepted from their side - the payment is done successfully!

Please note that failure to provide a sufficient response in 72h after receiving the questions may result in potential rejection of the payment in future.

## Can I use Auto Round Up for Foreign Currencies 💸

Payments made to Foreign Currency Savings Accounts can also be used with your [Auto Round Up](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5148-how-do-i-enable-auto-save-for-my-savings-goal-account/2>). Further information on Foreign Currency Savings accounts can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57095>).

You can only choose to save in one Savings account at a time — this applies to all currencies available.

## Does the app automatically deduct money from my Foreign Currencies Account?

With [AutoSelect](<https://together.bunq.com/d/53929>), wherever you go and with whichever card you pay, the app will automatically deduct the money from the Bank Account with the corresponding currency.

**Example** : You go on a trip to the USA. Your card is linked to your EUR Bank Account, but you have an active USD Bank Account. When you make a card payment in the USA, the app will automatically deduct the money from your USD Bank Account so that you can save on conversion fees and extra costs 🚀
