# How can I add and convert money to my Foreign Currency Account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-add-and-convert-money-to-my-foreign-currency-account
**Topics:** Travel, Account

---

**Hey bunqer 🌈**

Is your Foreign Currency Account low on money? No worries, this article shows you how you can bring your balance back to desired levels 💪

## How can I add money to my Foreign Currency Account?

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on your Foreign Currency Account

  3. Select 🟣 Add Money

  4. Chose your topping-up method

  5. Ka-ching! All done 🌈

## How can I convert my Foreign Currency Account money to EUR?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on the Foreign Currency Account you want to top-up

  3. Tap on 🟣 Add money

  4. Tap on 🟢 Currency Conversion

  5. Go with the flow

## How can I convert EUR to my Foreign Currency Account?

The following steps are for both **converting and withdrawing your money** :

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on an EUR Bank Account of your choice

  3. Tap on 🟣 Add money

  4. Tap on 🟢 Currency Conversion

  5. Go with the flow
