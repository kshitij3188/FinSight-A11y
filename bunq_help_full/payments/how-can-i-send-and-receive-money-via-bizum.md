# How can I send and receive money via Bizum?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-send-and-receive-money-via-bizum
**Topics:** Payments, Spain 🇪🇸

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

With Bizum, a mobile payment service, Spanish users can now send and receive money instantly using their mobile phones.

## How can I set up Bizum?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️Settings

  3. Select Bizum

  4. Turn the toggle on

  5. Choose the Bank Account you want to link to Bizum

  6. Confirm the phone number linked

## How can I change the linked Bank Account?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Go to ⚙️Settings

  3. Select Bizum

  4. Tap on **Linked Bank Account**

  5. Select the Bank Account you want to use

  6. Confirm the change via SMS confirmation code

## How can I pay via Bizum?

  1. Tap on the Bank Account linked to Bizum

  2. Tap on **More**

  3. Select **Bizum**

  4. Tap on 🟠 Pay

  5. Choose the recipient and select the amount

  6. Review the payment and send it

## How can I request money via Bizum?

  1. Tap on the Bank Account linked to Bizum

  2. Tap on **More**

  3. Select **Bizum**

  4. Tap on 🔵Request

  5. Choose a person from your contact list and select the amount

  6. Review the request and send it

## How can I pay online via Bizum?

Whenever you place an online payment using Bizum as a payment method, you’re going to receive a push notification. Once you tap on the notification, you’ll be redirected to the bunq app to either **approve** or **decline** the request. 

If you’ve missed the notification, follow these steps:

  1. Tap on the 🛎️ bell icon in the top left corner

  2. Tap on the notification

  3. **Approve** or **Decline** the payment

## Can Bizum payments be recalled? 

At the moment, you can’t recall your Bizum payments in-app. However, you can receive Bizum payment recalls and easily approve or deny them. 

To recall a Bizum payment you made, you can reach out to our Support in your bunq App. You can find more information on how to recall a payment [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/59564>). 

## Who can use Bizum?

In order to use Bizum with your bunq account, you have to meet the following criteria:

  * You have a verified Personal account with bunq Core, bunq Pro, or bunq Elite

  * You have a Spanish IBAN account

  * You are 18 years old or older

## What can I do if I get an error during verification? 

Running into an error when submitting your Spanish DNI document during verification? If the app says "**This type of identification is not accepted** ", we've got a quick fix for you. Simply re-submit your document three times in a row. This will automatically allow you to continue your verification.
