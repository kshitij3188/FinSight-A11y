# How do I add a bunq.me widget to my website?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-add-a-bunqme-widget-to-my-website
**Topics:** bunq Web

---

**Hello bunqers 🌈,**

Are you an artist, a blogger, or a content creator looking for an easy way to accept donations on your personal website? We've got an exciting solution for you. 

We've developed a widget for **bunq.me** — a simple and secure **payment request** feature. This widget allows you to add a donation button directly to your website, making it easier for your followers to support your work.

## How does the bunq.me widget work?

With the **bunq.me widget** , you can receive payments and donations directly to your bunq account, without the need for any third-party payment processors. It's a seamless and straightforward way to monetize your online content while keeping full control over your funds.

## How can I add it to my website?

  1. Copy the following embed code `https://widget.bunq.com/embed?type=BUNQ_ME_BUTTON&id={id}` and paste into your blog or website’s HTML editor.

  2. Be sure to change the contents of the data alias field `{id}` to match your actual URL extension, which is your bunq.me link name. 

  3. Done! You should now have a **Pay with bunq** button available on your website 🎉

**Important to note** : The embed code contains a tracking code. Rest assured, we only use this code to monitor the activity and usage of the widget: 

  * In terms of data, we receive the URL of the page where the button is displayed, and the widget's number of page view events. That’s it! 

  * We use this data only to monitor the widget’s use, for the purpose of measuring the tool's usefulness.

  * We do not share this information with anyone, and we’ll only use it internally to improve bunq.me for end-users.
