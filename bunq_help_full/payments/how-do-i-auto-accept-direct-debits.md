# How do I auto-accept direct debits?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-auto-accept-direct-debits
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can enable Auto Accept for direct debits and automatically approve all future payments to your selected list of merchants 🚀

To set up Auto Accept, the merchant or friend needs to have sent you a Direct Debit request at least once before — this is so they appear in your app and you can auto accept them. Once their first request arrives, simply pay it manually. From then on, you'll be able to set up Auto Accept for all their future payments straight from that payment or by following the steps below: 

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Tap on **Security**

  4. Scroll down to **Auto Accepted**

  5. Select **Direct Debits**

  6. Choose a payment of the Direct Debit you want to add

  7. Set the Auto Accept limit 

  8. All set ✨

To keep you safe from any unexpected payments leaving your account through a direct debit payment, we set the default payment limit to the original amount of the first payment you approved. You stay in control the entire time, so you can always adjust the limit whenever needed. 

## How can I auto-accept payments on bunq Web?

  1. Go to <https://web.bunq.com>

  2. Click on your Profile

  3. Click on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Click on **Direct Debits** under the **Auto Accepted** tab

  5. 🔵 + Add Direct Debit

  6. Add the Direct Debit you want

  7. Set the payment limit 

  8. All set ✅

## Can I change the limit of my auto-accepted direct debits?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Tap on **Security**

  4. Scroll down to **Auto Accepted**

  5. Select **Direct Debits**

  6. Tap on **Per Payment** and adjust the limit

  7. Tap on **Save**

  8. All set ✅

## How can I disable the auto-accept?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Tap on **Security**

  4. Click on **Direct Debits** under **Auto Accepted**

  5. Select the direct debit you want to remove

  6. Tap on **Stop Auto Accept**
