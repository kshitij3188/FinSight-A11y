# How do I auto-accept payments from specific merchants?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-auto-accept-payments-from-specific-merchants

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

For optimum control, we always make sure that you authorize a payment before it’s done. From now on, you never have to manually approve payments to your favorite online stores or to your best friends. You can simply enable Auto Accept and automatically approve all future payments to your selected list. 🚀

You can also add your preferred merchants by following this simple process:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Tap on "Security"

  4. Under "Auto Accepted" tap on "Card Payments"

  5. Choose a payment of the merchant you want to add

  6. Set the Auto Accept limit 

  7. All set ✨

When you Auto Accept payments from merchants, the default limit is set to the original payment amount. In some cases, it may be set slightly higher to account for FX-related fluctuations. You can change this limit anytime.

## How can I auto-accept payments on bunq Web?

  1. Go to <https://web.bunq.com>

  2. Click on your Profile

  3. Click on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Select "Card Payments" under the "Auto Accept" tab

  5. Click on 🔵 + Add Merchant

  6. Set the Auto Accept limit

## How can I disable the auto-accept?

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Select "Card Payments" under "Auto Accept" 

  4. Select the merchant you want to remove

  5. Select "Stop Auto Accept”
