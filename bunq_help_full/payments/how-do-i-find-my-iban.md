# How do I find my IBAN?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-find-my-iban

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈 ,**

IBAN stands for International Bank Account Number and is a number attached to all EU bank accounts. You can find out more about them below. 

## Where can I find my IBAN in the bunq app? 

With bunq, you can have up to 25 Bank Accounts, each with its own unique IBAN! To find your IBAN in the bunq app, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the Home screen

  2. Select a Bank Accounts

  3. The IBAN will be shown in the middle of the screen. It'll resemble:

  * NL xx BUNQ xxxx xxxx xx

  * DE xx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx

  * FR xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx x

  * ES xx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx

  * IE xx BUNQ xxxx xxxx xxxx xx

  * GB xx TCCL xxxx xxxx xxxx xx

  4. You can also tap on the IBAN to easily save it or share it.

## How can I get a bunq IBAN?

You can get one by creating a Bank Account and following the easy steps below:

  1. Go to Home tab

  2. Tap on Add an Extra Bank Account

  3. Select a Payment Account 

  4. Personalize your Bank Account

  5. Done! 

## Where can I find my CVC code?

You can find your CVC code for all of your bunq cards within the app by following the steps explained below:

  1. Go to the 💳 *_Cards tab_

  2. Select the card you want to check

  3. The CVC info will be displayed under the card

## Where can I find my Routing Number?

In order to do faster transfers with your USD Foreign Currencies Account (for €0.70 only), you can use your Routing Number. You can find it by following these easy steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home screen

  2. Click on your USD Bank Account

  3. Scroll down to Routing number

  4. Done!

## What is bunq's BIC/SWIFT Code?

The BIC/SWIFT code for our IBANs are as follows:

  * NL 🇳🇱 IBANs = BUNQNL2AXXX

  * DE 🇩🇪 IBANs = BUNQDE82XXX

  * FR 🇫🇷 IBANs = BUNQFRP2XXX

  * ES 🇪🇸 IBANs = BUNQESM2XXX

  * IE 🇮🇪 IBANs = BUNQIE22XXX

  * UK 🇬🇧 IBANs = TCCLGB31XXX

## How do I find my SORT code?

  1. Tap on 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on your UK Bank Account

  3. Find your Sort Code under "IBAN & BIC"
