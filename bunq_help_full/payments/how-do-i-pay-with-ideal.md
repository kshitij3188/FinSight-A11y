# How do I pay with iDEAL?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-pay-with-ideal
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Want to shop online with ease? Let's talk about iDEAL - the go-to payment method for Dutch web shops 🛍️

**Here's how iDEAL with bunq works for you:**

  * It's free 🆓

  * Payments are instant ⚡

  * Available on both your phone and computer 📱💻

  * It’s safe and secure 🔒

## How do I pay with iDEAL?

**On your phone:**

  * Choose bunq as your bank on the iDEAL page

  * You'll be sent straight to the bunq app to confirm

**On your computer:**

  * Select iDEAL at checkout

  * You'll be redirected to bunq Web to verify the payment

> **Pro tip: Look for https://pay.ideal.nl/ and a lock icon (🔒) on the iDEAL page to ensure it's legit!**

## How to create an iDEAL Profile?

If you want to create an iDEAL Profile follow these easy steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Select My iDEAL

  4. Go to My iDEAL

  5. You will be redirected to an iDEAL page. Follow the steps there to create an iDEAL Profile.

## iDeal Fast Order

With an iDEAL Profile, you can use iDEAL Fast Order for quick checkouts at participating stores. You can check a complete availability list [here](<https://www.ideal.nl/winkelplein>) ✅ 

**Remember:**

  * You need a Dutch bank account (NL IBAN) to use iDEAL 🇳🇱

  * You must be 16 or older

## Good to know

The merchant sees the following info when they receive your iDEAL payment:

  * BIC

  * Your IBAN

  * Your name

  * Amount
