# How do I top up my account with IBAN transfer?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-top-up-my-account-with-iban-transfer

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

You can also send money from another bank account directly to your bunq IBAN.

### How do I top up?

To locate your IBAN, you can [click here](<https://go.bunq.com/link/ibans>) or follow the steps below:

  1. [Click here](<https://www.bunq.com/tutorials/topup>) or go to 🏠 Home 

  2. Tap on 🟣 + Add Money

  3. Select Bank transfer (SEPA)

  4. Fill in the banking info and top it up 🎉
