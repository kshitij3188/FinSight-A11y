# How do I use the filter tool to Search for a payment?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-use-the-filter-tool-to-search-for-a-payment

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Are you looking for past payments that you made but are struggling to find them? Maybe you would like to know your spendings during your last holiday? Or you would like to know who sent you money to a specific account/find out who still owes you money?

Look no further, as the search tool is exactly what you need.

### How does it work?

  1. Go to Home 🏠

  2. Tap on the desired Bank Account

  3. Use the search bar 🔍 and use the filter tool to locate your payment

  4. Tap **Search**

  5. Your Search Results will be displayed here
