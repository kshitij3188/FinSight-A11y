# How to add note or receipt to a payment?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-add-note-or-receipt-to-a-payment

---

Hey bunqers 🌈

You can keep track of all your payments and keep them organized by simply adding notes and receipts to them. It’s really easy! Simply follow these steps:

  1. Go to Home

  2. Select **See All** under Recent Transactions 

  3. Click on the event in question 

  4. Click on **Add Note or Receipt**

Once you do, simply write down the note you want to add or click on the 📎 to attach a receipt of your choice.

**Note: Payment notes and receipts are only visible for the owner/s of the sub-account.**
