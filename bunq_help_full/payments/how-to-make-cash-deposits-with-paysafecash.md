# How to make cash deposits with PaysafeCash?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-make-cash-deposits-with-paysafecash
**Topics:** Cash, Security, Payments

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

We always work towards making life easy. And what way to make it easier than safe and fast deposits for your account? That’s why we’ve teamed up with PaysafeCash and now you can use their services with just a few clicks in the app. Read below to see how.

You can find a list of Paysafe cash locations [here](<https://www.paysafecash.com/>) 💸

### How does it work? 

Simply create a deposit in the app by [clicking here](<https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/link/topup>) or by going to Home and clicking on “Add money”. The first time you do this, we create a Paysafe account for you. If you have an account already, you can use the credentials to log in 🚀

If you don't have an account yet, click on the register button when you first land on the PaysafeCash screen.

When you want to make a deposit, we create a Paysafe barcode for you. The barcode can be downloaded as PDF, added to a wallet, or sent as SMS or e-mail. Of course, you can also find it again in the app.

You can use the barcode to deposit your cash at any of the supported stores. These will also show up in your app on the map. Find a store nearby and ask at the counter about the deposit.

The staff will help you make the deposit by scanning the barcode. Right after, the money will be credited to your bunq account 💸

PaysafeCash deposits are available in **Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Republic of Cyprus, Czech Republic, France, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, The Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain and Sweden.**

For our users in Germany and Austria, you can check our Barzahlen Cash Deposit feature [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/14531>) 💪

## Fees and limits

**Fees**

With this feature, you can deposit **up to €100 free of charge** per billing month. After this limit has been passed, the deposit fee is 2.5%.

**Limits**

  * Minimum (per deposit): €10

  * Maximum (per deposit): €250

  * Daily limit: €999

  * Monthly limit: €9,999

Your monthly limit is calculated based on the last 30 days, not by the calendar month. This means it updates daily to reflect your activity over the past 30 days.

  * The limits apply for both Personal and Business users.
