# Organize Your Income 💸

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-sort-payments-automatically-with-payment-sorter

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Organize your income — and enjoy effortless budgeting and investing. Set it up once and we'll automatically split your incoming payments between Bank Accounts, Stocks or ETFs! 

### How can I Organize my Income? 🔎

Automatically direct the money you receive to your Bank Accounts, stocks, or ETFs so you can budget and grow your wealth without having to think about it. This feature can be used for any recurring incoming payment, like a salary or a payment made regularly from a friend, relative or organization.

When you receive this payment, you only need to set it up once. You can also enable it at any another time by following the steps below:

  1. Tap to the **Net Wealth tile** from your Home tab

  2. Scroll down and select **Organize Your Income**

  3. Select the incoming payment you'd like to be sorted

  4. Choose to sort by percentage (%) or a specific amount

  5. Select the amount you want to sort to different accounts, Stocks, and ETFs

  6. Tap on **Organize This Payment**

  7. Done ✅

You can check all your organized payments right there in the Net Wealth screen. 

_Note: You can't re-organize a recurring payment which was already split this month. You'll have to wait for the next recurring payment in order to organize it again._

## Can Business users organize their payments while AutoVAT is enabled?

Yes! These can both be enabled at the same time. If both are set up, AutoVAT will be deducted first and the rest of the amount will be organized according to your preferences.

## Who can organize payments automatically?

As a bunq Pro or bunq Elite user, you can easily organize your salary or any recurring payments you receive.

**Important note:** The feature can't be used with any of the [Foreign Currencies](<https://together.bunq.com/d/45414>) we currently provide.

## What if the amount differs from one month to another?

That’s not a problem! As long as the amount is high enough to make the sorting possible, the organizational tool will do its job and transfer the amounts you chose to all the Bank Accounts, stocks and ETFs concerned.
