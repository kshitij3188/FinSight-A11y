# How to split tips at US restaurants?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-split-tips-at-us-restaurants
**Topics:** Tips

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

If you're in the US and often dine out with friends, you might be familiar with the process of adding a tip after paying with your card. This can sometimes cause confusion if the amount changes after you've already asked your friends to pay you back. But worry not! With this new feature, you can now **split the payment including the tip right away.**

## How can I split the tip?

  1. Go to the 🏠Home tab

  2. Select the Bank Account with the payment you want to split

  3. Tap on the payment you want to split

  4. Tap on “Split”

  5. Select the person you want to split it with and tap **Next**

  6. Tap on **Add Tip** and choose a tip percentage or enter the amount you tipped

  7. Enter how much each person should pay

  8. Tap on **Next** This way, you can request the full amount, including the tip, avoiding the hassle of sending a second request later.
