# I sent a transfer but the recipient hasn't received it: what should I do?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/i-sent-a-transfer-but-the-recipient-hasnt-received-it-what-should-i-do
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When you send a payment from your bunq account, we'll process it right away and send it to the receiving bank. It can then take a couple of days for the payment to arrive to the receiving bank account.

If you have a suspicion that your transfer hasn’t arrived yet, here’s some things to consider:

## 1\. Take into account the waiting time for your transfer’s processing

Since some banks work with different transferring channels, some transfers may take longer to process than others. Usually, bank transfers between banks in the **SEPA Region** take **1 to 2 business days** to arrive to the recipient.

Learn all about the processing times for outgoing transfers[ here](<./when-will-my-payment-arrive>) 🚀

## 2\. Check with the recipient

You can always contact the recipient to see if they’ve received the transfer or have received an indication that the transfer is on its way. You can also advise them to check their online banking app or export a bank statement to check if the payment has entered their account officially.

## 3\. The transfer hasn’t arrived even after the normal processing time is up, what next?

In such cases, you can **export a single transaction statement** and provide it to the recipient as a **proof that the transaction has left your account** (and our system too). Then the recipient should request a **payment investigation** from their relevant bank and provide the same statement to them for further reference. If the receiving bank requests more information regarding the missing transfer, which cannot be found in the app, be sure to contact our Support Team and ask them for further assistance 🙌

### Rejected or returned payments

If a payment you sent is rejected or returned by the beneficiary bank, it may take up to 4 working days for the money to return to your account. In some cases, the process can take longer if the beneficiary bank performs additional checks.

If the beneficiary bank requests more information about your outgoing payment:

  * You can export your payment statement and share it directly with the receiving bank.

If the payment was successfully sent from our side but the beneficiary bank cannot locate it, we require a statement confirming that they didn’t receive the payment using the provided reference to investigate further.
