# How do I schedule a payment?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/schedule-a-payment

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Scheduling payments is quick and convenient, just like making a one-time payment. After entering your payment details, simply choose a future date or set up automatic recurring payments. This way, you’ll never miss a bill or transfer, and your finances stay on track effortlessly.

### How do I schedule a payment?

  1. Go to your 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on a Bank Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings 

  4. Select Scheduled payments

  5. Tap on **Create a Scheduled Payment**

  6. Enter the amount and choose the recipient

  7. Schedule the date and how often you will be making the payment

  8. Tap on Next and then Schedule

  9. The payment is now scheduled 🗓

## How can I edit a scheduled payment?

Please follow the steps below to edit one of your scheduled payments:

  1. Go to tab **Home**

  2. Open your **Bank Account**

  3. Tap on **Settings** in the top right corner 

  4. Tap on **Scheduled payments**

  5. Tap on the **Scheduled payment** you would like to change

  6. Tap on **Edit Scheduling**

## How can I schedule a payment from my Savings Account?

  1. Go to 💰Savings tab

  2. Select your Savings Account

  3. Fill in the amount and tap on the sending account to choose your Savings Account

  4. Tap on **Choose Recipient** and fill in the information

  5. Tap on **Pay: Immediately** and choose the time and frequency

  6. Tap on Schedule

  7. Done!

## How can I edit a scheduled payment from my Savings Account?

  1. Go to 💰Savings tab

  2. Tap on the Savings Account

  3. Tap on the scheduled payment and then on **Scheduled From**

  4. Tap on "Edit scheduling" or "Cancel future payments"

  5. Done!

## How can I schedule pocket money for my child?

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on your name at the top of the screen 

  3. Select your Child

  4. Tap on your child’s Bank Account

  5. Tap on **Set Up Pocket Money**

  6. Choose how often to top up, how much, and which Bank Account to use

  7. Tap **Confirm**

or

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Switch to your child’s Home tab 

  3. Tap on your child’s Bank Account 

  4. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  5. Select **Pocket Money**

  6. Choose how often to top up, how much, and which Bank Account to use

  7. Tap **Confirm**

## How can I schedule payments to my child’s savings goal?

  1. Go to your Savings tab

  2. Tap on your name at the top of the screen 

  3. Select your child’s name

  4. Tap on your child’s Savings account 

  5. Tap on "Set Up Scheduled Savings"

  6. Select your Goal Amount 

  7. Tap Set

or

  1. Go to your child’s 🏠 Savings tab

  2. Tap on your child’s Savings Account

  3. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select "Savings Goal"

  5. Select your Goal Amount 

  6. Tap Set

## Do you want to schedule payments like a pro?

You can set up a Scheduled Payment Request by following these steps:

  1. Go to the 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap on the Bank Account you want to schedule the request from

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Select Scheduled Requests

  5. Tap on Create a Scheduled Request

  6. Enter the amount and choose the recipient

  7. Select the date and frequency

  8. Confirm with your security code and you're done ✅

Find out how you can edit and cancel scheduled requests [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/38405>).
