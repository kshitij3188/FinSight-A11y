# What are SEPA Business to Business (B2B) Direct Debits and how do I accept them?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-sepa-business-to-business-b2b-direct-debits-and-how-do-i-accept-them
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

In the world of banking and finance, **SEPA Business to Business (B2B) Direct Debits** play a crucial role in facilitating transactions between businesses. But what exactly are they, and how can you, as a bunq user, accept them? 

We’ll provide a quick run down of **SEPA B2B Direct Debits** , explaining how they function, and the steps you need to follow to accept them through your bunq account.

## How do I accept SEPA B2B Direct Debits?

Normally, accepting a business-to-business direct debit requires you to send a signed form to your bank and, once accepted, it can’t be easily changed. 

We decided to do this differently. When another company sends a mandate form you can simply write down your bunq IBAN and send it back to them. There’s no need to send us a copy. When that company’s bank sends the first direct debit, you’ll see this as a request in your bunq app, which you can accept or decline with a single tap. It's that easy 👍

Once you approve the first one, you’re all set. We’ll automatically accept all future direct debits from that company for you. We'll also set a default payment limit based on that first amount, which you can easily change anytime you want.

## How does it work in the app?

Here's how it works in the app:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on the 🔔 Icon

  3. Select the Direct Debit event 

  4. You can choose between **Always accepting this Direct debit** or to **Accept only this direct debit**

  5. Enter your security code

  6. Done 👍

> You have 1 day (24 hours) to accept it before it expires and is automatically declined.
