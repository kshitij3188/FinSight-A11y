# What are the bunq.me daily limits and fees?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-bunqme-daily-limits-and-fees
**Topics:** Pricing

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

[bunq.me](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>) is a convenient feature that allows you to send and receive money with just a link. However, to ensure security and maintain a healthy financial environment, there are certain limits and fees associated with its usage.

## bunq.me limits

Total weekly bunq.me limit: €1,500

  * iDeal payments: €1,500 maximum for the _day_.

  * Bancontact: €1,000 per _transaction_.

  * Debit / Credit card payments: from €10 to €500 per _transaction_. More info [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/24346-accept-credit-card-payments-and-never-set-up-a-psp-again>).

  * bunq Web: €1000 per _day_.

## bunq.me fees

**Receiving instant bunq.me payments via iDEAL or Bancontact**

  * **Personal** : Free 

  * **Business** : €0.27 per payment

**Receiving bunq.me payments with debit or credit card**

  * **Personal** : 2.5%

  * **Business** : 2.5% + €0.27 per payment

You can check all bunq-related fees in our [Pricing Sheet](<https://www.bunq.com/pricing-sheet>).
