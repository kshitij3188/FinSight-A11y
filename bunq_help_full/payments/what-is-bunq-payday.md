# What is bunq Payday?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunq-payday
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq Payday is your automatic weekly payment summary of everything you’ve earned with bunq over the past week. Payday helps you track your interest earnings effortlessly. 

## What’s included in my bunq Payday?

  * **MassInterest 💸** \- Your interest is paid weekly, with a daily breakdown of what you’ve earned. Interest is calculated based on the lowest balance in your savings account each day.

  * **Total earnings 🤑** \- Track how much you’ve earned in interest or Cashback Partner rewards over time.

  * **Trees earned 🌳** \- See how many trees you’ve earned.

## When will I receive my next Payday?

To find out when your next Payday is, follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your 👤 Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Scroll down and tap on **Rewards**

  4. Your next Payday weekday will be shown here

## Where will I receive my Payday earnings?

To check the Bank Account you’re going to receive your payout on, follow the steps below:

  1. Tap on your 👤 Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Scroll down and tap on **Rewards**

  4. See the payout Bank Account next to **Paid to**

If you earn in different currencies, payouts are made to the **oldest active Bank Account** in each currency. To receive earnings in a specific currency, you must have an **active Foreign Currency Bank Account** in that currency. If you don’t have a Bank Account in a certain currency, we won’t be able to pay out your interest in that currency.

## How can I change my payout Bank Account?

You can only change it to another Bank Account in the same currency as your Payday earnings. To do so:

  1. Tap on your 👤 Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Scroll down and tap on **Rewards**

  4. Tap on “Paid to”

  5. Select the desired Bank Account

## Can I change my Payday day?

No, the weekday of your Payday is automatically assigned and cannot be changed.

## How can I disable bunq Payday? 

You can disable this feature by simply following the steps below:

  1. Tap on your 👤 Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️Settings

  3. Scroll down and tap on **Rewards**

  4. Disable the toggle **bunq Payday**

 _Note: Disabling bunq Payday means you will stop receiving MassInterest._

## Can I check my past bunq Paydays? 

Yes, Payday notifications are incoming payments and always stay in your Bank Account payment history!

If you’d like to revisit your Payday from a specific week to see the breakdown again, you can simply do so by following the steps below:

  1. Open your 👤 Profile

  2. Go to 🏡 Home

  3. Search for “bunq” in the **Search Bar**

  4. Tap on the incoming payment from bunq on the Payday date you want to check out

  5. Check out your Payday summary here ✨
