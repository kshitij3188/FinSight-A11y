# What is bunq.me and how do I create my own personal payment link?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link
**Topics:** Payments

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

bunq.me is the easiest way to get your money back: it works with any bank, and you can set your link up in seconds. 

You can even share your link as many times as you’d like through WhatsApp or other messaging apps – it never expires!

### What is a bunq.me link?

A[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link is a personalized link which allows anyone to send money to your Bank Account, simply by scanning the QR code or tapping on the link. 

After creating your bunq.me link, we'll set up your very own bunq.me page. When you share your link or someone scans your QR code, they’ll land on your bunq.me page. Here, they can:

  * **Enter any amount** they want to send you.

  * **Add a description** so you both know what the payment is for.

  * **Select their favorite way to pay** , like iDEAL or a credit card transfer.

To make sure your friends know it's you they're sending money to, your bunq.me page will display your full legal name, as you set it up in your Profile.

## How do I create my bunq.me link?

To create your bunq.me link,[ click here](<https://www.bunq.com/link/create-bunq-me>) or follow the steps below:

  1. Go to **Your Profile**

  2. Tap on the **Request Your Money Back** banner at the top of your screen 

  3. Choose your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link name

  4. Tap on **Create**

  5. Select the Bank Account you’d like the incoming money to be deposited into 

  6. Your link is live and ready for use! It’ll appear as a QR code in Your Profile and you can share it by sending it via WhatsApp or other messaging applications. 

You can also create a [bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link by selecting any of your Bank Accounts and tapping on **Create a**[**bunq.me**](<http://bunq.me/>)**Link** right below the icon. 

## How do I deactivate my bunq.me link?

  1. Go to Your Profile 

  2. Tap on your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link below your QR code

  3. Select the[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link you’d like to deactivate 

  4. Tap on **Deactivate This Link**

If you want to deactivate an old bunq.me link created with an IBAN from a different bank in the past, contact our support team to do that for you 🙌

## Can I create multiple[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) links?

Absolutely, you can continue to budget in a breeze with multiple[ bunq.me](<https://www.bunq.com/personal-account/banking-features/payments>) links. Create up to 10 personalized and shareable[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) links and connect them to different Bank Accounts: one[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link per Bank Account. 

To create an additional[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link, simply: 

  1. Go to **Your Profile**

  2. Tap on the **Request Your Money Back** banner at the top of your screen 

  3. Tap on **Create**[**bunq.me**](<http://bunq.me/>)**Link**

  4. Choose your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link name

  5. Tap on **Create**

  6. Select the Bank Account you’d like the incoming money to be deposited into 

  7. Your link is live and ready for use! 

**Tip:** This makes crowdfunding multiple projects or requesting money to different monetary accounts truly simple. 

## How do I edit my[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link?

Looking to edit the name or the bank account connected to your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link? You can edit your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link, straight from the bunq app, in a few, easy steps! 

To edit your bunq.me link, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to Your Profile

  2. Tap on the bunq.me link below your bunq.me QR code

  3. Select the bunq.me link you’d like to edit 

  4. Tap on **Change My Link** or **Personalize Page** to edit your[ bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) link

## How can I check if my bunq.me request has been paid?****

To check whether your [bunq.me](<http://bunq.me/>) request has already been paid, you can simply: 

  1. Go to your Home Tab 

  2. Tap on **See All** under your Recent Payments 

  3. Select Requests 

  4. Search and find the payment. Pro-tip: use the Transaction Search to help you find it faster! 

  5. If the request has been paid, you’ll see a breakdown of when it was paid out and by whom. 

## What's the difference between bunq.me and bunq.to?

While **bunq.me** is your personal payment link, designed for receiving money, **bunq.to** is a way for you to send money to someone without needing their bank details, like an IBAN. To send money with bunq.to, all you need is the phone number or email address of the person you'd like to send money to.

Think about it this way: **bunq.me** is for getting paid, and **bunq.to** is for sending money. Both easy, secure, and make managing money a whole lot simpler ✨

## Good to know

**Payment Practicalities:**

  * We only accept Visa and MasterCard for card payments via bunq.me

  * bunq.me payments over €2000 can't be paid with iDEAL

  * bunq.me works with all banks. Is someone trying to pay you through your bunq.me link and is this payment not going through? Then there's likely an issue with the payment at the bank of the sender. We recommend contacting the other bank to help them solve this issue for you. Learn more[ here.](<https://together.bunq.com/d/55070>)

**Naming Conventions:**

  * As long as a **bunq.me** name is not currently in use, you can get it right away 🙌

  * You can't disable or edit the possible payment methods of your bunq.me link or bunq request.

  * Keep in mind that if you disable your **bunq.me** link and want to enable it again later, another user might have set it as their own **bunq.me** link while you were away, making it unavailable for you 👈

  * Special characters aren't allowed.
