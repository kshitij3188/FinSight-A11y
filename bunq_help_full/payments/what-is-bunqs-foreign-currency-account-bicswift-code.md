# What is bunq's Foreign Currency Account BIC/SWIFT code?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqs-foreign-currency-account-bicswift-code
**Topics:** Travel, Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

If you’re sending money from an international account to a bunq account, you’ll need a **BIC (Bank Identifier Code)** , also known as a **SWIFT code**. This code helps banks outside the SEPA region recognize that you're making an international transfer to a bunq account.

#### The BIC/SWIFT code for our IBANs are as follows:

  * **NL 🇳🇱 IBANs = BUNQNL2AXXX**

  * **DE 🇩🇪 IBANs = BUNQDE82XXX**

  * **FR 🇫🇷 IBANs = BUNQFRP2XXX**

  * **ES 🇪🇸 IBANs = BUNQESM2XXX**

  * **IE 🇮🇪 IBANs = BUNQIE22XXX**

  * **UK 🇬🇧 IBANs = TCCLGB31XXX**

#### Your Foreign Currency Account's BIC/SWIFT code is:

  * TCCLGB3LXXX

## Where can I find bunq's BIC/SWIFT code?

  1. Go to the 🏠 **Home tab**

  2. Tap on a **Bank Account**

  3. **BIC/SWIFT code** will be displayed below your **IBAN**

## How do I find my IBAN?

  1. Go to the 🏠 **Home tab**

  2. Select a **Bank Accounts**

  3. The **IBAN** will be shown in the middle of the screen. Here’s how IBANs are formatted for each country:

  * **NL xx BUNQ xxxx xxxx xx**

  * **DE xx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx**

  * **FR xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx x**

  * **ES xx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx**

  * **IE xx BUNQ xxxx xxxx xxxx xx**

  * **GB xx TCCL xxxx xxxx xxxx xx**

You can also tap on the **IBAN** to easily save it or share it. This is located above the **BIC/SWIFT code** in the app. Check out [this topic](<./how-do-i-find-my-iban>) to learn more!
