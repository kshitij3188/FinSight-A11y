# What's the difference between bunq.me and bunq.to?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-s-the-difference-between-bunq.me-and-bunq.to

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

At bunq, we're all about making your life easy. When it comes to moving money, we've got two simple tools to do just that: [**bunq.me** for getting paid](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunqme-and-how-do-i-create-my-own-personal-payment-link>), and [**bunq.to** for sending money](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-send-money-to-my-phone-contacts-with-bunqto-link>). Here’s how you can use them to make managing your payments and requests a whole lot simpler.

### Get paid back with bunq.me 💰

Ever needed to split a bill with friends or get paid for a freelance gig? **bunq.me** is your personal payment link that makes getting your money effortless. Just create your unique link and share it with anyone, anywhere.

**Use it to:**

  * **Split costs effortlessly.** From that pizza night with friends to your shared household expenses. Just share the link and they can pay you back instantly.

  * **Collect money for a group gift.** Planning a surprise for a colleague or friend? You can gather all the contributions in one place, hassle-free.

  * **Invoice your clients like a pro.** If you've got a side-hustle, you can give your clients an incredibly simple way to pay you with iDEAL, SOFORT, or card.

No more awkward reminders or sharing long IBANs. Just send your link and you're all set! 

### Send money with bunq.to 💸

Ever wanted to pay someone back but got stuck asking for their bank details? You can send money to someone when you don't have their IBAN with bunq.to — all you need is the receiver's phone number or email address. 

**Here’s how it works:**

  1. You send a payment to your friend's phone or email.

  2. They get a secure link.

  3. They enter their own IBAN and the money is on its way to their account!

The payment link is valid for 5 days, so they have plenty of time to claim their cash. It's the fastest and easiest way to send money to someone when you don't have all their details.
