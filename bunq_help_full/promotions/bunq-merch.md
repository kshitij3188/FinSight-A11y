**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/bunq-merch

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Turn your bunq Points into exclusive rewards - get a bunq cap, bag, or t-shirt.

### Important Update on bunq Points and Merch 

We’re introducing a simpler, more exciting way to get rewards for your everyday spending with the new Wheel of Fortune. To make room for the new reward system, we’re phasing out the ability to earn new bunq Points. Here’s what this means for your bunq Points and Merch:

  * **Already a bunqer?**

You can keep earning bunq Points until April 13, 2026. You’ll then have one full year, until April 13, 2027 to spend all your earned points on rewards, including bunq Merch. After this date, your bunq Points will expire.

  * **Joining bunq after February 12, 2026?**

You’ll be able to use the new Wheel of Fortune straight away. This means you won’t receive bunq Points on your payments, and as such won’t be able to redeem your Points for bunq Merch. 

You can learn more about the Wheel of Fortune [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/win-your-payments-back>). 

If you’ve still got a stash of bunq Points which you’d like to redeem for our bunq Merch, keep on reading to find out how you can get your bunq-branded merch.

### How can I order bunq Merch?

  1. Go to your 💳Cards tab 

  2. Tap on **Points**

  3. Browse through your rewards under “Rewards”

  4. Tap on bunq Merch item of your choice

  5. Confirm your shipping address or change it accordingly

## When will I receive my bunq Merch?

You’ll receive an email from our shipping partner once your merch is on its way. It typically takes between 1 and 2 weeks for your merch to arrive.

## Can I get a refund and return the merch?

Returns and refunds are not available for bunq Merch items.
