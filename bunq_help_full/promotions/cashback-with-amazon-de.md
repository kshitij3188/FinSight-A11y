# Cashback with Amazon.DE

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/cashback-with-amazon-de
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

Hey bunqer 🌈

Sign up to bunq in Germany and earn up to €40 in Cashback on your next 2 payments at [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>). Pay directly with your bunq card at checkout to qualify. You’ll get up to €20 Cashback per payment, with a minimum order value of €20 per payment.

This offer is available on bunq personal plans in Germany: Free, Core, Pro, and Elite XL. It applies to payments made at [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>) with your bunq card.

## How to get your Amazon.de Cashback

Follow these steps to make sure you earn your Cashback:

  1. You must sign up to bunq on a personal plan in Germany via this [**link**](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/1bunqxamazon40>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Use your physical or virtual bunq card to pay directly at checkout on [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>).

  4. Make up to 2 eligible payments of at least €20 each.

  5. We’ll add your Cashback automatically after your eligible payment is completed (up to €20 per payment, €40 total). You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. For more information on this, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

**Important:** Pay with your bunq card directly at Amazon checkout. Don’t select PayPal, bank transfer, or other payment methods—these won’t qualify.

## Who’s eligible and where it works

  * Country: Germany

  * Store: [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>) (other Amazon country sites don’t qualify)

  * Plans: All plans

  * Accounts: Personal accounts only (business plans aren’t eligible)

## How much Cashback do I get?

  * Total: up to €40 in Cashback

  * Per payment: up to €20

  * Number of payments: up to 2

  * Minimum order value: €20 per payment

You are only eligible to receive all cashbacks until 1 month after signup.

## When do I get the Cashback?

We’ll add the Cashback to your bunq account after your eligible payment is completed by Amazon. It’s automatic—no need to submit anything.

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## Terms and conditions

To keep it clear and fair, these rules apply:

  * Make your purchase at [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>) and pay directly with your bunq card.

  * Minimum order value per eligible payment: €20.

  * Cashback is capped at €20 per eligible payment, up to 2 payments (€40 total) per eligible user.

  * Available for personal bunq users in Germany on Free, Core, Pro, and Elite XL plans.

  * **Canceled or returned orders won’t earn Cashback. If a payment is refunded, the related Cashback will be reversed.**

  * **Payments via third-party services (for example, PayPal or bank transfer) don’t qualify.**

  * The offer applies when you sign up to bunq and make eligible payments as described above.

  * We may review eligibility and update these terms to prevent misuse and keep things fair.

  * You can claim both cashbacks until December 12th 2025 AMS?

## Didn’t get your Cashback?

If you don’t see your Cashback after an eligible payment:

  * Check that you paid at [www.amazon.de](<http://www.amazon.de/>).

  * Make sure you paid directly with your bunq card (not PayPal, bank transfer, or other methods).

  * Confirm your order value was at least €20.

  * See if you’ve already reached the limit: €20 per payment and 2 payments in total.

  * Ensure your account is a personal plan in Germany.

  * Check whether the order was canceled, returned, or refunded.

  * Cashback is paid out after you've made an eligible payment, on the next bunq Pay day - Check your account settings to see when your bunq Payday is, more information can be found [**here**](<./what-is-bunq-payday>)**.**

If everything looks right, contact us through the app and we’ll take a look.

## Useful tips

  * You can use either your physical bunq card or your virtual card at checkout—both qualify when used directly.

  * Split your purchases if helpful: 2 separate eligible payments can get you the full €40 (€20 + €20).
