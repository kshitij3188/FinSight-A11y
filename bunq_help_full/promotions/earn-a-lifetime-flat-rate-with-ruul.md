# Earn a lifetime flat rate with Ruul 💻

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-a-lifetime-flat-rate-with-ruul

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Whether you're a freelancer, contractor, or digital nomad, invoicing clients across borders can be a pain. That’s where **Ruul** comes in — and now, **bunq users get an exclusive lifetime commission rate of just 3%** on all Ruul transactions! 🎉

Normally, Ruul charges a commission of over 5% (like 6.5% for payments from the US to NL), but thanks to our partnership, bunq users enjoy a significantly lower flat rate — **forever**. No subscription fees, just more money in your pocket.

### How does it work?

  1. Go to ruul.io/bunq and sign up via the dedicated partnership link.

  2. Link your **bunq account** as your payout method.

  3. That’s it! The **3% lifetime rate** will be automatically applied to your account.  
Whether you’re already using bunq or just getting started, the offer is open to all eligible users who sign up through the [partnership page](<https://ruul.io/bunq>). Easy as that.

## Who's eligible? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You’re using a bunq Business plan.

  * This offer applies to both new and existing Ruul users, as long as you sign up via the bunq link and connect your bunq account.

## What is Ruul, excatly? 

Ruul is an external partner of bunq that helps independent professionals invoice clients globally — without needing to register a business. It’s fast, compliant, and super freelancer-friendly.

## Ruul FAQs

#### 1\. Do I need to be a bunq user to partner with Ruul?

No, but you’ll only get the 3% lifetime rate if you use bunq and sign up through our [partnership link](<https://ruul.io/bunq>).

#### 2\. How much does Ruul cost?

No subscriptions - just a commission on each transaction. bunq users get a discounted 3% rate for life. Check out [Ruul’s Pricing](<https://ruul.io/pricing/>) for the most up-to-date details.

#### 3\. What if I sell both services and digital products?

That’s fine—Ruul is built to handle both services and digital goods as a reseller, all under one platform.

#### 4\. Is it safe?

Absolutely. Ruul employs robust fraud detection, data encryption, and adheres to strict compliance standards. Pair that with bunq’s rock-solid security, and you’re in good hands.

#### 5\. How do I start?

Go to ruul.io/bunq and click one of the sign-up links, create an account, and link your bunq IBAN. The discount is applied automatically
