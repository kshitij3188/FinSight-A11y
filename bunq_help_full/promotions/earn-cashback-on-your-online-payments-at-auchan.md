# Earn Cashback on your online payments at Auchan

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-on-your-online-payments-at-auchan
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, France 🇫🇷

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

All new 🇫🇷 French users who sign up from March 23, 2026 get €10 Cashback on their next 3 online payments at [Auchan.fr](<http://auchan.fr/>) when paying with their bunq Card (digital or physical).

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Auchan sign-up link

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Make a minimum online payment of €20 at [Auchan.fr](<http://auchan.fr/>)

  4. Earn €10 Cashback on each of your next 3 payments, received with your next weekly Payday in your bunq account

**Please note:** This Cashback can be earned once **every 7 days**.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇫🇷 France

  * Register through the Auchan sign-up link

  * Are 18 years old or older

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €20 within 90 days of your sign-up

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of [the available bunq plans](<./what-plans-are-available>). 

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check out [this page](<./what-is-bunq-payday>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Auchan.fr in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the**💳 Cards Tab**

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. Your earned Cashback will be displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (digital or physical) in order to receive the Cashback. Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
