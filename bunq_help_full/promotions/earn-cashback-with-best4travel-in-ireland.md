# Earn Cashback with Best4Travel in Ireland 🇮🇪

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-best4travel-in-ireland
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €50 Cashback by signing up with us through Best4Travel Agency!

All new 🇮🇪Irish users who sign up between August 15, 2025 to November 15, 2025 get €50 Cashback on their next Best4Travel deal when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through Best4Travel’s [link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/best4travel>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Make your first travel purchase of a minimum value of €300

  4. Earn €50 Cashback on your payment, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇮🇪Ireland

  * Register through this [sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/best4travel>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between July 30, 2025 to October 30, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €300

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Best4Travel Agency in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## What if I haven't received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
