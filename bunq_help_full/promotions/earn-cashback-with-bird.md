# Earn Cashback with Bird 🚲

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-bird
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €30 in Cashback by signing up with us through Bird!

All new 🇪🇸 **Spanish users** who sign up between **May 1st and September 30th, 2025** get **€30 Cashback** on a total of **10 Bird rides** when they pay with their **bunq Credit Card** (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the **Bird promotion link.** All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Pay for your Bird ride

  4. Earn **€3 Cashback per ride** up to 10 trips, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

To get this promotion, you must meet the following conditions:

  * You live in 🇪🇸Spain;

  * Register through **Bird’s promotion link** ;

  * Be **18 years old or older** ;

  * Sign up between **May 1st and September 30th (inclusive), 2025** ;

  * Ride a Bird with a **minimum value of €2.99**

  * Have a **verified Personal account** with any of the available bunq plans.

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback on your next weekly Payday. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check this topic.

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Bird in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven’t received my Cashback. What now?

The payment must be made directly with your **bunq Credit Card** (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
