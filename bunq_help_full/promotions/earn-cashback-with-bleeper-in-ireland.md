# Earn Cashback with Bleeper in Ireland 🚲

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-bleeper-in-ireland
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, Ireland 🇮🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

From February 28, 2025 to August 31, 2025, new 🇮🇪 Irish users can save up to €20 on Bleeper rides when paying with their bunq Credit or Debit Card (virtual or physical).

#### How to claim your Cashback?

  1. Sign up through Bleeper;

  2. Link your bunq card to your Bleeper account and set it as your payment method;

  3. Ensure your bunq Bank Account linked to that card has sufficient funds;

  4. Take 4 Bleeper rides or top up your account at the minimum amount of €5;

  5. Earn €5 Cashback per ride or top up on your next weekly bunq Payday 🚴

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You must live in Ireland;

  * Only new bunq users who sign up for a Personal account using the link are eligible;

  * Promotion is available for users aged 18 and above;

  * Available for all bunq plans.

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive your earned Cashback per ride on your next weekly Payday after meeting the eligibility criteria. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<./what-is-bunq-payday>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Bleeper in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven’t received my Cashback. What now?

  * The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

  * Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
