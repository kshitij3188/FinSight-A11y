# Earn Cashback with Broke and Abroad 🏝️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-broke-and-abroad
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

Hey bunqers 🌈,

Get up to €30 Cashback by signing up with us through Broke and Abroad!

All new 🇫🇷French users who sign up between June 27, 2025, to December 31, 2025, get €10 Cashback on their next three months of Broke and Abroad’s Bro’Kit subscription when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the link shared by Broke and Abroad. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Pay for your Bro’Kit order.

  4. Earn €10 Cashback on each of your next 3 subscription payments, received with your next weekly Payday 🏝️

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in France

  * Register through the Broke and Abroad link

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between June 27, 2025 and December 31, 2025.

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €10

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Broke and Abroad in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
