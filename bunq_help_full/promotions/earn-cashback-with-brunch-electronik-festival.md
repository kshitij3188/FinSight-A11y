# Earn Cashback with Brunch Electronik Festival 🎉

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-brunch-electronik-festival
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €30 Cashback by signing up with us through Brunch Electronik Festival!

All new 🇪🇸Spanish users who sign up between June 7 to August 10, 2025, get €30 Cashback on their Brunch Electronik Festival tickets when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Brunch Electronik Festival promotion link. 

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Spend at least €29 on BRUNCH Electronik Festival tickets

  4. Earn €30 Cashback, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in Spain

  * Register through Brunch Electronik Festival

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between June 7 and August 10, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €29

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Brunch Electronik Festival in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven't received my Cashback. What now?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
