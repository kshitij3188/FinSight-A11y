# Earn Cashback with bunq Thursdays in Ireland 🍺

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-bunq-thursdays-in-ireland
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

From May 21, 2025 to May 21, 2026, new 🇮🇪Irish users who signed up for bunq through one of our **partnering pubs’ offers** can **save up to €8 on their next 6 payments** _at any of our partnering pubs on Thursdays when paying with their bunq Credit or Debit Card (virtual or physical)._ Limit of 2 transactions per Thursday

### How to claim your Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through one of our partnering pubs;

  2. Ensure your bunq Bank Account linked to that card has sufficient funds;

  3. Visit one of the partnering pubs and pay with your bunq card;

  4. Earn up to €8 Cashback on your next 6 pub payments, paid out on your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You must live in Ireland;

  * Only new bunq users who **sign up for a Personal account using the link** are eligible;

  * Promotion is available for users aged 18 and above;

  * Available for all bunq plans.

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the **Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday**. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://www.bunq.com/articles/what-is-bunq-payday>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to one of our partnering pubs in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your **bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical)**. Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
