# Earn Cashback with Cooltra 🛵

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-cooltra
**Topics:** Spain 🇪🇸, Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €20 Cashback by signing up with us through Cooltra!

All new 🇪🇸Spanish users who sign up between July 7 to August 31, 2025, get up to €20 Cashback on their next ten Cooltra rides when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Cooltra [link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/cooltra>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Book your ride with a minimum payment of €2.50

  4. Earn €2 Cashback on each of your next ten rides, received with your next weekly Payday. 

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇪🇸Spain

  * Register through the [Cooltra link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/cooltra>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between July 7 and August 31, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €2.50

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Cooltra in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
