# Earn Cashback with Freenow 🚕

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-freenow-%F0%9F%9A%95
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €50 Cashback by signing up with us through Freenow 🚕

All new users living in 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇪🇸 Spain and 🇮🇪 Ireland who sign up between December 29, 2025 to March 15, 2026 get €10 Cashback on their next 5 Freenow rides when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through Freenow’s [sign up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/Freenow>)

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Book a ride with Freenow, for a minimum amount of €10

  4. Earn €10 Cashback on each of your next 5 bookings, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in Germany, Spain or Ireland

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between December 29, 2025 to March 15, 2026

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €10

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of [the available bunq plans](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-plans-are-available>)

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check this [page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060.>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Freenow in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
