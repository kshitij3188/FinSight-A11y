# Earn Cashback with Frichti at Work 🥗

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-frichti-at-work
**Topics:** France 🇫🇷, Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €30 Cashback by signing up with us through Frichti at Work!

All new 🇫🇷French users who sign up between June 16 and October 31, 2025 get €5 Cashback on their next 6 Frichti at Work orders when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the QR code on Frichti at Work leaflets. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Pay for your Frichti at Work meal of at least €5 using your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical)

  4. Earn €5 Cashback on each of your next 6 orders, received with your next weekly Payday

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇫🇷France

  * Register through the Frichti at Work QR code

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between June 16 and October 31, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €5

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Frichti at Work in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven't received my Cashback. What now? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
