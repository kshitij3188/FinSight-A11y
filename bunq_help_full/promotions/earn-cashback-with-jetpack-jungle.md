# Earn Cashback with Jetpack Jungle 🎥

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-jetpack-jungle
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €50 Cashback by signing up with us through Jetpack Jungle!

All new 🇮🇪Irish users who sign up between August 8, 2025 to November 8, 2025 get €50 Cashback on their purchase from Jetpack Jungle when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Jetpack Jungle [signup link](<https://app.adjust.com/1rd9x8fj?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.bunq.com%2Finvite%2Fpartner%2Fjetpackjunglexbunq>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Make an order from Jetpack Jungle of a minimum amount of €50

  4. Earn €50 Cashback, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

To be eligible for this promotion, you must: :

  * Live in Ireland

  * Register through [signup link](<https://app.adjust.com/1rd9x8fj?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.bunq.com%2Finvite%2Fpartner%2Fjetpackjunglexbunq>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between August 8, 2025 to November 8, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €50

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Jetpack Jungle in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
