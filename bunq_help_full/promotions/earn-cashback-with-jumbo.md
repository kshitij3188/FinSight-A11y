# Earn cashback with Jumbo 🇳🇱

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-jumbo
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €40 Cashback by signing up with us through Jumbo! 

All new 🇳🇱Dutch users who sign up through the promotion link get €10 Cashback on their next 4 purchases when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Jumbo sign-up link. All you need is your phone and ID🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Make an in-store or online purchase at Jumbo with a minimum value of €20

  4. Earn €10 Cashback on each of your next 4 payments, received with your next weekly Payday.

Please note: This Cashback can be earned once every 7 days. 

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇳🇱the Netherlands

  * Register through the Jumbo sign-up link

  * Be 25 years old or older

  * Make an eligible payment with a minimum value of €20 within 2 month after your sign-up

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>). 

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Jumbo in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.

## Signed up between November 25, 2025 and December 15, 2025?

Great news! If you joined bunq using the Jumbo link during this period, your Cashback reward is locked in for two months after your sign up. Simply make a qualifying purchase and get your Cashback with your next Payday. The specific details of your offer are waiting for you in your bunq app, including how many eligible purchases you have left and the Cashback reward amount. 

Here’s a quick reminder of the conditions which must be met to claim your reward: 

  * Make 2 separate payments at Jumbo.

  * Ensure each payment has a minimum value of €25.

  * Complete these purchases within 2 months of your sign-up date.

  * Pay directly with your bunq Card (using Apple Pay, Google Pay, or the physical card).

  * Select "Pay upon delivery" if you're ordering online.

  * Have a verified Personal bunq account.

Just remember, you have two months from the day you signed up to make your qualifying purchases and claim your reward. Happy shopping!
