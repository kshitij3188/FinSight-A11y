# Earn Cashback with Just Eat Takeaway 🍽️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-just-eat-takeaway
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

From May 28 until July 11, 2025 all new 🇳🇱 Dutch and 🇪🇸 Spanish users can receive up to €45 Cashback on their first 3 orders from Thuisbezorgd (in the Netherlands) and Just Eat Takeaway(in Spain) when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

From June 2 until July 16, 2025, all new 🇮🇪 Irish users also receive up to €45 Cashback on their first 3 orders from Just Eat Takeaway when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for a bunq account through the Thuisbezorgd or Just Eat Takeaway’s post-order banner link;

  2. Add money to your account;

  3. Place an order of minimum €15 from Thuisbezorgd or Just Eat Takeaway with your bunq card;

  4. Earn €15 Cashback per order on your next 3 orders, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You are in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands, 🇪🇸 Spain, or 🇮🇪 Ireland when placing your order;

  * Register for bunq with the Thuisbezorgd or Just Eat Takeaway’s post-order banner link;

  * Sign up between May 28 and July 11, 2025 if you are in the Netherlands or in Spain;

  * Sign up between June 2 and July 16, 2025 if you are in Ireland;

  * You are 18 years old or older;

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

  * You make the payment directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical)*

_ **Please note** : Cashback won’t be paid out for payments made via PayPal, Curve, iDEAL, or Klarna_

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

_**Note:** Make sure your Payday feature is enabled so you can successfully receive your Cashback._

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Just Eat Takeaway in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here
