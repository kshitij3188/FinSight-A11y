# Earn Cashback with Kleinanzeigen 💻

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-kleinanzeigen
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

All new 🇩🇪 German users who sign up between November 25, 2025 and December 30, 2025 get up to €50 Cashback on qualifying purchases at Kleinanzeigen when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [dedicated sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/KleinanzeigenA>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Make qualifying payments to Kleinanzeigen

  4. Earn Cashback on your payments, received with your next weekly Payday

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in Germany

  * Register through[ dedicated sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/KleinanzeigenA>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between November 25, 2025 and December 30, 2025

  * Make an eligible payment with the minimum value displayed in your app

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>)

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Kleinanzeigen in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
