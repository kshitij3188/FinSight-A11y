# Earn Cashback with Lidl in the Netherlands and Germany

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-lidl

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to **€40 Cashback** by signing up with us through Lidl! 

All new 🇳🇱Dutch and 🇩🇪 German users get €10 Cashback on their next 4 Lidl payments when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Lidl sign-up link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Go grocery shopping and make an in-store or official online webshop purchase at Lidl, with a minimum value of €20

  4. Earn €10 Cashback on your next 4 payments, received with your weekly Payday.

**Please note: This Cashback can be earned once every 7 days. If you make multiple purchases at Lidl in the same week, only your first purchase will be eligible for the Cashback.**

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands or 🇩🇪Germany

  * Register through the Lidl sign-up link

  * Be **18 years old or older**

  * Make an eligible payment within 90 days of your sign up

  * You make a payment with your bunq Card, with a minimum value of €20

  * You do your groceries at the right store: pay in a Dutch Lidl if you’re living in the Netherlands, and in a German Lidl if you’re living in Germany. 

  * Have a **verified** Personal account with any of the[ available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check[ this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Lidl in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via iDEAL, PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
