# Earn Cashback with MSC Cruceros 🛥️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-msc-cruceros
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

**Get a €100 Cashback by signing up with us through MSC Cruceros!**

All new 🇪🇸Spanish users who sign up between May 12 and December 31, 2025 receive a one-time Cashback by booking a MSC Cruceros cruise with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the MSC Cruceros promotion link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Book a MSC Cruceros cruise of a minimum €300 using your bunq card (virtual or physical)

  4. Earn €100 Cashback, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇪🇸Spain;

  * Register through the MSC Cruceros promotion link;

  * Be 18 years old or older;

  * You make a minimum payment of €300 to MSC Cruceros

  * Sign up between May 12 to December 31, 2025

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the available bunq plans.

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check this topic.

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to MSC Cruceros in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven’t received my Cashback. What now?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
