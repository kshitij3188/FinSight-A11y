# Earn Cashback with Playground Berlin ⚽

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-playground-berlin
**Topics:** Germany 🇩🇪, Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to **€25 Cashback** by signing up with us through **Playground Berlin**!

All new German users who sign up between May 27 and September 30, 2025, get €25 Cashback on their 5x5 turf booking when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through Playground Berlin. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Set your bunq card as the payment method in your Playground Berlin account.

  3. Add money to your account.

  4. Make a 5x5 turf booking of at least €50.

  5. Earn a one-time €25 Cashback, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in Germany

  * Register through Playground Berlin

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between 31 May to 30 September, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €50

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Playground Berlin in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven't received my Cashback. What now? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
