# Earn Cashback with Quiksilver 🏄

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-quiksilver
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €20 Cashback by signing up with us through Quiksilver!

All new users who sign up between September 20 to September 30, 2025 get €20 Cashback on their next Quiksilver purchase when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [Quiksilver sign up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/quiksilver>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Make a purchase on the Quiksilver website or in-store with your bunq card.

  4. Earn €20 Cashback on your next payments, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promowtion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in a EEA country 🇪🇺

  * Register through the [Quiksilver sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/quiksilver>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up until September 29, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €20

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Quiksilver in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
