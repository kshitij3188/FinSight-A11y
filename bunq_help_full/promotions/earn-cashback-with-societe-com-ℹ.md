# Earn Cashback with Societe.com ℹ️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-societe-com-%E2%84%B9
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to**€50 Cashback** by signing up with us through Societe.com! 

All new French users who sign up for a Business Account through the [Societe.com sign up link ](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/referral/b40ef550-02e3-4f38-8790-b320e7f857bf>)from November 27, 2025 can get €50 Cashback on their Personal Account when their Business Account gets verified. 

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for a bunq Business Account through the sign up link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Make sure your Business Account is verified Add money to your account.

  3. Earn €50 Cashback, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇫🇷 France

  * Register through the [Societe.com sign up link ](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/referral/b40ef550-02e3-4f38-8790-b320e7f857bf>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up from November 27, 2025

  * Have a verified Business account with any of the [available bunq plans ](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>)

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check this [page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060.>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Glovo in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
