# Earn Cashback with Spar University 🎓

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-spar-university
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €20 Cashback by signing up with us through Spar University! 

All new 🇳🇱Dutch users who sign up between August 25, 2025 to September 21, 2025, get €5 Cashback on their four next purchases from Spar University when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [Spar University registration link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/Sparcashback>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Make a minimum purchase of €4.99

  4. Earn €5 Cashback on each of your next four payments], received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in the Netherlands

  * Register through the registration link

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between August 25, 2025 to September 21, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €4.99

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback? 

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Spar University in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven't received my Cashback. What now? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
