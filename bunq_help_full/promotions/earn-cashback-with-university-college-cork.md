# Earn Cashback with University College Cork 🇮🇪

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-university-college-cork
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, Ireland 🇮🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get €20 Cashback by signing up with us through University College Cork!

All new 🇮🇪Irish users who sign up between August 22, 2025 to September 18, 2025 can get €20 Cashback on the University College Cork’s Student Union Freshers Ball tickets when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/uccsufreshersxbunq>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Buy the Freshers Week Ball ticket at the Ball. 

  4. Earn €20 Cashback on your payment, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇮🇪Ireland

  * Register through the sign up link

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between before September 18, 2025

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €20 at the Freshers Week Ball

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to University College Cork in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven’t received my Cashback. What now?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
