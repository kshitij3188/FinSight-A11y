# Earn Cashback with Vinted in Ireland 👕

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-vinted-in-ireland
**Topics:** Ireland 🇮🇪, Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €40 Cashback by signing up with us through Vinted in Ireland via this signup link!

All new 🇮🇪Irish users who sign up between September 11, 2025 to December 31, 2025 get €20 Cashback on their next 2 orders from Vinted when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the sign up link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account.

  3. Place an order on Vinted.ie

  4. Earn €20 Cashback on each of your next 2 orders, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇮🇪Ireland

  * Register through the signup link

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between September 11, 2025 to December 31, 2025 

  * Make 2 payments, each with a minimum value of €20 before January 31, 2026

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Vinted.ie in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
