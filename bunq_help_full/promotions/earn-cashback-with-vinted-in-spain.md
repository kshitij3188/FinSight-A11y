# Earn Cashback with Vinted in Spain 👕

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-vinted-in-spain
**Topics:** Spain 🇪🇸, Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €30 in Cashback by signing up with us through Vinted!

All new 🇪🇸 Spanish users who sign up between June 6 and December 31, 2025 get €15 Cashback on their next 2 purchases when paying with their bunq Card (virtual or physical).

### How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Vinted promotion link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Make a Vinted order of at least €14.99 and pay with your bunq Card (virtual or physical).

  4. Earn €15 Cashback on each of your next 2 orders, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇪🇸Spain

  * Register through the signup link

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between June 6 and December 31, 2025

  * Make 2 payments at Vinted, each with a minimum value of €14.99 before January 31, 2026

  * Order on Vinted with a minimum value of €14.99

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Vinted in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
