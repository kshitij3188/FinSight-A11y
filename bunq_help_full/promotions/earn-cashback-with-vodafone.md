# Earn Cashback with Vodafone 📱

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-vodafone
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

From April 14, 2025 to June 14th, 2025, new 🇮🇪 Irish users who signed up for bunq through Vodafone can save €15 on their next 3 top ups when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical).

## How to claim your Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through a sign up link provided by Vodafone Happy Loyalty Program;

  2. Link your bunq card to your Vodafone account and set it as your payment method.

  3. Ensure your bunq Bank Account linked to that card has sufficient funds.

  4. Top up at least €20 with Vodafone and pay using your bunq card.

  5. Earn €15 Cashback on your next 3 top ups with your next weekly payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You must live in Ireland;

  * Only **new bunq users** who sign up for a Personal account using the link are eligible;

  * Promotion is available for users aged 18 and above;

  * Available for all bunq plans.

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<./what-is-bunq-payday>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Vodafone in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## I haven’t received my Cashback. What now?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
