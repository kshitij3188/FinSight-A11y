# Earn Cashback with Wallapop in Spain ☁️

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-wallapop-in-spain
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, Spain 🇪🇸

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €30 Cashback by signing up with us through Wallapop! 

From August 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025, all new 🇪🇸 Spanish users who sign up for bunq through the Wallapop app can get €15 Cashback on two Wallapop purchases when paying with their bunq Credit or Debit Card (virtual or physical)..

## How can I claim my Cashback?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Wallapop app. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Link your bunq card to your Wallapop account and set it as your payment method.

  3. Add money to your account.

  4. Make 2 payments to Wallapop before January 31, 2026 — with a minimum of €35 per payment.

  5. Earn €15 Cashback on each of your next two orders, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

To be eligible for this promotion, you must:

  * Live in 🇪🇸 Spain;

  * Register through the Wallapop App;

  * Be 18 years old or older;

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the **Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday**. More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<./what-is-bunq-payday>). 

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to Wallapop in your bunq app. **If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment** :

  1. Go to the Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment.

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). **Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.**
