# Earn Cashback with WotsOn Events 🇮🇪

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-cashback-with-wotson-events
**Topics:** Cashback Partners, Ireland 🇮🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get up to €20 Cashback by signing up with us through WotsOn Events!

All new 🇮🇪Irish users who sign up between September 30, 2025 to September 30, 2026, get up to €10 Cashback on their next 2 payments at WotsOn Events when paying with their bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical)

### How can I claim my Cashback?

Sign up for bunq through the [WotsOn Events signup link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/wotsoneventsxbunq>). All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  1. Add money to your account.

  2. Make a payment at WotsOn Events with a maximum amount of €10

  3. Earn Cashback on each of your next 2 payments up to €10, received with your next weekly Payday.

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇮🇪Ireland

  * Register through the [WotsOn Events signup link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/partner/wotsoneventsxbunq>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between September 30, 2025 to September 30, 2026

  * Make a payment with your bunq Card

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my Cashback payout? 

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to WotsOn Events in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback? 

The payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (virtual or physical). Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via PayPal, Curve, or Klarna.
