# Earn Cashback with your exclusive bunq x HYROX card

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When you first join bunq through a HYROX referral across 🇧🇪Belgium, 🇫🇷France, 🇩🇪Germany, 🇮🇪Ireland, 🇮🇹Italy, 🇳🇱the Netherlands, 🇵🇹Portugal or 🇪🇸Spain, you get a **bunq x HYROX Digital bunq Card** — straight in your bunq app. 

Our bunq x HYROX cards are packed with the following benefits: 

  * **Immediate availability** : your card is ready automatically when you open your bunq account. 

  * **Cashback** : get **10%** Cashback on eligible purchases at the online HYROX shop and participating on-site locations.

  * **Exclusive perks:** enjoy special benefits like fast track check-in for athletes at HYROX races, which we’ll be rolling out soon. 

### How can I get my bunq x HYROX Card? 

We’ve partnered with HYROX to create a **bunq x HYROX** Digital Card, engineered for elite performance. From January 21, 2026, you’ll see bunq QR codes across participating HYROX competitions or when making HYROX purchases from the official HYROX webshop. 

Here’s all you need to do to open your new account and get your new bunq x HYROX card:

  1. Scan the bunq QR code from official HYROX communications

  2. Add your phone number 

  3. Download the bunq app 

  4. Tap on **Get Started** and complete your sign-up, using the same phone number 

  5. Once your account has been verified, your brand new bunq x HYROX card is immediately set up and ready to go. You can find it in your Cards Tab. 

💡**Pro-tip:** You can immediately add your HYROX card to Apple or Google Pay. 

### Who’s the bunq x HYROX card available to? 

Currently, the bunq x HYROX card is available exclusively to new bunq users who signed up via the official QR codes across participating HYROX competitions and the official HYROX webshop.

### How can I claim cashback with my bunq x HYROX Card? 

To claim your Cashback: 

  1. Add money to your bunq account

  2. Make a purchase at the [HYROX Shop online](<https://hyrox.com/>) or at participating HYROX events using your HYROX card. 

  3. Pay using your bunq x **HYROX card**.

  4. Earn **10% Cashback** per purchase, received with your next weekly Payday. 

## Who's eligible for the bunq x HYROX cashback promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * Live in 🇧🇪Belgium, 🇫🇷France, 🇩🇪Germany, 🇮🇪Ireland, 🇮🇹Italy, 🇳🇱the Netherlands, 🇵🇹Portugal, 🇪🇸Spain

  * Make eligible payments with your bunq x HYROX card 

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between January 14, 2026 and December 31, 2026

  * Have a **verified** Personal account with any of the[ available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## When will I receive my cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday.

More information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check[ this page](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57060>).

## How do I see if I've earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can check your payment to HYROX in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the 💳 Cards tab

  2. Select the bunq x HYROX card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. If you’ve earned Cashback, you’ll see it displayed here

## Why haven't I received my Cashback?

The payment must be made directly with your digital bunq x HYROX card. Cashback won’t be paid for payments made via iDEAL, PayPal, Curve, or Klarna connected to your bunq account. 

## Important notes

  * Cashback is calculated on the **final amount paid** (after discounts and VAT)

  * Cashback is not granted on cancelled or refunded orders

  * bunq reserves the right to modify or terminate the promotion in case of fraud or abuse

  * The promotion duration can be extended or terminated according to bunq’s promotional terms 

  * [T&Cs apply ](<https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/lp/hyrox-card#terms>)

## Can I get a physical card? 

The bunq x HYROX card is digital but you can easily order a regular, personalized or metal physical bunq card if you’ve signed up for a bunq Core, Pro or Elite plan. Here’s how:

  1. Open your bunq app

  2. Go to the Cards tab

  3. Tap on Order a new card and pick your favorite design

And that’s it — your new card will be on its way. Learn more about expected delivery times for physical cards [here](<./when-will-my-card-arrive>).
