# Earn Interest with Amplifier Effect

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/earn-interest-with-amplifier-effect
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Earn up **€50 Welcome reward** with Amplifier Effect, a women growth community based in Berlin! 

From January 14, 2026 to May 1, 2026, all new users get **€50 Welcome reward** when they sign up through Amplifier Effect and deposit **€1,000** into their Savings Account. 

How can I claim my Welcome reward?

  1. Sign up for bunq through the [Amplifier Effect sign-up link](<https://web.bunq.com/invite/referral/3392dbe1-d2dd-43c8-98a8-6e00b6e2698c>)

  2. Once your account is verified, add at least **€1,000** into your Savings Account 

  3. Get your €50 reward automatically — straight into your Savings Account

## Who's eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * Add €1,000**** into your bunq Savings Account — and keep it in your Savings for 12 months*

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between January 14, 2026 to May 1, 2026 

  * Have a **verified** Personal account with any of the[ available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

*If your Savings Account balance drops below €1000, your reward will be automatically revoked.

You can read more about our Terms & Conditions - [here](<http://bunq.com/documents/rewards-terms-conditions>).

## When will I receive my Welcome reward?

Here’s when you can expect to get your reward: 

  * **In your Savings Account:** right away! You’ll receive the €50 Bonus Interest reward you earned **immediately** after you’ve made your Savings deposit. 

  * **To withdraw from your Savings Account** : after 1 year. You’ll be able to withdraw the €50 reward after you keep it in your Savings Account for a period of 12 months. 

## How do I see if I've got my reward?

To see if you’ve earned your reward you can check your incoming payments from bunq in your bunq app. Simply: 

  1. Go to your Savings Tab

  2. Select the Savings Account you deposited the money into 

  3. Check for incoming payments from bunq 

  4. Find the €50 reward from bunq

## Why haven't I received my reward?

In order to get your reward, you must have: 

  * An active bunq account 

  * Deposit €1,000 into a bunq Savings Account!
