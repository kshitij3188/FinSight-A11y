# EEA residents can earn up to 2.01% interest on savings

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/eea-residents-can-earn-up-to-201-interest-on-savings
**Topics:** Savings

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can count on us to always give you the best possible interest on your savings. 

Although the European Central Bank has lowered its interest rates recently, we are offering **a bonus interest rate of 2.01%*** to reward extra savings.

## MassInterest rate for all EEA residents

You can earn an annual bonus MassInterest on your savings in your Saving Account all year long when you save more than your personalized starting point, called a "threshold." This threshold is based on the highest amount you saved in your account over the past 6 months. It gets calculated twice a year - on January 1 and July 1.

_Please note: To start earning MassInterest, you must open a bunq Savings Account. You can open a Savings Account by following the steps outlined_[ _here_](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5142>) _._

#### Here’s how it works:

  * You earn an annual base interest rate, currently 1.51%*, on savings up to your threshold.

  * If you save more than your threshold, you’ll get the current annual bonus rate of 2.01%* on the extra amount.

If you didn’t have any savings in your Savings Account or if you've just joined bunq and opened a Savings Account during the 6-month period before the threshold calculation, your threshold will start at €0. This means you’ll receive the current annual bonus rate of 2.01% on all savings until the next calculation.

So, the more you add to your savings, the more interest you earn 🎉

 _**The base rate and the bonus rate are variable and are subject to change**._

## MassInterest calculation

*❗️MassInterest rates are variable and may change. We’ll always inform you in case of change.  
Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57095>).

The rest of the MassInterest terms remain the same:

  * Your interest will be paid out in weekly installments, so you can watch your savings grow faster each and every week!

  * You’ll get interest on deposits up to €100.000. For deposits over €100.000, no interest is earned.

  * All interest rates are annual (variable) and nominal rates.

  * All your savings are 100% protected **up to €100,000** per person by the Dutch Deposit Guarantee Scheme. Therefore, if you share a Joint (Savings) Account at bunq, the protection extends up to €200.000 in total.

  * There are no fees for withdrawing from your savings account.

  * You can make a maximum of 2 instant withdrawals per month from your Personal Savings Account(s). Learn more about this limit [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/5147>) 🙌

  * Regular Terms & Conditions apply to the new MassInterest rate _._
