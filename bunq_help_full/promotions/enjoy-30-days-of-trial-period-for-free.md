# Enjoy 30 days of trial period for free!

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/enjoy-30-days-of-trial-period-for-free
**Topics:** Signup

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

When you sign up for a new Personal or Business bunq account, you’ll automatically get a 30 days free trial of our [bunq Elite plan](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-are-the-benefits-of-the-bunq-elite-plan>). This way, you can explore and enjoy the full benefits of bunq, including our savings benefits, right from the start!

You can then easily choose any of our [other available plans](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-plans-are-available>) before or after the end of your trial period.

💡 Please note: Child Accounts get bunq Pro at no cost, and Company Directors get bunq Free.

## What happens when my trial period ends?

If you don’t change your plan before the trial period ends, it will automatically continue, and you’ll be charged accordingly. To change your plan, simply follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Select Plan

  3. Go to Switch Plan

  4. Switch to a plan of your choice

## Good to know

  * The trial period starts at the moment you sign up with us.
