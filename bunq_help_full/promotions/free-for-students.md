# Free for Students 🎓

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/free-for-students
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

University students in 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇮🇪 Ireland, 🇮🇹 Italy, 🇳🇱 the Netherlands, 🇵🇹 Portugal, and 🇪🇸 Spain can use: 

  * **bunq Pro** (worth €9.99/month) for free, or 

  * **bunq Elite**(worth €18.99/month) for just €9 per month. 

With bunq Pro or Elite, university students can enjoy up to 25 dedicated Bank Accounts to manage budgets and spend abroad at the real exchange rate.

## Who qualifies for the student discount?

You're eligible for a student discount at bunq if you're: 

  * A university student in 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇫🇷 France, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇮🇪 Ireland, 🇮🇹 Italy, 🇳🇱 the Netherlands, 🇵🇹 Portugal, and 🇪🇸 Spain

  * Are aged 25 and under 

  * Share a valid student ID card or an accepted proof of enrollment from your university

## How do I get the student discount?

If you're a university student between the ages of 18 and 26, your discount will be activated once you provide us with your valid student ID card or **proof of enrollment**. Once we accept and register you student status in our system, you're all set! 

Your discount will be applied on the first day of the **next** calendar month. So, if you're approved anytime in January, 2026, your discount will officially kick in on February 1, 2026. 

## What's an accepted proof of enrollment? 

We'll need **proof of enrollment** from your educational institution in **PDF format**. It should always be for the current academic year, and we won't be able to accept expired proof of enrollment documents. 

✅ Here are the accepted proof of enrollment documents, which you can upload securely through our Support Chat: 

  * Official enrollment certificate 

  * University-issued confirmation letter

  * Student registration statement

  * A digital certificate downloaded from your university's portal

  * Student ID cards 

## How do I provide my proof of enrollment?

To provide us with your **proof of enrollment** for the first time, [tap here](<https://go.bunq.com/link/student-enrollment>) or follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Scroll down and tap on **Plan**

  3. Select **Students get bunq Elite for just €9 per month** or **Bank for free as a student**

  4. Tap on **Upload Proof of Enrollment**

  5. Chat with Finn and upload your document

 _**Note** : This plan option will only be visible if you meet the residency and age requirements._

## Do I need to verify my student status again?

Yes, once a year, we're required to re-verify your student status by requesting a new **proof of enrollment**. This ensures the student discount is always applied to currently enrolled students.

We'll need **proof of enrollment** from your educational institution in **PDF format** which you can upload it securely through our Support Chat. Here's how you can do this:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Tap on **Support**

  3. Tap the **+** icon in the bottom left of the Support chat window

  4. Attach your proof of enrollment in **PDF format**

## Will the student discount work if I’m on another bunq plan or in a bunq Pack?

You need to be on **bunq Pro** or **bunq Elite** for the student discount to apply. If you're currently on a different bunq plan or part of a bunq Pack, the discount won't apply, even if you’ve provided us with **proof of enrollment**. If you're already student meeting the discount requirements, and want to benefit from the student discount, you can change your plan by following these steps:

  1. Tap on your **Profile** in the top left corner

  2. Scroll down until you see your name

  3. Tap on **Plan**

  4. Go to **Switch Plan**

  5. Choose **bunq Pro** or **bunq Elite**

## What will my costs be when I’m no longer a student or turn 26?

When you either complete your higher education or turn 26, the **bunq Pro** plan will cost €9.99 per month, and the **bunq Elite** plan will cost €18.99 per month.
