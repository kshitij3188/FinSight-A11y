# Get a €100 reward when you open a Business account with bunq 📂

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/get-100-when-you-open-a-business-account-with-bunq
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

All new 🇳🇱 Dutch users can get €100 when signing up for a bunq Business account through the QR code received in a letter from bunq. Learn how you can get the reward below — and let's get you started. 

## How can I claim my Cashback? 

  1. Scan the QR code in the bunq letter you received after registering your business with the KvK

  2. Sign up for a bunq Business account

  3. Once your account is verified, claim your €100 reward from the in‑app event.

_**Disclaimer** : This promotion is independently conducted by bunq and is in no way affiliated with or endorsed by the KvK._

## When can I claim the reward?

You can claim the reward as soon as your Business account is verified. The bunq app will send a notification inviting you to claim your €100 reward — simply follow the prompt in the app! 

If your account isn’t verified yet, complete the [verification](<./how-to-open-a-bunq-business-account>) first. 

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

  * You live in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands

  * Sign up for a bunq Business account through the QR code from your bunq letter

  * You're a new bunq user — you haven't had an active bunq account within the last 12 months

  * Have a verified Business account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

**Important:** You need to sign up through the QR and have a verified bunq Business account to receive the €100 reward. Personal accounts aren’t eligible.

## Why didn't I get the reward notification?

If you meet the criteria but don’t see the notification event to claim your reward:

  * Confirm you joined bunq using the **QR code on the letter you received.**

  * Check that your Business account is fully verified

  * Make sure your app is up to date

Still no event? Reach out through the app and our Support will help you out.
