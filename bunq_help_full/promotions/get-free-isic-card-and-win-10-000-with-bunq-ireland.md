  1. **How can I get a free ISIC card?**

Sign up for bunq through ISIC before October 31, 2025 and get your ISIC card at no cost when you activate your bunq account. The ISIC card is your key to over 150,000 discounts in 130+ countries, on everything from food and travel to tech and entertainment.

  

  2. **Can I still get a free ISIC card?**

This promotion **ended** on **October 31, 2025**. Therefore, new users who signed up for bunq after this date can’t obtain a free ISIC card. 

  

  3. **I have a question about this promotion — who can I contact?**
