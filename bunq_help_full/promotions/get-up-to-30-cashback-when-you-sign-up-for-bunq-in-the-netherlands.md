# Get up to €30 Cashback when you sign up for bunq in the Netherlands 🇳🇱

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/get-up-to-30-cashback-when-you-sign-up-for-bunq-in-the-netherlands
**Topics:** Cashback Partners

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get **up to €30 Cashback** , simply by signing up for bunq through one of the promotional links you see across your favorite online retailers, like ASOS, Ticketmaster, Wayfair, or Thuisbezorgd! 

All new 🇳🇱 Dutch users who sign up through the promotional links get €5 Cashback on their next 6 eligible payments when paying with their bunq Card (digital or physical). 

## How can I claim my Cashback?

Here’s how you can claim your Cashback: 

  1. Sign up for bunq through one of the eligible promotional links

  2. Add money to your account

  3. Make a payment of minimum €10 at any merchant with your bunq Card

  4. Get €5 Cashback on your payment — paid out to you with your next weekly Payday

You can earn your next €5 Cashback 7 days later. This promotion is valid for the first 90 days from the moment you sign up. 

## Who’s eligible for this promotion?

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands

  * Sign up for bunq through the promotional links shared by Rokt’s advertising partners, for example: ASOS, Ticketmaster, Wayfair, or Thuisbezorgd

  * Are 25 years old or older

  * Make a payment with a minimum value of €10 within 90 days of your sign-up

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of[ the available bunq plans](<./what-plans-are-available>)

## When will I receive my Cashback payout?

You’ll receive the Cashback you earned on your next weekly Payday. To find more information about what Payday is and how to check yours, check out[ this page](<./what-is-bunq-payday>).

## How do I see if I’ve earned Cashback?

To see if you’ve earned Cashback you can find and check your eligible payment in your bunq app. If you’ve earned Cashback, it will be displayed alongside the payment:

  1. Go to the**💳 Cards Tab**

  2. Select the card you used for the payment

  3. Find your payment and tap on it

  4. Your earned Cashback will be displayed here

## Why haven’t I received my Cashback?

This Cashback applies only to direct card payments for purchases. Your payment won’t be eligible for a Cashback if: 

  * Your payment was made via PayPal, Curve, Klarna, or iDEAL. An eligible payment must be made directly with your bunq Credit Card (digital or physical) in order to receive the Cashback. 

  * Your payment wasn’t a purchase. Financial services, money transfers, and gambling transactions are excluded.

  * Your payment isn’t completed. Cashback is only granted for settled transactions. If you get a refund for a purchase, the Cashback for that transaction will be reversed as well.
