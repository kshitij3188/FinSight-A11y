# How can I invite my friends to join bunq?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-invite-my-friends-to-join-bunq

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Know someone who needs a bank account? Give them a head start with bunq 🌈

When your friend joins bunq from your referral, you both receive rewards like a free Metal Card, extra bonus interest, or up to €100. Find out which rewards are available and how you can refer your friends below.

### How does this work?

We’re making it easier than ever to spread the love! You can invite your friends to enjoy all the benefits bunq has to offer by simply sending an invite through any of your Tabs in the bunq app.

The Home, Cards, Savings, Stocks and ZeroFX tabs each come with a different reward. You can simply choose the reward you’d like to claim and invite a friend through a text. You can invite up to 5 friends to join bunq per calendar month – 1 per invite you generate in each tab.

### Which rewards are available?

  * 💸 Up to €50* if you invite a Business and the Business makes a qualifying one-time deposit.

  * 💳 Free Personalized Card if you invite a friend and they make a €50 card payment with their bunq card.

  * 💰 Get Bonus Interest* if you invite a friend and they make a qualifying one-time deposit to their Savings account, keeping it for a qualifying amount of months.

  * 📈 Get months* of Free Stock Trading if you invite a friend and they set up AutoInvest.

  * 🍀 Up to 100 extra Wheel of Fortune spins* if you invite a friend and they pay with their bunq card at a bar or a restaurant. 

  * 💸 Up to €100* if you invite a friend and they activate Switch Service to bunq. To access this reward, your friend must live in the 🇳🇱 the Netherlands and use bunq for a qualifying amount of months. 

  * 🌎 12 free months* of bunq Core for your friend, if you invite a friend from the ZeroFX screen in your Cards Tab — and they make a €50 card payment with their bunq card.

## How do I invite my friends and choose rewards?

You can earn different rewards depending on the way you invite your friend. Each reward is tied to a specific tab. If you’d like to get a particular reward, you need to invite your friend directly through the associated tab — the reward you earn depends on which tab you use to send the invite.

#### Invite a friend, get up to €100

Invite your friends, who live in the Netherlands, to join bunq from your Home Tab. When they join bunq and enable Switch Service, you both receive up to €100!

To invite your friend and receive up to €100, you can:

  1. Go to your Home Tab

  2. Scroll down and tap on Get up to €100 with Switch Service

  3. Tap on Invite now

  4. Select the phone number of the friend you want to invite from your Contacts

  5. Tap on Send Invite

  6. Your invite is now in progress ✨

Once your friend completes their sign up and activates the Switch Service to bunq, you’ll get a notification from us that you’ve unlocked your reward. The Switch Service activation can take up to 2 weeks to complete. To claim your reward, simply:

  * Tap on the notification or the pending event directly in your bunq app

  * Tap on Claim Reward

  * You’ll receive a reward payment* from bunq to your Main Account

*Reward payment depends on campaign and deposit amount.

**Please note** : Your friend should keep their Switch Service active for a **minimum of 13 months**. If your friend cancels their Switch Service before the qualifying period ends, a cancellation fee of €100 will be charged. 

#### Invite a Business, get up to €50

Know someone with a new Business and want to help kickstart their journey? When you invite a Business to join bunq and they make a qualifying one-time deposit, you both get up to €50.

To invite a Business to bunq and get up to €50, you can:

  1. Switch to your Business account’s Home Tab

  2. Tap on Invite a business and get up to €50

  3. Tap on Invite now

  4. Select the phone number of the Business you want to invite from your Contacts

  5. Tap on Send Invite

  6. Your invite is now in progress ✨

Once your invited Business completes their sign up, you’ll get a notification that you've unlocked your reward. To claim it, simply:

  * Tap on the notification you receive

  * Tap on Claim Reward

  * Receive a reward payment* from bunq

*Reward payment depends on campaign and deposit amount.

#### Why can't I invite my friend?

You can invite anyone to join bunq via their phone number. Invites are sent as an SMS so if you’re noticing that your invite isn’t going through, please check that you’re using the correct phone number. You can’t send an invite via email or a direct link at this time.

Just try again by using your friend’s phone number and they’ll get the invite right away!

#### Why am I not receiving my rewards?

  * Your friend needs to complete the sign up for bunq via the link you shared

  * Your friend needs to have a verified account

  * Your friend needs to complete the necessary, qualifying steps

  * If you invite a Business, the Business has to make the qualifying one-time deposit

#### How many invites can I send?

You can send 1 invite per reward, per month. As such, you can send up to 5 individual invites per month in total — one from each screen.

Example: If you’ve sent an invite via the Card Screen, you’ll need to wait another calendar month before sending another invite via the Card Screen. 

#### My friend didn't receive my invite

Did you send an invite over to your friend but they didn't get an SMS? Here are a few things to check:

  1. **Double-check the phone number** : check if you entered the correct number when you sent the invite.

  2. **Check the SMS inbox and spam folder** : sometimes SMS filters or settings might block unknown senders.

  3. **Download the bunq app directly:** If your friend haven’t received the invite but the number is correct, they can download the bunq app and sign up using the same phone number you used for the invite. That way, the referral will still be linked and you both get your rewards.
