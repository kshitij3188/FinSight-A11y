# How can I use bunq Deals with my Business account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-use-bunq-deals-with-my-business-account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Get discounts from your favorite brands and services - simply by using bunq. Enjoy deals tailored to you, from gadgets to travel bookings.

### Where can I find Deals?

  1. Go to your Business 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap your Profile in the top-left corner

  3. Tap on 🏷️ Deals

## What bunq Deals can I get?

Here’re some of the discounts you can enjoy as a Business account user:

  * Save up to 60% on international shipping with FedEx Express;

  * 25% on your first year with Google Workspace;

  * 10% on car rentals with Sixt or Rentalcars.com;

  * Up to 30% off with DocuSign;

  * 10% off with Personio;

  * Enjoy 6 months free with Notion;

  * 4% credit on all bookings with Booking.com;

  * 10% off your factoring fee with Finqle;

  * 1 free month of loan repayment with BridgeFund.

Explore these and other discounts [here](<https://www.bunq.com/nl-nl/business-account/banking-features/deals>).

_**Note:** Please note that some offers are only available in specific countries._

_**Note:** bunq Deals change over time, so the offer shown above may not always be available. Terms and conditions apply, please check them _[_here_](<https://www.bunq.com/documents/bunq-deals-general-terms-and-conditions>) _._

## How can I claim a deal?

To claim a discount offer, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Business 🏠 Home tab

  2. Tap your Profile in the top-left corner

  3. Tap on 🏷️ Deals

  4. Select the deal you’re interested in

  5. Follow the on-screen steps to claim it ✨

## Who can use these bunq Deals?

Business account users and employees based in 🇳🇱 the Netherlands, 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇫🇷 France, 🇮🇹 Italy, 🇪🇸 Spain, and 🇮🇪 Ireland can enjoy the bunq Deals, regardless of their plan.

## Is there any limit on deals used? 

The limit for each discount offer varies by partner. You can find more details on bunq Deals’ Terms & Conditions [here](<https://www.bunq.com/documents/bunq-deals-general-terms-and-conditions>).

## What is the expiration period for the deals?

Discounts come with different expiration dates, depending on the deal. When a discount is no longer available, it’ll disappear from the Deals tab.
