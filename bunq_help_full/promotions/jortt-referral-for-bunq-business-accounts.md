# Jortt referral for bunq Business accounts

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/jortt-referral-for-bunq-business-accounts
**Topics:** Business

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Earn €50 Cashback when you open and verify your bunq Business account after signing up through the [**Jortt referral link**](<https://app.adjust.com/1tgpfeky?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.bunq.com%2Finvite%2Freferral%2Fa6ab91cc-853f-4e73-b675-8d510a4b80ea>). As soon as your Business account is verified, you’ll see an in‑app event to claim your reward—quick and easy.

## How it works: 

Follow these steps to get your €50 reward:

  1. Sign up to bunq through the Jortt referral link. All you need is your phone and ID 🪪

  2. Open a bunq Business account.

  3. Verify your Business account.

  4. Claim your €50 reward from the in‑app event.

Tip: If you haven’t opened a Business account yet, get started here: [How do I open a bunq Business account?](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-to-open-a-bunq-business-account>)

## When can I claim the reward?

You can claim the reward as soon as your Business account is verified. The bunq app will show an event inviting you to claim your €50 Cashback. Just follow the prompt in the app.

If your account isn’t verified yet, complete verification first: [How do I verify my business account?]

## Who’s eligible?

  * New bunq users ONLY

  * Plans: bunq Free Business, bunq Core Business, bunq Pro Business, and bunq Elite Business

  * Country: The Netherlands

  * Sign-up method: You must join bunq via the [**Jortt referral link**](<https://app.adjust.com/1tgpfeky?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.bunq.com%2Finvite%2Freferral%2Fa6ab91cc-853f-4e73-b675-8d510a4b80ea>)

**Important:** You need to sign up through the Jortt referral link and have a verified bunq Business account to receive the €50 reward. Personal accounts aren’t eligible.

## Don’t see the in‑app event?

If you meet the criteria and don’t see the event:

  * Confirm you joined bunq using the [**Jortt referral link**](<https://app.adjust.com/1tgpfeky?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fweb.bunq.com%2Finvite%2Freferral%2Fa6ab91cc-853f-4e73-b675-8d510a4b80ea>)

  * Check that your Business account is fully verified

  * Make sure your app is up to date

Still no event? Reach out through the app and our support will help you out.
