# Let’s empower women in tech!

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/lets-empower-women-in-tech

---

Studies show that the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) have been predominantly occupied by men. bunq is joining forces with [Ironhack](<https://www.ironhack.com/>) to change that and to help women jumpstart their tech career by joining one of Ironhack’s intense bootcamps! 💪💻

Sign up for Ironhack’s 9 week (full time) or 24 week (part-time) courses - you can participate in them remotely, or, if you’re around Amsterdam or Berlin and you’d prefer to do this in person, that’s also possible! Ironhack’s campus is open, safe, respects the current regulations around COVID-19 and they can’t wait to meet you! 🤝

And if you sign up now, **you could have a chance to win a scholarship for one of Ironhack’s courses!**

You can read more about the Ironhack initiative [on the bunq blog](<./lets-empower-women-in-tech>) 📝
