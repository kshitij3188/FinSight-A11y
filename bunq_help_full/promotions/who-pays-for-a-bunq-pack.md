# Who pays for a bunq Pack?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/who-pays-for-a-bunq-pack
**Topics:** Plans

---

Hey bunqers 🌈 ,

The bunq Pack's starter pays the **€28.99** a month from his billing account, all extra costs (extra cards or withdrawals) fall unto the individual members.

Want to split the costs amongst the Pack's members? You can always [split the costs from the invoice](<https://together.bunq.com/d/742>) itself.

Want it done automatically? You can ask the members to set up a [monthly payment](<https://together.bunq.com/d/84>) to you 📆

### How can I create a bunq Pack?

Follow the steps below to create your own Pack:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner of your 🏡Home tab

  2. Tap on **My plan**

  3. Tap on **Explore All Plans**

  4. Select **bunq Pack**

  5. Tap on **Switch to bunq Pack**

  6. Tap on **Add a friend**

  7. Select the person/business you want to invite

  8. Send the invitation!
