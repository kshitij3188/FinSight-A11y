# Win a Metal Credit Card with Deutschrap Plus 💳

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/win-a-metal-credit-card-with-deutschrap-plus
**Topics:** Signup, Germany 🇩🇪

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

New German users who sign up between July 8, 2025 to August 31, 2025 can win a Metal Credit Card when signing up for bunq through Deutschrap Plus!

### How can I enter the giveaway

  1. Sign up for bunq through the Deutschrap Plus [promotion link](<https://linktr.ee/deutschrapplus>).

  2. Confirm your phone number.

  3. Top up your account.

## Who's eligible for this promotion? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You live in Germany

  * Register through the Deutschrap Plus [promotion link](<https://linktr.ee/deutschrapplus>)

  * Be 18 years old or older

  * Sign up between July 8 and August 31, 2025

  * Have a verified Personal account with any of the [available bunq plans](<https://together.bunq.com/d/3892>).

## How are winners determined

There will be 3 randomly selected winners. Each winner will receive a bunq Metal Credit Card.

For more information about the Metal Credit Card, check [this topic](<https://together.bunq.com/d/23424>)

## How will I know if I won?

All winners will receive an official email from us on September 1, 2025.

## Frequently Asked Questions

The prize is non-transferable and can’t be exchanged for cash or other bunq products

  * You can only win once

  * You may be disqualified from the giveaway if we suspect fraudulent activity

  * By participating in the giveaway, you agree to our [T&Cs](<https://www.bunq.com/en-de/documents/deutschrap-plus-campaign-terms-conditions>)
