# Win up to 10x of your payments back with the Wheel of Fortune 🍀

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/win-your-groceries-back
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

We’ve combined the best of bunq Points, Cashback and Winning back Groceries to bring you daily rewards, simply for using your bunq Card!

Every time you make a payment with your bunq Card, you earn a spin on the **Wheel of Fortune**. You can spin the wheel for a chance to win**up to 10x** the value of your payment back. 

Ready to spin the Wheel? Simply update your bunq App to the latest version, 30.5.0, to get access to this exciting update ⚡️

## How do I win my payments back? 

You earn spins each time you pay with your bunq card. Your bunq plan determines where you earn spins when you make payments. When you’re on: 

  * **bunq Free** : you earn spins**** from bars and restaurants payments 

  * **bunq Core and bunq Pro** : you earn spins**** from bars, restaurants, and groceries payments 

  * **bunq Elite** : you earn spins**** from bars, restaurants, groceries and travel-related payments

You can use your spins on the Wheel of Fortune for a chance to win your payments back. Here’s how: 

  1. Make an eligible payment with your **bunq card**

  2. Go to the Home Tab 

  3. Tap the **Clover Icon** **🍀** on the top-right of your screen to find your lucky **Wheel of Fortune**

  4. Tap on**Spin the Wheel**

  5. See if you won! 

All spins you earned between Monday and Sunday will expire on the Sunday of the _following_ week so make sure to use your spins before the following Sunday at **23:59 of your Local Time**. 

## Do I need to register to play? 

No need to opt-in! Just spend with your bunq Card, spin the Wheel, and enjoy the rewards ✨

## Where can I find my Wheel of Fortune? 

It's easily accessible via the **Clover Icon 🍀** , located in the top-right corner of your Home Tab. Simply tap on it and start spinning to win your payments back!

## What can I win?

Every spin gives you a chance to win:

  * **💸 Cash Rewards** : Win 10x, 1x, 0.5x, 0.25x, 0.1x, or 0.01x of your payment back, up to a **maximum** amount of €2,000 per payment 

  * **🌳 Plant Trees:** Grow your forest with 1, 5, 10 or 100 trees 

  * **🎡 Bonus spin** : 1 extra wheel spin to keep your momentum going 

There’s no cap on the amount of rewards you can win. This means that the more you use your bunq Card, the more spins and chances to win you get. 

## How do I get extra spins?

Invite your friends to bunq and earn **100 extra spins** for every person who joins. Share your bunq.me link or invite code and start spinning 🎡

## What determines if I win or lose?

There are predefined winning times scheduled at specific times throughout the week, each linked to a particular reward. The first person to spin the Wheel after one of the winning time slots wins the corresponding reward. 

## Who’s eligible to play? 

You have to meet the following conditions to be eligible for this promotion:

  * You’re 18 years old or older.

  * You have a Personal verified account with any of the available bunq Plans.

💡 **Please note:** The Wheel of Fortune isn't available for bunq Business accounts, so payments made from a Business account won't earn spins. 

## Where will I get my payout? 

If you win a prize, you’ll get the money you won right away. We’ll pay it out through an immediate incoming payment from bunq to the bank account connected to your bunq Card. 

## Who earn spins from a Joint Account? 

It's simple! On a payment made from a Joint Account, the spin goes to the person who made the payment from their bunq card. 

Keep in mind that the payments which earn spins always depend on the cardholder's bunq plan. Let's imagine you're on bunq Free and you earn spins from bars and restaurant payments, while your Joint Account partner is on bunq Core and earns spins**** from bars, restaurants, and grocery payments. 

This means that: 

  * If you go on a dinner date, and **you pay** with your card from the Joint Account, **you get the spin**.

  * If **your partner pays** for groceries with their card from the Joint Account, **they get the spin**.

  * But, if **you pay** for the groceries with your card from the Joint Account, your payment **won't earn a spin** because that isn’t included in your bunq Free plan.

## What if I forget to spin the wheel? 

No worries, we know life gets busy! To help you stay on top of your spins, we’ll display a red blinking dot on the **Clover Icon** in your Home Tab when your spins are about to expire. 

When you see the red dot, make sure to spin the Wheel and use your earned spins for a chance to win a reward!

To keep the game fair for everyone, there’s no cap on the amount of spins you can earn or the amount of times you get to spin the Wheel of Fortune!

## Why didn't I earn a spin?

Only payments made directly with your bunq Card can earn spins. iDEAL payments aren't eligible to earn spins on the Wheel of Fortune. 

Sometimes, a card payment you’d expect to earn you a spin might not. In this case, this may come down to how businesses are categorized by their own bank. Mastercard assigns a special code to every business to identify its type, like: restaurants, bars, and supermarkets. A payment automatically earns a spin if the business is registered under one of the eligible categories.  
  
Smaller shops, like your local corner store or an independent cafe, may not be registered under the correct category in Mastercard’s system. For example, your favourite bakery might be classified as a “General Retailer” instead of a “Supermarket.” When this happens, the payment isn’t recognized as eligible, and you won’t get a spin. This is determined directly by Mastercard’s system.  
  
Our team is working on implementing a solution to better recognize payments from smaller retailers. In the meantime, sticking to larger, well-known merchants for your grocery and travel purchases will give you the best chance of earning spins every time. If you didn't earn a spin on a payment which you believe should have qualified, simply reach out to our Support team, and we’ll look into it for you!
