# What is a Joint Savings Account and how do I create one?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/what-is-a-joint-savings-account-and-how-do-i-create-one

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

With a Joint Savings Account, you can easily save together with your friends, family or partners and receive a high MassInterest rate.

### How do I create a Joint Savings Account?

  1. Go to the 💰Savings tab

  2. Tap on **Add an Extra Bank Account**

  3. Tap on **Joint Savings Account**

  4. Select Partner(s) 

  5. Personalize your account 

  6. Tap on **Create** ✅

## Who can create a Joint Savings Account?

This feature is available for bunq Core, Pro, and Elite users!

bunq Free users can join an unlimited number of Joint Savings Accounts but can't create them. These Joint Savings Accounts don't count toward the 3 Bank Account limit of the bunq Free plan.

## How does MassInterest work with my Joint Savings Account?

The [MassInterest](<https://together.bunq.com/d/8298>) for your Joint Savings Account is calculated by dividing the balance of the account by the number of owners, since it's legally equally divided between all owners. The interest earned will be paid out in the individual billing sub-accounts of both owners. Further information about the calculation and payout of MassInterest can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57042>).

**For example** : If you and a friend have a Joint Savings Account with €10.000, you'll each receive interest for €5.000 each (the total balance of your Joint Savings Account divided by two)

Keep in mind that for your individual Personal Account you can receive MassInterest up to €100.000. This limit covers your Personal Savings Account/s as well as your Joint Savings Account/s.

## How can I enable Auto Round Up on my Joint Savings Account?

If you’re a bunq Pro or bunq Elite user, you can round up your purchases to the nearest €1 and automatically send the spare change to your Joint Savings Account. Here’s how:

  1. Go to the 💰Savings tab

  2. Tap on your Joint Savings Account

  3. Go to ⚙️ Settings

  4. Tap on Auto Round Up

  5. Select the Bank Account(s) to Auto Round Up from

## How do I close a Joint Savings Account?

In order to close your Joint Savings Account follow these easy steps:

  1. Go to the 💰Savings tab

  2. Tap on the Joint Savings Account you want to close

  3. Withdraw all remaining balance 

  4. Once done ➡️ Tap on ⚙️ (Settings)

  5. Tap on Close Bank Account

  6. Done ✅

Keep in mind that before closing your Joint Savings Account, you should withdraw all remaining balance. Since automatic fund division isn't currently available - you should divide the balance between you and the rest of the owners once you've withdrawn the remaining balance.

## What MassInterest am I earning?

#### Personal Accounts [Changes Effective as of July 19, 2024]

Your threshold amount for receiving bonus interest is the highest savings balance reached between January 1, 2025 and June 30, 2025. From July 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025, you accrue a base interest rate of 1.51%* on your savings balance up to this threshold amount. Any balance exceeding this threshold amount earns a bonus interest rate of 2.01%. 

  * **For 🇪🇺 EEA Residents who signed up for bunq on/after July 1, 2025**

    * You didn’t have any savings balance between January 1, 2025 and June 30, 2025 so your threshold will be 0. Therefore, you’ll receive a variable bonus interest rate (currently 2.01%*) on all your savings up to €100,000 until December 31, 2025

  * **For 🇪🇺 EEA Residents who signed up for bunq before July 1, 2025**

    * The highest savings balance that you reached between January 1, 2025 and June 30, 2025 determines your threshold amount. 

    * Between July 1, 2025 and December 31, 2025:

    * You’ll get an annual variable base interest of 1.51%* up to your threshold amount

    * You’ll get an annual variable bonus rate (currently 2.01%*) on any additional amount deposited between July 1, 2025 and December 31, 2025 that are above your threshold amount.

The system will automatically reset itself on January 1, 2026: your threshold amount will then be recalculated to become the highest savings balance you reached between July 1, 2025 and December 31, 2025. Then, between January 1, 2026 and June 30, 2026, you’ll earn the base interest rate up to your new threshold and the bonus rate on balances over the threshold amount.

*The base rate and the bonus rate may be subject to change.

Further information can be found [here](<https://together.bunq.com/d/57095>). 

## Can I create a Joint Savings Account with a user who doesn't have a bunq account yet?

Want to create a Joint Savings Account with a user who doesn't have a bunq account yet? Simply invite them when you create the Joint Savings Account by choosing their contact (phone number or email) in the contact list as a partner. You'll send an automatic invite to your friend so that they can join bunq, sign up and start saving with you right away!

## Good to know

  * Regardless of their individual plans, all bunq users can enjoy the convenience of sharing a Joint Savings account since the feature is available for all plans.

  * Withdrawals from any Joint Savings Account **count** towards your limit of up to 2 (or 3) savings withdrawals per calendar month. Learn more about how many free Savings Withdrawals you get [here](<https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-withdraw-money-from-my-savings-account>). 

  * Keep in mind that [Deposit Guarantee Scheme](<https://www.bunq.com/features/deposit-protection>) up to €100.000 is in place for each individual bunq user. The balance in your Joint Savings Account are divided equally by the number of owners and accordingly protected by the Deposit Guarantee Scheme for your Personal Account.

  * You can't add new people after the Joint Savings Account was created.

  * Joint Savings Accounts will come with a Dutch (NL) IBAN by default. For sharing an account with an IBAN from a different country, the best approach is to open an [International Bank Account ](<./foreign-ibans>)and then grant [Shared Access](<./what-is-shared-access-and-how-to-use-it>) to the other person.

  * A Joint Savings Account is owned by all the users who accepted the invite, just like with a Joint Account.

  * If not everyone has accepted their invite yet, you can easily check who’s participation is still pending: in the app, you select the Joint Savings Account > Co-owner > See who is still ‘waiting on response’.
