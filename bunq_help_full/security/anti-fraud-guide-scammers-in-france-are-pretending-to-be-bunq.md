# Anti-Fraud Guide: Scammers in France are pretending to be bunq!

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/anti-fraud-guide-scammers-in-france-are-pretending-to-be-bunq
**Topics:** Security, France 🇫🇷, Anti-Fraud Guide

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

We’ve been alerted of multiple fraudulent websites and individuals pretending to be bunq in the French market. We would like to protect anybody from scams to the best extent possible - not only our existing customers, but any non-bunq users, as well. 

That’s why our anti-fraud specialists have gathered some pro-tips on how to stay safe in such cases, you can find all the insights below!

## How to recognize a scammer pretending to be bunq?

The criminals approach individuals via social media and offline via phone by pretending to be **bunq sales agents**. They contact victims who are considering opening a bunq account and offer services such as **opening a Savings Account**.   
  
Scammers have also been found to offer **investment opportunities, related to “bunq France”**. In order to deceive more victims, the fraudsters exaggerate interest figures, or other beneficial opportunities.   
  
For your information, you can always check the benefits bunq offers on our official website - https://www.bunq.com 

## What does a fraudulent link look like?

Every link which is different from https://www.bunq.com/ or https://help.bunq.com/ claiming to be a source of information is fraudulent. Examples of the fraudulent websites also include: 

  * https://www.bunq.com/fr-fr 

  * https://www.bunq.com/personal-account/banking-features/travel-insurance-and-perks etc.

## How can I stay secure?

Always keep in mind the following information: 

  * The only reliable source of information about bunq is our **official website https://www.bunq.com/** and our **official social media pages** , linked on the bottom of our official website. 

  * Please also note that we are registered in France as a branch of bunq B.V. and we don’t approach potential customers with such offers.

  * **Be aware that bunq will NEVER call you.**

  * The only way you can open a bunq account is through the bunq app. We don’t make use of sales agents or any other representatives in our relationship with our target customers.

  * Always remember to be vigilant when you are approached with an offer that might sound just a little too lucrative, possibly even unrealistic. Always check the information you’re given against official sources

  * If you spot any suspicious activity such as websites or shady ‘representatives’, please report to us immediately by following the flow [here](<https://www.bunq.com/contact/report/phishing>) 🙏

## What can I do if I'm a victim of fraud? 

Dealing with fraud can be a really unpleasant experience. It’s completely natural to feel angry or frustrated in a situation like this, but please know that you’re not alone and bunq has got your back.

We've put together a guide on what you can do if you find yourself to be a victim of fraud which you can check out —[ here](<./anti-fraud-guide-ive-been-scammed-what-can-i-do>).
