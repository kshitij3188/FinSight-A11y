# How we’d handle a data breach

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-wed-handle-a-data-breach
**Topics:** Security

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

At bunq, we follow banking regulations to protect your personal data. We have an internal process in place to ensure that if a data breach occurs, all regulations are followed and you're informed immediately.

## What is a data breach?

A **data breach** is a **security incident** where **personal data is compromised** , affecting **confidentiality** , **availability** , or **integrity**. If a breach occurs and poses a high risk to users, we will notify the Dutch Supervisory Authority within 72 hours.

## Types of personal data breaches

  * **Confidentiality breach:** Unauthorized or accidental disclosure of personal data.

  * **Availability breach:** Unauthorized loss of access to or destruction of personal data.

  * **Integrity breach:** Unauthorized or accidental alteration of personal data

## What happens in such cases?

  * If such breach occurs, our employees are required to inform the **Data Protection Officer** as soon as possible.

  * The **Data Protection Officer** determines if the **Personal Data Breach** is still ongoing and ensures that appropriate steps are taken immediately to contain and minimize the impact of the **Personal Data Breach**.

  * The **Data Protection Officer** will determine if a particular **Personal Data Breach** should be notified to the **Dutch Data Protection Authority**. If necessary the **Personal Data Breach** will be notified to the **Dutch Data Protection Authority** as soon as possible but no later than 72 hours using the online form.

## Is my data protected?

Absolutely! In bunq we always make sure your data is secure - all of your personal data is encrypted and secured with an extremely strong authorization gate.
