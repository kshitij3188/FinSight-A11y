# Illegal and repeated transactions protection

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/illegal-and-repeated-transactions-protection
**Topics:** Security

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

At bunq your safety is our top priority! That’s why you can count on us to use the latest technology to keep you safe: thanks to Artificial Intelligence your account now enjoys extra protection against irregular and unknown card payments 🦾

## What would happen if a high-risk payment is made with my card?

Sometimes it happens that your card details get exposed while using your card on fraudulent terminals. However, we make sure each bunqer’s card is protected from unrecognized payments 🛡️ When our system detects a **high-risk payment** or a **repetitive one** , this payment will be denied. You’ll get notified for that action via a notification.

## What If the payment was done by me?

If the payment was done by you, you can follow the steps below to AutoAccept this merchant:

  1. Tap on the payment notification

  2. Tap on **Enable AutoAccept**

  3. The default limit is set to the payment amount

  4. Done ✅ 

## What If the payment wasn’t done by me?

You don’t need to worry about it, as we’re going to block the payment for you. However, we strongly recommend blocking the card and ordering a new one. This is the only way we can ensure no more fraudulent payments can be done on your account. You can now easily block your card and order a new one with just one tap within the payment event 🙌

Once a fraudulent payment is attempted, you’ll receive a notification in which we strongly advise you to block the card the payment was done with..

However, if the payment wasn’t blocked and was successfully done, you can proceed by requesting a chargeback. Further information on the matter can be found [here](<./how-can-i-request-chargebacks-card-payment-refunds>).

## Can I block a merchant?

  1. Go to 🏠 Home

  2. Tap on the desired Bank Account

  3. Select the card payment from a particular merchant

  4. Tap on the merchant’s icon

  5. Block Merchant 

Further information on the matter can be found [here](<./can-i-block-a-merchant>).

## Where can I review my AutoAccepted and Blocked merchants?

You can get a clear overview of the merchants you’ve blocked and auto accepted by following the steps mentioned below:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on ⚙️ Settings

  3. Security

  4. Select the AutoAccepted or Blocked merchants
