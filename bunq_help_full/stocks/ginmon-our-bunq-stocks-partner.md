# Ginmon - our bunq Stocks partner 🚀

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/ginmon-our-bunq-stocks-partner

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈**

For our new Stocks feature, we’ve partnered with Ginmon, a leading robo-advisor, to provide you with an easy and efficient way to buy and sell stocks directly from the bunq app.

## Who’s Ginmon?

Ginmon is a trusted and reliable platform that uses advanced technology to manage your investments. They handle all the complexities of stock trading, allowing you to focus on your financial goals.

> **Note:** Remember, investing in stocks always comes with risks. It's important to understand these risks and consider your financial situation and investment goals before you start trading.

## What does this partnership mean for me?

When you place an order to buy or sell stocks in the bunq app, your order is directly received and processed by Ginmon. This means that **bunq doesn’t receive or transmit the order itself, but does so on behalf of Ginmon**. This partnership ensures a **seamless and efficient trading experience** for you. For more details about GInmon, you can check the FAQs on their website [here](<https://help.ginmon.de/en>).
