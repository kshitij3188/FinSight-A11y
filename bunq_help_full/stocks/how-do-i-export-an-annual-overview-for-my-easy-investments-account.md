# How do I export an Annual Overview for my Easy Investments account?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-do-i-export-an-annual-overview-for-my-easy-investments-account
**Topics:** Account

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

You can easily export an Annual Overview from your Easy Investments account with these fast steps:

## Exporting Your Annual Overview

  1. Go to Your Profile 

  2. Tap on Archive 

  3. Select your Investment Account 

  4. Tap on Settings

  5. Select Reports 

  6. Export your Annual Overview 🚀

Keep in mind that your **Annual Easy Investments Overview is provided by Birdee**. If you need an annual overview of your whole bunq account, check out [this topic](<./how-do-i-export-my-annual-account-overview>) to see how you can get your annual overview.
