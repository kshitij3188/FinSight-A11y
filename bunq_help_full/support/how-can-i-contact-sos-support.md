# How can I contact SOS Support?

**Source:** https://help.bunq.com/articles/how-can-i-contact-sos-support
**Topics:** Support, SOS

---

**Hey bunqers 🌈,**

Need SOS support? No worries - we’ve got you covered! If you’re facing an urgent issue with your app or cards, you can use our new **SOS feature directly from the bunq app**. Once we receive your SOS request, it becomes our top priority. Here’s how you can start an **SOS Support chat** , call our **Automated Emergency Line** , or speak directly with a **bunq employee**.

❗️**Note: We will never call you to ask for personal information, like email, security code, card details, etc. Remember that private information is only shared through our secure chat in the app. If you’re receiving a phone call from a person claiming to be a bunq representative, hang up the phone and report the phishing with us.**

### Open an SOS Support chat📱

When opening a support escalation, you can choose the **SOS Chat option** to share your issue via screen recording, voice message, or text. We’ll get back to you within **10 minutes**. Follow these simple steps to start an SOS chat in the app:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on 🟢 Support

  3. Tap on the 3 dots in the top right corner

  4. Select "Get Emergency Support"

  5. Tap "Chat with SOS Support"

  6. Press "Continue"

  7. Record screen and submit, or submit text/attachment

## Call us through SOS Hotline

For **24/7 emergency support** , bunq Core, Pro, or Elite users can use the bunq app to speak directly with a bunq employee. Translate your conversations in real-time with AI voice translations. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on 🟢 Support

  3. Tap on the 3 dots in the top right corner

  4. Select "Get Emergency Support"

  5. Tap **Call our secure SOS Hotline**

_**Note: The SOS Hotline is a one-way communication channel that can be initiated only by you.**_

### How can I translate my conversation?

To translate your conversations in real-time with the help of AI, follow these steps:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on 🟢 Support

  3. Tap on the 3 dots in the top right corner

  4. Select "Get Emergency Support"

  5. Tap **Call our secure SOS Hotline**

  6. Tap on **Translate using AI** and choose your language

## Call us through the Automated Emergency Line ☎️

If you don’t have access to your account, simply call **+31208083666** and the automatic support flow will take you through the process from thereon. Keep in mind, we'll never call you from this number. If you are on the phone with someone pretending to be us, please hang up and inform our team immediately!

## How does the Automated Emergency Line work?

Once you call **+31208083666** , you’ll be able to choose an option on how we can help you and follow the automated flow in your native language (English, Dutch, German, Spanish, French): 

  1. **Freeze card:** To make sure your account and funds are safe and secure at all times, the option to **freeze your card and / or account** is just one phone call away. Just press the button needed and your card will be frozen. Once we've confirmed the security of your account, we'll assist you with the necessary steps to unfreeze them.

  2. **If you suspect being hacked or someone else has access to your bunq account:** We'll send a SMS or email to you with instructions to report your case via https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/contact/report

  3. **If you suspected fraud:** We'll send a SMS or email to you with instructions to report your case via https://www.bunq.com/en-nl/contact/report

  4. **If something isn't working and you need urgent help:** You’ll be able to describe your case and once you’re done, you’ll be contacted through Support Chat from a Support Guide.

  5. **If you have a different, non-urgent question** : During the call, we’ll ask if you have access to your bunq app. If you do not have access, we’ll ensure to reach out to you either through SMS or email, based on the contact information you have verified and linked to your account.

## I was advised on the phone to contact Support from the app. What do I do now?

Don’t worry, finding personal Support in the bunq app is easy and fast. Just follow this process to contact the [Support team](<https://go.bunq.com/link/support>) through our **Support Chat** in the app:

  1. Tap on your Profile in the top left corner

  2. Tap on 🟢 Support

  3. Tap on 🟢 Get Support

  4. Categorize and describe your issue

  5. We'll get back to you as soon as possible

For these and other improvements, update your [app](<https://app.adjust.com/dqvbt6?campaign=homepage&adgroup=discoverbunq&engagement_type=fallback_click&fallback=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com%2Fget-it%2F&redirect_macos=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bunq.com/signup>) today! 🏃‍♂️ Stay tuned for more exciting updates and features in the future!
