How do I file a formal complaint?
At bunq, we’re all about making life easy, and that includes handling complaints when
things don’t go as expected. If you need to file a formal complaint, we've got you covered!
The easiest and fastest way to submit your complaint is through our online form at
https://www.bunq.com/report. This will reach our complaints team directly, so you don’t have
to wait.
If you live in Spain, you’re welcome to send your complaint directly to
reclamaciones@bunq.com.
Just a heads-up – our Support chat can’t process formal complaints, so be sure to use the
right method to make sure your concern gets handled properly.
To help us better assist you, please provide us with the following information:
● the email address associated with your bunq account;
● date of the complaint/when has the issue started; and
● a clear description of what led to your formal complaint.
For users residing in Spain 󰎼
If you're filing a formal complaint from Spain, local regulations ask for a few extra details. To
make sure we can process yours smoothly, please include:
● Name, surname, and address of the person filing the complaint (or their authorized
representative, if applicable), along with their national identity document number
(for individuals) or business registration information (for companies).
● Reason for the complaint – A clear and specific explanation of the issue(s) you
encountered.
● Where the issue occurred – The office, department, or service involved.
● A confirmation that this complaint isn’t already being handled through a court,
arbitration, or another legal process.
● Place, date, and signature to make it official.
🔍
How and when will my formal complaint be handled?
Once we receive your complaint, it’s carefully logged into our system and a specialist will
take a deep dive into the matter to understand it thoroughly. Rest assured, you’ll receive our
response via the email address used for the submission.
We know how important it is to resolve issues quickly. It’s our aim to solve your issue within
15 business days, and in exceptional circumstances, up to 35 business days, but depending
on the type of complaint, it could take up to 6 weeks from the point we receive the

What do I do if I disagree with the decision?
If you're not completely satisfied with our decision, don’t worry—we’ve got procedures for
that too. You can appeal by replying to our response email with "Appeal" in the subject line
and explain the reasons why you're appealing. You can do this within 1 month of receiving
our initial response. We will aim to provide you with a response to your appeal within 15
business days, in exceptional circumstances up to 35 business days, but depending on the
type of complaint, it could take up to 6 weeks of receiving your appeal. If exceptional
circumstances means we won’t be able to provide you a response within 15 business days,
we will notify you accordingly.
If you’re still not satisfied, you have the option to reach out to the Netherlands Financial
Services Complaints Tribunal (KiFiD) within 90 days or take the matter to a civil court.
At the end of the day, your feedback helps us make bunq better. We take complaints
seriously because we want bunq to be the best bank for you. Thanks for sharing your
