purpose that data is processed, what principles we have in place to make this process safe,
and what rights you have as a data subject and user of bunq.

1. Introduction
At bunq, protecting your privacy is one of our top priorities. We will handle your personal
data with care and confidentiality when we process it. The content of this Policy expands
on how we process your data and how we pursue our goal of serving you with integrity
and transparency.
To make sure bunq only processes personal data in line with the relevant laws and
regulations, this policy was created and adopted in line with the General Data Protection
Regulation (GDPR), the Dutch implementation decree of the GDPR (Uitvoeringswet
Algemene Verordening Gegevensbescherming), and other data protection regulation. If
you have any questions about the processing of personal data after reading this policy,
you can contact our Data Protection Officer via privacy@bunq.com.
The purpose of this policy is to:
● Establish rules for the processing of personal data by bunq;
● Inform Eva what personal data we are processing;
● Make the rules on processing implemented by bunq known to Eva.

4. Why Do We Process Data ?
bunq processes your personal data to efficiently and effectively service you.
In particular, we process your personal data to:
● Assess and accept you as a user;
● Execute agreements:
○ for example, payments, production of bunq cards, securing bunq accounts,
identification, and customer support;
● Learn more about how you use our products and services so we can improve them;
● Carry out and personalize (targeted) marketing activities through different
channels and measure their effectiveness;
● Conduct analyses for statistical and strategic purposes;
● Prevent and combat misuse, fraud, and crimes;
● Ensure the safety and integrity of the financial sector, bunq, and our users;
● Comply with our legal obligations that follow from applicable law and any
provisions issued by the supervisory authorities or judicial institutions;
● Use it for training and coaching purposes;
● Improve our current and future products.
We only process personal data that is strictly necessary. Your personal data will be shared
only with authorized employees who are bound by a confidentiality agreement and with
third parties with whom protective agreements have been concluded and protection
measures have been implemented. Personal data is only provided on a need-to-know
basis!

5. Principles Governing the Processing of Personal Data
Now that you know what data we collect and why we collect it, you can get to know the
principles which guide data processing within bunq. These principles are based on
applicable law and can be seen as protections or guidelines put in place by bunq to
ensure the legitimate and safe processing of your data.
● Following the GDPR: Personal Data is processed in accordance with the General
Data Protection Regulation and other data protection regulations, meaning that it
is processed in a proper and careful manner required under this regulation.
● Purpose-specific approach: Personal data is only collected for specific, explicit, and
legitimate purposes.
● Grounded in law: Personal data will be processed if and as long as its consistent
with at least one of the following legal grounds:
○ You gave your explicit consent for the processing of your personal data;
○ The processing is necessary for the performance of an agreement with you;
are a part of, or for pre-contractual measures used at your request and
which are necessary for the conclusion of an agreement;
○ The processing is necessary for bunq to comply with a legal obligation;
○ The processing is necessary to protect your vital interest;
○ The processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of bunq or a third
party to whom personal data is supplied,
■ Unless fundamental rights and freedoms of the user, in particular
the right to privacy, prevail.

● Consistency of purpose: Personal data will not be processed for a purpose other
than the one for which it was collected;
● Only what is necessary: Processing, given the purpose for which it was done, is

● Erase after some time: Personal data will be erased at the end of the retention
period, which is a period determined by bunq.

6. What are Your Rights as a Data Subject ?
Once again, in order to provide its services and comply with the law, bunq must process
the information of its users. However, you are not a bystander in this process! You have a
number of rights that allow you to have a say in how your personal data is treated. This
section will list them.
Protection of personal data
To guarantee that processing is as safe and transparent as possible, bunq implemented
measures that protect the personal data that we process, such as:
(i) use the latest state of technology for the protection of data;
(ii) avoid or mitigate the risks that inevitably occur in the processing; and

## Page 17

(iii) protect Personal Data against (intentional) destruction, loss, falsification, unauthorized
disclosure or access, and any other form of unlawful processing of personal data.
Access
You have the right to request an overview of your personal data that is being processed by
bunq. The overview includes:
I. A description of the purpose or the purposes of the processing
II. The categories of personal data which are processed
III. Copies of requesting user’s data
IV. The recipients or categories of recipients of the personal data; and
V. The available information on the origin of the personal data (ex: from the user, from
other sources, etc).
If bunq is not processing any personal data of the requesting user, bunq will inform them
of that within one (1) month after receiving the request.
Rectification and Deletion
If you notice from the overview that your personal data is incorrect, incomplete, or
irrelevant, you may request the rectification, addition, deletion, or protection of personal
data. For rectification, you must specify which piece of personal data you wish to correct.
For deletion, you have this right if your request fits any situation listed under Article 17 of
the GDPR. We will make the decision on such requests within one (1) month after
receiving the request.
These requests can be made by contacting Support through the bunq app or by emailing
us at privacy@bunq.com. Within one month of receiving said request, we will inform you
in writing if and to what extent your request can be granted. In the event the request is
not or partially granted, we will explain the reason behind that.
Objection and Consent
You have a right to object to your personal data being processed if there are special
circumstances. You can reach out to us and explain said circumstances and bunq will
assess within one (1) month after receiving the notice of objection if the objection is
justified. If it is, the processing of the personal data will be stopped immediately.

7. Data Transfer to non-EEA Countries
In connection with its services, bunq exchanges personal data with Processors and Third
Parties whose services it uses. As a result, personal data may be transferred to Processors
and Third Parties located outside the EEA.
The transfer of personal data by bunq to countries located outside the EEA is permitted if
the principles of the processing of personal data of the Policy are complied with and if the
country in question ensures an adequate level of protection in regard to the transfer of
personal data. The adequacy of a country’s protection can be determined by the
European Commission.
Transfer of personal data by bunq is permitted if at least one of the following apply:
I. you gave your explicit consent for the transfer;
II. the transfer is necessary for the execution of an agreement between the user and
bunq, or for taking pre-contractual measures to be carried out at the request of the
user which is necessary for the conclusion of the agreement;
III. the transfer is necessary for the conclusion or execution of an agreement
concluded or to be concluded between bunq and a Third Party, in the interest of
the relevant user;
IV. the transfer is necessary for an important public interest or for the establishment,
implementation, or defense of any law or right;
V. the transfer is necessary to protect your vital interest; or
VI. the Minister of Justice has issued a permit for a transfer or categories of transfers.
8. Urgent Reasons and Data Breaches
Urgent reasons
The consistency of the purpose of data processing, transparency of processing, and the
rights of the user may be put on pause for urgent reasons. Such urgent reasons arise
within the context of:
18

## Page 20

A. the prevention, detection, investigation, and prosecution of violations of laws,
regulations, or internal rules and regulations of bunq (including collaboration with
(international) (supervisory) authorities for these purposes);
B. protecting and defending the rights and freedoms of bunq, our users, the financial
sector, our employees, and other individuals. This includes among others:
a. the safety of employees and users or the financial sector;
b. the continuity of bunq or the financial sector;
c. confidentiality in connection with, for example, a (proposed) merger or
acquisition; and
C. involvement of advisers in the fields of law, tax matters, and insurance.
Data Breach
In case of a data breach bunq shall, without undue delay or where feasible, not later than
72 hours after becoming aware of it, notify the AP of the breach. If bunq notifies the AP
after more than 72 hours have passed, the notification shall be accompanied by reasons
for the delay. If the Personal Data Breach is unlikely to result in a risk to the rights and
freedoms of natural persons, we will not contact the AP. When the Personal Data Breach
is likely to impact the rights and freedoms of our users or employees, bunq may (also)
communicate the personal data breach to the relevant people without undue delay.
More information on how bunq handles data breaches can be found here.
