## 1\. Introduction 📄

At bunq, your privacy matters. We’ll never sell or rent your personal data to anyone without your permission - because your trust is everything. In this Privacy Statement (“**Statement** ”) you’ll find everything you need to know about what personal data we collect and how we use it.

bunq B.V. is the data controller responsible for processing your personal data.We’re officially registered with the Dutch Chamber of Commerce under registration number 54992060. Our headquarters are at Basisweg 32 1043 AP Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

To make sure we always handle your data in line with the law, we have a dedicated Data Protection Officer (DPO). If you still have any questions about the processing of personal data after reading this Privacy Statement, feel free to reach out at [privacy@bunq.com](<mailto:privacy@bunq.com>). Need to cancel your bunq account or have other account-related questions? Our support team is happy to help via the bunq app or [support@bunq.com](<mailto:support@bunq.com>).

## 3\. What is the lawful basis for the processing of my personal data? ⚖️

bunq processes your personal data in line with the law and only when there’s a solid legal reason for it. Here’s what that means:

**Legal obligations**

We’re required to process certain personal data to comply with the law. This includes verifying your identity and sharing necessary data with governmental institutions when needed.

**Contract performance**

To provide you with our services smoothly and securely, we process your personal data.For example, we use your contact information to keep you informed about relevant developments, and we store correspondence data to improve our customer service.

**Consent**

Your privacy, your choice. For certain features, we’ll always ask for your explicit consent.For example, while using the app you can choose to provide access to your contact list, to upload images/content or to create a public **bunq.me** link. If you have given consent for a specific feature, you can withdraw it anytime directly in the app.

**Legitimate interests**

Sometimes, we process data because it’s necessary for a legitimate reason, like:

  * Preventing and fighting fraud, misuse, or crime.

  * Analyzing how our products and services are used to make them even better.

  * Ensuring information and system security.

  * Enhancing your overall user experience.

## 4\. For what purposes does bunq use my personal data? 🎯

We use your personal data to:

  * **Follow the law** – We comply with all relevant regulations and any requirements from supervisory authorities or judicial institutions.

  * **Provide you with our services** – This includes processing payments, producing your bunq cards, securing your account, verifying your identity, and offering customer support.

  * **Improve our products and services** – We analyze how you and other users interact with bunq to make things even better.

  * **Make our marketing more relevant to you** – We personalize (targeted) marketing activities through different channels and measure how effective they are, based on your personal circumstances and how you use bunq.

  * **Prevent and fight fraud, misuse, and crimes** – Your security is our priority.

  * **Let you create a Stocks account** – If you open one, we process your data to enable it.

  * **Let you open a Credit line** – If you’re eligible and choose to open one, we process your data accordingly.

  * **Keep bunq and the financial sector safe** – Protecting the integrity of bunq and its users is key.

  * **Train and coach our team** – We use data to improve how we help you.

  * **Build the best banking experience for you** – We’re always working on making bunq even better, now and in the future.

## 5\. Does bunq make automatic decisions based on my personal data? 🤖

Yes, when you use bunq, some decisions about you may be made automatically—this includes profiling. This is necessary for entering into a contract with bunq.

You always have the right to request a manual review of automatic decisions. For more information, please take a look at the ‘What are my rights?’ section of this statement.

Automated decisions are made in the following processes:

**Opening an account**

Our 5-minute rule is made possible by an automated system that reviews your application and approves or rejects it.

**Fraud detection**

We monitor**** how our services are used to detect fraud, money laundering, and other crimes, making sure our platform stays safe. If misuse is detected, we take action to prevent further harm.

**Determining your eligibility to open a a credit line**

If you apply for a credit line, we check your credit score using relevant credit bureaus. If you're not eligible based on this automated decision, you'll be notified via the bunq app. You can find more information in our Terms & Conditions.

**Direct marketing**

**Uploaded images/content**

Any images and content you upload to personalize your bunq account or improve your app experience are processed by bunq. You can update or remove these at any time within the app. 

**Public bunq.me profile**

When you create a public bunq.me profile, your nickname and IBAN are visible for anyone that visits your public bunq.me profile for making payments. You can deactivate your bunq.me page with the accompanying data at any given time via the bunq app.

**Phone number, email aliases and bunq Elite Plan**

At signup, we link your phone number and email address to your bunq account as aliases. This allows other bunq users to easily and safely transfer money to your bank account(s), as your bunq account (and IBAN) will show up in their contact list if they have added you to their phone contact list with your phone number and/or email address. The processing of your aliases for these purposes is based upon bunq’s legitimate interest in ensuring seamless transactions and fraud prevention. Furthermore, it gives bunq users certainty that money is transferred into the correct account. If you wish to opt out, you can disconnect your aliases via the account settings. 

We will also show other bunq users on Together and in the app that you are on bunq Elite plan. You can disable this feature in your account settings.

**Cookies**

For more information on how to manage or block cookies, please see our Cookie Statement.

**Recommendations and Activities**

To offer you bunq Recommendations, we share certain information with other bunq users who have you in their contact list.Users who have your alias (see above) in their contact list will be able to see your recommended places, public nicknames and public avatars.

Sharing these data under Places occurs only with your explicit consent, which you can revoke anytime via the bunq app.

**Stocks**

When it comes to sharing the details of your stock purchases in the bunq app, the choice is entirely yours.

For anyone to view your stock trades, the following conditions must be met:

  1. Contacts List : You must have the individual in question saved in your contacts within the bunq app. Only those who you have deliberately added can be considered to view your trades.

  2. Sharing Preferences : You must actively choose to enable the "Share trades with friends" feature. This ensures that you have explicitly agreed to share your trading activities with your selected contacts.

In summary, you are in full control over who sees your stock trades. You decide with whom you share this information by managing both your contacts and your sharing settings. This empowers you to share your trades only with trusted individuals.
