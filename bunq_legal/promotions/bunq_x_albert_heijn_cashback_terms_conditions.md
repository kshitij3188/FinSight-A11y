1. Eligibility
This promotion is available exclusively to new bunq personal account users residing in The
Netherlands who sign up through the dedicated Albert Heijn link and:
● Successfully complete all required KYC checks to verify their bunq account.
● Participants must be 25 years or older at the time of participation.
● Each user can participate once. Business account holders are not eligible for this
promotion.
2. Offer Details
Eligible users will receive €10 cashback for each of their next 4 purchases at Albert Heijn
made with their bunq-issued card. Each individual purchase must have a minimum value
of €20.
Cashback can be earned once every 10 days. If you make more than one eligible purchase
within a 10-day period, only the first purchase qualifies for cashback. After 10 days you can
make another eligible purchase. Cashback will be credited automatically to the user’s
bunq account on their next bunq Payday after completing an eligible purchase.
Payments are only eligible if made within 60 days after signup and directly with a bunq
Card (virtual or physical).
Payments via PayPal, Curve, Klarna, iDEAL, or other comparable mediums are not eligible.
3. Additional Terms
Cashback applies only to settled transactions. If a transaction is later refunded or
cancelled, bunq has the right to reverse any previously granted cashback.
bunq and Albert Heijn may modify, suspend, or end this promotion at any time without
prior notice.
bunq is not liable for Albert Heijn tracking failures.
All changes apply only prospectively and any cashback already earned will be paid out,
except where bunq reasonably determines that fraud, abuse, or misuse has occurred.
