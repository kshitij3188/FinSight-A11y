1. Eligibility
This promotion is available exclusively to new bunq Personal Account users residing in
France. This new bunq Personal Account user must sign up through the dedicated
Auchan promotional link and:
● Successfully complete all required KYC checks to verify their bunq account.
● Participants must be 18 years or older at the time of participation.
● Each user can participate once. Business Account holders are not eligible for this
promotion.
● Residing in France means the user’s registered home address in the bunq app is in
France at the time of participation.
2. Offer Details
Eligible users will receive €10 cashback for each of their next three (3) purchases at
(Auchan.fr) in France, made with their bunq-issued card. Each individual purchase must
have a minimum value of €20, with a maximum total cashback of €30.
Cashback can be earned once per rolling seven (7) days. The 7-day period starts at the
time when your previous eligible purchase is made. If you make more than one eligible
purchase during that period, only the first qualifies.
Cashback will be credited automatically to the user’s bunq account on their next bunq
Payday after completing an eligible purchase.
Users have 90 days since signup to make use of the promotion before it expires.
