1. Eligibility
This promotion is available exclusively to new bunq personal account users residing in The
Netherlands and Germany who sign up through the dedicated Lidl link and:
● Successfully complete all required KYC checks to verify their bunq account.
● Participants must be 18 years or older at the time of participation.
● Each user can participate once. Business account holders are not eligible for this
promotion.
2. Offer Details
Eligible users will receive €10 cashback for each of their next 4 qualifying purchases at Lidl
made with their bunq-issued card. Each purchase must have a minimum value of €20,
with a maximum total cashback of €40. This promotion is valid for both in-store and
online purchases on the official Lidl webshop in the Netherlands and Germany.
Cashback is credited automatically to the user’s bunq account on their next bunq Payday
after completing an eligible purchase.
You can earn cashback once every 7 days. If you make more than one eligible purchase
within a 7-day period, only the first purchase qualifies for cashback. After 7 days, you can
make another eligible purchase.
This promotion is valid for 90 days from the day the user signs up.

● Fraud or attempted fraud,

● Technical, operational, or system errors or disruptions,
● Legal, regulatory or supervisory requirements or guidance, or
● Changes to, suspension of, or discontinuation of Lidl’s relevant services (for
example, if the partner stops offering the underlying service).
