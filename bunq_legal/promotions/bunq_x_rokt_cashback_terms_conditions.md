Eligible users will receive €5 cashback for each of their next 6 purchases made

Payments via PayPal, Curve, Klarna, iDEAL, or other comparable mediums are not
eligible.
Cashback will be credited automatically to the user’s bunq account on their next
bunq Payday after completing an eligible purchase.
