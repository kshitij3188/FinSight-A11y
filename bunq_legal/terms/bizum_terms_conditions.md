3. What does the BIZUM Service consist of?
The BIZUM Service allows you to make instant transfers anytime, any day, with funds
available immediately, using the bunq app. With the BIZUM Service you can:
● Send money instantly, whether you start it or another BIZUM user requests it.
● Receive money directly, whether you request it or another BIZUM user sends it.
● Pay for purchases online at participating merchants.
It's quick, easy, and all done through the bunq app!
You don't need to know the recipient's account details (IBAN) to make a transfer:
(i) Payment between individuals: You only need to enter the recipient's mobile phone
number that's linked to the BIZUM Service. Other identifiers such as email address
address or similar may be introduced in the future.
The recipient of the transaction will be checked to see if they are registered with the
BIZUM Service. If they are not, you can send them an informational message inviting
them to register. This message will be sent via SMS or other means, at no cost to you. If the
recipient registers within 2 days of receiving the message, they will automatically receive

4. How do I join the BIZUM Service?
bunq is adhered to the BIZUM Service and all our users holding a bunq monetary account
with a Spanish local IBAN can sign up for this service as end users. This does not apply to
Accounts.
To provide BIZUM Service, a single, centralised Directory has been created, which is
accessed by adhered member institutions and contains the necessary information for
processing transfers. The ownership of the database that makes up the Directory is held
by Bizum, S.L.
You can register for the BIZUM Service via the bunq APP. To register, you must have a
bunq Personal Account and you would have already provided the following information:
I. Your full name;
II. Your tax identification number (NIF or NIE) or other similar identifier number;
III. Your mobile phone number, which will be used as an identifier for the purposes of
providing the BIZUM Service. The telephone number you confirm must be the same as
the number you gave us when you opened your bunq Account. If you change your
telephone number in our database, you must also change it in the BIZUM Service. We will
not be liable for BIZUM Service failures due to failure to update your identifier.
You cannot register the same mobile phone number as an identifier with more than one
institution participating in the BIZUM Service. Additionally, you must select the bunq
monetary account (IBAN) to be registered in the BIZUM Service as the monetary account

5. What is the duration of the BIZUM Service?
The BIZUM Service is available indefinitely. You can unsubscribe at any time, with
immediate effect, through the bunq app. Additionally, you have 14 calendar days from the
date you sign up for the BIZUM Service to withdraw from it without giving a reason or
facing any penalties. To do so, you must notify bunq through the APP within this period.
If we decide to cancel the BIZUM Service, we will inform you two months in advance.
The BIZUM Service will automatically end if your bunq Account is closed or if we are no
longer a member of the BIZUM Service.
Additionally, bunq may block the BIZUM Service for security reasons, such as suspicion of
unauthorized or fraudulent use, misuse of the service, or if there's a significant risk that
you won't be able to meet your payment obligations.

8. How to Make a Return
If you made a payment to an e-commerce store, you can request a refund based on the
store's return policy, within 365 calendar days from when the payment was charged to
your associated monetary account. If the purchase was made in "Two-Step Mode", the
refund period starts when the merchant confirms the charge. Keep in mind, merchants
can set a shorter return period according to their policy. After 365 days (or any shorter
period set by the merchant), refunds cannot be processed via immediate transfer, but the
merchant may offer another refund method.
Refunds within the allowed period will be processed when you request it and the
merchant agrees. The refund will be sent as an immediate payment. The refund can be
full or partial, and will be credited to the same associated monetary account used for the
original payment. If your bunq Account is no longer registered with bunq, the refund will
be rejected and will need to be processed another way, as agreed with the merchant.
bunq will notify you about the return, but please note that bunq has no control over your
relationship with the merchant or any disputes that may arise.
