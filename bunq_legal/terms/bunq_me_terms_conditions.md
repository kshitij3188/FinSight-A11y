1. Welcome!
Hi, we are bunq. And the fact that you are reading this document means that you have
received either a bunq payment request or clicked on a bunq.me link. To explain to you
all about what this means, we have drafted this document. Read on to learn more!
2. Who or what is bunq?
We are bunq; a fully licensed Dutch bank offering innovative payment solutions in Europe.
Our address is Basisweg 32, 1043 AP Amsterdam, The Netherlands and in the Dutch trade
register you can find us by searching for number 54992060.
Do you want to know more about our banking license? Have a look at the Dutch Central
Bank's bank register. You can find this register on dnb.nl.
3. What is bunq.me?
bunq users can create a payment page via our mobile app, this page is called a bunq.me
page. The creator of a bunq.me page can share the link to his/her page with others, for
example to collect money for shared expenses, receive donations for charities, to realise a
creative project.
When you visit a bunq.me page you can choose a payment amount and a payment
method, and initiate a payment to the owner of the page.
4. What is a payment request?
A payment request is a request sent by a bunq user to ask a certain amount of money
(back) from someone else. If you receive a payment request, you can either reject the
request or initiate a payment to the sender with one of the supported payment methods.
5. Payment order
In case you initiate a payment via a bunq.me or payment request, you give us an order to
facilitate the respective transaction, this is a service we offer to you.
In case you give a payment order, your bank and/or the payment scheme you choose to
use is responsible for transferring the payment amount to us. Subsequently we are
responsible for crediting the payee's bunq account and we will have fulfilled our
obligation to you the moment this account has been credited.

6. When does my payment arrive?
We always try to process your payment as soon as possible. Most of the time a payment
will be received by the payee instantly, however it can take up to a couple of days, and in
exceptional cases even longer.
7. Limits
To prevent fraud, misuse or business use of bunq.me, we have implemented several
payment limits. Payments above these limits will be blocked. We can change the
applicable limits from time to time without prior notice.
8. Security
Before you make a payment, always check the following:
● check whether you know the payee (check the IBAN) and whether the amount is
correct; and
● check whether the address bar of your browser displays a small lock followed by
bunq.me (followed by other characters).
Never make a payment, if the requested amount or address is incorrect!
